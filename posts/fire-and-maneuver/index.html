Game Description#

Fire and Maneuver is a strategy game set in the Victorian era. Muskets turned into full-fledged rifles, and Gatling guns began to be used instead of infantry. During the passage you will need to quickly adapt to constantly changing battle conditions. In the new era, it is not courageous knights who win, but iron and blood.

Fire & Maneuver’s gameplay relies on believable story aspects for multiplayer and single-player encounters. The project features many realistic units with authentic weapons and equipment. They were developed through long-term historical research. The gamer can use the landscape, natural objects or his own buildings to gain an advantage. In the story campaign, you have to defeat the enemy who is trying to exhaust the user with constant attacks.

Battles take place turn-based. Generals influence morale and can give hints, anticipating the opponent’s movements. The “simultaneous moves” system involves the actions of both sides at once. Participants will not need to wait for a reaction to a move they have already made. Instead, you need to consider your own capabilities and anticipate your opponent’s decisions. There are 5 empires to rule: British, Russian, Ottoman, Austrian, Second French and North German Confederation.
