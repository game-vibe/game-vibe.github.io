Game Description#

SUPER CRAZY RHYTHM CASTLE will take you to a bizarre world filled with absurdity and frivolity. The mission is clear: defeat the eccentric King Ferdinand and restore rhythmic order to the kingdom. The game features a motley crew of unlikely heroes who must overcome bizarre challenges. From launching beans in an ancient summoning ritual to drowning out a colossal eggplant DJ and smearing gravy on tiny meat people - all while keeping the beat - the journey is a surreal mixture of music and adventure.

Featuring a playlist of over 30 infectious rock, hip-hop and dubstep tracks, as well as a varied and immersive soundtrack, the game will guide gamers through the twists and turns of the story. The game’s cooperative gameplay adds an extra layer of chaos and is suitable for rhythm lovers and newcomers alike. Choose from three- or four-button mode, or dive into musical challenges while your partner solves puzzles, the experience remains accessible and fun.

SUPER CRAZY RHYTHM CASTLE puts players in a variety of challenging scenarios, from music escape rooms to elevator arcades. To succeed, they must have a nimble mind and the ability to multitask while navigating the complex web of music, puzzles and frantic action. For those who crave pure rhythmic challenges, there’s the Music Lab, where you can hone your skills and compete for gold medals on every track. The innovative and unique video game seamlessly blends the rhythm, adventure and puzzle genres into a fun and chaotic escapade. Featuring an unusual storyline, varied musical landscape, and co-op, it offers a challenging and enjoyable experience for players of all skill levels.
