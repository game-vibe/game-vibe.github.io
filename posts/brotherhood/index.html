Game Description#

Brotherhood is a first-person shooter game about escaping from a secret scientific facility. The main character is an experimental subject whose body turned out to be immune to a dangerous mutagen. This substance not only did not harm him, but also endowed him with almost invulnerability, turning into a source of strength. The protagonist will have to fight his way to freedom through the dark corridors of an ancient prison located in the mountains of Switzerland. It is here that a mysterious religious brotherhood conducts inhumane experiments on hundreds of people.

The gameplay is fully consistent with the canons of action films of the nineties. There are no mission markers or any hints, and survival depends solely on reflexes and skills. Moving forward requires the gamer to be constantly vigilant and explore the intricate levels. To get out of the complex you need to:

carefully examine locations in search of ammunition and first aid kits. find key cards to access closed sectors. discover numerous secrets and upgrades for your arsenal.

The protagonist’s journey covers 6 acts and includes 24 large-scale locations. The escape route runs through secret laboratories, hot deserts and an ancient fortress that preserves the centuries-old history of the brotherhood. Brotherhood will face more than 20 types of enemies, from ordinary soldiers to cyborgs and huge combat mechs. To destroy them, an arsenal of 9 types of weapons is provided, including a shotgun, a flamethrower and a mysterious device called the Butterfly.
