Game Description#

eFootball PES 2020 is a realistic sports simulator in which you will take control of a football team, develop it and participate in grand championships. Fight against the champion of Spain, or play for world famous clubs: Juventus, Manchester United, FC Barcelona and many other teams. Here you can participate in battles against another player, go through a campaign, or play in custom mode against artificial intelligence. Enter the training process to learn new feints, improve your ball-striking skills, practice free kicks and learn many new skills. Hone your passes and develop your own strategy on the field, in which several athletes would interact.

In this part you will find new dribbling abilities in motion, as well as technical techniques that were not present in the previous series of the game. The ball received a physical and realistic model. All features were developed with the participation of professional players. Play Match of the Day, choose your side, and compete with newcomers and veterans in exciting battles for dominance in this new mode. The Master League has been completely redesigned. You will be able to participate in an interactive dialogue system, and the transfer market has become more realistic due to frequent data updates.
