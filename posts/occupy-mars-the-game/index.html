Game Description#

Colonizing a planet in Occupy Mars is a challenging but fun task. Here the player will have to build his own base, explore the expanses of the red desert, extract water, grow food and find new ways to obtain oxygen. An open world with great sandbox potential will allow you to seriously immerse yourself in the role of a pioneer on Mars.

Using advanced technologies, Occupy Mars will create a developed network of buildings. A greenhouse, oxygen tanks, generators - all this will help you obtain vital resources and survive independently on a distant planet. The buildings are connected to each other by sealed tunnels, and solar panels can be built around them. It is also necessary to lay pipes and cables between them, and in case of breakdown, repair them using special repair kits. Soldering, hot air control, instrument measurement of indicators - all these skills will become indispensable in case of any breakdown. And in some tasks, a technologically advanced robotic dog will serve as an assistant.

The main goal will be to study the surface of Mars, search for valuable minerals and choose a place to build a future city. An eight-wheeled all-terrain vehicle or ATV will help you navigate steep terrain. Over time, the transport will be improved in the workshop. But you should be careful, because with the change of day and night the temperature drops significantly. After dark, it’s better not to leave the base; besides, it’s important to stock up on energy in advance, because the solar panels don’t work at night. The main thing in colonization is the ability to quickly respond to any disasters, because anything can happen.
