Game Description#

Interstellar Espionage Inc., a strategy and management simulator, focuses on running an interstellar spy agency. The main task is to methodically ruin competing companies through industrial espionage. To do this, you need to explore a procedurally generated universe, find vulnerabilities in information security and strategically sell the obtained information to direct competitors. Each mission is designed for short sessions, allowing you to complete a full game in a relatively short period of time, which makes the gameplay accessible for regular visits.

However, the confrontation will not be one-sided, since corporations are actively responding to aggression. Thanks to the implemented retaliation system, they are able to strike back and even eliminate valuable spy network operators, without forgiving failed operations. Walkthrough of Interstellar Espionage Inc. requires adaptation to increasing complexity: the initial stages are designed as easier, but the ending becomes a real test. To diversify your tactics, there are characters with unique abilities, including Cracker, which is considered the most difficult to master successfully.

Galactic Spy Code: Survive and Win:

Achieving the highest Cosmic Top Secret rank is the ultimate goal and measure of mastery. Corporations quickly increase their power and begin to eliminate competitors in the early stages. The developed technologies provide a significant advantage and open up new opportunities for sabotage. A scalable interface and improved hints provide comfortable management of the spy network.
