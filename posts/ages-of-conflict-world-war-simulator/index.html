Game Description#

Ages of Conflict: World War Simulator is a unique map simulation game where gamers will witness epic battles between self-created or random AI-controlled nations. Here you can control the chosen powers, adjust world events to your preferences and follow their struggle for dominance in an infinite number of worlds. Get the unique experience of watching your own or random nations fight for control of the world. Players will face massive battles, uprisings and political upheavals, and only they will be able to determine who will be the great winner.

The game comes with several ready-made maps and scenarios, but you can also create your own in a special editor, as well as use those created by others. Play with nation names, change world events, and force nations into battle or peace with the touch of a button! Ages of Conflict: World War Simulator is not only a game, but also a world-building tool. With its ability to track long-lost civilizations and countries, you can use this game to simulate a world tens of thousands of years old. The game has statistics that will help you compare ancient states and track their changes over time.
