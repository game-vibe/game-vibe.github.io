Game Description#

The goal in Forgotten Fragments is to recover lost artifacts by completing puzzle levels. Gamers control the protagonists Enid, Ryder and Dayen, and the main mechanics are based on throwing spheres. Using projectiles, you need to light torches to complete the stage. The gameplay combines precise platforming and physics-based aiming, with successful throws needing to be made before time runs out. This key action underlies all location challenges.

The Forgotten Fragments game is divided into two completely independent campaigns with their own plot and unique location design. The single player mode follows Enid’s story as she collects the fragments. The second mode is designed for two people to play together and offers to reveal other secrets together with a partner. Each of them features over 60 unique stages, distributed across 4 themed worlds, providing two complete and independent adventures.

At the end of each world, the player will face a battle with a boss that has unique mechanics and tests all previously mastered skills. The project encourages exploration of locations in search of hidden content. Some of them contain collectible items that unlock skins for customizing the appearance of the heroes. You can also find and light special torches to access secret stages. All levels can be repeated to improve your time and increase your position in the leaderboard.
