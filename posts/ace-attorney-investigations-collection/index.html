Game Description#

Ace Attorney Investigations Collection, a collection of two games featuring the famous prosecutor Miles Edgeworth, invites users to step into his shoes and go on an exciting investigation. Unlike the courtroom drama of the original series, this collection focuses on the detective work that precedes the trial. Gamers will have to collect evidence, interview witnesses and solve intricate mysteries using logical deduction. The collection includes various quality of life improvements to make gameplay more enjoyable. Features like Story Mode and Chapter Select allow gamers to easily progress through the game and re-watch their favorite moments.

Additionally, bonus materials such as character design sheets and in-game background music make the gameplay richer and more exciting. Ace Attorney Investigations Collection introduces new gameplay mechanics that add depth to the investigation process. Players can switch between different art styles, offering a mix of classic and modern visuals. The emphasis on detailed storytelling and character interactions makes each case engaging and thought-provoking. This collection is a significant addition to the Ace Attorney series, providing both longtime fans and newcomers with a solid and fun gameplay experience.
