Game Description#

HELL OF FEAR: Mind Breach is a sci-fi project with horror elements, where the main task is to preserve life in the oppressive environment of an underground scientific center on a distant planet. Exploration of this large-scale facility, consisting of 26 separate compartments, requires extreme caution, as any area contains lethal threats and hidden secrets. The chances of survival are determined by careful management of supplies and careful tactics in confrontations with the monstrous creatures generated by the complex. Further passage requires finding solutions to logical problems integrated into the environment, as well as the ability to remain unnoticed by creatures that react to light sources and movement. Reconstruction of the picture of the tragedy that occurred is carried out through the analysis of discovered diaries, video archives and the remains of station employees.

Major Axel Wex, an experienced employee of the special squad for working with alien incidents, sent to check the emergency beacon from the Tsengon facility, comes under control. What initially seemed like an ordinary task quickly turns into a struggle for one’s own existence. In HELL OF FEAR: Mind Breach, the character will have to confront a mysterious organic entity that has the ability to control physical objects and consciousness. The adaptive mechanism for instilling fear reacts to the style of passage and the steps taken by the character.

The feeling of constant anxiety is formed through a combination of psychological pressure and direct biological threat. Successful overcoming of difficulties is impossible without meticulous management of limited supplies. The atmosphere is complemented by elaborate sound design, which not only enhances the suspense, but also serves as a tool for navigation in space. The narrative part is revealed through environmental elements: found recordings and video materials allow us to reconstruct the chronology of the tragic events in parts.
