Game Description#

Car Dealership Simulator 2 puts the player in charge of a growing car trade. The main occupation is exploring the open city in search of cars to buy at various markets and from other dealers. You can purchase both new and used vehicles, each of which opens up the opportunity to make a profit. Through a continuous cycle of strategic purchasing and effective sales, you will expand the business and achieve a dominant position in the car market, turning the initial enterprise into a large business.

In the Car Dealership Simulator 2 title, fleet management is not limited to simple acquisitions and requires careful financial control. Each vehicle must be inspected in detail to make an accurate pricing decision before resale. The preparation process includes repairing existing damage and washing the cars, bringing them to showroom condition. Cars can also be modified with exterior customizations, increasing their appeal and value.

These actions, coupled with smart investments in the dealership itself, are critical to controlling costs and maximizing the value of every transaction. The first-person view makes it easy to directly interact with the city and dealership operations. Free movement allows you to find the best deals, and gamers can physically inspect potential cars for purchase. To promote the assortment, a photography system is used, which allows you to arrange vehicles and take pictures from good angles.
