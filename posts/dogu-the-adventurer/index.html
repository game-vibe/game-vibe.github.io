Game Description#

Dogu the Adventurer tells the story of a protracted confrontation in worlds filled with ancient technology. The main character, Dogu, finds himself embroiled in a conflict with ruthless bandits who pursue him at every turn. The deceptively cute style of the locations hides serious threats: from traitorous pigs to ancient demons and powerful bosses guarding secrets. The survival of the protagonist directly depends on improving his equipment and timely repair of equipment. Crafting healing potions and new items to use in battle becomes an integral part of this dangerous adventure, where every wrong step could be your last.

The main goal of the explorer’s wanderings is to find the way home. Being cut off from his native lands, Dogu is forced to travel to different worlds, using his hot air balloon to move between floating islands. In the Dogu the Adventurer project, to achieve the goal you will have to not only fight, but also look for allies. The gamer can make friends with the local piglets, enlisting their support in protecting the worlds from raiders and aggressive creatures. Improving the balloon opens up access to new, even more remote and unexplored territories, bringing the hero closer to his cherished return.

The overarching goal of the entire journey is to collect special soul stones scattered across numerous locations. Only by collecting them all will Dogu have a chance to escape his pursuers forever and finally return home. Exploring islands floating in the sky and using portals to quickly travel form the basis of the gameplay. Each new location offers unique challenges and brings him closer to solving the mystery surrounding his sudden transfer to this amazing, but deadly archipelago of worlds.
