Game Description#

Dreaming Blue is a romantic visual novel in which the main goal is to get rid of an ancient curse. Fate brings the main character, a young woman, with two powerful deities who can help her in this difficult task. The first of these is Zenith, the patron of good fortune, who has a playful disposition and is associated with cats. His complete opposite is Sirius, a mysterious ancient deity whose symbol is the wolf. This unfriendly god is mysteriously connected with the protagonist’s past. It is interaction with these two entities that becomes the key to breaking the evil spell.

Characters and deities:

The main character is a young woman who is under a curse that attracts supernatural beings. Zenith is a deity of luck with a playful nature, whose symbol is cats. Sirius is an unfriendly and ancient god associated with the heroine’s past and symbolizes the wolf.

The story takes place in a fantasy world where humans, spirits and shrine deities are forced to coexist. The curse placed on the heroine makes her a magnet for various otherworldly creatures, which forces her to go on a dangerous journey. The core of Dreaming Blue’s gameplay is building relationships with divine companions, which directly determines the success of the mission to lift the spell. The project invites you to immerse yourself in a story where mystical secrets are closely intertwined with personal experiences and difficult choices that determine the ending of the adventure.
