Game Description#

Comic Book Store Simulator is an opportunity to try on the role of a comic book store owner and go from a modest establishment to a real paradise for connoisseurs of graphic novels. The action takes place in a colorful world inspired by Japanese streets, where every day brings new challenges and surprises. The daily routine includes not only arranging goods on shelves, but also interacting with eccentric visitors. You will have to maintain cleanliness, as well as protect the establishment from troubles, repelling shoplifters with the help of a sandal. Organizing special events such as autograph sessions will help attract more customers, and the in-game calendar will make it easy to keep track of all upcoming events.

The core business is collecting and licensing comic books, with over 300 fictional titles available for purchase from over 75 series that parody popular real-world works. In Comic Book Store Simulator, gameplay is closely tied to economic planning, which requires finding rare versions to sell at a high price. To ease the daily load, you can hire staff who will take on some of the responsibilities. As it develops, it will be necessary to expand the retail space, opening up new opportunities for your business. Both the interior and exterior of the building are subject to design, which allows you to create a unique look for the store.

Key aspects of a successful business - from mops to rare publications:

Decorating the interior and exterior of the store to create a unique atmosphere. Gradual expansion of retail space to increase the range and attract new customers. Hiring employees to optimize work processes and perform daily tasks. Host special events and hunt for rare releases to sell at a premium price.
