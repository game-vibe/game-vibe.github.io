Game Description#

Street Food Simulator is a street food management simulator where you need to outperform your competitors to succeed. The confrontation unfolds with other entrepreneurs, each of whom has their own strengths and business strategies. Learning their tactics and mastering various areas of the city becomes the key to market dominance. The urban environment lives its own life: people communicate on benches, creating the feeling of a busy metropolis. An important tool in this fight is a mobile retail outlet, which can not only be equipped with improved equipment, but also visually transformed. Changing the color and adding accessories allows you to stand out from the rest.

The basis of a thriving enterprise is impeccable customer service and expansion of the product range. The cooking process in Street Food Simulator requires precision and timeliness, as satisfied customers are the key to growing your reputation. The ultimate goal is to transform the humble van into a true street food empire known throughout the city. To form a loyal audience and a stable income, you will need to perform a number of tasks:

masterfully fry hot dogs and hamburgers. constantly improve existing recipes. find the busiest places for trading. Serve orders to hungry customers in a timely manner.
