Game Description#

Vault of the Void is a well -planning contractions, where users need to collect abyss stones and make their way through the fantasy worlds of emptiness. We’ll have to fight against various monsters and aggressive creatures that penetrate the universe of people. The game is an atmospheric map of the Rowlaik project, where the users will be able to influence the storyline and the events taking place with their actions and decisions during the passage.

Distributing to the Vault of the Void universe, the players await:

The procedural generation of tests and fights with opponents, which increases levels of levels. Unique decks that will be able to adapt and adjust to the desired build and gameplay style (up to 14 variations for maps). The development of a strategy for the destruction of complex rivals or bosses of locations, which have strong resistance.

Contractions occur in a dynamic format, where users have to spend a limited supply of energy to use maps. It is necessary to combine and adjust your tactics during the battle in order to prevail over enemies. The users have a choice of 4 classes of the character (each for 2 decks and a spell), more than 380 cards (with unique improvements), secret skills and 6 stones of the abyss (runes that increase the strength of the hero’s abilities). In battle, you will have to face 90+ monsters, as well as knock out 250+ magic artifacts.
