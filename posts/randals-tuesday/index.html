Game Description#

Randal’s Tuesday is a classic point-and-click quest, the main intrigue of which is built around a false accusation brought against three students. The protagonists are Randal, Matt and Charlie, each of whom has exclusive access to certain locations, items and dialogue options. The narrative is told through flashbacks, where each of the characters presents their own version of the events that took place before the student council. At the same time, the plot periodically returns to the events of the present time, creating tension. Solving the puzzles that arise along the way does not always come down to simply using objects. Often, in order to move forward, you need to successfully pass interrogations or solve logic problems, and the methods of overcoming them depend on the chosen character.

The main setting is a university campus, filled with the atmosphere of student life and surreal humor. The process of exploring locations in Randal’s Tuesday is encouraged by the presence of many collectibles, among which you can find replicas of famous projects. In addition, to be completely immersed in what is happening, gamers will have to face various situations that require a non-standard approach:

cheat on an exam to get credit. attend a themed party in togas. take part in a role-playing game competition. pass difficult tests to join a closed club.
