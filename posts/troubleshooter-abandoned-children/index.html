Game Description#

In the exciting turn-based strategy TROUBLESHOOTER: Abandoned Children, the player will take control of a desperate character and begin to explore a huge and beautiful world where it is worth fighting opponents, finding new allies, and more.

So, the hero will be a certain boy Albus, it was he who was lucky enough to return after some time to his native metropolis and become a “problem raker” - a kind of person on whom all people place great hopes and responsibilities. At the same time, there is nothing specific here, you will have to deal with a huge range of different things, and you also need to explore the world, although there are more important missions.

First you need to do reconnaissance of the situation. The world around us is filled with terrible secrets, beautiful landscapes, trials, traps and other things and events. Many decisions made during the passage will directly affect further events and the player’s capabilities. Another important aspect of the game is creating conversations with various characters. Incredibly great attention is paid to communication here. The main part of the game is occupied by dialogues, and with each new person the player meets, the player will learn something special for himself. Forks in the answers can affect the future of the character under his care.

And you shouldn’t ignore the battles themselves with various opponents. Battles take place in a step-by-step mode. You will have to think outside the box and think ahead, choose reliable positions and fight not just for fun, but like in chess - to outwit and demonstrate to your enemies your intelligence, and not just brute strength.
