Game Description#

Cargo Simulator starts with managing one logistics warehouse. The main task is to process incoming goods, selecting the most suitable packaging box in terms of size, shape and fragility. Correctly packaged and addressed parcels become the basis of the company’s activities. Accurate and timely fulfillment of orders lays the foundation for business growth and creates a reputation in the local market, allowing you to expand later from a small start.

Business expansion in Cargo Simulator involves strategic investment and task distribution. Gamers can hire employees and train them to optimize warehouse operations and speed up the processing of orders. As productivity increases, opportunities arise to partner with renowned brands, securing larger contracts. The warehouse itself can be equipped and expanded to accommodate a growing volume of goods, and the purchase of various vehicles is necessary to increase carrying capacity and speed up delivery within the city.

Each package must be delivered from the warehouse to the designated address, overcoming city obstacles in the form of dense traffic and narrow streets. Each successful shipment directly affects customer satisfaction, contributing to the expansion of the logistics network. Efficient completion of tasks provides access to an improved fleet and makes it possible to enter into agreements with large distribution companies. The player’s goal is to use this production cycle to become the most powerful logistics force in the region.
