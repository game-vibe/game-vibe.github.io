Game Description#

Love Craft is a dating simulator where the search for a soulmate is based on the principle of selection within a creative circle. Events take place within the walls of Cubiversity University, where the protagonist arrives as a new student. The central element of the story is joining the prestigious Handicraft Club. Here you will have to form a working group of 5 candidates, choosing them from 15 available classmates. The main task is to identify a single partner within one week of working together on a project. The passage is built on the basis of cyclical races, the successful completion of which opens access to new characters for interaction.

Conditions and tasks for passing:

The action takes place at Cubiversity University as part of the Handicraft Club. It is necessary to form a group of five candidates, choosing them from fifteen classmates. The main goal is to identify a single partner within one week of working together.

High replayability is achieved through a modular system of dialogues, the content of which directly depends on the composition of the group, locations visited and other variables. Each decision made has an impact on the development of the plot, leading to one of 30 provided endings. At the same time, the Love Craft project does not punish single incorrect remarks, making the process of building relationships more organic. The narrative emphasizes inclusivity and sincerity, demonstrating that romantic feelings can come in many different forms.
