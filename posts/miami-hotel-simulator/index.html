Game Description#

Miami Hotel Simulator gives the player control of the hotel complex. The task is to turn an old establishment into a profitable business through direct management and renovation. Gamers can decorate the interior of the building and expand its capabilities to attract more visitors. In multiplayer mode, friends work together to manage business, sharing responsibilities for operation and development. Together they improve the hotel and increase its income, coordinating their actions for well-coordinated teamwork.

Gameplay in Miami Hotel Simulator involves a constant cycle of dealing with guests and maintaining order. Preparing rooms for new arrivals means clearing away trash and fixing problems such as clogged toilets. Customer satisfaction is the basis for generating income. The profitability of the establishment increases with the addition of new facilities, including food courts, bars and slot machines. Visitors spend money in these places, contributing to overall earnings and stimulating business expansion.

As your business grows, you can hire staff to perform different tasks. Hiring employees, such as cleaners and administrators, automates management processes. Beyond the walls of the hotel lies an open world that can be explored, allowing you to buy decor and sell unwanted items at the pawn shop. This game gives you the opportunity to purchase cars for travel, find hidden items and build a souvenir shop to sell goods. You can explore the city either alone or with partners in the cooperative mode.
