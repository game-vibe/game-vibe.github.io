Game Description#

LEGO STAR WARS The Force Awakens is a LEGO adventure that can be played online with a friend using an online or local connection. The plot is completely the same as Star Wars 7: The Force Awakens, but is filled with humor and parody. Not only the network mode is available on the pirate, but also all the characters: Ray, Finn, Poe, BB-8. Kylo Ren and others. Some characters are available at the start for free, and additional ones are unlocked as you progress. During the adventure you will meet both many familiar heroes and some completely new ones.

The adventure allows you to explore all the locations presented in the full-length film, and also contains a lot of unique content that was not presented on the big screen. The opportunity for free online play in the company of friends to go through a rich storyline filled with references and signature Lego humor. Gameplay LEGO STAR WARS The Force Awakens is a 3D platformer with beat-em-up elements. At the center of the confrontation is the First Order, eager to enslave the entire galaxy. Key features of the gameplay: single playthrough and online play; true Lego humor, pleasing with a funny parody; all the familiar and favorite characters; many secrets and hiding places; fan service for true fans of the series; multiblocks and blasters to fight enemies; simple platforming and increased combat dynamics.
