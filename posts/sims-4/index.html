Game Description#

The Sims 4 is a life simulator in which you take control of a Sim and control their life. Choose one of the families provided by the developers, or create your own character or several. You can choose your gender, hair and skin color, change your physique, facial expressions and much more, creating a unique hero. If you’re creating a single character, maturity is an important aspect. A child and an old person have fewer opportunities than a mature person or teenager.

After creating a Sim in The Sims 4, you can move into an existing house, or create your own, based on the amount of funds issued to the protagonist. Create a foundation, install walls, doors and ceiling. Fill your rooms with a variety of objects: chairs, tables, nightstands, appliances, electronics, entertainment and more. More than 500 different objects have been provided for you. You will need to calculate the money before installing items. For example, some bedrooms restore energy faster, but also cost more.

The city lives its own life. All Sims existing in the area will come to visit you. Meet them, make new friends, invite them to parties, go shopping or just chat. Each character has their own happiness scale. You must constantly monitor the nutrition, vigor and other needs of each person’s life. Get a job, create your own campaign, start a business, or find other ways to earn in-game money.
