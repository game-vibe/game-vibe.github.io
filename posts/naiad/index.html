Game Description#

NAIAD is a casual, top-down arcade video game with a plot that goes back to ancient Greek myths. The main heroine of the story is Naiad. This unique creature is a lower deity, and also the daughter of the Olympian himself - Zeus. Naiads were created to protect a body of water - each of them was assigned a small river or lake. The creatures had strong magical skills and a strong will, with the help of which they defend their territory and protect its inhabitants.

At NAIAD, the emphasis is on peaceful passage and enjoying very colorful views. As part of the passage, the user will not encounter opponents and will never use force. The user needs to overcome 16 detailed hand-drawn locations, where each subsequent one becomes more difficult than the previous one. All levels consist of a constantly moving river, many sections of which are closed to further movement.

Controlling this amazing creature, the gamer must overcome all obstacles, smoothly swimming through dangerous places. Also, as the game progresses, the gamer is quite capable of interacting with the local inhabitants. There are a huge number of fish, turtles and other cute animals. From time to time they will need to be pulled out of trouble, and they, in turn, will also provide good help.
