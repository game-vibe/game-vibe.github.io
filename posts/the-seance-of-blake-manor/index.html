Game Description#

The Seance of Blake Manor is a mysterious investigation taking place within the walls of the secluded Irish hotel Blake Manor in 1897. Mystics from all over the world came here to the western part of Ireland to hold the Great Seance on Halloween and tear the veil between the worlds of the living and the dead. Into this tense situation arrives private investigator Declan Ward, tasked with finding a missing guest named Evelyn Dean. He will have to act under the strictest time constraints, because only 48 hours are allotted to solve the case before the start of the ritual.

Every action of the detective, be it interrogating one of the eccentric guests or exploring the secret corridors of the estate, inexorably brings the denouement closer. The inhabitants of the hotel live according to their own schedule, and key events occur regardless of the presence of the protagonist, which forces every step to be carefully planned. In The Seance of Blake Manor, a special journal is used to collect evidence, where all the clues found and the motives of the suspects are recorded. By analyzing the information received, Ward will be able to put forward hypotheses and reveal lies in the testimony, getting closer to the truth about the fate of Evelyn Dean.

The first-person narrative immerses you in the atmosphere of gothic horror, inspired by Irish mythology and Victorian spiritualism. The comic book-style visuals highlight the dark and mysterious setting of an ancient estate that holds many dark secrets. The environment is filled with puzzles, and the dynamic soundtrack enhances the feeling of anxiety, reacting to the actions and location of the hero.
