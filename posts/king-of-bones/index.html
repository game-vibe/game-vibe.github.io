Game Description#

In King of Bones, the main goal is to dominate prehistoric arenas in battles with realistic physics. The combat system conveys the weight and power of every encounter as all bites and strikes interact with physically simulated bodies in real time. Gamers dash, stomp and headbutt in fast-paced battles where creatures fight to the last. An attacking Triceratops can throw an enemy into the air, and small raptors can grab onto larger opponents right during a fight.

The Path to Arena Domination in King of Bones:

Physically authentic encounters that reflect the weight and strength of ancient lizards. Fight with 27 types of dinosaurs, each with their own fighting style. Several game modes, including free battles and survival. An interactive environment that can be used to your advantage.

The gameplay offers several unique modes. King of the Hill is a free-for-all deathmatch, and Ascension takes you from hatching from an egg to giant size, constantly fighting for survival. Onslaught challenges you with endless waves of enemies whose numbers are constantly growing. With 27 species, you can adapt to different fighting styles, from the tail swings of an Ankylosaurus to the powerful attacks of a Pachycephalosaurus, ensuring tactical variety.

The environment itself becomes part of the battle, allowing you to use a nearby log or bone as an improvised weapon. Hidden geysers can throw creatures into the air, changing the course of the fight. The project includes an elaborate mutilation system: flesh is torn into pieces, and bodies can be completely dismembered. For fans of observation, there is a spectator mode that allows you to set up battles between dinosaurs under AI control and follow the action while controlling the time.
