Game Description#

Plains of Pain is a dark, post-apocalyptic multiplayer action game that takes you into a world of survival where hope has faded and all that remains is the ultimate end of days. This is no ordinary game - it’s a journey into a sun-scorched dystopian world that will push you to the brink of your sanity. The backstory is chillingly real. When the planet was engulfed by monstrous solar flares, Earth’s wealthiest people fled Earth as part of a secret government initiative, leaving the remaining population to fend for itself. In the face of unimaginable challenges, humanity has fallen apart and the few survivors are driven to madness, driven by the search for food and water.

On the Plains of Pain, two factions fight to establish order, each using their own unique methods. Your task is to survive in this harsh wasteland as a neutral survivor, not loyal to either side. Will you build relationships and ally with one of the factions or will you remain neutral and fight for yourself? This decision will have consequences. But the game isn’t just about survival - it’s also about exploration and discovery. You will have the opportunity to explore the open world, trade and build your shelter. The key to success lies in your own hands. Will you make it out alive?
