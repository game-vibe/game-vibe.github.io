Game Description#

Disaster Band is a rhythm game that you can play online with a friend on a pirate for free or compete online online with other players from all over the world. Each participant chooses his own musical instrument to play a particular song. The opportunity for free online play offers the opportunity to create your own group to perform an entire song together. You can import your music into Disaster Band and share your work with other musicians.

Multiplayer allows your team to go down in history as the greatest disaster group ever. The controls are presented in a very simple and intuitive scheme, offering to get into the rhythm and timings. There are several difficulty levels that add intense gameplay, where one mistake can cost the group universal recognition. By participating in competitions and performing musical compositions well, you need to rise in the world rankings, showing yourself as a true master of your craft.

It’s very easy to ruin a jam session. If you constantly screw up, then not only the musician, but the entire team receives the status of a disaster group. The higher the skill, the more victory points you can earn. Multiplayer is designed for 4 people. Each member of the team picks up his own individual instrument. Choices include violin, cello, trombone and flute. The combination of these strings and horns creates a truly unforgettable musical journey. Your tracks are created via MIDI. You can either add them to your library or share them with your friends.
