Game Description The Curse We Made is a hand-drawn puzzle adventure where the main goal is to bring life back to a cursed world. The protagonist arrives in the city of Gravebrook by train and finds himself in a place completely absorbed in shadows and devoid of any colors. Each of the 32 handcrafted riddles represents a key to healing the land. Successfully solving puzzles not only opens up new locations, but literally fills the lifeless environment with light and color....

Toy Smash Kaboom!

Release Date: 2025/12/04

Game Description Toy Smash Kaboom! is a hybrid project that combines elements of spatial organization, auto-battler and physical clashes. The playing field is a littered table on which you will have to command armies of animated toys. Squads are managed through a backpack-like inventory system, where each slot has strategic importance for the outcome of the upcoming battle. The troops can be led by unique captains, such as the Chef Doll, who specializes in defense, or Doctor Hamster, who relies on the numerical superiority of his charges....

In parallel with landscaping the territories, you will have to arrange your own settlement on the ruins of a former civilization....
