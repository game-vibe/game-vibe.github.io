VEIN

Release Date: 2025/10/24

Game Description VEIN is a journey into a world that has experienced the collapse of civilization, where survival depends on the ability to adapt to constantly changing conditions. The change of seasons and random global events continuously transform the landscape and influence tactics. Here, every sound matters, as opponents controlled by artificial intelligence are able to track down survivors, relying on sight, hearing and smell. This forces you to be constantly on alert, because even abandoned buildings can harbor a mortal threat in the form of mutated zombies....

Battle On! Hero Academy

Release Date: 2025/10/23

Game Description Battle On! Hero Academy is a strategic simulator with elements of idle mechanics, the main goal of which is to create a self-sufficient educational institution for heroes. The fundamental concept of the project is built on the principle of iteration and legacy, where the release of characters at the maximum level is not a loss, but a strategic investment in the future. Each graduate leaves behind valuable relics that are used to unlock permanent global improvements in the academy’s technology tree....
