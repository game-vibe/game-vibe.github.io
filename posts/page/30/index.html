Forestrike

Release Date: 2025/11/17

Game Description Forestrike is a tactical kung fu fighting game with a supernatural element, built on the unique ability to predict the outcome of a battle. The project proposes to hone your skills in mental duels before entering into a real fight. Using the gift of Foresight, the hero can repeatedly play out various battle scenarios in his mind, finding the only correct sequence of movements for victory. However, in a real conflict, any mistake is fatal, because defeat means having to start the whole journey from the very beginning....

Have You Seen This Man?

Release Date: 2025/11/17

Game Description Have You Seen This Man? is a first-person psychological horror, the plot of which takes you into the atmosphere of Texas in 1975. The gameplay is based on a methodical exploration of the streets of Emsdorf County, where you will have to knock on doors and communicate with local residents. Each encounter is unique: from conversations with eccentric conspiracy theorists to clashes with aggressive hermits with shotguns at the ready....

Interstellar Espionage Inc.

Release Date: 2025/11/17

Game Description Interstellar Espionage Inc., a strategy and management simulator, focuses on running an interstellar spy agency. The main task is to methodically ruin competing companies through industrial espionage. To do this, you need to explore a procedurally generated universe, find vulnerabilities in information security and strategically sell the obtained information to direct competitors. Each mission is designed for short sessions, allowing you to complete a full game in a relatively short period of time, which makes the gameplay accessible for regular visits....

Leaf Blower Co.

Release Date: 2025/11/17

Game Description Leaf Blower Co is a meditative simulator that invites you to start your own cleaning business in a cozy town located in New England. The main task is to restore order with the help of a powerful blower, turning chaos into a peaceful landscape. The process is not limited to just blowing away autumn leaves; You will also have to deal with scattered boxes, coconuts and other debris that interfere with harmony....

Sheepherds!

Release Date: 2025/11/17

Game Description Sheepherds! is a cozy cooperative project that invites you to take on the role of adorable herding dogs. The main task is to manage herds of fluffy sheep, which must be led through picturesque flower fields. This process allows them to dye their fur in vibrant colors to create fun clothing and accessories for their four-legged friends. To achieve the goal, the shepherds will have to run a lot and bark in time, discovering magical corners of the local world....

Storebound

Release Date: 2025/11/17

Game Description Storebound is a co-op survival horror game that turns a huge furniture store into a death trap for random visitors. With the onset of darkness, bright shop windows and cozy exhibition rooms give way to impenetrable darkness, and the bustle of the day gives way to ringing silence. Local employees begin to patrol the endless trading floors and warehouses - creepy entities obsessed with the idea of ​​​​providing their last service to lost souls....
