Game Description#

Cosmic Coop is an adventure that takes gamers to their own extraterrestrial farm, beyond which lie uncharted territories full of mysteries and strange NPCs. Descent into the mysterious mines will require courage and a faithful sword, because not only treasures await there, but also various challenges. It is in such forays that one can discover rare materials necessary to create unique items that can transform a personal farmstead and attract the attention of amazing creatures to it. Interaction with local inhabitants allows you to reveal the secrets of this world.

The main task at the alien ranch is to create ideal conditions for bizarre creatures known as Critterlings. To attract them to Cosmic Coop, you will have to decorate your property by placing buildings, ornaments and special objects that will interest curious creatures. Each species has unique needs, and only after those needs are met will the creature decide to stay. However, not all creatures get along with each other, which requires careful planning of their placement to maintain harmony. Among them are Flufflets - charming fluffy and jumping creatures that spend most of their time eating.

The foundation for the prosperity of the alien farm is the cultivation of exotic plants. The harvested crop becomes a versatile resource: it can be eaten, sold at the local market, or used as food for the Critterlings. The proceeds are reinvested in the purchase of new seeds and improvement of tools, which opens up access to further development of the farm and crafting. The first steps in this matter are helped by Fenna, who on the very first day gives the initial task of planting ten seedlings, introducing the basics of agriculture.
