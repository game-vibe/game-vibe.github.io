Game Description#

Elemental Reforged is a reimagining of the epic fantasy saga, combining the best elements from War of Magic, Fallen Enchantress and Sorcerer King into a single project. This title offers you to lead a civilization in a world where each playthrough is unique thanks to procedurally generated maps filled with tasks and dynamically changing environments. Gamers will create their own character to lead an entire faction to dominance, using magic to terraform the land and capture mystical Shards of Power.

The process of managing an empire is closely intertwined with role-playing mechanics. Each unit, from the common warrior to the mighty hero, has a unique history, characteristics and equipment, and their strength increases with experience. Elemental: Reforged features a deep customization system that allows you to not only create armies, but also design cities, research technologies, and form dynastic alliances. Battles can be resolved either in turn-based tactical mode or using advanced artificial intelligence to automatically calculate the outcome of the battle.

The narrative unfolds through three massive campaigns set in The War of Magic, The Fallen Enchantress, and The Sorcerer King, delving into the rich history of the Elemental world. The developers completely redesigned the engine for 64-bit architecture, which made it possible to implement the original plan without the technical limitations of the past. The project also includes an updated user interface, improved graphics, and built-in modding tools with Steam Workshop support.
