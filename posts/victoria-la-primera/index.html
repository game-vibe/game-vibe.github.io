Game Description#

Victoria La Primera tests your wits by solving puzzles and overcoming physical obstacles based on the realities of the 16th century. Interaction with characters of the era and participation in events that shaped the course of maritime history create a unique educational experience. Players will navigate routes through previously uncharted seas, discovering secrets and new paths. Every decision made directly affects the further development of events, requiring the captain to think strategically and adapt to changing circumstances on a long and dangerous journey.

The central character in the story is the ship NAO Victoria - a ship with its own spirit and character, which was destined to be the first in history to circumnavigate the world. The adventure of Victoria: La Primera begins on the banks of the Guadalquivir River, where an expedition to the farthest corners of the planet starts. Exploration of mysterious places is implemented through point-and-click mechanics, allowing you to find hidden secrets and interact with the environment. Resource management and navigation become important aspects of survival on the open ocean.

Atmospheric soundtrack and detailed graphics provide complete immersion in the era of great geographical discoveries. Realistic sound effects and an exciting soundtrack complete the picture, transporting you to the world of sailing ships and brave sailors. This title allows you to learn a lot about the history of shipping, life on board a ship and the difficulties that the discoverers faced. The fate of the entire crew and the success of the expedition entirely depend on the actions and choices made during the passage.
