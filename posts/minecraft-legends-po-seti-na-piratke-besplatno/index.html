Game Description#

On the pirate version ofMinecraft Legends you can explore various biomes with a friend for free, collect resources, fight monsters and build your own base. You can play online with players from all over the world, gathering your team of researchers. In the story, the Upper World is invaded by an evil army of piglins from the Void, which wants to destroy all life. You can take part in confrontations online via the Internet or go through a campaign together with your partners. The mechanics of Minecraft Legends combine elements of action and strategy. You will need to send units to build outposts or engage enemies in battle. The main character is able to fight with melee weapons.

The online game offers you to join forces with friends to fight together against the enemy army. Free online play in Minecraft Legends allows you to fight in teams. You can build castles and outposts by collecting various resources on the map. It is also necessary to build fences, defensive towers, gates, workshops for repairing buildings and other structures. Each biome contains its own unique resources. Rare items drop from defeated enemies.

You can summon up to 20 units as assistants. Having gathered your personal army, you can order soldiers to build a base, send them in search of rare loot, or engage in a duel with fantasy creatures. For quick resource items, you can use a special box that collects all items within range. The main resources are wood and stone. The protagonist is able to move around the world on a horse or other large animals.
