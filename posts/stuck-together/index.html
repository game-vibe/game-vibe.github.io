Game Description#

Stuck Together is a co-op challenge filled with 1990s nostalgia. The space is filled with recognizable items from the era, including videotapes and furniture with distinctive tacky patterns. However, this retro world is actually a house of horrors from which you have to make a daring escape. Five full-scale rooms, including the Kitchen and Bedroom, conceal numerous dangers, traps and mutilated toys, creating an oppressive and tense environment for a bold ascent to freedom.

The plot centers on two children’s toys, mercilessly glued together by their tyrannical teenage owner. Their only goal is to break free, but the path to it is constantly complicated by the intervention of a malicious antagonistic hand. This little tormentor is ready to knock down the characters at any moment with one slap, throwing them far back and resetting the progress made. Successfully completing Stuck Together becomes a real test of both skills and friendships, requiring extreme concentration and patience from your partners.

The project offers a cooperative climb that requires two people to flawlessly coordinate either locally or online. The controls are physics-based, requiring all four limbs to work together to swing, grapple, and climb through levels. Any mistake is fraught with fall, and many threats await you on the path to salvation.

What’s hiding in the cursed house:

Boiling kettles and hot fireplaces, turning everyday life into a deadly obstacle course. Headless dolls and other mutilated inhabitants of the children’s room. Falling debris and treacherously designed sections of levels. The constant threat of being knocked down from a height by the sudden appearance of a tormenting hand.
