Game Description#

Gori: Cuddly Carnage is a dynamic action game with platformer and horror elements. You find yourself locked in with crazed toys out for blood. Using a special surf, you will have to cut circles around the location and chop up monsters. You will play as an anthropomorphic kitten riding a hoverboard. Chop unicorns and don’t get caught by a huge gorilla, eager to devour the protagonist.

The gameplay of Gori: Cuddly Carnage has been jokingly described as Skate and Slash. The main character’s name is Gori. He uses the Frank jet board and uses the services of the AI ​​CH1-P. The board can transform into a whip with blades as sharp as blades. You will have to fight against the Charming Army, which are entire hordes of mutated “cute” creatures. They were once ordinary toys, but now they are bloodthirsty monsters. While in flight, you can perform extreme stunts and perform combinations of blows, killing enemies as efficiently as possible and cutting them into small pieces. Gameplay features: dynamic and fast gameplay; a huge amount of blood, dismemberment, meat and violence; a future world ruled by the Charming Horde; adaptive music that adapts to what is happening on the screen; the possibility of vertical gameplay: take off to the upper tiers and fly throughout the location.
