Game Description#

Maid of Salvation is a dark fantasy adventure where the dynamics of fast-paced battles are intertwined with the exploration of mysterious locations. The combat system is built on a skillful combination of swords, firearms and special moves, requiring sharp reflexes to dodge, parry and launch surprise attacks. You will have to confront more than 20 powerful bosses and hordes of demonic creatures known as Fiends that have infested Purgatory.

Each fight with them is a tactical task, to solve which you need to competently use found objects and secret techniques, as well as build long chains of attacks. The story centers on Shizuka, a servant sent to Purgatory by the Sanctuary’s Master on a mission to save lost souls. However, as she moves through the bleak, but in its own way beautiful lands, the heroine begins to realize that the true nature of salvation and the truth about this world are much more distorted than she could have imagined.

The Maid of Salvation project offers not just action, but a gothic story full of mysticism and unexpected discoveries, revealed through dialogues and hidden events. In addition to the main plot, the world is filled with side quests and secret boss battles that shed light on the stories of other servants who can become both allies and rivals. Character development is carried out through a detailed skill board, which allows you to fine-tune your fighting style to your own preferences. Journeying between life and death, Shizuka must not only battle monsters, but also confront the deception at the heart of her sacred mission.
