Game Description#

Strategic Command WW2: War in Europe covers the greatest conflict in modern history. Take part in turn-based battles with deep immersion, controlling the Axis or the Allies. Control the forces of each nation included in your alliance. Experience the role of commander-in-chief of all operations. Determine priorities during battles, create your own tactics and coordinate your alliance on the global map.

Your task is to direct the army to victory and get it by any means necessary. Your side consists of numerous factions with strengths and weaknesses, industrial capacity, technology levels, troops and equipment. Make the most of every opportunity to achieve your goals. Participate in large-scale clashes in the air, sea or land using a wide range of historical units. The ability to upgrade technology will help you upgrade tanks, armored vehicles or infantry weapons. In each battle, you use limited resources, trying to gain an advantage over your opponent. Diplomacy helps to achieve a truce or lure a state to its side. Through espionage, you can find out what next actions your opponents will take and how to stop them. There are neutral powers on the map with whom you can fight, or agree on cooperation during diplomatic missions. Help your allies with resources or military efforts, and refuse support to your enemies. Features of Strategic Command WWII: War in Europe:

Great campaign starting in 1939. Strategic scenarios starting from 1940 to 1944. The realistic “fog of war” simulates a believable period atmosphere. Make decisions based on partial knowledge of the enemy’s mood and intentions. 3D unit graphics. The ability to upgrade units by choosing from an extensive tree of skills. Diplomacy, which helps to win new allies and, with the help of intelligence, weaken the enemy. 100+ randomly generated events during the scenario.
