Game Description#

Demeo x Dungeons and Dragons Battlemarked, a full-fledged crossover of two famous franchises, offers an immersive tactical adventure in virtual reality. Events take place in the Forgotten Realms, where a military conflict brewing in the North threatens the entire Sword Coast. A group of heroes must join forces to expose a sinister conspiracy and prevent the impending chaos. The project is designed to be played together by a team of up to 4 people, although there is also a single player mode for those who prefer to act independently. All mechanics are built on a system without a Dungeon Master, which shifts the emphasis from roleplaying to joint discussion and strategic decision-making.

The gameplay is based on a turn-based combat system, where success depends on proper planning and the use of unique abilities presented in the form of cards. Each game in Demeo x Dungeons & Dragons: Battlemarked forces you to adapt your tactics to the ever-changing conditions on the battlefield. Two full-fledged story campaigns are available from the very beginning, Embers of Chaos and Crown of Frost, within which you will explore such iconic places as Neverwinter Forest and Cragmo Castle. In the future, the world will expand with downloadable add-ons that offer new challenges.

To confront the dark forces, you can assemble a squad of colorful characters representing classic D&D classes. Available heroes include the dragonborn paladin Boltrax, the halfling sorcerer Tibby, and the human ranger Jessix. They are accompanied by the tiefling rogue Ash, the gnome warrior Tarok, and the elven bard Lyria. Each of them has their own set of skills, the combination of which becomes the key to victory in battles with monsters. Effective joint use of their skills allows you not only to control the battlefield, but also to successfully overcome traps on the way to legendary treasures.
