Game Description#

Strings of Fate 11: Magic Dream transfers a detachment of 5 characters united by ancient prophecies under the control of a player. The awakened evil manifests itself through dreams, invading the life of the heroes, influencing their actions and becoming more and more intrusive. The group needs to determine the origin of these visions in order to prevent a global catastrophe. This task will make them encounter many enemies, develop their abilities and solve centuries -old secrets before their souls and the entire planet are destroyed. The main task is to fulfill its purpose.

The game world Strings of Fate 11: Magic Dream has its own worked out story. It includes many biomes for research: swamps, deserts, forests and oceans. In the open spaces of these various regions, Gamer will find more than 50 dungeons. Inside these locations, the detachment will have meetings with monsters, battles with bosses and a solution to puzzles. These areas are also hidden by ancient artifacts found during a trip with a first -person view.

The development of the characters is carried out through an individual increase in levels for each detachment member. Gamers are available for the development of more than 20 skills, as well as dozens of sets of weapons and armor. The magical component is represented by 9 various schools that provide 99 spells for use in PVEs. For the implementation of special assignments, the game issues medals that note special achievements. The plot moves forward while the heroes are faced with the consequences of their actions.
