Game Description#

Cyberwar: Neon City immerses players in a neon-drenched cyberpunk world ruled by mischievous monkeys. As a brave hero armed with a weapon in the form of a living shark, you will fight against fearsome cyber-monkeys and go through various floors, each of which has unique enemies and mini-bosses. Thanks to its roguelike structure, the games will not be similar to each other. Procedurally generated levels keep the game fresh, and a huge number of items and upgrades open up endless possibilities.

Cyberwar: Neon City is an exciting game with elements of action-adventure and shooter: Neon City is an exciting mixture of sci-fi exploration and intense combat. Cyber ​​monkeys, with their cunning and agility, pose a formidable challenge. Each encounter requires careful planning and strategic use of your shark weapon. Upgrade your equipment, learn new skills, and adapt to an ever-changing environment to survive.

The cyberpunk aesthetics in the game are stunning and immersive. Neon-lit streets, towering skyscrapers and futuristic technology create an immersive atmosphere. The vibrant energy of the city adds even more excitement and tension to the gameplay. As you progress through the game, you will encounter many enemies, each with their own unique attacks and abilities. From swarms of drones to massive cyborg creatures, you’ll have to think quickly and adapt to different combat scenarios.
