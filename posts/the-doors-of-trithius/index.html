Game Description#

The Doors of Trithius is a RPG -Rogalik in the fantasy world with procedurally generated content. Here you have to fight monsters, avoid traps, collect prey and much more, traveling along the mysterious world of Enalia. Every time you start the game, you have to explore the new generated city, dungeon and map of the world. Movement and battle use a unique time system, returning the priority of the player based on the speed of weapons or action, maintaining tactical decisions, as well as knowledge about your enemies.

When creating a character, you have to choose from 7 available backgrounds and more than 30 starting characteristics. Castomization of the battle also does not leave indifferent: more than 50 abilities of battle and 12 types of weapons, each with its own unique style. Fight dozens of unique views of opponents, using hundreds of objects such as potions, armor, medicines and books with literature readable in the game. Dynamic tasks and awards are generated on the basis of NPC roles in each city. Do not forget about skills! A simple increase in the level of skills does not unlock all the possibilities. Players must explore the world, visit coaches and find independently new ways to strengthen their skills. Each skill has a passive bonus and many improvements that can be studied by unlocking new bonuses and abilities. Use craft to create food, potions and medicines, improve and maintain your armor, as well as strengthen your craft capabilities, buying or finding recipes.
