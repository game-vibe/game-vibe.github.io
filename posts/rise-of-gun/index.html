Game Description#

Rise of Gun invites gamers to immerse themselves in a post-apocalyptic world where survival depends not only on finding food or fighting zombies, but also on the ability to create weapons from garbage. After a global epidemic turns most of humanity into mindless undead, the protagonist, having lost everything, sets off on a journey to find other survivors. Along the way, he stumbles upon a settlement in which there is hope for the restoration of civilization. In a world where guns are necessary for survival, the protagonist stands out as the only person capable of creating the tools needed to fight both zombie and human threats.

At the heart of Rise of Gun is a robust weapon crafting system that is flexible and powerful. The game provides players with over 200 different parts that can be mixed and matched in a variety of ways. As a result, more than a million different weapon combinations can be created. The crafting system allows you to experiment, creating weapons from parts that at first glance may seem completely incompatible.

The lack of hard restrictions means there is no limit to creativity, and users can focus on creating effective or perhaps just fun weapons without worrying about traditional crafting restrictions. Creativity isn’t limited to just weapons. As they progress, gamers also manage a store where they can display and sell the weapons they create.
