Game Description#

Katamari Damacy REROLL takes users to a bizarre alternative reality. The King of All Space destroys all the stars. The ruler did this not on purpose, but by accident. He instructs the Prince to correct his own mistake to see how the heir copes with the task. He flies to Earth with his father’s crazy plan. You have to play for your little son.

Katamari Damacy REROLL is an improved version of the game that was released back in 2004. In the re-release, the creators improved the quality of screensavers and graphics, and also added HD support. The modern remaster only improved the visual part. All features of the original remain intact. The developers only added a cooperative mode. It can be played by two people on one PC.

This is an arcade puzzle game with a third-person view. The project has a very unusual gameplay. The protagonist owns a Katamari ball. This artifact is capable of sticking to itself any object if it is smaller in size. With an increase in mass and volume, this sphere will be able to attract larger objects. The hero rolls everything that gets in his way into a ball and then sends it into the sky. Thus, a replacement star appears in space. Then the cycle repeats. This original activity is offered at 20 levels. A limited amount of time is given to complete them. Non-linear stages are divided into zones. The user chooses the route himself. Sometimes various obstacles block his way. To overcome them, you need to help the ball grow.
