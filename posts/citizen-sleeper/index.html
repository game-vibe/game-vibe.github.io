Game Description#

Citizen Sleeper is a bright-looking, dark-looking space adventure where human lives don’t matter. Everyone is someone’s property or a hermit who is trying to find a job and feed himself among the stars. There are many enemies and potential friends around. Having strong connections and alliances in this place is very important, as they can help you get out of a dangerous situation or even save a life.

The action takes place in a remote part of space on an abandoned station, which was chosen by space researchers, forming a full-fledged city living according to its own laws. The main character is one of the government experiments. During his lifetime, his entire consciousness was digitized, and after death it was transferred to an artificial body. But it so happened that he accidentally ended up in this place and is now forced to survive and adapt. During a simple walk around the map, you can meet many characters with their own history and characteristics. Types of NPC Citizen Sleeper:

Rescuers - keep order and help in emergency situations, especially fires Engineers work tirelessly to improve living conditions at the station, providing it with new equipment and extensions. Hackers - can teach their skills and open access to any secret data or areas Bartenders are silent listeners who pour drinks behind the bar; sometimes you can work instead of them and hear a lot of interesting things Street food vendors are generally harmless and sell a variety of international cuisines that can be purchased from them.

The secrets of this universe will have to be revealed gradually, even during everyday tasks. Every day the user is free to choose which direction to go and what to do, improving the skills of the characters. The protagonist will have to find out what he really is and for what purpose he was created. Perhaps it would be better if he surrendered to the government or should he remain in hiding until the very end?
