Game Description#

The King Is Watching is a unique combination of strategy, Roguelike and controls. In this Title, users take on the role of the ruler, whose presence affects the productivity of the kingdom. The central mechanics revolves around the strength of the gamer’s gaze, which dictates the effectiveness of various tasks, including resources, military training and much more.

At any moment in time, the ruler must choose what to focus on, balancing between agriculture, mining and protection from external threats. The kingdom of The King Is Watching flourishes under the vigilant eye of the monarch, but neglect of one area in favor of another can lead to collapse. Resource management plays a key role in gameplay. Water, wheat, coal and stone are necessary to maintain the economy and ensure the survival of the kingdom. The strategic distribution of these resources helps strengthen the walls, hire soldiers and even explore new territories.

The user should carefully decide when to give preference to defense or expansion, and when to invest in magical improvements. Each solution affects the future of the kingdom, making each passage of the game unique. The title presents a variety of trials that develop with each new attempt. Be it damned land or an insidious dungeon, the gamer will have to adapt to changing conditions. Each failure opens up new opportunities, offering constant improvements and opening various strategies for future boards.
