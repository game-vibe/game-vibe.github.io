Game Description#

Cubic Cosmos throws fighters into a deadly competition orchestrated by the goddess. The main goal is to break the endless cycle of testing. The gameplay combines roguelike deckbuilding with turn-based tactics containing tower defense elements. Gamers form an army of pawns and place them on the battlefield to confront enemy forces. Survival depends on the placement of units and the proper use of a unique card deck, and failure turns the character into a sport for the deity.

The strategic depth of Cubic Cosmos lies in its deckbuilding system, which uses a 200-card pool to create unique combinations. The gamer can equip 60 different relics that enhance the characteristics of pawns and allow you to more precisely customize your style. Battles allow you to combine fighters' skills into chains, forming large-scale combos to gain extra turns or deal colossal damage throughout the arena. Successful completion unlocks access to 10 different heroes, each offering unique starting conditions and special skills to build new tactics.

Each escape attempt becomes unique thanks to random events that change the further path. The project offers several modes, including Endless Adventures with pre-defined and random boss battles, as well as daily tasks. The progression system contains 10 difficulty levels available to complete. The final test is Mastery mode, which offers intense conditions and is designed for perfectly matched map sets and refined tactics, challenging the most experienced players.
