Game Description#

Mamon King is a creature breeding simulator where the tactical depth of confrontations is determined by the unique abilities of each monster. Turn-based duels in the 1v1 format are built on a team basis, where the timely use of skills decides the outcome of the match. Activating skills requires the expenditure of special energy, and the use of the most powerful techniques can require serious sacrifices from the creatures, which adds an element of calculated risk to battles. At the same time, repeated use of abilities leads to their gradual evolution, opening up access to new strategic opportunities. Success depends on understanding the strengths and weaknesses of the mentee, because each of them has their own life cycle and predisposition to certain types of training.

The process of becoming a champion is not limited to battles alone. The development of the potential of the wards directly depends on the weekly planning of activities on the ranch. In Mamon King, the path to the pinnacle of glory includes not only participation in tournaments, but also a comprehensive approach to preparing monsters. To achieve the title of the strongest ruler you must:

go on expeditions through uncharted territories in the format of a tabletop adventure. win victories in numerous tournaments against opponents. use inheritance mechanics to transfer power to new generations of creatures. resort to the help of scientists to reveal the hidden abilities of Mamons.
