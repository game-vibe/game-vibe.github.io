Game Description#

Aurora Defense combines Roguelike Deckbuilding with Tower-Defense gameplay with the construction of the base. The player’s task is to build defensive structures in the last stronghold of light, restraining the onslaught of the advancing darkness. The protection of the last goddess is the main goal of the mission throughout the passage. The tool for all actions is maps that allow you to place new buildings, modernize existing buildings and use spells. Gamers form a personalized deck that laid the basis of their strategy against attacking forces.

With the onset of the night, the army of darkness initiates the attack on fortifications. In Aurora Defense Title, all detailed detachments and protective towers enter the battle with the enemy autonomously, creating a phase of an automatic battle. Success depends on the synergy created by the combinations of units, and on the use of specific effects of strengthening. This game offers a choice of several goddesses, each of which provides a unique starting relic and a different set. Developing over time, these deities open access to additional improvements for future attempts.

After the successful reflection of each wave, Gamer receives awards to expand its capabilities. Acquisitions can be new cards for decks, powerful relics with passive effects or collisions with random events directing the gameplay in an unforeseen course. The project allows you to freely combine the collected cards, relics and improvement of buildings to create a more formidable protection line. The confrontation between the advancing darkness and the protection of the goddess are a constant challenge.
