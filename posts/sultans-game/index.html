Game Description#

Sultan’s Game allows you to play the role of a minister managing the treacherous court of a ruthless ruler. Each week, the Sultan requires participation in a ritual during which a card is played, each representing a different challenge. Quests can range from indulging in excess and satisfying dangerous desires to conquest and bloodshed. If you fail to complete the task within seven days, you will be executed.

Sultan’s Game combines strategy and storytelling, forcing gamers to manage resources, relationships, and personal morality while trying to survive. Each choice affects the unfolding plot, leading to multiple possible outcomes. The palace is a labyrinth of shifting alliances, secrets and dangerous temptations. Trust is a rare commodity, and every conversation carries a hidden meaning. Ministers make plans behind closed doors, rivals plot in the shadows, and even those closest to the user may have their own plans.

The Sultan watches what is happening with interest, enjoying the struggle of those who are trapped in his title. Balancing personal ambitions with the need for survival becomes increasingly difficult as the consequences of past decisions begin to unfold. Behind the walls of the palace, the setting continues to move, being formed under the influence of the user’s actions. Wars break out, economies collapse, and alliances change depending on the choices made within the court. Unraveling the secrets of the reign can open up opportunities for escape, power, or even revenge. The line between loyalty and rebellion remains thin, and every step leads deeper into a web of intrigue and danger.
