Game Description#

Caravan Sandwitch takes you to a fictional desert planet where the protagonist ends up as a result of a spaceship crash. The only thing that survived the fall was a small truck. It turns out that the new world is also inhabited by people who have exactly the same problems as on Earth. As part of the journey, the protagonist needs to explore a huge planet in search of secrets. There are a lot of interesting activities in adventure games - from completing routine secondary tasks to searching for traces of an ancient civilization.

Caravan Sandwitch focuses on communicating with locals, who tell interesting stories and give simple mini-quests. However, each of them is unique in its own way and offers a rich experience. The gamer spends most of his time in his truck, which he will periodically have to refuel with the money he earns. The same ones are given as rewards for completing missions. The car is equipped with futuristic tools that allow you to interact with the environment.

You can remove obstacles on the road, for example the same blockage, using a special gravity gun. The user can also safely get out of the vehicle and explore the virtual world on foot. In this mode, the protagonist interacts with the environment, including communicating with NPCs and even wild animals. Numerous mini-games can also keep you busy for a long time. But the most interesting thing is the investigation of strange events associated with certain mythological sand witches.
