Game Description#

Carrier Command 2 is a modern continuation of the strategy that came out back in the eighties. Compared to other titles, the sequel stands out due to its gameplay features. This is not only an RTS, but also a simulator of an aircraft carrier commander. The gamer’s task: to liberate the Telos colony located on the archipelago from the terrorists who captured it.

In Carrier Command 2, the user controls a huge warship. It is capable of destroying opponents with torpedoes, cannons, turrets and missiles. On its deck you can place ground and air units (there are a total of 8 varieties of each type). Here you can construct military vehicles from modules. You can choose the chassis, weapons, additional equipment, etc. In this project you need to capture islands in order to receive supplies for the army. The equipment is controlled in a way unusual for the genre. Here you cannot highlight combat units with a frame and direct them with the mouse.

The user sits in the captain’s cabin and gives orders from there. The player runs between combat posts, presses buttons on panels, and receives information about the battlefield from radars and other devices. Here you need to manually switch map modes, turn on and off the engines of the main transport, increase and decrease speed, turn the steering wheel, inspect the battle site using a drone or periscope, and so on. The game has training, a story campaign, separate battles with bots and a bunch of settings, multiplayer with support for up to 16 participants. Online there is co-op and PvP.
