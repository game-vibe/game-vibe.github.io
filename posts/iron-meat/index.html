Game Description#

Iron Meat is a dynamic platformer made in the style of arcade shooters of the last century. The game takes place on Mars, when a group of scientists disappeared from radar without a trace. The case is entrusted to a member of an elite squad who goes in search of missing people. He must find out what really happened and who is behind this mysterious event.

The gameplay takes inspiration from classic action games such as Contra. The player advances through the level and destroys a huge number of different enemies. Often all the enemies run straight towards the protagonist, so you need to dodge their attacks or react quickly and eliminate them instantly. Iron Meat features platforming elements.

It is necessary to move not only along a horizontal trajectory, but also along a vertical one, rising higher in locations. Various boosters and weapon upgrades can be scattered throughout the levels, changing the type of fire on the cannon. At the end of the chapter there will be an intense boss battle. All leaders are presented in a unique design and require individual strategy and battle tactics.
