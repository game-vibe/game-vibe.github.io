Game Description#

Sprocket will give gamers an unusual experience, because here they will create their own designs and models of tanks. Here you can not only manipulate the type of hull and guns, but also study the specifics of armor to make your model more practical. Variations from different periods are available here, from the early stages of the First World War to the last years of the Second World War.

The main feature of Sprocket tank design is that the gamer is able to customize and modify literally every fragment of the combat vehicle. All manipulations are carried out using the mouse. For example, it is enough to pull the table so that it stretches out, or “grab” a sheet of frontal armor, changing the angle of inclination appropriately. So, for example, even a not very thick layer located at the right angle can reflect large-caliber projectiles. And if, in addition to this, the hull is tightened, the armor rating will be significantly higher.

You can study in detail not only the body of the combat vehicle, but also the cannon. Size matters, and its effectiveness directly depends on well-chosen indicators. Accuracy, projectile range, caliber and other factors can be adjusted depending on what the player wants to achieve in the end. It is also important to have a great team, because the tank will not be able to function without the influence of people. Therefore, the gunner, loader, radio operator and commander should be trained so that their interaction is effective.

When the preparatory work is completed, the turn of tests comes. To do this, the combat vehicle is located in an open field, and targeted fire is fired at it. If the armor can withstand hits or does not allow full damage to be dealt, this can be considered a success. However, if the tank is destroyed, this means that it is necessary to carry out modifications and adjust the indicators again.
