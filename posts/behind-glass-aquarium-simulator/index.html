Game Description#

Behind Glass: Aquarium Simulator, as the name suggests, is an aquarium simulator. The title allows you not only to admire underwater inhabitants, but also to engage in construction. Thanks to this game, owners of real aquariums from real life will be able to recreate them in a digital environment and share their creations with other users. Players are allowed to rotate objects and adjust their sizes. In addition, here you can set the lighting by installing lamps and adjusting the color, angle, radius and light intensity separately for each of them. Users also have access to a wide range of algae and other plants. There are also plenty of stones and other decorations here. There is also the ability to change the background of the room.

Behind the Glass: Aquarium Simulator can be used instead of a screensaver (like those found on older operating systems) on the first or second screen using windowed mode. This indie project features 87 creatures, including freshwater saltwater fish and crabs. They interact with the environment and each other, and do so realistically thanks to elaborate animation. In the built-in virtual crossbreeding laboratory, you can recolor these creatures and breed hybrid individuals. They are also allowed to feed them here. The gameplay is accompanied by realistic sound effects and relaxing background music.
