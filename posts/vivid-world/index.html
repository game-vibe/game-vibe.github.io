Game Description#

Vivid World is a role-playing adventure where the souls of heroes are trapped in gems for battle in the depths of the Underworld. This demonic kingdom is ruled by the protagonist Lemuria, who has mastered the new Gems Magic to conquer procedurally generated dungeons. The basis of the gameplay is the formation of a combat squad from these crystalline creatures. Characters can be bought and sold in the jewelry store, and collecting identical gems significantly enhances their characteristics, opening up powerful combinations for tactical automatic battles.

Tactical depth is achieved through competent resource management and detailed customization of fighters. In Vivid World, each squad member is equipped with special Orbs, and additional upgrades are acquired through the use of stickers and perfumes. Strengthening the bonds between heroes opens up access to unique joint skills that can turn the tide of the most difficult battle. You can explore the intricate labyrinths either alone or in co-op mode, joining forces with a friend to reveal all the secrets of this bizarre world with a bright pop style.

Alchemy of Crystal Souls in the Underworld:

Collecting fighters with the same symbol will activate powerful group boosts. Combining different types of gems unlocks unique abilities. Combining duplicates of one hero greatly increases his personal power. Using Support Gems during auto-combat allows you to strategically influence the battle.
