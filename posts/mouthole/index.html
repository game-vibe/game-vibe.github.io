Game Description#

Mouthole - adventure game combines the study elements with complex tasks, requiring navigation from confusing routes to open and unraveling secrets from gamers. The project will require people to manage temporary resources, while solving everyday problems. The fundamental prerequisite includes literal penetration into its own mouth.

Inside, the Mouthole environment offers a variety of directions for the action of the user and their consequences. Individuals determine how to spend their limited time, choosing specific classes and research lines. This varies from ordinary things, such as physical exercises or participation in the financial market, to a deep investigation of hidden secrets in their own body. Interactions with a small group of characters change their circumstances, while the actions of the user directly affect the narrative trajectory, ending with 11 different endings in several potential plot branches.

The study applies to the internal landscapes of the body, revealing extensive, hidden passages in the oral cavity. These paths provide access to 10 different worlds, each presenting their own set of tests and requiring a reaction, sometimes including rapid evasion. The detection of these paths requires perseverance, offering a chance for a player’s personal metamorphosis. The composition of the surrounding space changes as it deepens, while the internal biological structures undergo transformation. The Title also includes the stock market mechanics, a system of hats, reflection in the mirror and more than 1000 lines of dialogue guiding interaction.
