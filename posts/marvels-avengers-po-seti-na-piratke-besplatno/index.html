Game Description#

Marvels Avengers allows you to play alone or with a friend online, including pirate. This can be done online via the Internet. This is an action-adventure game with beat-em-up elements and a third-person view. Another product can be classified as a service game. It is dedicated to the Avengers and other characters. Fans of Marvel comics can meet many familiar heroes and villains here, as well as find many references and Easter eggs.

The campaign is dedicated to the adventures of Kamala Kahn. During its passage, you can control not only this heroine, but also more popular heroes, like Iron Man or the Hulk. They also allow you to chat with them between missions. The plot begins with a terrorist attack, after which Captain America dies, the Avengers disintegrate, and many superheroes become outcasts. The protagonist receives special abilities and tries to reassemble a squad of Earth defenders to confront the new threat. In addition to single-player content, Marvels Avengers also features online play.

In addition to story missions, there are missions designed for co-op. They can also be completed solo. They offer to get somewhere in time, defeat a boss, protect an object or destroy it. The possibility of free online play allows gamers to form teams with a capacity of up to 4 participants. Gameplay may vary depending on the fighters selected. One prefers to scatter enemies in close combat, while the other can fly or shoot. As you level up, the difference between superheroes becomes even more noticeable. Additional active skills are gradually unlocked. You can alternate between strong and weak attacks, as well as perform combo attacks. In addition, they allow you to customize your charges by giving them different items or even replacing costumes completely. Equipment not only gives an increase to characteristics, but also has various modifiers. Loot is divided into classes according to rarity.
