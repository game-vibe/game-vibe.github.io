Game Description#

Temple of the Green Moon sends a skeptical blogger to the Zhigulevsky mountains to expose local myths about abnormal zones. Its goal is to ridicule and refute all rumors for his audience, guided by a firm disbelief in the supernatural. Soon he is faced with an unthinkable reality for himself, where mysterious forces change his perception. This journey makes the hero doubt his worldview when meeting with ancient rituals, rare collections and mythological secrets that are not amenable to simple explanation.

The gameplay in the Temple of the Green Moon is built in the first person, with an emphasis on the study of the world and direct interaction with the environment. A key tool is a camera that allows you to photograph locations and detect objects hidden from an ordinary gaze. During the investigation of the player and its opening, they directly create unique entries in a personal diary. Thanks to this system, attentiveness and the choice made constantly form the course of the narrative, creating the personal chronicle of the events of this thriller.

The passage includes movements in various historical eras and overcoming locations using Parkuru. Survival is a key element that makes you overcome physical obstacles and enter into battle with mysterious entities living in the region. Meetings with the unknown occupy a central place on the way along the abnormal Samara zone. The decisions made during the investigation directly affect the fate of the protagonist, creating a story with the consequences depending on the choice for its completion.
