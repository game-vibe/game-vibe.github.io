Game Description#

Wordatro! Combines the compilation of words with step -by -step mechanics Roguelite. The players are given a set of letters, each of which has point value, and they form expressions from them to achieve a target. The creation of words with a high cost activates the combo-abdominal, increasing points for subsequent moves. The strategic selection of lexemes and the use of symbols are of key importance for the completion of each stage.

Key features on the path to verbal domination:

The formation of expressions from a unique set of letters. Activation of powerful combo to increase glasses. Strategic choice for passing levels.

Each call in Wordatro! It offers a special combination of symbols and affordable strengthening, ensuring the uniqueness of the races. The project includes more than 50 bonuses for unlocking, which introduce new mechanics of interaction and change the principles of a set of glasses. Some improvements can increase the cost of specific characters or give additional points for words of a certain length, which pushes to experiments with different strategies.

If the current set of letters is unprofitable, you can update the available characters in the hope of the best combination. This game contains 50 special achievements that set additional tasks in addition to passing the final level. Tasks differ in complexity, and one of the tests requires the name of the game itself. A daily competition offers a key for a global competition, where gamers compare their results in the record table. Title supports compilation of words in 7 languages, including English, French and German.
