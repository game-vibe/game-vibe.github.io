Game Description#

In Pawn Planet you will plunge headlong into the crazy world of an interstellar pawn shop. Forget dusty earthly stores; it’s a galaxy filled with strange clients and even stranger objects. Here you will have to test your customer service skills by dealing with grumpy Glurgs and outrageous Zilarians who are eager to make a sale or a profitable deal. But in “Planet of Pawns” you need to do more than just bargain. You’ll need a keen eye to spot a good deal, the savvy to buy low and sell high, and the know-how to distinguish a fake from a real space shuttle engine.

Auctions become your hunting ground, where rare artifacts and forgotten treasures await the discerning pawnbroker. The game doesn’t hold your hand. You’ll have to get your hands dirty by scrounging for parts, fixing broken devices to maximize profits, and keep a close eye on the sticky fingers of space pirates. Every decision has weight. Should you invest in risky repairs or play it safe? The choice is yours, but one wrong move could leave you deep in debt with a galactic ransomman on your tail. As your reputation grows, so does your store.

Updates are unlocked to allow you to display a wider range of products and attract even more eccentric customers. But the adventure doesn’t end at your store door. The galaxy beckons! Explore unfamiliar planets, find hidden treasures, and maybe even pull off a daring heist or two. The universe is your pawnshop, and the possibilities are endless. Pawn Planet is a breath of fresh air in the simulation genre. It’s a quirky, challenging and ultimately rewarding experience that will keep you glued to the screen as you strategize, wheel and haggle your way to galactic pawn shop supremacy.
