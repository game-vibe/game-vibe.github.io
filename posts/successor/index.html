Game Description#

Successor is an exciting rogue-lite strategy adventure that combines the intensity of real-time combat with the tactical elements of turn-based games. You will have to travel through exotic regions, collect, equip and improve a brave team of heroes who can challenge the ruler of the kingdom. Everything happens at a fast pace, combining fast phase combat and tactical depth. To get to the king’s castle, you will have to go through many of its areas, defeating enemies on small diorama boards along with the heroes you acquire as the game progresses. Choose your battles wisely because you risk losing all your progress.

Choose a Lord with his unique story and special abilities to open up new possibilities in battle. Kingdoms and regions are generated procedurally, so each time you will find a new and unique gaming experience. Outdoors and indoors, dioramic battle boards with various environmental elements await you, which you can use as special modifiers. The battle takes place in real time with the possibility of a full tactical pause. You can stop the battle at any time, think through your tactics and watch its implementation in real time.
