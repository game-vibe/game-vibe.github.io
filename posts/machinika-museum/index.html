Game Description#

Machinika Museum takes the player to a museum, a special place where a variety of mysterious machines of extraterrestrial origin are displayed. Each mechanism is a separate puzzle, and to solve it you have to use logic and innovative thinking. The player will need to repair alien machines, opening access to the next levels, gradually increasing the difficulty of passing.

The events of Machinika Museum take place in the distant future, where the central character encountered artifacts of an alien civilization. The main character decides to find out who the Explorer is and go on an intergalactic journey. Along the way, he will solve tricky problems, open locked doors and interact with elements of extraterrestrial origin, which greatly appeals to him. By opening blocked passages, the protagonist will face even greater problems.

However, the ward did not receive any prompts or clear requirements. He does not know how to properly interact with such mechanisms and findings. The result is urgently needed, and for the first time he sees the work that he will face. All the questions in the character’s head will need to be solved with his own hands. The gameplay is made in the style of science fiction with a mysterious atmosphere. The passage lasts 2-4 hours of real time, and the controls are intuitive and will not cause problems even for beginners.
