Game Description#

Alien Market Simulator begins with the player tasked with running a market on a space station in a remote location, as a direct result of a connection with the Intergalactic Police. Restoring freedom depends on the success of the enterprise, requiring daily reports to authorities through the complex’s system. The main task is to develop the facility, which requires modernization, into a point with a large volume of trading operations for alien buyers.

Management involves manually scanning products at the checkout counter, issuing receipts, and restocking items, some of which are sourced through unofficial channels. The cleanliness of the base ensures a constant flow of visitors. In Alien Market Simulator, the gamer runs a fueling station, dispensing plasma, nuclear and anti-matter fuels to various interstellar ships, with improvements for speed of service.

This project also proposes to work on a street restaurant, which involves creating a food area, purchasing ingredients for alien recipes through robotic couriers and preparing food for numerous eaters. Arranging with items from AKEA, maintaining hygiene in the restrooms as this involves spending by customers, and preventing creatures from stealing inventory are additional tasks. Robotic staff automates processes; financial obligations must be met to prevent direct action by The Pardatrox. The surrounding world is inhabited by alien life, and some organisms are capable of scaring away guests of the outlet.
