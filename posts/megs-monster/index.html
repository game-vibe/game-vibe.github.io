Game Description#

Meg’s Monster is a fun indie RPG adventure where you take on the role of a grumpy ghoul from the underworld tasked with helping a lost little girl find her way home. But don’t be fooled by her creepy-cute aesthetic, because if this girl cries, your whole world will collapse before your eyes. Entering a twisted dungeon world where humans are just breakfast for the monsters and mutants that roam its lands, you will meet Roy, a huge ogre who has no interest in feeding on people. One day he stumbles upon Meg, a lost girl whose tears could bring about the end of the world. Thus begins your journey to help her find her mother and keep her out of harm’s way.

Meg’s Monster is a short but story-driven JRPG where you control Roy, a virtually indestructible character with 99,999 HP. But don’t let that fool you, because keeping Meg safe is the real challenge. You’ll have to use all your wits and skill to master the unique mechanics and mini-games that accompany each battle, while comforting Meg with her favorite toys so she won’t cry. Along the way, you’ll meet a variety of quirky and colorful characters, all rendered in Odencat’s signature pixel art style that makes even the scariest monsters look cute. As the story progresses, you will find yourself drawn into a world full of drama and intrigue, where even previously defeated enemies can become your allies thanks to additional events.
