Game Description#

16 Cyber-Personalities: Survivors is a dynamic top-down roguelight shooter, the arena of which is the protagonist’s subconscious. The concept is based on the MBTI personality typology, which is reflected in the key mechanics. The gamer controls four elemental spirits, each of which symbolizes certain character traits. The process of their improvement occurs through unique branches of pumping, which allows you to turn elementals into powerful companions. The main task is to repel the attacks of sinister forces hiding in the depths of the mind. Every fight becomes a chance to learn more about your true self.

Interaction with the inner world:

Control of four elemental spirit allies. The connection of each spirit with a specific MBTI personality type. Development of elementals through personal leveling branches. Battles with hostile entities in the hero’s subconscious. Creating unique combinations of abilities for combat. Exploring the depths of the mind as the main arena. Dynamic shootouts with a camera view from above. Overcoming internal fears as the main plot goal.

The high intensity of collisions requires the player to constantly concentrate and quickly react to changing conditions. Each battle in 16 Cyber-Personalities: Survivors provides an opportunity to gain deeper insight into yourself through the creation of unique combinations of abilities. The process of forming dynamic assemblages of skills becomes a metaphor for exploring one’s own versatility. The gameplay encourages the establishment of strong connections between the character and his internal assistants. The ultimate goal of this extraordinary adventure is to conquer your deepest fears and gain complete control over your inner world.
