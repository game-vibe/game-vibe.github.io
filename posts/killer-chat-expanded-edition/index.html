Game Description#

Killer Chat! - Expanded Edition - a dating simulator that plunges you into a deadly adventure, where the price of a mistake is your own life. The main character, being a reporter and aspiring writer, is forced to pose as a maniac in a closed online chat for six months. This risky game has a dual goal: to collect exclusive material for a future crime novel and not to become another victim of your new acquaintances. In this community of real killers, you will not only have to support the legend, but also build complex relationships that can develop into romantic feelings. The ending of the story depends entirely on the decisions made, offering either to find happiness or to die tragically.

The reason for getting into such a dubious company was careless search queries on the darknet related to methods of hiding evidence and methods of murder. It was this activity that caught the attention of the mysterious user ERROR! UNKNOWN, who sent the invitation. Gameplay Killer Chat! - Expanded Edition is built around communication with colorful characters, including the pyromaniac Felicie, a serial killer from the 90s named Vince, the poisoner Ai Hua and the surfer Luca, who commits crimes through negligence. Interaction occurs through private messages, group discussions, and voice and video calls.

A deep customization system allows you to create a unique protagonist by customizing his name, gender, appearance, character, and even a fictional biography of the pseudo-murderer. In addition to chatting, a personal computer desktop is available where you can write a book, play mini-games and change the appearance of your character. Every action and dialogue choice directly affects the progression of the story, giving access to more than 20 different endings. The project offers an unusual mechanic, where conventionally “good” endings often lead to sad consequences, and “bad” endings turn out to be life-saving.
