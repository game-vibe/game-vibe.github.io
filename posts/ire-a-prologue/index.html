Game Description#

Ire A Prologue is a story in which a young girl named Emily confronts the relentless pursuit of a creepy creature. The events take place in 1986 on board a ship lost in the Bermuda Triangle, where every rustle could be the last. The main threat is a monster called Drugen, whose behavior does not obey predetermined scenarios, which makes every meeting with him unpredictable. To survive, the heroine must hide in the shadows, use ventilation shafts and lockers, relying on stealth and wits rather than strength.

The purpose of being on this ship is to search for Emily’s missing father, and the keys to solving this mystery are hidden behind thirteen mysterious doors. Progress through the gloomy corridors is complicated by a time loop that constantly returns the girl to the beginning of her nightmare. However, each new cycle is not a meaningless repetition, since it allows you to find new clues and gradually piece together fragments of the tragic history of the ship. Project Ire: A Prologue immerses you in an atmosphere of horror inspired by classic works of the 80s, offering a look at the world through the prism of a child’s perception of fear. This story is just an introduction to a larger, darker universe full of secrets and monsters.
