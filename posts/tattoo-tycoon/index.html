Game Description#

Tattoo Tycoon is a management simulator in which you will revive the tattoo culture in the city of Tattooga Bay, which was once its center. The protagonist, whose appearance and backstory can be created independently, gets the last remaining salon. The main task is to turn a modest studio into a thriving chain of establishments, returning the city to its former glory. This will require not only skillfully applying designs to the skin, but also building relationships with a variety of residents, among whom there are eccentric clients and problematic artists, as well as an influential businessman known as the Tycoon.

Business development will require an integrated approach. Attracting visitors is achieved not only by the quality of work, but also by furnishing the premises with furniture from INKEA, as well as by creating a comfortable atmosphere. It is important to communicate with clients to understand their preferences and offer a design that they cannot refuse. In addition to tattoos themselves, the range of the salon at Tattoo Tycoon can be expanded with related products: skin care products, T-shirts and posters. Time and energy management becomes key as it is necessary to decide when to open and close the salon to complete daily tasks.

As popularity grows, it becomes possible to hire staff, from other tattoo artists to administrators, which helps cope with the growing flow of clients. Personal connections and word of mouth play just as important a role as marketing campaigns. Having gained the trust of the townspeople and earned a reputation, the most famous artists earn the right to participate in the prestigious Ink-Vasion competition, proving their skills.

Aspects of becoming a tattoo artist:

Unlock over 100 unique tattoo designs. Select and arrange one of the many salons in a busy city. Careful study of the preferences of each group of clients to create the perfect design. Create your own character and live a unique story. Management of all aspects of activity: from creativity to entrepreneurship.
