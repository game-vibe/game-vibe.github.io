Game Description#

FUMES plunges you into the heart of a chaotic wasteland world where vehicular combat reigns. Users take control of customizable vehicles, equipping them with weapons and armor to survive the unforgiving desert landscape. The title brings an old-school feel to car combat, drawing on the style of classic rally games with driving mechanics that reflect a time before flashy graphics and complex controls. It’s fast-paced, straightforward, and has genuine tension.

As users race through the wasteland, they will face waves of enemies, bosses, and challenges that require quick reflexes and even quicker decisions. The goal is simple: destroy and survive. From delivering suspicious packages during a chase to racing against some of the most ferocious racers in the wasteland, FUMES never slows down. Gamers can also unlock new cars and upgrade their cars, allowing for more customization and styling.

The more enemies you defeat, the more powerful you become, which keeps your adrenaline levels high. The world is huge, filled with endless procedural environments that encourage exploration. There are no loading screens to interrupt the action. Once you enter the game, you are already in it. The title encourages users to take a break after battles, drive through desert lands, soaking up the sights and sounds of the wasteland.
