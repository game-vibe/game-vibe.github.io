Game Description#

Pereelous is a role-playing adventure in which the fate of the character and all his actions are completely subordinated to the results of the rotation of the slot machine reels. The process of passing is not reduced to blind chance, since success largely depends on the ability to stop the rotation in time to form a profitable combination of symbols. Combinations of signs lead to both the activation of powerful effects and destructive consequences, which forces you to constantly adjust your tactics, based on current results and artifacts unlocked along the way. Events unfold in dark, procedurally generated dungeons with carefully crafted pixel graphics.

Each descent into the mysterious depths becomes a unique challenge, where competent risk management plays a key role. In Pereelous, you need to carefully balance your resources, assessing when it’s worth going deeper and when it’s better to save your strength. The presence of permanent death increases the value of every decision, because any mistake can be fatal. The outcome of each spin determines the entire range of available options:

attack on numerous opponents requiring a special approach. using defensive maneuvers to survive dangerous situations. collecting valuable loot and artifacts to strengthen your character. encountering random events that can radically change the course of the adventure.
