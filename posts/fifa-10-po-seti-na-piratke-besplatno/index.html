Game Description#

FIFA 10 is a football simulator in which you have the opportunity to play online with a friend, competing for free on a pirate platform online or on one device. The gameplay has been greatly expanded thanks to an improved physics engine. For example, the animations when fighting for the ball in the air have been updated, the system of shots, goalkeeper exits, etc. have been expanded. Football players in FIFA 10 can move in 8 directions. Network play allows up to 4 people to play in one team.

Artificial intelligence carefully monitors the player’s strategy and adapts to tactics. The higher the difficulty, the more intense the opponents' pressure. The list of licenses has been updated. The Dutch national team and the Russian Premier League have been added. The Pro Season has improved. Free online play includes creating your own team. You can create 11 football players from scratch, choosing height, appearance parameters, characteristics, etc. You can create two clubs. Innovations and improvements in FIFA 10: Russian Championship - 16 clubs available, including Spartak, Zenit, Akmar, Rubin, etc.; Dribbling - a 360-degree dribbling system has been introduced and ball possession has been improved; Practice - a separate mode to hone skills; Smart athletes - football players evaluate the field and adapt their strategy to the player’s actions; Various shots - many new animations are available, physics has been improved, etc.; Defense - various tackles based on timings have been introduced; Transfers - all current transfers have been added at the time of the game’s creation.
