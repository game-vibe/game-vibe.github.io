Game Description#

Peaceful Days immerses you in the atmosphere of a cozy town, where communication with local residents becomes an integral part of everyday life. Regular festivals allow you to get to know your neighbors, each with their own personalities and schedules, and take part in unique events. Behind the façade of outward hospitality there are numerous secrets waiting to be discovered. All this rich social life revolves around the main thing - the creation and development of their own prosperous economy, which has become the center of attraction for the entire community.

Building your dream farm starts with a clean slate. The protagonist will have to develop the territory from scratch, turning an abandoned area into a model estate. Freedom in decision-making allows you to arrange your property according to your own taste, choosing between different crops and buildings. At Peaceful Days, animal welfare is one of the pillars of the economy. Breeding livestock and poultry not only brings income, but also opens up new opportunities for expanding the economy. Watching everything that happens from above, you can effectively plan your every step and admire the fruits of your own labors.

Basics of a prosperous farm life:

Complete transformation of neglected lands into a thriving economy. Caring for a variety of animals and obtaining valuable resources from them. Building relationships with city residents through dialogues and joint events. Participate in seasonal festivals and city competitions to receive rewards. Explore the surrounding area in search of hidden stories and useful items.
