Game Description#

Kabuto Park immerses players in the summer atmosphere of collecting collectibles and competitions. The heroine, Hana, spends her holidays aiming for the Bug Fighting Championship. This task requires locating various insects, increasing their individual powers, and forming teams for structured meetings. This project allows us to observe a diverse population of these creatures in a specific natural environment.

Kabuto Park includes a collection system for over 40 different species of fauna such as beetles, butterflies, bees and dragonflies. Capturing these creatures depends on the precise timing of swinging the net. Competitive matches occur when bug fighters strive to push their opponents out of the ring. Each winged or crawling creature selected for your team adds unique cards to the battle deck, allowing for tactical moves during these small skirmishes. Increasing your pet’s combat power is achieved with the help of certain consumables.

Progressing in this title involves obtaining improved equipment, which opens up access to additional areas and makes rare insects more common. This type of equipment also simplifies the capture process. Players can purchase items such as specialized boots and nets to make collecting easier. The total gameplay duration ranges from 2 to 4 hours, which is dedicated to collecting fauna samples, participating in competitions that require planning, and immersing yourself in the summer atmosphere. This project offers a direct path to the championship goal.
