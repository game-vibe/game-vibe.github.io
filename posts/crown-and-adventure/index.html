Game Description#

Crown and Adventure is a project in which managing cities, appointing ministers, and issuing edicts form the basis for building a powerful dynasty. The path to dominance lies through the competent management of taxes, the development of technology and the strengthening of one’s faction, which allows one to turn captured territories into prosperous centers of power. Large-scale battles unfolding from a top-down side view make it possible to control armies numbering up to 1000 units on the screen at the same time.

To achieve your ambitious goals, you will have to gather an elite legion under your banner. Becoming a legendary lord is impossible without loyal companions, so more than 50 unique heroes are available for hire around the world, each of whom has their own history and abilities. Crown and Adventure’s troop arsenal includes nearly 100 different types of soldiers with unique tactical roles. The formation of the army occurs by attracting representatives of different cultures, be it warriors from the lands of Chen-Yuan, inspired by the ancient East, or fighters from the desert regions of Sasin.

The action takes place in the vastness of the continent of Eulogis, whose history was overshadowed by the ancient Cataclysm. This 2D pixel art world is made up of 17 unique regions that you can freely explore on horseback. As you travel across a huge, seamless map, you’ll discover hidden resources, dungeons, and the mysterious Mysterious Realm. Every decision made directly affects the further development of events, allowing players to create their own unique story in this fantasy world.
