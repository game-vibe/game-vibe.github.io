Game Description#

Where Is Mommy is a retro horror game in which the survival process is based on careful observation of the cyclically changing environment. With each new turn, reality undergoes greater distortions, and barely noticeable deviations from the norm penetrate into the usual scenery. The main task is to identify these anomalies, since this is the only way to break the time loop and not become part of this crazy world. The story begins in an endless bazaar, but over time, the distorted space opens up access to previously unseen locations, where a growing sense of anxiety creates a tense atmosphere.

Gameplay Basics:

Anomaly observation is the careful study of the cyclically changing world in order to detect the slightest deviations, such as the movement of objects, the appearance of new details, or changes in lighting. Spatial puzzle solving is a mental challenge that increases in difficulty with each new chapter and requires out-of-the-box thinking to progress through the story. Stealth movement is the need to hide from an inexorable pursuer, using elements of the environment as cover and creating distractions in order to survive. Overcoming the time loop is the main goal of the game, which can only be achieved through the accurate identification of all distortions of reality in one cycle. Exploring surreal locations - a journey through constantly transforming worlds, from an endless bazaar to other bizarre places born of a distorted consciousness.

Take control of a lost child who follows his mother’s subtle trail through surreal worlds. Progressing through the plot of Where Is Mommy requires solving a variety of puzzles, the complexity and type of which change from chapter to chapter, forcing you to show remarkable ingenuity. In addition to intellectual tests, the protagonist will have to hide from a dangerous pursuer. To survive and explore locations in search of the necessary items, you must constantly remain unnoticed and use elements of the environment to distract the enemy’s attention.
