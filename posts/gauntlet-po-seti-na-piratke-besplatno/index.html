Game Description#

Gauntlet is a 3D remake of the arcade classic. The re-release of Slayer Edition allows you to play online with a friend, and this can be done even on a pirated platform. Here you can have fun locally. The co-op is also available online and in Hotseat mode. The project itself is a fantasy action game with a top-down view. In Gauntlet there are 4 characters to choose from: Valkyrie - her name is Tyra, and she can fight with a one-handed blade and throw her shield like a boomerang; Mage - the wizard Merlin, who knows 9 spells and knows how to combine them; Warrior - melee fighter Thor , capable of accelerating attacks and delivering circular strikes; Elf - marksman Questor, who prefers to set traps and fire from a bow.

It is worth noting the possibility of free online play. Users can run in a group of two, three or four through the dungeons, fight monsters, dodge traps, compete with each other for the right to walk with the crown and collect gold. Money is spent in the store, which is accessible between levels. There you can buy armor and artifacts. Equipment changes the appearance of adventurers. And relics provide additional abilities. For example, a ring of cold allows you to freeze opponents, and special boots speed up movement. But to use these skills you need special bottles. Health is replenished with food, like turkey. Leveling up heroes is based on obtaining achievements, and each one has their own. If an adventurer kills a hundred mummies, he will begin to deal additional damage to that particular type of monster. For those for whom online play is not suitable, there is a single-player option.
