Game Description#

Outbreak: The New Nightmare throws you into a terrifying co-op survival horror experience for free. You can play online with a friend on a pirate game online to feel all the horror and atmosphere of the game. No tutorials - you are Lydia, and you are trapped in a city overrun by the undead. With limited resources and only one life, can you fight your way through the nightmare and escape in multiplayer?

Team up with a friend for a truly nerve-wracking co-op experience, or go it alone if you like your odds. Outbreak: The New Nightmare is a love letter to the classic Resident Evil and Silent Hill games, offering nostalgia along with chills. Remember the fixed camera angles and tank controls that made the early games so intense? Outbreak: The New Nightmare not only has them, but also offers a more modern control scheme for those who prefer it.

But it’s not just about shooting zombies. Outbreak: The New Nightmare forces you to act strategically. There are few cartridges, so every bullet counts. You’ll have to search the environment for supplies, solve puzzles to move forward, and be on your guard - the city is filled with hungry undead, and they’re unforgiving.
