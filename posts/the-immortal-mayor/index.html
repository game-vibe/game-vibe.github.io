Game Description#

The Immortal Mayor is a simulator of god and city building. It is also a strategy and sandbox game. In the game, the user plays the role of a deity. He can interact with other divine creatures. The project has a non-standard system for placing buildings. If you place a house next to another building, it will begin to influence it. You can also install various decorations. They also influence objects. It is possible to combine their effects. Hundreds of types of decorations not only make the settlement more beautiful, but also help it prosper. Construction does not happen instantly. First, the player allocates a place for a site, and then residents come there and begin phased construction.

In The Immortal Mayor, the user is offered not only to rebuild and develop a settlement. He also needs to meet the needs of the population, providing everyone with housing, jobs, infrastructure, entertainment, and defend the settlement from monsters. To do this, you need to build walls and place protective towers. If you ignore monsters, they will begin to break houses and steal resources.

The gamer can use various spells. He is allowed to use magic to support his charges in battles with monsters. He has a set of basic spells. To use more powerful magic, it is necessary to accept offerings from people and seek help from other deities.
