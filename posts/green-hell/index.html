Game Description#

Green Hell is a survival simulator that fans of real hero exhaustion in the wild jungle have been waiting for a long time! The main character will visit the dark forests of the Amazon with only a walkie-talkie from objects of “his time”, and he will be helped by his hands and head, which over time will begin to betray. Survival, exhaustion, mentally damaged health of the main character. You follow the entire process in the game.

Finding himself completely alone on an unfamiliar island, the hero finds a walkie-talkie in which he hears a native, human voice speaking his native language. The island has been studied geographically by cartographers, but the protagonist cannot even imagine what awaits him on the path to salvation. The desperate Amazons, with their rituals and customs, do not accept the new hero - they consider him a fiend from hell.

The difficulty of the simulator - Green Hell - is that the main character will gradually go crazy from what he sees around him; from the fact that he will always lack food to move on; from debilitating loneliness, from a basic lack of human touch. The ending of the game can be twofold: we will either begin to conduct our own rituals in order to lure people, or we will completely go crazy.

You can craft everything in the game. Starting from a spear to the roof of your small house. There are resources scattered around the hero that must be collected in any case. The further you go into the story wheel, the more items you find around you. Plus, the character has perks that need to be upgraded as the story progresses: increased survival, increased inventory, vigilance, dexterity and other human qualities necessary in the jungle.
