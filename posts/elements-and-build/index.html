Game Description#

Elements and Build is an adventure game that combines strategy and creativity, asking gamers to design their own paths to victory. The idea is simple: survive waves of monsters by building a labyrinth that will keep them at bay. However, the mechanics and challenges that adventure games offer make the process of building and surviving something that requires skill and insight. Each level is randomly generated, meaning players are faced with new obstacles and opportunities each time they play through.

The goal remains clear - defeat the monsters, but how you complete it is up to you. At the heart of Elements and Build is the ability to use elemental skills to create defenses and manipulate the environment. Each element has its own set of abilities that gamers can use to their advantage. For example, fire can be useful for damaging monsters, and water can be useful for slowing down their movement. However, the tricky part is knowing when to use each skill and how to combine them.

Strategy plays a huge role here: the right combination of elements at the right time can mean the difference between surviving the wave and defeat. A distinctive feature of the adventure game is the procedural generation of levels. Each card is different from the other, which allows users not to be caught off guard by the usual. This randomness forces players to think on their feet, adjust their plans on the fly, and adapt to new scenarios every time they start a level.
