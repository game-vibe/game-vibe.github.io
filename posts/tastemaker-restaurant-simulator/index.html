Game Description#

TasteMaker: Restaurant Simulator is a management simulator for a small restaurant that is constantly growing under the strict guidance of the player. The user will have to correctly distribute the available money in the account in order not to turn the food establishment into an unprofitable business that will be forced to close. The game from an indie developer, for whom this brainchild is a debut in the video game industry, gives the gamer full and extensive rights to manage this place, which obliges him to organize all the processes required for normal functioning independently.

The gameplay of any of the available modes begins with the need to lay the foundation of your future elite restaurant for the richest and most influential people. The player is given complete freedom in choosing the shape of the establishment, the color of the wall, the number of windows and the layout of the kitchen. Before opening, you need to recruit a team consisting of a chef and his subordinates, waiters, cleaners, hostesses, and so on, depending on needs. Maintaining your own premises in proper sanitary condition becomes quite an important task, along with preparing delicious dishes and providing quality service to visitors.
