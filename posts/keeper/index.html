Game Description#

Keeper’s narrative begins with the awakening of a long-dormant lighthouse on an island. Moved by an unknown purpose and accompanied by a seabird, the structure sets off on an odyssey through the surrounding area. This trek takes the duo towards the center of the island and into unexplored areas. This third-person puzzle adventure tells the story without a single word, conveying the story through cinematic and atmospheric elements. Events take place on a secluded island in a lost sea, at the foot of a distant mountain peak.

Keeper’s gameplay focuses on world exploration and puzzle solving in a surreal environment. Players guide the lighthouse and its feathered companion through a series of challenges. Progress through the plot is marked by a series of mysterious transformations affecting the main character. The task is to penetrate deep into the island by navigating a linear path in a stylized fantasy world. This game is a single-player adventure that focuses on its story and mystery, driving the central characters from one location to another.

A key gameplay mechanic revolves around the partnership between a moving lighthouse and a seabird. Their combined efforts are necessary to overcome the obstacles created by the natural and surreal elements of the environment. As they move forward, the withering shoots visibly grow and intertwine around them. This is a philosophical journey, an expedition leading two heroes through strange territories. The final destination is a place beyond ordinary understanding, completing an odyssey of metamorphosis.
