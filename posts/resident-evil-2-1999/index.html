Game Description#

Resident Evil 2 1999 is a continuation of the cult zombie horror series authored by Shinji Mikami and Capcom. This time the events move to the city of Raccoon City, after the T-virus escaped from Spencer’s mansion and infected the entire metropolis. The Umbrella Corporation sends a squad of specially trained fighters to steal a strain of the more dangerous G-virus from the hands of scientist William Birkin. Their plan fails and will bring disaster on a global scale.

At this time, Leon S. Kennedy, a newcomer to the local police department R.P.D., arrives in the city. and Claire Redfield - Chris’s sister from the first part, who arrived in search of her brother. By coincidence, their paths crossed, but an accident forced them to separate, and therefore several scenarios will be available to play through. The main innovation of the sequel lies in the method of storytelling, which can be called unique to this day. The game has two protagonists, but four different stories. Having started the campaign for Leon A, at the end of the game a save occurs, loading which begins the scenario of Claire B, where the player will see an alternative path for the heroine, trying to get out of this nightmare parallel to Leon. After that, you can start the scenario of Claire A, repeating the same action, but now going through the plot of Leon B. Moreover, in each of the four campaigns, new levels, characters and segments of history will be available. Instead of the estate from the first part, you will have to explore a huge police station, which is an analogue of a museum, visit the secret laboratories of a pharmaceutical corporation and rush through the streets of an infected city. The game, compared to its predecessor, placed greater emphasis on action, and therefore the combat arsenal has expanded significantly. Each of the two heroes has some special guns. For example, Leon can use the Ingram rapid-fire machine gun, and Claire can use the Crosbow crossbow. At the same time, exploring locations and solving puzzles and puzzles remained in place. In addition to shooting zombies and other monsters, the heroes will face the indestructible Tyrant, named Mister-X. Each of them will meet different minor characters, including:

The girl Sherry is the daughter of the scientist Birkin; Brian Irons - corrupt police chief; Ada Wong is a double agent and the game’s dark horse; Annette Birkin is a female scientist and William’s wife.
