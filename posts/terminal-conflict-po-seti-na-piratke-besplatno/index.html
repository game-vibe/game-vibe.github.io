Game Description#

On the pirateTerminal Conflict you can play online for free with a friend, fighting each other as two super-powerful powers. Events take place during the Cold War era. The style is based on retro simulators from the past. You can play both on a local network and online online. The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of one of the nuclear countries, trying to establish world domination.

The gameplay is represented by turn-based mechanics. Each participant performs a series of actions, trying to strategically outplay the opponent. The bottom line is an arms race. An online game is available, aimed at confronting two players, and a single player campaign. The game panel in Terminal Conflict is presented in the form of a retro computer. It also marks the Doomsday Clock. By performing certain actions and gaining superiority over the enemy, the clock hand takes one step forward.

Mechanics cover all aspects of strategy games, allowing you to deal with issues of economics, intelligence, war, etc. You can conduct diplomatic relations with other countries, exchanging resources or trying to gain the support of other powers. 40 leaders are represented, including Mao Zedong, Margaret Thatcher, Ernesto Che Guevara, etc. Each player chooses his own development path: join the space race, try to gain technological superiority, create nuclear bombs, etc. There is a built-in map and scenario editor.
