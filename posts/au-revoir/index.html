Game Description#

Au Revoir takes gamers to 2071, where the world has switched to synthetic brains to preserve human consciousness. People can live forever by transferring their minds into new bodies - concept. The main character, Tristan, works as a Guardian tasked with extracting these consciousnesses. However, what begins as a routine mission soon develops into something more complex. Tristan begins to doubt his own existence and the integrity of his synthetic brain, which creates the basis for a deeper exploration of the very nature of the world around him.

Au Revoir takes place in a neon-lit, retrofuturistic world and takes users into a labyrinth of mysteries and ethical issues. As Tristan navigates the futuristic city, the decisions he makes along the way carry weight and influence the course of the story. The mechanic relies on investigative skills to uncover clues and piece together the truth behind the conspiracy. The story is told through interactions with various characters, each of whom hides their own secrets.

This is a story where trust is fragile, and every choice made can change the direction of the plot. Throughout the game, you will face not only external threats, but also internal doubts, blurring the lines between what is real and what is not. The ’90s aesthetic serves as the backdrop for a complex journey where every decision can reveal more than just the case. As you delve deeper, expect unexpected revelations.
