Game Description#

Flappy Bat 3 offers an endless arcade challenge where each playthrough is unique thanks to procedurally generated obstacles. Gamers must perform precise wing flaps using a simple mouse click or gamepad tap to guide the bat through an ever-changing maze of dangers. Intense competition for the highest score unfolds on the global leaderboard, motivating you to hone your skills. The difficulty increases steadily with each level, presenting new challenges and forcing you to constantly adapt your tactics to survive.

The plot unfolds in the gloomy setting of a Halloween night, when the main character, the Bat, is desperate to return home. The Flappy Bat 3 project rewards brave pilots with a special scoring system that rewards the most risky maneuvers near obstacles. The atmosphere is complemented by beautiful dynamic lighting effects, immersing you in the dark, but cartoonish style of the adventure. Each collision with an obstacle means the inevitable end of the attempt, forcing you to start the dangerous flight all over again.

Halloween nightmares on the way home:

Treacherous witches and ghosts blocking the road. Swiftly flying brooms and ominous pumpkins. Deadly spiders and skulls scattered everywhere. Smooth difficulty curve, suitable for beginners. A unique reward system for risky actions.
