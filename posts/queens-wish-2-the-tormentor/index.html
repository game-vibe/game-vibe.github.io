Game Description#

Queen’s Wish 2: The Tormentor may be of interest to fans of classic RPGs on PC. This indie project is made in the old school style. It was created by a studio that had previously made several series of budget RPGs. This title is set in a medieval fantasy setting. It has 2D graphics with a top view. To understand the plot and lore, it is not necessary to go through the first part first. The sequel differs from its predecessor in a new story and the appearance of a fortress system. Now users can build, equip and decorate their citadels. In them you can build barracks, breweries, forges and other buildings that expand capabilities and provide bonuses. The number of abilities has increased from 50 to 60. The battles are still turn-based and with a tactical bias. There are many different artifacts and a rich arsenal of spells.

The video game Queen’s Wish 2: Tormentor takes place in the barbarian lands called Rokai. It was there that the queen sent her son to force the locals to submit to the Haven Empire. The queen herself became seriously ill in this sequel. In addition to her, her older brother issues instructions to the hero or heroine. Before you start, generate your character. On the very first outing, the protagonist is attacked by raiders, and then ends up in a fort, where the adventure begins. In this place you can recruit adventurers to your team. The maximum squad size is 4 creatures. It can take five dozen hours to complete the story campaign. There is a large world available for exploration. In it you can travel between settlements, capture strongholds, explore dungeons, subjugate wild tribes and deal with rebels. You can also engage in diplomacy.
