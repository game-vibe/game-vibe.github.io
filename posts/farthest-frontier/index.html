Game Description#

Farthest Frontier is a strategic game with the simulator elements. The game is made by all canons of 4x-mechanics. You have to develop your settlement and defend it from an attack by the enemy. Guide your people and try not only to survive, but also to develop. The world of wildlife is fraught with many dangers with which you have to face face every day.

Collect resources, go hunting, do fishing or farming - do everything possible to survive in harsh and wild conditions. From the collected raw materials, create objects and sell them to other settlers. Think the army, equipping your army with only better armor and weapons. Fight against the elements and fight with enemy tribes.

The game presents more than 60 items that can be engaged in the game. Create dozens of items for both units and to protect the city. In Farthest Frontier, you can build about 50 different structures. Turn a small village into a huge and noisy metropolis! Fear raiders and potential invaders! Provide reliable protection of your walls and territories. Increase the level of buildings and modernize technology.

The heat, frost and various diseases are also particularly dangerous, among which tetanus, rabies or a bubonic plague. In winter, try to wear the warmth of your citizens, and in the summer provide them with berries and herbs. Find the balance between the development of the city and the exhaustion of natural resources. Follow nature and do not let it die, since the life of your units depends on its resources.
