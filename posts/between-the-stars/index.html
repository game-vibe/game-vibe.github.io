Game Description#

In Between the Stars, a space simulator, the gamer discovers new territories, fights with numerous opponents and upgrades his own ship. The hero’s goal is to save the Interstellar Republic. The game has procedural generation of the environment, enemies and stations.

The protagonist’s universe is suffering from attacks from the Children of the Sun clan. Enemy representatives have appeared from other worlds and are trying to capture these spaces. To complete the mission, the character needs to explore the galaxy and go through a lot of tests. In the process, the user will have to make many difficult decisions, on which not only the fate of the ward, but also his spacecraft, the entire crew and the Universe as a whole depends.

The spacecraft must be constantly upgraded, acquiring additional weapons in order to survive any battles with the enemy. To obtain new air transport, the character receives achievements. Spaceships differ in control style and equipment. The crew can also be fired and other, more useful employees hired. At the start, the gamer chooses a ward from the many available, which differ in certain abilities, combat skills, appearance and skills.

By completing numerous tasks, the hero will have the opportunity to defeat the main enemy - the Children of the Sun. The shootouts here are dynamic and you won’t be able to hang in place. Maneuverability is the main key to victory, because starships have energy shields. Hitting and destroying the shield will cause damage to the ship itself. During the battle, it is worth maneuvering so that enemy bullets fly past their target.
