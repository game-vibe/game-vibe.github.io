Game Description#

Navigating The Labyrinth immerses you in the exploration of underground structures from a first-person view. The basis of the gameplay is managing a group of characters within the role-playing system. Movement is implemented according to a grid scheme, and collisions with opponents are resolved through turn-based combat scenes. The plot is connected with the city of Fera, a settlement experiencing a period of decline.

The backstory of Navigating The Labyrinth describes the situation of Fera: a once rich place now lies in the shadow of the Labyrinth Kingdoms and their minotaur ruler. Every year, hikes are organized for daredevils who go into labyrinths in search of solutions and resources to save their native walls. None of those who leave return, which only worsens the depletion of the city’s human resources, threatening its future. This RPG offers to gather and develop a squad of up to 6 adventurers of different classes.

Progression involves strengthening team members through experience gained in dungeons. Heroes master spells and skills specific to their specializations. These skills are used during combat skirmishes with the inhabitants of intricate locations. The gamer’s goal is to penetrate further into the system of moves, overcoming the challenges encountered there. Research aims to uncover the contents of these underground networks and find a way to change the fate of Fera.
