Game Description#

Deliver At All Costs is a courier simulator that allows you to experience the real madness and bustle of this profession. Trying to deliver a package, you will end up in constant traffic jams, ridiculous situations, etc. You need to move around the city in your used pickup truck. However, it has one feature: the ability to accelerate using nitro. Deliver At All Costs is a project that places a huge emphasis on humor and madness.

The game implements infrastructure and traffic in a believable way. Rushing along the roads at all times, you must not forget about the rules of the road, and also in no case allow damage to other people’s property. Getting from point A to point B is not so easy, so you have to make decisions and think strategically on the go. The wrong action can trigger a chain of the most unexpected and crazy events. You can put the whole city on its ears!

After completing the task, the final result is shown and entered into the global leaderboard. What adds to the complexity is that the boss is constantly calling and yelling at the performer to complete tasks faster. In the most stressful situations, you need to be able to pull yourself together in order to do everything as quickly and perfectly as possible. With each new stage the difficulty increases.
