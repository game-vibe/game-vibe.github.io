Game Description#

Spooky Express is a logistics puzzle set within a spooky theme park where the main challenge is the safe transportation of some very unusual passengers. Human visitors must reach the exit while a variety of monsters pursue their goals: vampires aim for coffins and zombies for burial pits. The main difficulty is planning routes in such a way that people do not end up next to creepy travel companions. Otherwise, proximity to monsters will lead to passengers being frightened or bitten, joining the ranks of evil spirits.

Control of this paranormal cast rests on the shoulders of the player, acting as a ghostly conductor. Each of the over a hundred maps presents a unique railroad-laying challenge. There is limited room to maneuver, so careful strategic thinking is required to successfully get all passengers to their destinations in the Spooky Express. You must navigate routes by solving block puzzles and taking into account the unique movement rules for each type of creature to avoid chaos and meet all the conditions to complete the level. Gradually, the complexity of tasks increases, introducing new mechanics and types of monsters.
