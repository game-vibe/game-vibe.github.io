Game Description#

Aloft is an exciting indie game that takes you on a journey through a world of floating islands and an eternal hurricane. Survive and thrive as you build your base, discover new islands, and fight the destructive forces of the fungi. In this sandbox you have complete freedom to create and build sails for your island. Create your own sails that can be customized to any shape or size, and use them to control wind and jet currents to move faster. As you explore the islands, you’ll discover secrets from the ruins of a long-lost civilization and learn more about your origins. But be warned, traveling through a hurricane will not be easy. A hurricane is fraught with dangers, from rock fragments and lightning strikes to toxic spores and freezing temperatures.

But don’t worry, you can build windmills to harness the power of the wind and automate your machines all over the island. Optimize the placement of wind turbines to catch the best winds and power your production. Build bridges and zip lines to quickly move across the archipelago, grow crops and raise animals by collecting water from passing clouds. And beware of the destructive powers of fungi. Some islands are infested, with dangerous fungi growing on top of the flora and animals falling prey to decay. Learn to develop treatments for various forms of fungi and cleanse islands of corruption to gain access to new animal companions, livestock, crops, and resources. With stunning graphics and beautiful scenery, Aloft is an atmospheric game that will keep you entertained for hours. So why wait? Begin your journey today and reach the greatest heights as you overcome the challenges of the hurricane and discover the secrets of the past and future.
