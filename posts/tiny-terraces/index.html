Game Description#

Tiny Terraces is an idle farming simulator where players watch an evolving landscape. Gameplay centers on small figures called Tiny Guys who autonomously perform various tasks on the terrace. These creatures manage garden plots, contributing to the expansion and transformation of the environment. The system is designed to work in the background, ensuring constant development while the user is busy with other things.

This framework supports the resource management and construction cycle that forms the core of the gameplay. The farming system in Tiny Terraces involves a specific sequence of actions to grow plants. The gamer initiates the process by planting seeds, after which the tiny guys take over watering, weeding and subsequent harvesting. Crafting mechanics are integrated into this production cycle. Certain crops combined with specific parts are necessary components to create additional workers.

This process directly increases the available labor force for operations in the expanding territory. The economic model is directly related to the farm’s output, as the harvested crop generates in-game currency. This money serves a separate purpose in the game’s control loop. Gamers can spend money on purchasing a variety of jewelry from the available range. The placement of these objects allows you to add detail to the landscape. This mechanism provides a way to change the appearance of the controlled space using profits from farming activities.
