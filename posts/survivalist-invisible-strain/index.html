Game Description#

Survivalist: Invisible Strain - continuation of the game Survivalist. Events take place near Vancouver in Canada. The zombivirus epidemic has been going on for 10 years. People managed to erect settlements, well -guarded from revived dead. However, the defense of the shelters fell for unknown reasons. The survivors go to the hill, hoping to build new outposts.

In the game, the survivor: an invisible strain can create your own community. Gather followers around you and take care of their well -being. You can try 2 approaches - both merciful and despising. Become a respected leader or tyrant, to whom they obey from fear. Ensure security to settlers. Eleat the protective structures and put the sentries. Protect from walking corpses, bandits and other dangers.

In addition to a solo game, a cooperative mode is available, which allows you to unite with your friends. Explore the world with random generation of locations and perform plot quests. Communicate with others to achieve your goals. Each person has characteristic features and remembers your actions. You can establish a strong relationship or vice versa to destroy them. Hire your favorite survivors.

Create your hero, setting up appearance, clothes, equipment, abilities and character. Use a wide range of weapons - from onions to a firearm. Aim in different parts of the enemy’s body to slow down or stun. Detlate and block the response blows. Try silent movement, sneaking up to the enemies. Enter the battle or imperceptibly steal things.

Equip the base by collecting resources. Take care of the closest trees, prey stones and other materials. Dry the disguised pits for uninvited guests and install traps for rabbits. Give each ward a role in society. Someone will become a farmer, the other with a shooter, etc.
