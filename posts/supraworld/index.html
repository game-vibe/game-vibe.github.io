Game Description#

Supraworld takes a solver’s guild student on a journey through a man-made toy world. These lands suffer from storms of riddles that create obstacles for the inhabitants. To pass the final exam, the protagonist must rescue the princess from the castle. The main task can be ignored in favor of other activities in a huge, interconnected environment. The exploration of the world takes place from a miniature perspective, and each object pursues a specific gameplay goal, being devoid of unnecessary decorations.

Exploring locations in Supraworld is built on the principles of a metroidvania from a first-person perspective, and progression through the plot is tied to finding abilities that open up previously inaccessible regions. The project does not use invisible walls; the only barrier is physical geometry, which opens up access to numerous secret areas. The game also presents detective cases, offering to investigate 5 murders. The deduction system allows you to convict any suspect found guilty following an investigation.

Much of the time is spent exploring the environment, solving puzzles, and doing detective work. Battles are less common and often involve puzzle elements. The title provides a minimum of hints, forcing the gamer to overcome difficulties on their own. This approach is aimed at experienced connoisseurs of the genre who are tired of standard adventures and are looking for new challenges. The world here and its mechanics require quick wits to navigate, rather than following strict instructions.
