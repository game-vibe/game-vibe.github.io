Game Description#

KoboldKare is an online game that can be played online. Unlike traditional farming simulators, forget about crops and livestock. Instead, you breed kobolds, which makes the gameplay unique and unusual. You can play with a friend online online, even on a pirated site for free.

This multiplayer game combines elements of a farming simulator and a first-person shooter, while adding a hefty dose of adult content. Players cultivate their farms, creating kobolds, and this process is not without… intimacy. However, this is not all that concerns childbirth. You can also provide services and sell your, ahem, product to make a living.

The game boasts a rich set of features. Fans of classic shooters will appreciate the Quake/CS:S inspired movement system, which allows you to bunny hop and do tricks for maximum efficiency. The game also features a complex chemical system where you create mixtures to feed the kobolds. Each kobold boasts unique stats, parts and… proportions, which guarantees a varied harvest.

The economic side of the matter also did not go unnoticed. You can sell your kobolds, their eggs, and even random foods for cold, hard cash. But KoboldKare isn’t afraid to target a specific niche. The game features a complex GPU-powered sex simulator with detailed mechanics.

Finally, the social aspect received a boost thanks to functional multiplayer. Team up with friends to manage your farm, or get intimate online with a server full of players. Whether you’re looking for a unique farming experience or something more…adult, KoboldKare offers a wild take on the genre.
