Game Description#

Ember Knights is a multiplayer roguelite with dynamic gameplay. Each run allows participants to create new synergistic attacks, customizing their character’s equipment and leveling up their abilities. As you progress, you gain access to new skills, relics, and spells that can impact every match. There is a network cooperative for up to 4 participants and the creation of a room online.

The combat system focuses on slashing attacks, dynamic techniques and fast movements. Heroes have offensive and defensive spells that can be combined with other skills. By killing bosses and exploring the surrounding area, you can discover ancient artifacts and rare things that can greatly strengthen the wearer. Hundreds of combinations of equipment and abilities allow you to create an individual warrior with numerous features, strengths and weaknesses.

The gameplay features 50+ original opponents using slashing attacks, dodging, rolling and jumping to make the gamer’s task more difficult. At the end of each world you will need to meet a particularly dangerous enemy. Different strategies, talents and weapons will help several users interact with each other to strengthen their attacks and kill a horde of opponents together. The Knights use the power of the Great Flame to reach the Mad God and put an end to his tyranny.
