Game Description#

Daisy Flies to the Moon takes you on a journey beyond Earth, set in the year 2083. The title tells the story of Daisy, an engineer who, after an unfortunate failure in her studies, ends up at a lunar outpost. She originally went there to study lunar rock formations, but the yearlong internship turns into a much longer ordeal when the outpost loses contact with Earth. Over time, the colony is divided into those who remained uninfected and those who were consumed by a strange virus of plant origin.

Daisy, along with her robot companion Herbert, sets out to find help and uncover the secret of her isolation. The plot of Daisy Flies to the Moon unfolds as Daisy and Herbert travel across the surface of the Moon, battling infected mutants and other dangers. Gamers will guide them through a vast environment, confronting enemies and solving difficult problems. The survival of the colony depends on whether they can establish contact with Earth and cope with the growing threat of the virus.

This crisis evolves into a larger narrative that spans all galaxies. The gameplay offers an open world with hundreds of original enemies. The combat system is dynamic, and Daisy has dozens of weapons and spells at her disposal. As they progress, users will uncover story layers, learning more about the virus, the dark secrets of the lunar colony and the forces at play. Role-playing game elements provide hours of gameplay, making every decision and encounter meaningful.
