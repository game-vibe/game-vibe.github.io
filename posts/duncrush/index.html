Game Description#

Duncrush is a role-playing clicker game where the depth of character development is achieved through a well-developed skill tree and unique equipment. Creating your own strategy becomes possible thanks to powerful blades, shields and accessories found in battle. All this equipment is necessary to strengthen the hero, preparing to meet stronger opponents. Exploring numerous dungeons, each with unique challenges and rewards, forms the core of the gameplay. In these dangerous journeys, the protagonist is helped by mercenaries who fight shoulder to shoulder. The visual style of the project refers to retro aesthetics, giving the adventure a special charm.

The art of victory in the depths of dark dungeons:

Selection of unique equipment to create a unique fighting style. Develop talents through an extensive skill tree for maximum efficiency. Clearing dangerous dungeons in search of rare treasures and glory. Recruiting loyal allies for joint battles with the forces of darkness.

The process of destroying waves of enemies is based on simple and intuitive actions - clicks. This is how Duncrush accumulates gold and produces valuable equipment for further enhancement. Dungeon conquest isn’t limited to run-of-the-mill skirmishes, as adventurers can expect intense battles with powerful bosses. For those looking for an endless challenge, there is a special mode with never-ending levels. The combination of fun progression and strategic race planning creates an exciting experience. The fate of the dark catacombs is literally in the hands of the adventurer, turning every click into a step towards victory.
