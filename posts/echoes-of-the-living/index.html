Game Description#

Echoes of the Living is a chilling classic survival horror game that will keep you on the edge of your seat! Taking inspiration from the greatest hits of the 90s, this game takes horror to a whole new level. You will be completely immersed in a dark and frightening world, filled with unspeakable horrors and secrets that have yet to be revealed. As you explore a small town located in northern Europe, you quickly realize that something is wrong. A mysterious fog has descended on the city, and local residents are falling ill and dying en masse. To make matters worse, the dead began to rise and walk the streets. The few remaining people are simply trying to survive.

You’ll take on the roles of Laurel, a deputy sheriff, and Liam, a former special forces soldier, who are desperately trying to escape the horror that has consumed the city. Laurel is determined to find out the truth about what happened to her colleagues, while Liam searches for his fiancé. But as they soon discover, their survival depends on working together and unraveling the mystery engulfing the city. Featuring fixed cameras, updated visuals, and fully 3D environments, Echoes of the Living delivers an experience that will leave you shaking in fear. Will you be able to survive the horrors that await you and uncover the truth about what happened? Play now to find out!
