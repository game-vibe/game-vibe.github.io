Game Description#

Cozyrama is a meditative sandbox, the main goal of which is to create and arrange magical 3D dioramas. The creative process begins with a blank canvas, gradually turning into the creation of miniature worlds filled with life. The initially empty stage is transformed into whimsical locations with flowering plants, glowing lanterns and tiny animals roaming everywhere. The visual design is made in a stylized manner, giving each corner a special atmosphere of comfort and warmth.

Creative freedom allows you to realize any ideas on a variety of natural backgrounds, from flowering meadows and cherry groves to sandy beaches or rocky hills. The Cozyrama project offers tools for changing the landscape, placing buildings and populating the world with fauna. The entire gameplay is built around relaxation and contemplation, completely eliminating competitive elements or time limits. To fully immerse yourself in the creative process you must:

sculpture the terrain and place bodies of water at your own discretion. select the appropriate color palette for the environment being created. fill the stage with hundreds of decorative objects and cute creatures. interact with the inhabitants of the diorama to give it more life.

For those who prefer a structured approach, there is a special mode with ready-made models. It serves as a source of inspiration and gradually introduces the available opportunities, offering new ideas for implementation. High replayability is ensured by the ability to start over at any time, experimenting with different concepts and discovering fresh combinations of elements. Completed compositions can be viewed in different lighting conditions, be it a bright sunny day or the dim light of a starry sky.
