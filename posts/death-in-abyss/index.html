Game Description#

Death In Abyss becomes a real test of strength for connoisseurs of intense shooters, because the project requires honed reflexes and virtuoso control of the combat apparatus. The flight system is designed for the fast maneuvers, including evasive maneuvers and sharp turns, needed to survive high-dynamic collision environments. Confrontation with hostile life forms is based on the use of a powerful arsenal. Each successful elimination of an enemy allows you to absorb his vital energy to restore your own reserve of strength, which forces you to constantly be on the move and conduct an aggressive battle.

Events unfold in the uncharted depths of an alien ocean, where the oppressive atmosphere is emphasized by a unique visual style. Diving into the world of Death In Abyss involves meeting nightmarish bioluminescent creatures and giants that cause attacks of thalassophobia. The main task is to move deeper into the abyss in order to discover the source of the mysterious outbreak of aggression. The visual style harkens back to the early 3D era, complemented by the aesthetics of old computer terminals, while the soundtrack enhances the immersive feel of retro horror.

The inhabitants of the abyss are the main threats of a hostile world:

Bioluminescent brains are aggressive organisms that pose a major threat in the upper ocean. Insatiable tadpoles are fast predators that attack in large groups from ambushes. Giant Nightmares are huge creatures that act as particularly powerful opponents. Infectious life forms are unique creatures, encountering them can lead to unpredictable consequences.
