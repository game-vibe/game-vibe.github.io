Game Description#

[justify][/justify]Door Kickers 2 Task Force North is a top-down action game in which you can play online with a friend on a pirate, unlocking all the in-game content for free, consisting of numerous guns, equipment, auxiliary equipment and etc. Teaming up over the Internet online for a cooperative game, you will take command of a special forces detachment, performing many tactical missions in the vastness of the Middle East. The main task is to prevent terrorist organizations from carrying out their plans.

The gameplay of Door Kickers 2 Task Force North, whether single-player or multiplayer, allows you to use a huge selection of intelligence assets, manage your personal unit and participate in raids, neutralizing or capturing terrorists. The opportunity for free online play invites all participants to travel to the fictional country of Noveraki. The heroes will have to develop their own tactics to counter suicide terrorists using large-caliber weapons, including grenade launchers and RPGs. It is necessary to use advanced technologies such as light machine guns for suppressing fire, mini-traps, reconnaissance drones, night vision goggles, wall-breaking charges and much more. Gameplay features: camera with a top-down perspective; dynamic and fast-paced gameplay in real time; random planning and development of tactics; non-linear locations generated by a random method; units with their own unique fighting style; complete customization of character and weapons; interactive and destructible maps; extensive mission editor.
