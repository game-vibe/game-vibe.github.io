Game Description#

Edge Of The Abyss Awaken is a role-playing adventure with the ability to play online with a friend, fighting on a pirate ship for free with a huge number of enemies. Over the Internet, you will need to take part in intense battles with one or more partners. The action takes place in the world of the Middle Ages. The hero is controlled from a third person. Mechanics include action, exploration, strategy and roguelike elements. You can create your own hero by selecting various talents for him. When you set off to explore the continent, you will need to pass many tests.

The possibility of free online play allows you to assemble a team of up to three people. Together or alone, you need to complete many main and secondary quests, collect loot, level up, fight monsters and various bosses. The online game Edge Of The Abyss Awaken is aimed at interaction between participants. Each has their own role in the squad, individual weapons, etc. Every enemy you meet along the way poses a threat. By defeating enemies, experience points are awarded. They can be used for pumping. With each new level, additional abilities are unlocked. Mechanics and content: Combined elements of action and role-playing adventure; Permanent death; A huge variety of builds for your hero; Random generation and manually created locations; Many challenges based on skills; Walkthrough with partners.
