Game Description#

DOOMBRINGER is an uncompromising arena shooter, the gameplay formula of which is built on rapid movement, merciless destruction of enemies and masterful use of the arsenal. Confrontation with opponents is devoid of an element of chance, since each attack is predictable and can be prevented, which transfers all responsibility for survival to the skill of the gamer himself. The visual style refers to the classics of the genre, and the movement system with an emphasis on expressive jumping and jumping allows you to navigate the well-designed maps as efficiently as possible. Battles require full concentration, and to win you need not only to shoot accurately, but also to wisely use the features of the locations.

Events take place in a war-torn world, where the remnants of humanity are divided into two warring clans - the Brotherhood of R’vin and the Order of Sil’Axis. The protagonist, speaking on the side of the Brotherhood, penetrates a vital enemy bunker in order to provide his people with a better future. To achieve this goal in DOOMBRINGER you will have to master a variety of weapons. For total domination in the arena, you need to effectively use all available tools:

send opponents flying with a precise shot from a rocket launcher. knock enemies to the ground with the power of a shotgun. immobilize targets with lightning bolts. turn your opponents into a sieve using a rapid-fire Gatling gun.
