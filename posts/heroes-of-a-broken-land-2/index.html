Game Description#

Heroes of a Broken Land 2 is a procedurally generated strategy RPG that immerses gamers in a fractured fantasy realm where they take control of multiple parties and navigate countless worlds. User steps into the role of one of the few remaining wizards, crystallized but determined to undo the destruction caused by the corruption of the former Wizard Council.

Heroes of a Broken Land 2 begins with gamers recruiting heroes, building cities from scratch, and delving into treacherous dungeons - all with the ultimate goal of restoring a ruined landscape to its former glory. Gameplay revolves around managing different aspects of this ruined setting at the same time. The gamer will have to manage several squads of heroes, carefully selecting character classes in order to create a balanced team that can overcome the upcoming challenges.

City management is another important component of the game. Users build and develop settlements to attract experienced characters, as well as provide training grounds and equipment for existing party members. A distinctive feature of the video game is its procedural generation system, thanks to which no playthrough of the game will be identical. The user can customize the parameters of the world for himself, determining what he prefers - compact, focused quests or large-scale epics on vast landscapes.
