Game Description#

Toy Smash Kaboom! is a hybrid project that combines elements of spatial organization, auto-battler and physical clashes. The playing field is a littered table on which you will have to command armies of animated toys. Squads are managed through a backpack-like inventory system, where each slot has strategic importance for the outcome of the upcoming battle. The troops can be led by unique captains, such as the Chef Doll, who specializes in defense, or Doctor Hamster, who relies on the numerical superiority of his charges. A total of 4 heroes and 8 different classes are available, and the collection of units includes over 350 toys.

After the preparatory stage is completed, battles unfold automatically on the tabletop. The outcome of fights largely depends on physics, since knockback effects and well-placed traps can radically change the course of the battle. Extensive arsenal in Toy Smash Kaboom! allows you to create hundreds of combinations of more than 200 types of magic and weapons. To achieve victory, gamers need to perform a number of tactical actions:

combine and create new items. carefully consider the placement of units in your inventory. use more than 20 types of traps to control the battlefield. confront opponents in asynchronous PvP battles.
