Game Description#

The third-person RPG Outward features some challenging survival aspects. Your traveler will go into the open world, where he will have to monitor his own temperature, the availability of water, food and energy. Without the proper level of these factors, the character will die faster. For example, you may get a cold if you don’t find a cure in time, and bad food will give you an upset stomach, so choose what you eat carefully.

While you are on the territory of any city, the environment will be preparing to grab you and hide you behind bars, so if you do not have enough strength to resist, the character will simply be captured. The spell system in Outward is designed to be incredibly difficult to obtain. They are available in the store or after leveling up the character, which depends on the quests completed for various NPCs. The plot develops on the basis of a monetary debt, which the main character undertakes to repay for his family. If he does not do this, then all family savings will be spent to at least somehow pay the amount. He goes to the open world, where he can earn a certain amount of money in order to return and provide for his family for the rest of his days, but this trip turns into the most interesting, but difficult story of survival in such a world.

Quests are designed to be completed over a period of time. Some are given 5 minutes - to find the answer to a question, and some last for months - to bring some object from the creature’s lair. Therefore, you will fight deserters, rebels and armies of various factions, kill animals, get meat and water, look for medicine, and also constantly spend time looking for accommodation for the night. The wild nature around you guarantees epic battles, challenging spell mining, split-screen multiplayer mode, and an interesting scenario dictated entirely by you.
