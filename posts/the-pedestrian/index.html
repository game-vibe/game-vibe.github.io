Game Description#

The Pedestrian invites you to immerse yourself in a dynamic 2.5D world with stunning graphics and challenging puzzles. The game is presented in the platformer genre, where you will find the adventure of an extraordinary little man living in a world of road signs and signs. You have to find out whether he can get out of his two-dimensional world into the real one, or whether this attempt will be in vain. In any case, he won’t be able to cope without help!

The gameplay here is unique in all areas and consists of two stages. The first involves collecting signs and pointers into one single picture. If at the beginning it may be only a couple of pointers, then with each level the game becomes more complicated, the number of pointers increases, reaching a dozen or even more. The second stage will involve the actual movement of the character along the placed road signs. Each time you have to create a new game location through which the main character moves, you will be drawn more and more into the game.

The general atmosphere will be complemented by a good soundtrack, which will help you to completely immerse yourself in the essence of what is happening. The main thing is to be attentive and ready for unexpected plot twists. The three-dimensional detailed world and its unusual inhabitants need your help and support! The gameplay will definitely captivate you!
