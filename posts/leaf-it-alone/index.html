Game Description#

Leaf it Alone is a simulator where completely clearing each individual area of ​​the yard is rewarded with permanent upgrades, such as running faster or being able to tie bags faster. The arsenal for tidying up includes a rake and a powerful blower, as well as the ability to collect leaves manually. It’s especially fun to work with the rake, which allows you to pull down large piles in one smooth motion thanks to the sticking effect at high pumping levels. The cleaning process is also speeded up by ventilation holes that automatically suck in nearby leaves. The ultimate goal of all efforts is to open access to a secret room, which serves as a kind of finale.

The main gameplay loop is based on collecting fallen leaves, for which coins are awarded when they are disposed of in a trash can. These funds are invested in upgrading tools, increasing their power or range, as well as purchasing bags with larger capacities. Leaf it Alone offers a balanced progression that avoids the feeling of monotony. The action takes place on a single, but detailed level, including a house with front and back yards, a swimming pool and a greenhouse. For convenience, there is a function to highlight foliage only in the current area.

In addition to cleaning, the title offers to find eight trading cards with funny illustrations, which are stored in a special magazine. To obtain all achievements, you will need at least two playthroughs, one of which is a speed run with the task of completing it in 40 minutes. After completing the main part, you can continue exploring the location to collect the remaining items and complete side objectives. This simulator creates a cozy and relaxing atmosphere, supported by pleasant visual style and the meditative process of putting things in order in the autumn garden, giving deep satisfaction from the work done.
