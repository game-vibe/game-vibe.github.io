Game Description#

HoloSeeker is a card roguelike with RPG elements, the main goal of which is to conquer the adventure map with the help of a well-built deck. Players will have to choose one of four available characters, each of which has its own fighting style. Initially, only Usada Pekora is available, using the Carrot accumulation mechanic to gain an advantage. As you progress, other talents are unlocked: Nekomata Okayu, who relies on high evasion with low defense, Takanashi Kiara, who is able to revive after death thanks to Phoenix Feathers, as well as the FuwaMoco duo, which accumulates the Mischief Stock resource to play powerful cards.

HoloSeeker’s strategic depth is revealed through a deck-building system and randomized events that make each playthrough unique. The combat system is based on more than 100 maps, inspired by memorable scenes and quotes from virtual idol broadcasts. They feature not only cute illustrations, but also a variety of effects that allow you to create countless combinations. New cards to strengthen your deck can be purchased in the store using a special currency, HoloCoins, earned during adventures. Success directly depends on the synergy between the abilities of the selected hero and the effects of the cards.

The key to victory is the synergy of talents and cards:

Accumulation of resources to gain tactical superiority in the battle for Usada Pecora. Using the ability to revive to survive in the most hopeless situations as Takanashi Kiara. Bet on dodging enemy attacks when playing as Nekomata Okay. Unleash powerful damage-based combos while controlling the FuwaMoco duo.
