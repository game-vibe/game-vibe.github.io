Game Description#

The Haunting of Joni Evers takes gamers into the isolated world of Joni, a woman living in a mansion in Harvest, Oklahoma. The plot tells a complex tale of family, loss and the unsettling presence of something dark in an old house. Joni’s life, once marked by distance from her family, begins to unravel after a tense argument with her only friend. Subsequent events in The Haunting of Joni Evers draw the user deeper into the secrets of the house, revealing fragments of the past, painful and mysterious.

As Joni faces growing anxiety, she must confront not only supernatural forces, but also the ghosts of her own memories. Exploring the Cunningham home is like peeling back layers of forgotten history. Each room holds potential answers, pieces of a larger puzzle. As you wander through its eerie halls, the space itself seems to live with untold stories. The mansion, with its creaky floors and hidden corners, seems to have absorbed the turmoil of generations past.

It’s easy to get lost here, not only physically, but also under the weight of unresolved problems. Joni’s journey is not only about uncovering the house’s secrets, but also about realizing the emotional scars she carries within herself. The title is not shy about inconvenience. A ghostly presence in the house is not just a mysterious force, it is a manifestation of unresolved regrets and grief. As players delve into Joni’s memories, the line between the real and the imaginary blurs.
