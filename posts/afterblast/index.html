Game Description#

AFTERBLAST is a fast-paced first-person shooter with rogue-lite elements that takes you on an epic journey across a devastated planet. As you progress through randomly generated dungeons, you’ll encounter hordes of monsters and bosses, and you’ll need dozens of guns and other weapons to defeat them. In this world, chaos came without warning and wreaked havoc on the once beautiful planet of Aryasan. The skies dimmed, the vegetation withered, and animals began to attack each other as resources dried up. Even the spirits that once radiated warmth and light have disappeared. The people of Aryasan were also plagued by chaos, causing constant conflicts and disputes that quickly escalated into open war.

But there is still hope. Deep in the heart of the planet lie mysterious underground structures that are said to be the birthplace of magic - both good and evil. You play as Eremit, a survivor of chaos who has been chosen for an extremely important task. You must penetrate these underground passages to find magical stones that can restore life to Aryasan. Over 100 collectibles and a character progression tree will allow you to develop your skills and arsenal to defeat your enemies. Play alone or gather your team to take on challenges together in co-op mode. Take on the gods and defeat the monsters to save your planet. Are you ready to embark on this epic adventure? Join me at AFTERBLAST and let’s find out.
