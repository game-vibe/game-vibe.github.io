Game Description#

Rogue Command is a strategy with elements of a bagel and a card game. You need to collect resources, form a unique deck of cards and fight against robots. When gathering an army, you need to study the enemy’s tactics in order to deal a crushing blow to him. While exploring locations, you need to obtain resources to build buildings and hire units. There are factories, auxiliary buildings, turrets and special weapons of mass destruction. For example, Rogue Command has a superpower called nanodarkness. She is capable of slaughtering crowds of enemies or raising fallen soldiers from the dead.

You can use the environment for your own purposes. For example, drive the enemy into thick fog in order to launch a surprise attack. There are electrical clots in the locations that stun the opposing army. You can find power-ups and various forgotten technologies in chests and ancient ruins. More than 20 procedurally generated maps are presented. You will need to enter the battle with 25 different types of enemies. Each unit has its own unique characteristics. The strategy and style of battle depends on the assembled deck. There are heroes, ordinary warriors, equipment and many auxiliary cards that determine tactics in battle. There are hundreds of different variations of tactics and styles that can be used against an enemy squad. The visual part of the game is presented in a stylish low-poly style.
