Game Description#

cheapskate is an arcade skateboarding simulator where the gameplay philosophy is based on complete freedom of action and self-expression. The mechanics are based on predictable and responsive arcade physics, completely eliminating any elements of uncontrolled realism. Precise digital controls provide complete control over every movement, allowing you to perform tricks with great stability. The project is aimed at both veterans of the genre and newcomers, offering a low barrier to entry, but at the same time motivating them to hone their skills. Players will have to explore unique locations, specially designed for performing creative tricks and building spectacular combinations. The level design is replete with various obstacles and jumps, creating conditions for smooth and continuous movement.

The basic concept allows you to experiment with tricks and routes at your own pace. The process of mastering cheapskate does not impose strict limits, allowing everyone to find their own style of skating. There are several formats available for this purpose.

Network and solo capabilities:

Fully offline single player mode, allowing you to ride anywhere without the need to connect to the network. A multi-user session without registration, organized through a direct connection using an invitation code.

In co-op mode, players see each other on location, watching each other perform tricks in real time, which opens up space for friendly competition. Thus, the project offers a concentrated skateboarding experience, where the main element is the skill of the rider himself, and not chance.
