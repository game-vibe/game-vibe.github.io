Game Description#

The Exorcist’s Story is an atmospheric 2D adventure game in which you will find yourself in the shoes of a young priest, Antonio, who had experience in performing exorcism rituals. But one day everything went wrong and a girl died during the ceremony. You escaped prison, but you promised yourself never to perform such rituals again. But a few years later, strange things begin to happen in your small town and Asmodeus, the demon you once encountered, challenges you again.

Get ready to go on a dangerous adventure in which you will have to face the fiend and save the souls of ordinary people. Will you be able to defeat Asmodeus and other demons who have captured human souls? Your decisions affect the world in a game that offers an unusual setting and a non-linear story. You can work as an exorcist, conduct sermons and perform exorcisms if necessary. There are also plenty of Easter eggs and movie references waiting for you. In The Exorcist’s Story you will find an exciting adventure that will not leave you indifferent.
