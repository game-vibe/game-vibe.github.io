Game Description#

Evolings, a turn-based role-playing game, takes you on an exciting journey through a setting teeming with fearsome monsters and cute creatures. Your main task is to assemble a formidable team of Evolings, each with their own abilities and personalities, to face the dangers that lurk on each level. What distinguishes the project from standard RPGs is the ability to gain new, unique impressions from each level completed. New tasks, objects and plots are constantly appearing in the title. The title doesn’t follow the conventional roguelike formula. Instead of a dark aesthetic, Evolings uses a minimalist and vibrant art style. The stark contrast between the colorful images and the formidable opponents you’ll face creates an intriguing atmosphere.

What makes the game particularly charming is its light-hearted and clever tone, which will have you laughing and grinning as you interact with the diverse group of Evolings and watch their funny antics. Whether you’re a casual gamer or a dedicated enthusiast, the project will satisfy both. It has mechanics that are understandable to beginners, and at the same time, requiring experienced users to thoughtfully plan and make strategic decisions. Here you will go on a journey through a setting that is not like the usual one. Its unique charm, unpredictable gameplay and fascinating mechanics make the game an interesting and worthy addition to the roguelike genre, allowing you to spend more than one hour in it.
