Game Description#

Leaf Blower Co is a meditative simulator that invites you to start your own cleaning business in a cozy town located in New England. The main task is to restore order with the help of a powerful blower, turning chaos into a peaceful landscape. The process is not limited to just blowing away autumn leaves; You will also have to deal with scattered boxes, coconuts and other debris that interfere with harmony. As you grow in your career, you gain access to new tools and useful enhancements that allow you to cope with more complex orders and work significantly more efficiently.

The story campaign will take you through many different locations, including school playgrounds, tennis courts, museum grounds and even beaches. Each job at Leaf Blower Co. is made unique by the dynamic changes in weather: clear sunny days can give way to heavy rains, thick fog or sudden snowstorms, which directly affects the cleaning process. In addition to the main contracts, you can always take on side assignments, and after completing the story, a free mode opens. This format provides complete freedom to choose the location, time of day and weather conditions for the most relaxing gameplay.

For the most attentive, various secrets and optional bonus goals are hidden at each level, the discovery of which is additionally rewarded. The funds you earn can be spent not only on functional equipment upgrades, but also on purchasing new skins for tools, which allows you to give your inventory an individual style. Thus, the project encourages a leisurely and thoughtful passage, where the main emphasis is on enjoying the process of cleaning itself and contemplating the result of one’s labors.
