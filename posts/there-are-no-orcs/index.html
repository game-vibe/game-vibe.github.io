Game Description#

There Are No Orcs plunges you into a world of total war and continuous battles, where huge armies clash on the battlefields. Battles take place in four different biomes, from scorched lands to frozen tundra, requiring the commander to adapt tactics to the conditions of the terrain. In this chaos of large-scale clashes, the commander will have to lead his legions to victory. At the same time, one detail unites all warriors, regardless of their origin - a strange green shade of skin, the reasons for which are not usually discussed in this world.

Each race represents a new tactical experiment, forcing gamers to rethink approaches to forming troops. The basis for success is the competent placement of buildings, which creates powerful synergistic effects and reveals the full potential of fighters. In There Are No Orcs, victory depends on a combination of the skills of one of the 30 commanders and proper resource management. Over 100 relics and pieces of equipment open up endless possibilities for creating unique builds, allowing you to crush enemy orders in the most unexpected ways.

Three paths to absolute domination:

People rely on building optimization for a steady flow of gold and manpower. Demons call upon all forces from a single portal, feeding the army with souls. Dwarves skillfully manage the overload of buildings, balancing between gold and parts.
