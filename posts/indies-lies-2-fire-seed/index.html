Game Description#

Indies' Lies 2: Fire Seed is a 2D tactical roguelike where controlling a squad of three heroes becomes a chess match in an ever-changing arena. Every turn requires careful decisions, whether it’s eliminating healers or interrupting attacks from elite opponents. Terrain becomes a full-fledged weapon, allowing you to separate enemy forces and lure bosses into traps. Success depends on the right combination of character skills, such as Qili’s positional combinations, Diego’s counterattack ambushes, or Daniel’s explosions that spread negative effects. A deep build system allows you to combine 112 tactical components to achieve maximum effectiveness in battle.

The plot continues the saga begun in Indies’ Lie, where warriors from different eras, awakened by the Mistress of Death, must challenge the divine tyranny on the continent of Meka. Under the yoke of the evil god Indys, factions fight in procedurally generated battles. In Indies' Lies 2: Fire Seed, each expedition is unique with random maps, enemies and events, from surprise ambushes to mysterious altars. Forming a squad of mechanically diverse characters opens up endless tactical possibilities, while hundreds of combinations of equipment and Ignite abilities allow for devastating synergies.

Components of victory over divine tyranny:

Create unique builds like Blink Crit or Toxin Overload from a variety of relics and equipment. Adapt to unpredictable challenges, including random events and ambushes from elite enemies. Leverage the synergy of skills and terrain for tactical dominance on the battlefield.
