Game Description#

Super Raft Boat Together is an adventure for those who love danger and teamwork! In a world inundated by a mysterious flood, you and your trusty boat are all that’s left. But don’t be afraid! Along with three friends, you’ll battle ruthless sea creatures as you build and expand your raft to reach the final island, where the truth about the flood will finally be revealed. This multiplayer roguelike shooter offers endless excitement as you navigate a variety of breathtaking biomes, strategically choosing trinkets to create the ultimate, super-powered structure. But beware, one wrong move and you will become a victim of sea creatures that are inexplicably hostile and rich - wow!

Team up with friends for the ultimate co-op experience, or venture into the unknown on your own. Each race is unique, with new challenges and dangers lurking around every corner. Collect riches from fallen enemies and purchase upgrades to strengthen your team, unlocking new weapons and cosmetic items for your favorite characters. Feeling brave? Take on the loop challenge, but don’t say we didn’t warn you about the permanent sea life phobia that could follow! Super Raft Boat Together is the long-awaited multiplayer sequel to the beloved Super Raft Boat from Shattered Journal Games.
