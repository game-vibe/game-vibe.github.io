Game Description#

Knight Fall is an asymmetrical confrontation where each gamer chooses a side in an eternal battle. Controlling unique heroes endowed with special powers becomes the key to victory. The Fire Knight is able to surround enemies with flames, while the Bonecrusher summons skeleton minions to help. All actions are performed by playing cards from the hand, with each card offering several options for use. The battlefield, which is a monastery, is assembled from special location tiles, providing a unique layout for each session.

The defense of the sacred monastery of Thornmar is entrusted to the shoulders of the ancient order. Their task is to protect the sages who maintain the seal on the portal to the underworld. As night fell, hordes of demons poured out of the rift, seeking to destroy the elders before dawn. In the Knight Fall project, one team takes on the role of valiant defenders, and the other - ruthless invaders from another world. The fate of the world is decided within the walls of an ancient abbey in one night.

A story mode is available for one or two players. In it you have to explore the map of the cursed valley, independently plotting a route through ruined churches, an ominous forest and dark mountains. The main goal is to stop the demons before the final confrontation in Thornmar. It is important to take care of each knight, because the death of any of them means the loss of a valuable fighter in the decisive battle.
