Game Description#

Captain of Industry gives you the opportunity to build and manage your own factory. You need to hire your own team to use it to turn a small colony into the largest industrial empire. Events take place on an island cut off from the rest of the world. It will be necessary to build many factories, research laboratories and even a space station here, becoming a real industrial center of the world.

To survive, it is necessary to extract resources and raw materials, collect provisions and produce food. In Captain of Industry, you can manufacture products, design cars, research new technologies, and explore your environment. The world is in complete chaos. It will be necessary, with the help of our own developments, to return the world to its former greatness. However, the path will be thorny. Production chains are presented: iron smelting, concrete mixing, creation of building materials, etc.

You can refine crude oil, install solar panels, processing centers, etc. More than 100 products are available for interaction. All machines and factories need to be installed correctly by connecting them with conveyor belts. It is also necessary to save fuel. It is allowed to build bridges, ramps, excavators, etc. By exploring the map, you can find reserves of coal, copper, iron and gold. We definitely need to feed people.

Installing greenhouses will help grow potatoes and other vegetables. You are required to complete a list of tasks related to buildings, research, technology development, etc. Allows you to create trucks, harvesters, build a fleet and much more. Vehicles and ships are repaired and improved with updated engines, weapons and armor. If you find refugees, you need to take them to your camp.
