Game Description#

Tales of Kathay is a turn-based role-playing adventure where every decision leaves a mark on the fate of entire generations within the framework of a procedurally generated world. Battles are implemented in the format of tactical clashes, rewarding thoughtful actions and a non-standard approach, which refers to the classic tabletop counterparts. The depth of the process is given by a detailed system of wounds, in which injuries to specific parts of the body directly affect indicators such as mobility or the ability to interact with objects. Such a mechanism requires careful planning of each move in battle, as well as advance preparation for it through the development of skills and the creation of equipment.

The field for action is a procedurally generated universe with a sophisticated simulation of historical processes, where civilizations develop and empires decline over thousands of years. Tales of Kathay gives you complete freedom to create your own unique story. Impact on global processes is achieved through many activities:

Impact on global processes:

Exploring ancient ruins and dungeons in search of artifacts. Conclusion of diplomatic alliances with neighboring states. Participation in political intrigue to change the balance of power. Organization of trade caravans and control over resource points. Create unique equipment using the crafting system. Development of character skills for combat and peaceful activities. Managing the dynasty and passing on the legacy to the next generations. Participation in tactical battles with a detailed injury system.
