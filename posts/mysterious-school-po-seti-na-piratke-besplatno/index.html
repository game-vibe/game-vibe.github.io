Game Description#

InMysterious School you can play online with a friend for free on a pirate or team up online online with other participants to survive the worst night in the lives of schoolchildren. The online game brings players together to test their nerves and provide a huge amount of challenges within the school setting. As the sun sets, the ghost of a girl appears within the walls of the school, wandering within the walls of a long-abandoned building.

The exotic campus features exploration mechanics and a huge number of puzzles. The free online play option in Mysterious School generates a variety of tasks and quest items for participants to destroy in order to free the spirit of the suicide girl. Special objects contain and control the monster’s mind. Finding them is not so easy and initially you will need to solve several difficult problems.

You can use 5 types of spells. Each has its own unique purpose and is suitable for different situations. Many gameplay elements are randomly generated. There are many side activities: throwing a ball into a basket, chasing a chicken and much more. Within the school there are many spirits that can be accidentally encountered while exploring the buildings. For example, there is an opportunity to attend a funeral procession. The locations consist of classrooms, gyms, infirmaries, cafeterias, offices, dormitories, and lounges.
