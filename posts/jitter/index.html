Game Description#

Jitter is a sci-fi simulator that immerses you in the dark atmosphere of the consequences of a catastrophe in the Main Asteroid Belt. A once-bustling mining colony lies in ruins after a mysterious incident, and the truth behind what happened must be uncovered in the rubble. The danger comes not only from the destroyed environment, but also from pirates or even unknown life forms lurking in the shadows. To survive and complete the mission, you must carefully explore abandoned mines, colossal cave systems and remote outposts scattered across the surface of a giant asteroid.

All control is entrusted to the artificial intelligence of the spaceship, whose main program prescribes the search and rescue of survivors. Although the storyline is linear, Project Jitter offers significant freedom in achieving your goals. For example, to destroy an enemy outpost, you can use brute force by firing at it from guns, carry out a tactical operation to capture it from the inside, or use cunning by destroying a nearby asteroid and allowing its debris to pierce the station’s hull, causing depressurization.

To successfully complete missions, it is necessary to constantly modify the ship, adding or discarding modules to adapt to a combat situation or for a quick retreat. Deep simulation of on-board systems forces constant monitoring of oxygen generation, energy distribution and damage repair. An important role in the gameplay is played by the crew, consisting of characters with unique backstories and abilities that influence the style of play. Combat seamlessly switches between strategic battles in outer space and tactical action on deck when boarding enemy ships.
