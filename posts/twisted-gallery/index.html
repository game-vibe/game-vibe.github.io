Game Description#

Twisted Gallery immerses you in a chilling atmosphere of anxious loneliness in the sterile halls of contemporary art. The protagonist finds himself trapped in a space with white walls and polished black floors, where each exhibit is arranged with impeccable precision. However, this apparent calm is constantly disrupted by subtle changes in the environment. The horror here lies not in the screamers, but in the oppressive silence and growing tension that makes you doubt your own perception of reality. The gallery itself becomes an antagonist, silently observing every attempt to find a way out.

The basis of the whole process is an endless loop, forcing you to go through familiar corridors again and again. Each new orbit can be either an exact copy of the previous one or contain one of 40 anomalies. The gameplay of Twisted Gallery is based on attentiveness: a change in the angle of the picture, a different position of the statue, or a suddenly shortened passage serve as keys to advancement. The absence of traditional opponents shifts the emphasis to mental confrontation with the environment, where memory and observation become the only tools for survival and breaking the time loop.

Distortions of museum reality:

Systematic appearance of 40 unique deviations without repetitions until they are completely detected. Exploring creepy halls from a first-person perspective for maximum immersion. Search for secret Easter egg anomalies, accessible only to the most attentive visitors.
