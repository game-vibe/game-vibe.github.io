Game Description#

The hero of Songs of Conquest is developing his own kingdom and mercilessly destroys any enemies that stand in his way. A mix of turn-based strategy and RPG, you can become the head of a huge army that uses not only weapons, but also magic in battle to conquer distant territories in order to assert their own power. The game features a level editor, 4 factions to choose from, and the ability to go on a solo adventure or challenge a friend to battle locally or online.

Available factions:

Aleron are descendants of the Empire who use axes for battles and also support the Freys - magical forest creatures. Baria - merged with Harima to form a single state. Engages in crafting and inflicts the sound of Hell’s Breath on the enemy. The Rana are swamp dwellers who were long enslaved but finally managed to unite the people by discovering their powers, including the ability to summon furious Ravagers. The Barony of Lot is in decline, but wants to return to its former glory, which is what the local professors and occultists are constantly doing, trying to find any artifacts at the sites of ancient battles that will allow them to resurrect the dead.

The user finds himself in the role of a Lord who sets out to conquer distant lands and tries to destroy everyone in order to get to valuable artifacts that give maximum power in the world. To this end, the hero sets out to explore vast maps with different biomes, where various landscapes and enemies are also presented. To build an Empire, resources are needed, which are always in short supply, so they need to be distributed wisely, using only essential things, for example, research, which allows you to expand the boundaries of territories. There are also different paths to victory available here, for example, using witchcraft, strength, or combining these abilities. The combat system is well-developed, where each character has skills and magic, which allows them to deal a powerful blow to the enemy, including the ability to occupy heights and block passages.
