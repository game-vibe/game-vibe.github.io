Game Description#

Idlequarium is a simulator dedicated to the creation and development of your own digital aquarium, made in a bright cybernetic style. The process of arranging the underwater world is not limited solely to the visual component. Each element of decor, from colored stones to exotic plants, directly affects the ecosystem, accelerating the experience gained for feeding and the growth rate of inhabitants. Effective management involves the timely sale of adults to make a profit. The proceeds become a resource for the purchase of improved tanks and rarer decorative objects, which opens up new horizons for management. In total, there are 6 types of aquariums and about 100 different decorations available for exploration.

The virtual tank is freely located on top of all windows, allowing you to observe the leisurely life of its inhabitants during breaks between work tasks. The main progress in Idlequarium is achieved in passive mode, which frees you from the need for constant monitoring. Collecting is one of the central mechanics, offering hundreds of species of freshwater and saltwater fish to unlock. Among them there are both ordinary ones like clown fish and the rarest mythical specimens, the receipt of which depends on luck when opening sets. To simplify routine operations, an automatic feed supply function is provided.

The main tasks of an aquarium manager

Breeding and caring for fish until they reach maturity. Selling adults for financial gain. Personalize your aquarium with decorations that influence the speed of development. Gradual opening of access to new species of fish and design elements.
