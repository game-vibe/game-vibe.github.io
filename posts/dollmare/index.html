Game Description#

Dollmare is a psychological horror adventure in which gamers have to work in a mysterious doll factory. The factory is not just a background, it is a character in its own right, filled with secrets waiting in the wings. Entering the role of a factory worker, users quickly realize that there is more to this place than meets the eye. Strange occurrences become the norm, and the unsettling nature of the dolls hints at deeper secrets lurking in the shadows. The atmosphere deepens as players move through the factory, with each room introducing new puzzles and interactions that further the story.

Dollmare’s game mechanics are based on exploration and puzzle solving. Gamers will have to piece together the history of the factory and its creepy inhabitants. Clues are scattered throughout the environment, and attentiveness becomes a prerequisite. By interacting with various characters, players make decisions that ultimately determine the direction of events. The decision-making aspect adds depth to the gameplay, as users must carefully consider their choices, knowing that each action can lead to different consequences. The non-linear structure allows for varied results, encouraging users to return and explore alternative paths. Here the psychological tension increases as gamers delve deeper into the history of the factory. Every corner of the factory seems to tell its own story, and remnants of the past are just asking to come out.
