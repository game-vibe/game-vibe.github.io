Game Description#

Count Meowcula’s Barbershop offers an extraordinary experience where every action requires precision and quick reflexes. As the bloodlust builds, tool control is inverted, turning a routine beard trim into a tense ordeal. An unsuccessful cut or a bloody incident instantly damages the reputation of the establishment, forcing it to immediately hide any evidence. Ignoring even one spot can cost customers' trust and lead to failure. This simulator forces you to constantly be on your guard, because beauty, as you know, requires sacrifice.

The main character is Count Meowkula, a charming vampire cat who decided to open his own barber shop. Customers unaware of the hairdresser’s double life create comical and tense situations. Count Meowcula’s Barbershop immerses you in an atmosphere created by a whimsical soundtrack and stylized graphics. Every decision made and quality of work performed shapes the development of the plot, opening access to various endings - from hilarious to disturbing.

The consequences of one careless bite:

Tool management becomes chaotic, making it difficult to fulfill customer orders with the required accuracy. Built-in tests become more complex every day, testing endurance to the limit. The quality of haircuts directly affects the reputation and opens access to different finals. Evidence indicating a vampire nature must be quickly removed to avoid suspicion.
