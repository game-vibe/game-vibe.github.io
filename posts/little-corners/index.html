Game Description#

Little Corners allows you to customize your mystical alchemist’s tower, filling it with flasks, telescopes and ancient tomes, arranged to your liking. At the heart of it all is a meditative creative process of placing stickers, turning a blank canvas into a detailed 3D diorama. There are no time limits or challenging tasks, allowing you to immerse yourself in creating without any pressure. The relaxing atmosphere is supported by an original lo-fi soundtrack that delicately accompanies every action and helps you focus on creativity.

In addition to the Sorcerer’s Tower, other areas are available for decoration, including a bustling tavern for adventurers or a tranquil Japanese tea room. The Little Corners project offers great freedom for self-expression with a library of over 2,000 themed stickers, from frog statues to adorable Shiba Inu dogs. By experimenting with combinations and placement of items, you can discover secret stickers that will be a nice reward for your creativity. Each element, be it furniture, sleepy animals or trinkets, is added with a pleasant soundtrack that simulates tactile sensations.

Items for arranging the hero’s refuge:

Huge goblets filled with ale or apple juice to quench your thirst. Lots of candles to create the most cozy and warm atmosphere in the room. The best wooden stools without a single splinter, guaranteeing comfort to visitors. Fireplaces, windows and other interior elements to give the diorama a finished look.
