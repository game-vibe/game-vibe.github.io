Game Description#

The award-winning horror film The Bunker is made in the form of a live quest, where we are shown real people and a completely fictitious situation in which each of us could find ourselves. The protagonist’s name is John. He grew up in a shelter designed to protect him from radioactive particles in a post-apocalyptic world where World War III had recently broken out and, accordingly, many Atomic bombs had been dropped.

The first mission introduces us to anxiety. When it goes off, we must guide John inside the bunker to reach the most secure area in the bunker. While we move through the earth shaking from explosions, we begin to remember the actions of the main character and everything that has happened to humanity over the past 20-30 years. It is noteworthy that the horror filling in The Bunker is made not in the quality of monsters and screams, but in the quality of events and experiences of players who have completely lost humanity in a world where life has become simple material.

As gameplay, we are given the opportunity to control the actions of the main character in a point-and-click style, and everything that happens on the screen is a pre-filmed video clip. One thing that saves John is his prudence - a daily routine that keeps the character in good behavioral shape. When the general anxiety subsides, the character does not know how to get out to the ground. He begins to look for a way out, and we control him from a third person. Are you wondering who John has been living next door to all this time?
