Game Description#

On the pirate titleHunted: The Demons Forge, you can play online co-op alone or with a friend, using all the multiplayer features for free. Co-op mode is available online or locally. The project is a third-person action adventure. The heroes will explore abandoned underground complexes, ruins of ancient temples, forgotten cities and many other locations. Online play allows friends to uncover the mystery of the demonic forge. The setting is in the dark fantasy genre. There are two heroes to choose from: the archer Elara, who fights at a long distance, and Caddoc, a master of close attacks who fights with deadly swords. Key mechanics of the project: large open world for exploration; many different enemies; dark dungeons, ruined temples and other mystical locations; the possibility of free online play; two completely different heroes, tailored for melee and ranged combat; changing character in real time (in solo play); a wide selection of weapons and spells; editor with which you can create your own locations and levels.

The gameplay of Hunted: The Demons Forge is focused on interaction between players. Events take place in the world of Kala Moor. A wide selection of different weapons is available, ranging from melee and ranged weapons to powerful spells aimed at attacking or protecting a partner. Alone or in company, you must face a horde of deadly enemies. When playing in solo mode, you can switch between protagonists at any time. The Demon’s forges contain many secrets and dangers.
