Game Description#

Lifecraft is a management and construction simulator. The video game can also be classified as a sandbox and strategy game. It has elements of survival. The indie project has two-dimensional pixel graphics. The camera is located at the top and at an angle. This title differs from other similar games in its concept. Instead of managing a factory, you need to control the life support of the cell. The player is asked to organize the work of all its vital functions. Under the control of the user, it gradually evolves and becomes a more advanced organism. The user also needs to deal with internal and external threats. For example: with viruses, wounds, bacteria. Instead of buildings, he installs veins, capillaries, microtubules, and so on, using frames and tissue materials for this.

In the indie title Lifecraft, the body consists of various subsystems. For their normal functioning it is necessary to generate and compress energy. This is done through mitochondrial respiration or through glycolysis. The more complex a cell becomes, the more different resources it needs for its continued existence. They are mined in the outer primordial broth. Therefore, the user’s number of supply-related tasks is gradually increasing. He will have to establish blood circulation, regulate the functioning of neurons and the brain, and so on. As he progresses, he not only faces new challenges, but also receives additional tools, and also expands his capabilities in the microworld.
