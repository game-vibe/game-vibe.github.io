Game Description#

The Jokers Game is an FMV crime game where every decision has fatal consequences for the character. The process of overcoming challenges requires maximum intellectual and strategic concentration from the gamer. Competitors will face challenges such as drawing complex shapes using chocolate icing or navigating a maze filled with optical illusions. A significant part of the competition is devoted to exerting psychological pressure and eliminating opponents through a special voting system. Every action and every choice directly determines how the story will end: survival or death.

Challenges and survival mechanics:

Solving intellectual and strategic problems. Performing tasks for fine motor skills. Navigation in a maze with optical illusions. Using psychological pressure on opponents. Elimination of rivals through secret ballot. Irreversible consequences for the storyline.

The central character of the story is Jack, whose huge debts force him to take part in a deadly adventure. The arena for a brutal confrontation is a mansion under the control of a mysterious organization. There is an immutable law here: the winner gets life, and the loser gets death. In The Jokers Game, your competitors include not only desperate debtors, but also undercover cops and elites seeking redemption. The gradual revelation of the plot reveals the true motives of the organizers of this cruel entertainment, calling into question the very nature of justice.
