Game Description#

Athos is an immersion into the harsh realities of survival in a wild and dangerous world. Finding themselves in an unpredictable environment, migrants are forced to create living conditions from scratch. The construction of a fortified outpost from available materials, such as stone and wood, becomes a priority task for protection from aggressive fauna. To counter threats, you must constantly learn new skills and collect blueprints that allow you to create more advanced weapons and armor. Exploring vast territories opens up access to rare resources that are vital for further development.

The history of this world begins with a tragedy: the remnants of humanity, fleeing the devastated Earth, go to seek refuge on the distant planet Athos. However, due to the peculiarities of the atmosphere, the technology only allows people to teleport to the surface without the possibility of returning, dooming them to isolation among prehistoric predators. The environment is populated by various species of dinosaurs, from speedy velociraptors to giant tyrannosaurs.

The protagonists will not only have to hunt these creatures, but also master the art of taming them, turning mortal enemies into loyal allies and defenders of their shelter. The survival process offers a unique development path for each character. As you gain experience, new abilities are unlocked that help both in combat and in exploring the wild. Gameplay allows you to go it alone, honing your skills away from everyone, or team up with other survivors to face more serious threats.
