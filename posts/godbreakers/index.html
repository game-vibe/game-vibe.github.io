Game Description#

GODBREAKERS is a project in which an artificial intelligence called Monad has destroyed humanity and now threatens the sun itself, successively consuming planets. To save a dying star, the mysterious sisterhood of the Coven creates vessels from the void, endowed with human consciousness. These warriors must journey through six surreal biomes to challenge the crazed AI and his minions. Each mission becomes a unique challenge, forcing you to adapt to constantly changing conditions and enemies.

The combat system requires sharp reflexes and tactical thinking. Heroes can interrupt their own attacks to dodge, giving them complete control in the most intense fights. A key mechanic in GODBREAKERS is Godbreak, which allows you to weaken an enemy before absorbing their power and unleashing it on other enemies in the form of a devastating attack. Battles with multi-phase bosses with unique attack patterns test both individual skill and teamwork of up to four members.

Advancing deeper into enemy territory opens up access to new character archetypes, each with their own weapons and set of traits. This allows you to experiment with different fighting styles, creating powerful combinations of skills and items. Constant improvements, unlocked between runs, gradually strengthen the heroes, preparing them for new, even more difficult challenges in worlds where every corner is fraught with mortal danger.
