Game Description#

Friday the 13th The Game is a third-person action game aimed at playing online with a friend or competing with random participants online. The project is based on the Friday the 13th franchise. All multiplayer functions are available for free on the pirate site. The gameplay is represented by asymmetric multiplayer. Several players control the survivors, while a fifth takes on the role of Jason Voorhees. Events take place on Crystal Lake. Many locations repeat places from the original films, for example Higgins Haven, the famous pier, etc.

It’s 1980. A group of teenagers are relaxing at a summer camp when they are attacked by a maniac with a machete, all the time wearing a hockey mask. Network play includes teamwork for up to 8 players. 7 people play as humans and a random participant gets the role of Jason. Heroes don’t know how to fight. They need to work together, trying to leave the camp without falling under the maniac’s machete. There is only one monster in the game, but there are different skins from different parts of the film: New Blood, Last Friday, Jason Takes Manhattan, etc.

The monster has good hearing and is able to see in the dark. An important mechanic is teleportation, which allows you to quickly get behind survivors. This is a tribute to films where the maniac is always there at the most important moment. Free online play in Friday the 13th The Game allows teenage players to find one of several ways to escape the location. In this case, victory is awarded to the squad of survivors. You need to act secretly and make as little noise as possible. We need to act together. You can barricade yourself in a building and protect your comrades from the enemy.
