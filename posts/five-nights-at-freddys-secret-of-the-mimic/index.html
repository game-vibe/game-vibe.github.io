Game Description#

Five Nights at Freddy’s: Secret of the Mimic plunges people into the structure of Murray Costume Manor, an abandoned workshop containing unresolved questions. The characters must explore its depths to uncover the secret left behind by Edwin Murray, the solitary creator of this place. This location offers a space where any dark area can hide information or a secret passage, directly affecting the gameplay and understanding of past events. In addition, slight changes in light constantly indicate an active and omnipresent something that monitors every step.

The key enemy in this game, Five Nights at Freddy’s: Secret of the Mimic, is the eponymous Mimic - a prototype endoskeleton designed with remarkable adaptability. This mechanical creation is capable of connecting to any costume, giving it the ability to transform into any hero, including the material manifestations of the individual’s strong fears. The player must rely on his ingenuity, a small supply of devices and a greatly abbreviated official document that conceals the details of the actions. The main task sets the gamer the goal of returning Fazbear’s valuable development, while simultaneously dealing with encrypted hints and escaping from the persistent shadow, determined to get rid of all uninvited guests.
