Game Description#

Wagotabi: A Japanese Journey is a role-playing game designed to teach the Japanese language. The project offers speech acquisition through interactive dialogues and puzzle solving in a digital analogue of Japan. Progress is achieved through interaction with the environment and its inhabitants. The curriculum introduces hiragana, katakana, and kanji sequentially as vocabulary expands. This structure integrates language practice directly into gameplay, linking progression to linguistic understanding.

In Wagotabi: A Japanese Journey, learning focuses on the practical application of vocabulary. Gamers learn to act in specific scenarios, such as asking for directions or ordering food from a menu. Character personalization is provided before starting to explore the world. Further progress through the game adventure requires the direct use of acquired knowledge to overcome difficulties and complete tasks. The narrative also incorporates elements of Japanese history and geography.

The title contains special tools to support the learning process. The built-in spaced repetition system helps players focus on words that are difficult to remember. Knowledge tests use more than 15 question formats, including sentence construction and listening tasks. Additionally, a set of mini-games is available for practicing kana and kanji characters. An extensive dictionary allows you to repeat lexical units and grammatical structures at any time.
