Game Description#

The chushpan simulator puts you in the place of an ordinary guy in Uryupinsk. It’s not a life of luxury - you start out squalid in a shabby apartment and forced to hustle to make ends meet. But you are determined to climb the social ladder, one way or another. The game unfolds through a branching quest line that takes you through various locations. Every choice you make - moral or not - determines your destiny. Each decision carries weight, and you end up with one of several different endings. Your journey begins in that very modest apartment. You have to survive, so collecting garbage and handing over bottles become your primary responsibilities.

But every ruble counts! As you accumulate money, new opportunities open up for you. Do you want to take part in street fights to make quick money? Or maybe working as a watchman is more to your liking? But temptation lurks - the black market attracts with the promise of easy money through dubious transactions. Will you remain on your word of honor or succumb to the temptation of profit? The Chushpan simulator does not embellish reality. It brings down the harshness of life on you, and the choices you make determine your fate. Will you fight your way to the top through perseverance and determination? Or will you take a path that could lead to ruin? The ball is in your hands, so choose wisely, comrade. Remember that in the world of Chushpan Simulator, every decision has consequences.
