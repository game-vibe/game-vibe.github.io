Game Description#

Windswept takes users on an adventure filled with nostalgia and excitement. It features a dynamic duo consisting of a duck and a turtle who find themselves torn from their home due to a severe storm. The main character will have to lead them back to a safe place. The platformer has a ’90s feel, with vibrant visuals, catchy tunes, and levels that will test your skills.

Windswept features over 40 carefully designed stages spread across 5 authentic regions, each with its own unique atmosphere and hidden surprises, so there’s never a dull moment. Along the way you will meet friendly characters who will offer their help, as well as enemies and obstacles that will challenge you. A distinctive feature of the title is its multiple passages. For those who want to fully immerse themselves in the action, there are numerous collectibles, secret areas and bonus levels.

In addition, the app offers an additional challenge mode designed for the most experienced players, allowing them to test their skills to the limit. There is a local co-op format that allows you to team up with a friend and go on an adventure together. The ability to switch between controlling a duck and a turtle adds an extra layer of strategy and coordination to gameplay.
