Game Description#

Contraption Maker is a puzzle game that relies on the laws of physics, adding a pinch of absurdity and madness. Create puzzles based on chain reactions. Place trampolines, trampolines, springs, hamsters, alligators, cats, etc. Create the craziest, most complex mechanism you can imagine. The game is the spiritual successor to Incredible Machine.

The campaign includes about 200 original puzzles, as well as many contributions from the fan community. You have to deal with the placement of complex mechanisms or their repair. Collect the necessary parts to create continuous chains of action. Each Contraption Maker level offers a specific goal: turning on the lights, opening a fireworks factory and celebrating the crocodile’s birthday, etc.

Using the Maker Lab editor, you can create your own map filled with a variety of puzzles and obstacles. 100 elements and parts are provided, consisting of gears, dynamites, cats, etc. Create complex and multi-stage levels filled with chains of devices that interact with each other. JavaScript modding is built into the game, allowing you to manually write additional mechanics and modules. You can play with friends in multiplayer mode, supporting up to 8 players simultaneously.
