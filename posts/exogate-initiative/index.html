Game Description#

The Exogate Initiative presents the possibility of creating and managing a base for exploring other worlds discovered by humanity in an alternative future. It is necessary to develop the possibilities of technological progress, allowing one to move to any desired point in the universe using portals. The states are joining forces and want to form a special research center where scientists will be able to observe unique regions and conduct reconnaissance activities in the field. Gamers will have to take control of a specific scientific project in order to achieve success in studying the galaxy.

Upon immersing yourself in the Exogate Initiative, players can expect:

Research in 6 different biomes, where you will have to observe alien flora and fauna, trying to decipher the cultural code of the aliens and deliver the necessary materials to the base. Creation of squads consisting of collectors and soldiers protecting forwarders from aggressive creatures living in alien locations. Establishing connections with alien creatures and races, trying to understand the languages ​​of local wild communities. Training hunters and ensuring the needs of each employee enhance physical and mental well-being.

The laboratory is located deep in the mountainous area, so expansion will require digging deeper. On the territory of the headquarters there are specialized premises and equipment that help in teleporting scouts to unknown worlds. The player will gradually build up rooms, creating barracks for recreation, training fields for preparing for missions, and scientific centers for studying samples brought from other parts of the universe.
