Game Description#

An unusual hybrid of real-time strategy and god simulator with roguelike elements, Zombie God offers you to take control of legions of the undead. The main task is to control hordes of zombies who will have to break through the desperate defenses of the survivors. Players take on the role of an ominous deity, directing unstoppable streams of the walking dead to destroy the remnants of civilization. Each mission turns into an ironic test, where the divine will is manifested through the hungry roar of reanimated corpses, ready to crush everything in their path.

Progress is closely related to the absorption of opponents, because eating civilians, police and soldiers allows you to accumulate valuable resources. The development mechanics of Zombie God are based on a meta-progression system, where collected biomaterials are used to genetically modify the DNA of each type of zombie. This unlocks new abilities and allows you to unlock unique types of undead, forming the ideal army composition to overcome increasingly difficult challenges in special resistance modes.

The confrontation will not be easy, because humanity is constantly strengthening its defensive lines. Over time, players will have to deal not only with infantry, but also with serious military equipment. Patrol cars are being replaced by powerful armored personnel carriers and tanks, and the battlefield is becoming more complex with new threats. To achieve victory, you will need to skillfully maneuver the horde between minefields, evade airstrikes and crush mortar positions, proving your right to be called the zombie god.
