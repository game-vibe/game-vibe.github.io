Game Description#

Approaching Infinity offers a fast-paced, never-ending adventure through space. The title is built on the basis of a huge procedurally generated universe, which gives users the freedom to create their own path. Gamers can do a variety of things: conduct tactical battles, mine asteroids, trade goods, create items and explore mysterious alien worlds. At its core, Approaching Infinity allows for endless development, each time offering a new challenge.

Whether you choose to battle, explore, or complete quests, the title offers countless opportunities for immersion. There’s a highly customizable character creator, allowing gamers to shape their appearance and choose from 10 different space-themed classes. Once you create a character, you can recruit up to five officers, each of whom will bring their own unique abilities to your team.

You can build relationships in Approaching Infinity with over a dozen alien factions, each with their own history and quests, or simply travel from place to place completing various tasks for rewards. Flexibility of choice is key, giving you control over the direction of your adventure. Victory is one of many possible outcomes, but it is not guaranteed. The universe is full of dangers - from hostile factions to a deadly environment.
