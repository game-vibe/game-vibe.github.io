Game Description#

Jester A Foolish Ritual - a descent into despair, where the only way to salvation is the completion of an ancient sacred ritual. Within the walls of a gloomy medieval castle, fallen knights have awakened, resurrected for one last mission - to drive out the monstrous Jester that haunts them in the dark corridors. Freedom awaits only those who can find the hidden runes and open the treasured ritual chamber. However, with each artifact found, the monster’s rage and intelligence only increase, making its hunt more ruthless and sophisticated.

Survival depends on teamwork and stealth. In Jester: A Foolish Ritual, up to four protagonists can either stick together for safety or split up to search the cursed halls faster. Interaction is complicated by voice chat, which only works at close range, forcing you to carefully plan every step. The corridors are fraught with many threats, including clever traps and ominous surprise boxes, the careless activation of which will immediately summon the main antagonist. Each playthrough feels different thanks to unpredictable encounters and changing locations of key items.

Mad Hunt Rules:

Any loud sound attracts the attention of the Jester, who has keen hearing. The monster adapts to the actions of the knights, becoming stronger as they approach the target. You can face the horror alone or team up with friends in online co-op mode. Each new foray into the castle differs from the previous one in the location of runes and traps.
