Game Description#

Polylithic takes players into the heart of the Paleolithic/Neolithic era, where survival depends on cunning, resourcefulness and ingenuity. As the newly minted leader of your tribe, you’ll face the daunting task of leading your people through a vibrant, low-poly wilderness teeming with both opportunity and danger. You will spend the first days in a frantic struggle for survival. Hunger gnaws at your belly, the elements threaten your fragile existence, and every rustle in the underbrush can attract a predator. Picking berries, making simple tools and finding shelter become your top priorities. And yet, amid this struggle, a glimmer of hope began to glimmer. Every garbage collected, every tool made unlocks a new level of technology, bringing your tribe closer to civilization.

With new knowledge and stronger tools in hand, the hunt begins. The rabbits scatter through the tall grass, their timid movements revealing their rich flesh. Mammoths, once revered giants of the prehistoric landscape, now pose a formidable challenge: their skins promise warmth and their meat a feast for the entire tribe. Each successful conquest will not only satisfy your hunger, but also strengthen your reputation, attracting more souls to your nascent settlement.

As your tribe expands, its needs grow. Rough shelters become huge communal huts requiring constant maintenance and expansion. You have the heavy responsibility of being a leader. You must distribute tasks, oversee the well-being of the tribe, and navigate the subtle social dynamics that bind your people together. Elders give wise counsel, children frolic in the huts, and experienced hunters bring home the spoils, and each life story is intertwined with the fate of the tribe. Beneath the immediate concerns of survival lies a deeper secret, a gnawing unease that permeates the very fabric of your existence. Whispers of a forgotten past, ancient technologies and mysterious structures pique your curiosity.

Driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, you venture deep into the wild, unearthing the remains of lost civilizations and piecing together the scattered history of your own tribe. Polylithic is not just a game of survival, it is a journey of discovery, a chance to rewrite forgotten chapters of humanity’s past. In this vibrant, low-poly world, every sunrise brings a new challenge, every moonlit night a new revelation. So strap on your loincloths, sharpen your spears, and get ready to lead your tribe into a bright dawn.
