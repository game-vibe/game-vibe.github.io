Game Description#

STAR WARS: Squadrons takes place after the sixth episode of Return of the Jedi. Events unfold during the period when Imperial forces are defeated on Endor and lose the second Death Star. The game has two main characters. One serves on the Titan squadron of the Galactic Empire, and the second is at this time on the Defender and helps the rebels.

Online modes in STAR WARS: Squadrons support up to 10 users. Each team consists of 5 people. The modes include “Fleet Battles” and “Air Battles”. Allowed to fight with bots.

You can fly ships from the original Star Wars trilogy and Rogue One. There’s a Tie Fighter/Bomber/Reaper/Interceptor and an A/U/X/Y-Wing. Starships are divided into fighters, supports, bombers and interceptors. Military equipment differs not only in class and appearance. Each vehicle has its own cabin design. Sensors on the panels in it display the real state of the ship.

For participating in battles and killing opponents, users receive experience. They will gradually unlock new content. Here you can open weapons, shields, upgrades and cosmetics for the ship or pilot. The developers brought about 50 items for customization. In addition to multiplayer, a single player campaign is also available. In the story part there is also the opportunity to play for both sides of the conflict.
