Game Description#

Sun Haven mixes role-playing game and farming simulator together. The game has two-dimensional graphics made in the style of pixel art. The camera is located on top. The indie project has a single player mode and co-op mode. Online multiplayer supports up to 8 people. Events take place in a medieval fantasy world. The main action takes place in the village of Sun Haven and its surroundings. There is also the elven settlement of Nel’Vari and the monster city of Withergate. In the character editor you can choose your hairstyle and race. There are 7 of them: people, amari, elementals, demons, angels, elves, nagas.

An indie title gives you freedom of action. You can build and improve a farm to engage in agriculture. For those who don’t like digging in the garden, the creators added fishing, cooking, mining, and blacksmithing. It is possible to fight monsters, find or craft equipment, earn experience and level up. Melee and ranged combat, as well as magic, are available.

The user himself decides in which direction to develop his ward. There are also story and side quests. There is an opportunity to build relationships with local residents. Some NPCs are allowed to have affairs with. If the user chose the peaceful path, he will still be able to complete the game by finishing the battle with the final boss in an alternative way.
