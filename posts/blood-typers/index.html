Game Description#

Blood Typers offers a unique twist on survival horror gameplay, combining traditional horror elements with unusual control mechanics. Users find themselves in a cursed film studio, where the line between fiction and reality is blurred, and monsters from horror films invade the real world. Whether you play alone or in a group of up to four gamers, the challenge remains the same: survive the chaos and escape. The title takes place on procedurally generated levels, making each session different from the previous one.

As you progress through increasingly bizarre environments, your weapon of choice becomes typing. Combat, inventory management, and even simple tasks like opening doors require quick thinking and typing skills. Enemies can be defeated by targeting their punch words, so typing speed and accuracy is critical to survival. There are various difficulty levels, designed for both beginners and experienced typists, so users can adjust the task to their preferences.

Blood Typers features co-op, which allows gamers to share resources and fight the undead together. Proximity-based voice chat allows you to hear your teammates, adding immersion to the game as you strategize and survive together. However, even in co-op mode there is a sense of tension as you fight for your life against the terrifying creatures roaming the studio.
