Game Description#

Surviving Mars: Relaunched invites players to lead not just a survival mission, but the process of establishing an entire civilization on a hostile planet. The key task is to form an independent Martian society, free from terrestrial influence. Through a new system called the Martian Assembly, political factions within the colony will be managed. Settlers will make their own demands and adhere to certain ideologies, which will force them to make laws and develop long-term development strategies, balancing between different interest groups. The ultimate goal of this complex social and political process is to proclaim the complete sovereignty of Mars.

Before achieving political autonomy, it is necessary to lay a solid foundation for the colony, providing it with everything necessary for existence. This complex simulation from Haemimont Games forces you to carefully plan every step, from building the first domes and setting up life support systems to exploring the secrets of the planet, including the mysterious black cubes. Managing resources such as oxygen and water is the basis of survival in the world of Surviving Mars: Relaunched. Continuous expansion of infrastructure and scientific breakthroughs are opening up new opportunities for settlement growth and stability on the Red Planet.

Stages of formation of Martian civilization:

Developing a gastronomic culture to improve the comfort and sanity of the colonists through the creation of complex dishes from new ingredients. Full automation of production using powerful machines and colossal excavators, replacing human labor at all stages. Creation of an extensive transport network using trains to transport heavy goods and personnel between remote sites. Transformation of the planetary climate and subsequent settlement of Mars by animals to create a self-sufficient biosphere.
