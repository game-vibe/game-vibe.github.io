Game Description#

Sektori is an intense twin-stick shooter whose gameplay is inextricably linked to the aggressive rhythms of techno music and requires extreme concentration from the player. The project immerses you in a state of flow, where kaleidoscopic visuals, soundtrack and gameplay merge into one. The main task is to destroy a myriad of opponents in a constantly transforming arena. The battlefield dynamically changes its shape, expanding, contracting or even splitting into several parts, which forces you to constantly adapt battle tactics and does not allow you to rely on memorized patterns.

Under control is a maneuverable ship, protected not by traditional lives, but by energy shields. Destroyed enemies leave behind gold, which is necessary to purchase upgrades from a special deck. Modifications for the ship, such as increasing armor or missiles, are acquired sequentially from the proposed queue, and not by free choice. Sektori’s special tactic is the ability to use the ship itself as a powerful projectile, which allows you to inflict enormous damage, but carries the enormous risk of being defenseless in the midst of enemies.

Structurally, the title offers a campaign consisting of five unique worlds with epic bosses at the end of each level. Each playthrough becomes unique due to the procedural generation of waves of opponents and the random selection of final opponents from the available pool. Additional variety is provided by alternative ship types and customizable upgrade decks, allowing you to create your own playstyle. In addition, there are six alternative modes and several difficulty levels, including the maximum called Revolution, to test your skills.
