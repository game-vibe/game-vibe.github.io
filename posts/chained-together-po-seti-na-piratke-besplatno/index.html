Game Description#

You can playChained Together online for free, even on a pirated version. If you want to have fun with a friend, try this online game. Your journey will begin in the depths of hell, chained to your comrades. Your goal is to escape from this place, rising higher and higher. Each jump requires perfect coordination to climb onto the platforms and escape the scorching heat. Explore a variety of online worlds, each offering unique challenges. New tasks will require your attention and dexterity. You can play alone or with friends using local multiplayer for up to four people. The game adapts to the number of players, offering a variety of ways to play.

The chain is not just a decorative element. It interacts with physical laws, wrapping around platforms and affecting the movement of characters. The game has three difficulty levels: Beginner, Normal and Lava. In Starter Mode, you can teleport to the highest point you reach before you fall seriously. In Normal mode, you will not have any special assistants - if you fall, you will have to start over. In Lava mode, you need to quickly rise to avoid the lava, which consumes everything in its path.

In Beginner and Normal modes, an auto-save feature is available, allowing you to resume the game from where you left off. Player leaders can track their escape speeds using leaderboards, comparing game completion times across different player configurations.
