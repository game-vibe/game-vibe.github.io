Game Description#

Have You Seen This Man? is a first-person psychological horror, the plot of which takes you into the atmosphere of Texas in 1975. The gameplay is based on a methodical exploration of the streets of Emsdorf County, where you will have to knock on doors and communicate with local residents. Each encounter is unique: from conversations with eccentric conspiracy theorists to clashes with aggressive hermits with shotguns at the ready. Some of them will help in the investigation, while others will deliberately lead on the wrong trail. All these interactions are designed to immerse you deeper into the unsettling atmosphere of a place full of omissions and dark secrets. Gradually revealing the secrets of the district, the protagonist gets closer to the solution.

The main character is Weldon McCall, a former homicide detective from Dallas who now holds the post of sheriff in the quiet county of Emsdorf. His quiet life is shattered when violent crimes resume in the city, reminiscent of an old unsolved case. A ruthless killer who preys exclusively on parents and leaves children alive is on the trail again. This nightmare from the past, which Weldon thought was long forgotten, now threatens his own family. In the project Have You Seen This Man? The sheriff has to find the maniac before he himself becomes his next victim. This desperate investigation becomes the hero’s only chance to close the bloody chapter of his life.

Combing the streets of Emsdorf County:

Communicate with each resident to collect evidence and rumors. Thorough research of locations and crime scenes. Confronting hostile characters. Finding the serial killer before he strikes again.
