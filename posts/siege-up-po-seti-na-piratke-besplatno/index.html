Game Description#

On the pirateSiege Up! you can play online for free with a friend, competing with each other or cooperating with each other online to complete the story campaign. Single mode, multiplayer and an editor are available, allowing you to create your own maps and scenarios. You can compete, go through the campaign together, or play on different random maps alone or in co-op.

The gameplay allows you to build cities, erect walls, research technologies, capture resources and much more. Both PvP modes and cooperative missions are available. Free online play allows you to compete locally. Visual side of Siege Up! presented with stylish low-poly graphics. The gameplay is a classic real-time strategy. You can play with a partner or the whole team.

The campaign includes a set of 30 missions without a clear plot. Two races with their own unique abilities and characteristics take part in the conflict. Using the capabilities of a powerful editor, you can create single and multiplayer maps. They can be edited and shared with friends at any time. Scripts are created using various objects, commands and triggers. The gameplay is based on realistic physics. Objects are destroyed according to physics. The buildings consist of various cubes. The gameplay is presented from an isometric perspective. You can build both megacities and entire continents.
