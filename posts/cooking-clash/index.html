Game Description#

Cooking Clash invites you to try on the role of a chef and owner of a noisy diner in virtual reality format. The main task becomes not only masterful cooking, but also managing real chaos. The establishment attracts the most bizarre visitors, among whom there are not only people, but also various animals with their own strange requests. In addition, you have to constantly be on guard, because some guests are not averse to stealing other people’s tips. To protect your earned money from thieves, there is even a pistol hidden in the desk drawer, which adds comical absurdity to the events in a cartoonish visual style.

Cooking Clash’s gameplay encourages creativity and moves away from strictly following recipes. There is complete freedom to experiment with numerous ingredients, allowing you to create unique and sometimes wild dishes. The process is completely interactive: all objects in the kitchen must be picked up and moved manually, as well as interact with the equipment. The atmosphere of madness is also supported by the unusual components available for use, including grenades and special super-ingredients that can radically change the result. As the establishment works, the tension only increases, turning each shift into an unpredictable test.

What’s behind this diner’s door?

An unusual clientele that includes both humans and zombie animals. Complete freedom in combining products to create signature dishes. The need to constantly monitor tips due to thieving visitors.
