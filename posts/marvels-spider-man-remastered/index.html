Game Description#

Marvel’s Spider-Man Remastered is an open-world action-adventure game set in New York City. Supervillains break free in the form of characters such as Mister Negative, Rhino, Scorpio, Vulture and Electro. But before he can track them down, Peter Parker contacts William Fisk. After he arrests a businessman, a gang war breaks out. Now only Spider-Man can stop the crime raging in the city. Marvel’s Spider-Man Remastered includes an extensive expansion for The City That Never Sleeps.

The gameplay allows you to freely explore the open world, represented by an entire metropolis. The main feature of the adventure is verticality. You can fly on the web between skyscrapers. The hero is able to climb walls and buildings. On the web, you can stick to various objects or use the environment for your own purposes. The combat system consists of attacks and counterattacks. Getting into the timing, the protagonist reflects the blows of enemies and inflicts crushing damage on opponents. You can carry out combinations, use various Spider superpowers and use the interactive environment.

Role-playing elements are introduced. By defeating enemies and completing both main and secondary tasks, Peter gains experience, which must be spent on new skills. With the help of new moves, the list of combos increases significantly, thereby making fights more spectacular. The arsenal of weapons consists of various Spider gadgets. There are different types of webs, different drones, bombs, etc. available. As you progress, new costumes are unlocked. Some of them have additional properties. The story campaign consists of three episodes: Heist, Gang Wars and Silver Ray. The story features many familiar characters in the form of Aunt May, Norman Osborn, Otto Octavius, Silver Sable and Miles Morales.
