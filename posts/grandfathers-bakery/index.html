Game Description#

Grandfather’s Bakery is a management simulator in which a difficult mission falls on the shoulders of the protagonist. Having inherited an old stone bakery in a small town from his grandfather, he is faced with the need to restore the establishment to its former glory. This task is complicated by accumulated debts that require immediate repayment. Reviving the family business will require strategic thinking and hard work, as every decision will determine whether the bakery will once again become the pride of the local community. It is necessary to turn a dying business into a thriving enterprise, honoring the traditions of the past and achieving recognition from the townspeople.

Success directly depends on many factors that require constant monitoring. The process at Grandfather’s Bakery covers every aspect of the baking craft, from sourcing quality ingredients like flour or yeast to following recipes exactly. Optimization of production cycles and competent cost management allow us to fulfill daily sales plans. It is through satisfying customer requests and creating an impeccable reputation that the path to financial stability and prosperity of the legendary establishment, built by the hands of the grandfather, runs.

Secrets of reviving a family business:

Searching for reliable suppliers for purchasing the freshest flour, yeast and water. Precisely recreated ancient recipes to achieve the perfect taste of baked goods. Prudent expense management to effectively pay off old debts. Gaining the trust of citizens through quality service and an excellent product.
