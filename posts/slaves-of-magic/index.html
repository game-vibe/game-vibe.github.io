Game Description#

Slaves of Magic is a tactical project where every decision on the battlefield has enormous weight. The appearance of enemy reinforcements is heralded by purple magical seals, and over time the enemy onslaught only intensifies, becoming more frequent and powerful. The combat system, based on sequential activation of units depending on their initiative, forces you to carefully plan every step. Soldiers have action points to spend on movement and attacks, and it is critical to choose not only the correct position, but also the direction of the soldier’s gaze before completing a move.

The invasion of otherworldly forces using magic has brought humanity to the brink of complete enslavement. The responsibility for saving civilization falls on the shoulders of the commander leading the resistance forces. Opponents masquerading as gods are ravaging the lands in pursuit of their dark goals. In Slaves of Magic you will have to sabotage their operations and fight back against previously unseen creatures who wield false divine magic. Overconfidence here is a direct path to the death of units, so each battle requires a balanced approach.

Laws of survival in the new world:

It is necessary to maintain morale and hope in the surviving settlements. It is required to systematically expand influence, establishing new bases of resistance. Researching technologies to gain superiority is key. It is important to competently lead the troops in order to save the lives of soldiers for future battles.
