Game Description#

ChronoDojo is a co-op adventure in which a group of humble heroes embark on a journey through time to save the world. Under the guidance of their mentor Sensei, the characters will have to master the martial art of Chrono-Fu. Their headquarters is ChronoDojo itself, a martial arts school that can travel between eras and doubles as a tea house. You will have to confront waves of bizarre enemies in three different historical periods, using unique abilities and well-coordinated teamwork.

The gameplay is a hybrid of the tower defense and beat ‘em up genres. Gamers not only need to personally participate in battles, but also strategically build defensive towers to contain opponents. A key mechanic is time management: it can be speeded up or slowed down at each level, which opens up tactical scope for achieving victory. The project supports both single player and four-player co-op mode, which requires at least one controller for local multiplayer.

Heroes of unusual professions play the role of saviors of the world: Guardian, Cook, Gardener, Repairman, Courier and Nurse. As you progress, you gain access to a varied arsenal, including stylish shoes, unusual weapons and powerful pets. In addition, to strengthen the characters, various drinks are provided that provide temporary advantages in battle. Battles take place on colorful two-dimensional levels, where the heroes will not only have to fight off enemies, but also confront powerful bosses.
