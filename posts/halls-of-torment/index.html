Game Description#

Halls of Torment invites you into the world of terrifying monsters in this unique action game with roguelite elements, where you will face new challenges and dangers in each game. Dive into the dark and ruthless dungeons where the lords of the underworld await you. Find treasures and magical artifacts, and assemble a team of heroes who will help you defeat the horrors emanating from the depths of the earth. Will you be able to survive?

In Halls of Torment you will experience the atmosphere of retro games of the 90s, recreated in an old school pre-rendered style. Choose one of many heroes and go to the deadly Halls of Torment. Fight ungodly horrors and survive wave after wave until you face one of the torturous lords. Upgrade your hero with unique characteristics, abilities and equipment, creating a new powerful build in each new playthrough. Explore various underground spaces and find new powerful items that will help you go even deeper into the abyss. Make quick and fun games that take no more than 30 minutes. Choose from a wide variety of abilities, stats, and equipment to create interesting synergies. Fight fearsome bosses with unique mechanics and attack patterns. And, just as importantly, items found can be used to customize future passages. Over a dozen interesting characters allow you to create many different playstyles without limiting your options.
