Game Description#

Street Racer Collection (QUByte Classics) is a hybrid of arcade racing and combat competitions, where reaching the finish line is just one part of success. The main way to interact with opponents is not just overtaking, but direct physical elimination using special techniques. You can control a dozen colorful pilots, each of whom has a vehicle with unique attack capabilities. Competitions take place on routes full of dangers, running through both recognizable landmarks and completely fantastic areas. The driving process requires constant concentration, because you must simultaneously monitor the road and fight off aggressive opponents.

The edition includes four stand-alone interpretations of the original project, offering a varied experience. This content is the core of the Street Racer Collection (QUByte Classics), expanding the usual boundaries of the genre. In addition to the standard championship format, non-traditional disciplines are also provided. Among them there are competitions reminiscent of football matches, and arenas focused exclusively on transport battles with the use of weapons. Advancing through cups in career mode opens up access to new drivers and their cars. Local multiplayer mode enhances the chaotic nature of what is happening, turning each race into an unpredictable and comical confrontation.
