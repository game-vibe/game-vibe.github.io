Game Description#

Tyrannis: Co-Prosperity continues the story of its predecessor, placing gamers in the thick of a battle with the Kirihara Group, a dominant megacorporation. The title introduces new challenges as freelancers take on the Kirihara Group’s security division, using guerrilla tactics to fight back in a dystopian East Asia. With the addition of 40 new maps and a revamped campaign, the stakes become higher as players navigate a landscape filled with tension and conflict.

The gameplay of Tyrannis: Co-Prosperity is based on asymmetrical warfare. On the one hand, gamers control the Security Forces, a powerful and well-equipped faction aimed at suppressing the uprising. On the other hand, civilian players, who are outnumbered and underpowered, rely on cunning and strategy to disrupt the megacorporation’s operations. This dynamic creates strategic depth that forces users to think on their feet, adapting to changing conditions on the battlefield.

The title allows you to create your own factions, giving gamers the opportunity to adapt their forces to their preferred style. Whether it’s to fill the ranks of civilians or create a more formidable security unit, the game’s possibilities are very wide. Additionally, the branching campaign path provides replayability, allowing gamers to explore different scenarios depending on their choices. Loot is a key aspect of gameplay, with users earning rewards through strategic conquests and raids.
