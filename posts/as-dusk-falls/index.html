Game Description#

As Dusk Falls is an interactive movie game that will allow you to fully immerse yourself in the atmosphere that will take place here. You will be able to see different situations and literally experience them, diving deeper and deeper. You will need to choose, perform various kinds of actions that will allow you to better unravel the tangled tangle of the storyline. But you need to understand that all this, sooner or later, will have its weight. This means it will influence how events will develop.

In As Dusk Falls we will talk about 2 families. These are completely different people who, perhaps, would never have met, but fate decided otherwise. They are now closely intertwined and connected with each other, and will go through their own path in life, albeit at odds, but still connected. You will be able to watch how in this drama all the actions will slowly but surely change your life.

It all starts back in 1998, in the state of Arizona. Then a very unsuccessful robbery occurred. But it was precisely this that launched a chain of actions that now cannot be stopped. Every family has its own secrets and secrets, and therefore this is a story about betrayal, difficult choices, and self-sacrifice. The trick is that the gamer can replay the levels. Moreover, this will always give a different, alternative ending.
