Game Description#

First Feudal takes the gamer to the classic Middle Ages and invites you to become a feudal lord (landowner and lord). The game does not have a plot - users face a classic sandbox with endless gameplay.

Initially it will be difficult, because a small village is vulnerable and a small landowner is not able to protect it. But try to make good money and gather your own army so that no one tries to take the resources. The developers have created a project for fans with good opportunities for managing a settlement. During the passage you need to do:

the construction of a safe and comfortable town, where it is necessary to take into account the mood of the residents, build fortifications and competently plan the development; population management - all peasants and artisans can receive one of nine professions that will correspond to their role; development of the economy and trade - a merchant will periodically visit from whom you can obtain valuable items, and with proper development, the function of independently sending caravans appears; research into sciences that open up more opportunities and technologies; battles against robbers and wild animals who will carry out regular raids, however, there is always the option to pay off the former. Interestingly, it is possible to independently lead people into battle to carry out all administrative duties or transfer these affairs to hired and trained specially trained heroes.
