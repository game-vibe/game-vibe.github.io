Game Description#

ISLAND is an intricate adventure story, made in the visual novel genre with elements of a role-playing game, expressed in the non-linearity of the passage due to the choice of lines in dialogues and the possibility of playing a protagonist with different characters. The project has a huge number of endings and routes along which you can get to them. The gamer receives a sufficient number of hints on the way to completing the passage so as not to get confused in the intricacies of the storylines.

The setting is a fictional island called Urashima, located far from Japan, of which it is an administrative unit. The user needs to understand all the secrets of this place, which will soon disappear. The purpose of the journey is to try to save him by putting into action an ancient prophecy, which states that the territory can be saved with the help of three girls belonging to the 3 most ancient dynasties living on Urashima.

The gameplay consists of a huge number of dialogues taking place between the main characters of the game “Island”. The gameplay is based on their interaction. The changes in the characters of both the girls and the guy who washed ashore in an unconscious state will depend on the relationships in the team.
