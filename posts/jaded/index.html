Game Description#

Jaded is a 2D platformer where any mistake, be it colliding with an enemy or falling on spikes, immediately returns the hero to the beginning of the room, providing an infinite number of attempts to overcome the challenges. The main tool for survival is the ability to slow down time using a 360-degree dash. This mechanic allows you not only to maneuver in the air, but also to lock on to targets to deliver precise strikes. The special technique also makes it possible to bind two opponents in one movement, dealing damage to them simultaneously and introducing a tactical element to battles.

The journey of the jade creature, striving to fulfill its destiny, runs through diverse and dangerous locations. The route leads through snow-capped mountains, dense forests and scorching volcanic caves, eventually leading to a mysterious abandoned laboratory. In the world of Jaded you will face numerous opponents, including bizarre purple birds, warrior soldiers and blue crawling creatures. The basis of the gameplay is the precise overcoming of obstacles, which requires sharp reflexes. There are secret collectibles scattered throughout the world that require careful discovery to discover. The plot introduces various characters, including such a memorable one as Trot VIII.
