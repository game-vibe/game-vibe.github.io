Game Description#

The events of SpaceBourne 2 begin from the end of the first part, only now the player needs to build his own space empire and develop it in all directions. However, there is no need to rush with this global task. The game world is completely open to exploration of each solar system, where the user can first become famous or, on the contrary, become an enemy, and then make it easier for themselves to establish diplomatic relations.

The main character is a space traveler who is free to do whatever he wants. True, for this he needs funds to maintain and improve the ship, which means he will still have to carry out various missions and assignments. But he doesn’t need to navigate the expanses of the universe alone, because due to the size of the ship, he couldn’t do it without a crew. Each member has their own needs and tasks that can be assigned to them. Features of the protagonist of SpaceBourne 2:

Mining - minerals are valued everywhere, because they are the main resource for construction, fuel and other export goods, so in order to effectively develop the economy it is worth purchasing and launching several mines on asteroids and satellites Trade - space is full of abandoned or destroyed ships, but you can collect many valuable items from them and then sell them on the market at a good price, you can also engage in piracy, but this is not a safe business Hunting - rebels is a big problem for some states and in order to suppress the riots they are ready to pay a tidy sum for the head of the rebel leader or the conviction of his death Wormholes are portals that lead to random places in the universe or even dimensions, an excellent and convenient way to travel, as well as search for unique items

After the protagonist has accumulated enough strength and money through tasks, he can begin to create his own state on the territory of abandoned planets. Diplomatic agreements will become the basis of the country’s existence, since with their help you can exchange goods and expand your capabilities. But for this you need to convince each Starlord and various councils.
