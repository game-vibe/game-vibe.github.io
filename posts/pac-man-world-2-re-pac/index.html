Game Description#

PAC-MAN WORLD 2 Re-PAC takes the main character on a journey through the diverse regions of PAC-LAND. The adventure begins after the Ghosts steal the Golden Fruit from PAC-Village, inadvertently releasing a powerful ghost king named Spooky. The key task is to return the stolen artifacts and defeat the antagonist to restore order. The mission begins in an expanded village that serves as a central location from which gamers set out to explore the larger world.

Navigating the varied environments of PAC-MAN WORLD 2 Re-PAC requires a set of special platforming skills. The character runs, eats dots and performs push-off jumps to overcome obstacles and cross difficult terrain. The arsenal also includes a high-speed roll to gain momentum, a fluttering jump over dangerous rifts, and a PAC-Dot attack to deal with opponents. At some levels, the gameplay is complemented by unique mechanics, such as skating on Snowy Mountain or controlling the PAC-Marine bathyscaphe during deep-sea diving.

This project is a reworked adventure with expanded locations and modified boss battles, adapted for modern systems. New actions have been added to the protagonist’s arsenal, as well as additional collectibles and customization options. The story is accompanied by full voice acting and includes interactions with numerous characters. In addition, the title offers a two-player mode, allowing you to take on challenges together in cooperative and competitive formats.
