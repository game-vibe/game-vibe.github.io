Game Description#

Sorry We’re Closed is a tense survival horror game inspired by the old school genre. You’ll follow the gripping story of Michelle, a young woman who finds herself cursed by a powerful demon and given only days to live. Determined to make the most of the remaining time, the heroine sets out on a journey to uncover the secrets of her city and the world of demons, while simultaneously searching for a way to lift the curse and escape.

One of the standout features is the rich story and character interactions that play a crucial role in shaping your journey and ultimately determining your destiny. As you progress, you will have to choose how to deal with the various people and demons you encounter, and these decisions will significantly affect the outcome. There are multiple endings to discover, and your actions and choices can influence the course of the story.

Sorry We’re Closed seamlessly blends retro survival horror elements with modern gameplay mechanics. As you move around the game world, you will be greeted by fixed camera angles, creating a tense and atmospheric environment. To survive, you must cleverly use the environment to hide and avoid enemies. In addition, you will have the opportunity to fight back with the Demon Pistol, a unique weapon that allows you to shoot from a first-person perspective.
