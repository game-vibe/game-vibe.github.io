Game Description#

GLONK is an incremental project in which the entire gameplay is built around feeding the creature of the same name. The central task is to constantly provide the pet with food for its subsequent increase in size, which directly affects financial well-being. The viability and continuous growth of the ward is the only source of income, so maintaining his health becomes a paramount task for accumulating gold. Successfully nurturing one creature opens up the opportunity to have several more, thereby scaling up profits and accelerating advancement to new levels.

The funds you earn are invested in an extensive skill tree, which opens up access to new abilities and unexpected bonuses. In addition to enhancing basic characteristics, the talent system allows you to acquire additional pets. Progression in GLONK makes it possible to significantly speed up the enrichment process. However, the creatures' boundless gluttony has its price. Their insatiable hunger ultimately leads to unpredictable and potentially catastrophic consequences that radically change the usual course of events.

Pet development through the skill tree:

Boosting your base stats unlocks passive upgrades that directly increase your creature’s feeding efficiency and overall growth rate. Purchasing additional pets allows you to unlock new slots, which is key to scaling your passive income. Accelerating the enrichment process activates special talents aimed at multiplying the gold received and reducing the time to reach new levels.
