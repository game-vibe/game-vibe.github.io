Game Description#

Beneath is a dive into the chilling depths of the ocean, where experienced diver Noah Quinn must face the horrors generated by sinister experiments. In the cramped corridors of underwater research stations, a desperate struggle for survival unfolds. Ammunition is running low, and you have to confront not only the ruthless mercenaries of the Omnium corporation, but also the nightmarish Lovecraftian creatures that have awakened in the Arctic abyss. Every shadow lurks a threat, and the hero’s sanity is constantly tested by supernatural visions and attacks on his mind.

The driving force for Noah in this hell is not just the instinct of self-preservation, but a desperate race against time to save the life of his own daughter. This personal mission forces him to make his way deeper into the flooded complexes, where every step could be his last. Project Beneath forces you to carefully manage scarce resources, using not only an arsenal of upgradeable weapons, but also elements of the environment to eliminate opponents. Combat in confined spaces requires tactical thinking as enemies, be they humans or monsters, act with maximum aggression.

Unraveling the dark secret of the Omnium corporation occurs gradually, through the study of evidence scattered throughout the stations: emails, PDA entries and testimonies of other survivors. By piecing together pieces of information, the protagonist comes closer to understanding the true scale of the conspiracy that threatens all of humanity. He will have to find out what ancient evil was disturbed at the bottom of the ocean and stop it before the abyss swallows everything.
