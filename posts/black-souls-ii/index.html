Game Description#

BLACK SOULS 2 takes the player on a journey through a world inspired by Alice in Wonderland and Alice Through the Looking Glass, reimagined as a dark fairy tale with elements of psychological horror. In this crazy setting, the gaming experience is defined by interactions with 45 heroines, who are female versions of the characters in the original story. These figures, from the White Rabbit to the Queen of Hearts, are central to the narrative’s overt themes and deadly encounters with demonic creatures.

The plot focuses on exploration and survival in a hostile environment. The key mechanic in the title BLACK SOULS 2 is the SEN system, which directly affects the stability of the kingdom. Killing any NPC reduces the value, fundamentally changing the environment. As this indicator drops, the background music disappears, the inhabitants begin to communicate in an incomprehensible language, and previously invisible phenomena become obvious.

This mechanism adds weight to the implications when exploring a vast open setting with its many connected locations. Gameplay involves moving around Wonderland to collect souls and level up. Battles take place in a tactical turn-based mode with an ATB system that turns every encounter into a critical choice between kill and death. Defeat is not the end, but only a temporary obstacle, as progress resumes from the last activated bonfire.

Features of survival in a distorted fairy tale:

An exploration of a dark setting based on a famous fairy tale. Interact with 45 unique heroines who define the story. The SEN system, where every murder irreversibly changes the environment. Intense tactical battles in turn-based mode. Many endings depending on the decisions made.
