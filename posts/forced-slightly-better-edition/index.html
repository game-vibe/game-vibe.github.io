Game Description#

FORCED: Slightly Better Edition is a top-down action RPG about unique gladiatorial battles for your freedom in an unusual arena. Your character will be assisted by a spirit named Balfus.

You have to take one of the slaves presented to choose from, specially raised in a closed village, where everyone born becomes a slave. Upgrading your combat skills, you must defeat the guards of each of the mystical arenas. Balfus will become the main one for advancing the plot, because only he conducts a monologue, when both the protagonists are silent, and the antagonists can only mock you.

You are always given the choice to go it alone or invite up to 3 friends and start a co-op campaign. At the start you are given a choice of weapons; they all have 16 properties to upgrade. There are 4 specializations: Storm Bow, Volcanic Hammer, Daggers of Souls, Shield of Ice. When playing together and with the right combination of classes and skills, you will get a serious advantage over your opponents.

The first arena will immediately present a fight with the first boss – Wrathhoof. This will be a good test of your initial skills in this genre of games, because the battle will take place in several different stages. As you progress, you’ll become more familiar with the mechanics of battles, such as activating healing monuments or traps with Balthus, and you’ll be ready for increasingly difficult challenges.

Many bosses await you as you progress - Grogu, Slart, Mordar, Master. Defeat each of them with a well-coordinated team or alone. The difficulty will depend on the number of people, but well-coordinated teamwork will not notice the difficulties.
