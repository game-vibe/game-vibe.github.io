Game Description#

Wobbly Life is a bright and dynamic sandbox that relies on an open world and body physics. The gameplay is tailored for local and online co-op. Team up with friends to compete in various mini-games and competitions. There is a single-player game aimed at developing your hero. Earn money and spend it on new skins, clothes, equipment and much more.

You will explore the vastness of Wobbly Island, controlling cute characters that resemble children’s toys. The gameplay gives a lot of fun due to the funny animation of bodies and their physics. Complete various assignments and take on different jobs to accumulate funds. Work as a repairman, loader, courier, etc. Having saved enough money, you can buy your own real estate, transport or a whole wardrobe of clothes. Features of Wobbly Life: unique visual style; the ability to play with funny characters; co-op for up to 4 people; a well-developed open world represented by various secrets, vehicles, characters, etc.; full customization of heroes; a huge number of different tasks and errands; the opportunity to buy a house, cars and many other useful things.
