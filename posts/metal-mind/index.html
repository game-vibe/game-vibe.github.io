Game Description#

Metal Mind is a 2D action roguelike that gives you the opportunity to control a mech that has a mind. The gameplay combines many mechanics and various physical quantities: weight, heat, power, etc. Metal Mind contains elements of looting. It is necessary to look for pieces of armor, improved weapons and create useful items. You have to transform the robot using hundreds of different assemblies.

Events take place in a science fiction world where people have been able to build self-developing artificial intelligences that have managed to become self-aware. During the course of the game, players will encounter an anthropomorphized AI, an organization consisting of politicians, scientists, engineers and agents. They all have one thing in common: they believe that robots should not have intelligence and self-awareness. Therefore, they were declared outlaws and began to hunt for androids.

The game features robots such as Chappie, Robocom, Pilot and other characters. Heroes have guns on their shoulders, protective armor, enhanced equipment for fighting powerful opponents, etc. The gameplay allows you to design your own robot. The character consists of four parts and hundreds of different transformations. The main parameters are Core - the character’s power and speed, Drive - maximum load, Body - survivability and Weapon - the hero’s combat abilities.
