Game Description#

BRAZILIAN DRUG DEALER 3 is not a title for the faint of heart, because it throws users straight into a world where chaos reigns, and their main task is simple: to correct the disorder caused by an unforeseen and catastrophic ritual. The plot centers on a fan of the cult Brazilian funk musician MIT AIA, who, while trying to revive the star, accidentally opens a portal to Hell. At this point, everything takes a turn for the worse, and suddenly the entire state of Rio de Janeiro is overrun by demons and possessed creatures.

What is your task as a player? Restore order and close the portal before things get even more twisted. From the very beginning of BRAZILIAN DRUG DEALER 3, gamers are thrown into a fast-paced, unforgiving environment filled with strange and bizarre enemies. You’ll have to fight everything from armed demons to possessed Brazilian singers, zombies and even clowns driving around in trucks. It’s a bizarre and dangerous mixture that keeps you on your guard at all times.

To survive, users will need quick reflexes, smart strategy and the ability to use earthquake-like movement abilities. These abilities allow you to quickly move around the city, dodging all sorts of chaos while you try to figure out how to reverse the damage caused by the portal. The action never stops, and there is no time to think: every step can be the difference between life and death in this twisted world.
