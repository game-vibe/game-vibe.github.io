Game Description#

Erannorth Renaissance is an RPG where every decision shapes the unfolding journey. Users move around a vast world, freely creating their own path, participating in turn-based battles and interacting with a number of unique heroes. The title combines tabletop mechanics and expansive storytelling, offering a variety of possibilities in a Renaissance-inspired setting.

The characters evolve based on the choices made throughout their journey, affecting not only their personal growth, but also the world around them. Customization plays a central role, allowing gamers to customize abilities, skills, and allegiances. Erannorth Renaissance’s modular system supports flexibility, giving room for a variety of playstyles, be it diplomacy, combat or exploration. The character’s backstory lays the groundwork, but the progression remains fluid, giving each playthrough its own possibilities.

Thanks to its open structure, the title does not limit users to one approach, encouraging creative problem solving. The setting reacts to the actions of the gamer: factions, alliances and rivalries change dynamically. Encounters with various groups, including secret societies and supernatural beings, create evolving scenarios. Navigating this confusing social landscape requires strategy as relationships influence both immediate opportunities and long-term consequences. Some strive for power, while others prioritize loyalty or knowledge.
