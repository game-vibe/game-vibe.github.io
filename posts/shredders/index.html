Game Description#

Shredders is a snowboarding simulator. The indie project contains a large number of references for fans of this sport and related media products. You can play it alone or with other users online. For single-player fans, there is a single-player story mode. The plot begins with the protagonist and best friend Scotty trying to make entertaining videos about winter extreme sports. A representative of one of the brands named Lisa contacts them and offers sponsorship along with participation in the championship. The campaign is divided into missions. It gives you the opportunity to ride through various mountain biomes. If you wish, you can repeat tasks to get a higher result. There are also side quests.

Shredders has an open world. The main point of the game is to perform tricks by pressing certain combinations. There is no list of techniques, so users will need to understand the mechanics themselves. You can do somersaults, slide through pipes, fly up on trampolines, and spin in different directions while in the air. There is also the opportunity to hook onto a snowmobile and ride on a cable. The video game features official board and equipment manufacturers, as well as real-life snowboarders. For example: Arthur Longo, Leanne Pelosi, Thor Lundstrom. They gave the characters not only their appearance, but also their voices. In addition, the title recreated the atmosphere of the early 2000s.
