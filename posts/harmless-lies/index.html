Game Description#

Harmless Lies invites you to immerse yourself in an intense story where the consequences of one careless word become irreversible. The story revolves around the idea of ​​how a small lie, told with the best intentions, sets off a chain of devastating events. The protagonist finds himself trapped by circumstance, forced to weave an increasingly complex web of deception to hide the original untruth. The atmosphere of constant psychological pressure forces you to carefully weigh every decision, because it can lead to a completely unpredictable ending and radically change relationships with other characters.

The central theme of the project is the exploration of moral dilemmas and the gray area between truth and fiction. The gameplay in Harmless Lies is closely linked to the dialogue system, where every word has weight and shapes the further development of the plot. It is necessary to constantly analyze information, grasp the hidden motives of interlocutors and try to understand who is speaking sincerely and who is pursuing their own goals. This experience challenges the very concept of white lies, demonstrating their destructive potential in a closed society permeated with mistrust.

Spiral of deception and its price:

Facing a distorted reality created by your own words. Having to make difficult ethical decisions under pressure. Gradual destruction of trust in relationships with others. The realization that every secret has its consequences.
