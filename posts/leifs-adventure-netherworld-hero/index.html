Game Description#

Leif’s Adventure: Netherworld Hero is a co-op adventure about constant combat, friendship and teamwork. The boy never wanted to be a hero, but one day he learns that responding with force to rudeness is not always right. Users take control of two different characters, like yin and yang. They have to go through hundreds of dangers that have appeared in their lives and join forces.

The main characters find themselves in the Underworld, where evil sources will stand in their way. The gameplay features single-player and co-op modes. In a solo campaign, the gamer will switch between characters at will and customize his playthrough. Leif is necessary for running fast, jumping over chasms and participating in fights. The Ghost is best at exploring dark and secret areas, activating intangible objects, turning on elevators, platforms, and collecting collectibles. As they progress, they unlock new abilities and skills. Leif will unlock access to military weapons, increase the maximum amount of health, learn about throwing bombs or air attacks.

The arsenal includes long weapons, short swords, spears, sets of throwing items and armor. Armor helps protect against critical attacks from opponents. At the same time, you can wear 10 types of items with original abilities, strategies for battles and lethality. Ghost’s supernatural talents will allow you to climb ropes, crouch, and explore the far corners of each area.
