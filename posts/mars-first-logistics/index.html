Game Description#

Mars First Logistics physics and transportation simulator with an open world to explore. The game was invented so that the player could feel like a delivery driver of various cargo items with a capricious shape. To deliver the cargo that the game provides, you need to take a vehicle. You can make a car or a vehicle on wheels yourself from various mechanisms offered by the game. To promote and develop your mechanical creation, you need to receive virtual currency called credits. The gamer has the opportunity to make every effort, experiment with different parts and use his imagination to the fullest to create the newest special inventions. The main task is to create and manage your own colony on Mars. There are a lot of functions in Mars First Logistics, but you need to know the main ones in advance before you start playing.

Main features of the game:

create a working mechanism with your own hands; experiment in combining parts, rocket engines, hydraulic cylinders; transportation of awkward parts of unusual shape; the process is possible not only in single mode, but also in online co-op; battle with different enemy elements in a large, not completely open world.
