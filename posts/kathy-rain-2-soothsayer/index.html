Game Description#

Kathy Rain 2: Soothsayer is set in 1998, taking place in the urban environment of Cassidy, where disturbing events occur. The player takes on the role of private detective Katie Raine, who is faced with serious personal problems that require financial resources. Her vehicle is in need of fuel, her food supply is nearly exhausted, and she is facing eviction. At this moment, information appears about a large monetary reward for solving a series of murders that shocked the city’s residents.

The central pursuit in Kathy Rain 2: Soothsayer targets a man known as the Soothsayer, who is responsible for a string of serial deaths. The gameplay requires the detective to find clues, collect physical evidence and establish connections between disparate facts to form a complete picture of what happened. Successful progress leads to a direct meeting with the person who committed these crimes. Chasing this man involves significant personal risk for Katie herself; the investigator may become the next victim of the one she is looking for.

Solving this chain of brutal acts requires overcoming the immediate danger associated with tracking down the killer responsible for many fatalities within the city limits. The process in this game goes beyond simply recording the facts, heading towards the necessary, high-risk confrontation with who is behind these violent acts. Advancement in the story involves direct confrontation with the possibility of physical harm or worse from the pursued object during this urban challenge.
