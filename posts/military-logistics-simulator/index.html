Game Description#

Military Logistics Simulator is a first-person simulator dedicated to managing the complex support mechanisms of modern armies. The project proposes to concentrate not on front-line battles, but on rear operations, on which the outcome of any armed conflict depends. Players will have to go through professional growth from an ordinary supply chain to an experienced quartermaster, taking full responsibility for organizing and protecting supply chains in hostile territory. The main goal is not the destruction of the enemy, but the systematic restoration of the war-torn region, including the rescue of the surviving population and the revival of critical infrastructure.

The process of passing involves the constant development of our own operating base and vehicle fleet. Completing high-paying contracts opens up access to financing, allowing you to purchase new buildings and more advanced equipment. In Military Logistics Simulator it is necessary not only to deliver valuable cargo, but also to repel sabotage or drone attacks. Fulfilling the assigned tasks requires an integrated approach, because the gamer will have to:

evacuate surviving civilians. use the German Shepherd to detect explosives. transport and deploy air defense systems. drive reconnaissance trucks and heavy transporters.
