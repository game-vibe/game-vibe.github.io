Game Description#

Uktena 64 - Ultimate is a dark comedy horror game with visuals inspired by the aesthetics of the N64 console. The core of the gameplay is to track and eliminate infected fauna that are spreading an unknown deadly virus. The project offers a choice of several difficulty levels, from extremely simple to almost unbalanced. To diversify the passage, a system of unlockable cheat codes is provided that allows you to radically change the established rules. The atmosphere of the hunt is maintained through a variety of low-poly locations and encounters with creepy creatures that go beyond the human imagination.

The plot outline is built around two brothers with diametrically opposed tasks. Jebadiah, having received an offer from the Center for Disease Control, goes in search of sick animals in order to document their terrible bodily mutations using a camera. At the same time, his brother Jib receives a similar task from the CIA in Uktena 64 - Ultimate, but his goal is the complete destruction of the bodies of infected creatures. To make matters worse, Jib plans to use the meat to save his failing barbecue restaurant, adding a touch of absurd humor to the proceedings. The choice of one of the two protagonists determines both the goals of the missions and the general approach to their implementation.
