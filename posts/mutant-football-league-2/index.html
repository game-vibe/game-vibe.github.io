Game Description#

Mutant Football League 2 is a brutal arcade reimagining of American football, where athletes are replaced by teams of orcs, skeletons and deadly robots. The project is an ideological continuation of the cult classic, relying not on realism, but on dark humor and spectacular matches in the 8 vs 8 format. Each arena is a high-danger zone filled with circular saws, mines and other deadly traps. Unpredictability is also introduced by sudden events that can radically change the balance of power during the competition. Everything that happens on the field is accompanied by vivid comments from Tim Kitzrow.

The project offers extensive possibilities for personalization. Users can not only form their own teams, but also construct unique stadiums, placing obstacles on them themselves.

Dominating the league through tactics and management:

Development of mutants through the discovery of new armor sets. Use unique special skills to gain an advantage. The use of tactical tricks and prohibited techniques on the field. Selection of advanced play patterns to deceive the enemy defense. Roster management in the multi-season mode of the Dynasty Supreme franchise. Create your own teams with a unique set of players. Construction of stadiums with placement of traps and obstacles.

In addition to single-player modes, it is possible to compete with other players in online multiplayer matches.
