Game Description#

Slots and Daggers is a retro fantasy adventure wrapped in slot machine mechanics, where each spin determines the fate of the hero in dangerous lands. The gameplay is based on the rotation of reels, the symbols on which correspond to actions in battle: attacks, the use of potions or spells. Defeating eccentric opponents that include Kul Knights and Hammer-wielding Goblins will require not only quick reflexes and luck, but also strategic thinking. By winning battles, the gamer receives powerful modifiers that open access to additional reels, new weapons and abilities.

The journey takes place in a whimsical fantasy world, the visual style of which is made in retro aesthetics with deliberately rough character sketches. The soundtrack, consisting of sounds typical of arcade machines and hip-hop beats, creates a unique atmosphere. The Slots & Daggers project offers ten levels to complete, each of which ends with a meeting with a unique boss. As you progress, new equipment is unlocked, allowing you to flexibly customize your combat tactics.

Strategy and randomness in every spin:

Combat system. The outcome of battles is decided by combinations of symbols appearing on the reels, which requires the player to adapt to an ever-changing situation. Progression and customization. Coins earned in battles can be spent on unlocking new symbols for the machine or on permanent improvements, creating an individual playing style. Tactical depth. Despite the element of chance, success depends on the ability to correctly manage available resources and apply the received modifiers in a timely manner. Arena mode. For the most determined, there is the Egg arena mode, where you can compete for the maximum number of points.
