Game Description#

Gal Guardians: Servants of the Dark is the second part of the series, continuing the story of the demon sisters Kirika and Masha. The action of the title is transferred from one castle to the kingdom, inviting you to explore a much larger environment. Users take on the roles of two main characters, each of whom has their own playing style, which allows for dynamic and varied gameplay. As sisters, gamers must travel across the kingdom, searching for ways to revive their master, the demon lord Maxim.

To do this, you need to fight monsters and hunters, revealing hidden secrets and unlocking new abilities. Gal Guardians: Servants of the Dark is structured in a metroidvania style, with exploration, collecting items, and backtracking playing a central role. As users progress, they will unlock abilities for Kirika and Masha, which in turn will open up additional areas to explore.

The title implements a character improvement system, and gamers must use all available tools to navigate the various regions of the demon kingdom. With puzzles to solve, secret passages to discover, and enemies to defeat, there’s something interesting to find in every area. In addition to completing the main quest, the gamer will have to revive Maxim’s vassals. These characters help strengthen the overlord’s castle, which serves as the user’s home base. As your base grows, valuable bonuses and opportunities appear.
