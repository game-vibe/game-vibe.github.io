Game Description#

FANTASIAN Neo Dimension is an updated version of the popular mobile game that combines traditional RPG mechanics with some fresh ideas. Users take on the role of Leo, a character who wakes up in a strange world with almost no memory of his past. However, this world is not a peaceful one: it is threatened by a mysterious mechanical infection known as Mechteria. In the role of Leo, the gamer will travel through a variety of landscapes, trying to piece together his lost memories and uncover the truth about the infection that threatens everything. The story unfolds through over 150 handcrafted dioramas that combine physical settings with 3D characters to create a truly unique experience.

FANTASIAN Neo Dimension’s combat system is turn-based, a style that will feel familiar to fans of classic RPGs. However, there are a few notable additions that set it apart from the others. One of these is the ability to target attacks, which adds an extra layer of strategy to battles. Users can fine-tune their aim, which can be crucial in difficult battles. In addition, the Dimengeon system allows you to manage random encounters. Instead of getting involved in random battles, gamers can send enemies to the dungeon, thereby avoiding unnecessary confrontations. This system is not just a joke, it makes navigating the world more fun by giving the user the ability to control the frequency of battles.
