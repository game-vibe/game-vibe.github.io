Game Description#

DreadHaunt is a 4v1 multiplayer horror and deception game in co-op and PVP. Here one of the users becomes possessed and turns into a ghost, and the rest must survive. The action takes place in a country plunged into chaos as a result of mass demonic possession. Gamers take on the role of an E-Class field agent tasked with securing, containing and protecting the source of evil to investigate and save the country.

DreadHaunt’s gameplay is asymmetrical, meaning no two playthroughs will be the same. Gamers can be both survivors and possessed creatures hiding among them. Title involves providing security and establishing initial control over designated anomalous entities, objects or supernatural phenomena. The game takes inspiration from Indonesian-Asian lore, myths and legends, adding a unique cultural element to the ghost hunt.

To navigate the site, gamers use retro equipment and quirky local ghost hunting tools. The title promises to be updated with new content and horror stories, including new maps, ghosts, characters and game modes. The focus is on providing a challenging and immersive experience without microtransactions so that users can enjoy the game without additional costs. The title offers a unique blend of horror, deception and cooperation. Asymmetrical gameplay and Indonesian-Asian influences create a fresh and exciting experience.
