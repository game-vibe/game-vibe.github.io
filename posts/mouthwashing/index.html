Game Description#

Mouthwashing is a title in the Survival Horror genre, the action of which unfolded in the inexorable depths of the cosmos. History talks about a team of five astronauts who are on board the Tulpar spacecraft of a focus on the spacecraft. In the conditions of depletion of resources and the lack of hope of salvation, the crew has a desperate struggle for survival. In the center of the story is the psychological struggle of the crew with isolation, fear and constant threat of death. The captain, Kerley, crippled and silent, hangs over the team, and his past actions contribute to their terrible position. The users have to explore the ship, revealing the truth standing behind Kerley’s decisions and the fate of the team.

It uses an immersive narrative to create a tense and claustrophobic atmosphere. The game uses environmental signals to pump tension, emphasizing the worsening mental state of the crew. Gamers are invited to carefully monitor the environment, since, it would seem, ordinary details can contain hidden tips or portend an impending danger. The narrative is inexorable, it has no easy ways to save or a second chance. The survival of the team depends on their ability to make a difficult choice and navigate in the insidious environment of the ship. Mouthwashing makes the players face the harsh realities of the situation in which the crew was, which makes the game exciting and tense.
