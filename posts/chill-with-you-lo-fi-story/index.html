Game Description#

Chill with You: Lo-Fi Story immerses you in a world where the main goal is to establish a deep emotional connection with Satone, a gifted and imaginative girl who devotes herself to writing stories. Progress in relationships directly depends on the time spent working together at the same table. Each break turns into a valuable opportunity for communication, allowing you to get to know the young writer better and strengthen the budding friendship. These precious moments shared during a break from work are the key to building trust. Over time, if the bond becomes strong enough, Satone will be able to open up completely and share his deepest feelings.

The entire gameplay of this audio novel is centered around productive activities, which can be organized using the Pomodoro Timer, which helps you effectively manage your work and rest cycles. To create the ideal working environment, Chill with You: Lo-Fi Story provides a flexible system for customizing the environment. Gamers can choose from a variety of original lo-fi compositions created for different scenarios, from relaxing to increasing motivation. As an alternative to music, you can include soothing sounds of nature, such as the sound of rain or gusts of wind, which sharpen your perception. The chosen atmosphere not only promotes concentration, but also serves as a subtle reflection of Satone’s mood.
