Game Description#

Enemy Front may be suitable for those who like projects dedicated to the theme of World War II. The title allows you to fight with bots online or with live opponents online online. You can play Pirate with a friend or random users online. There is also content for single fans.

This is a first-person shooter with free online play. Enemy Front makes no claims to historical accuracy in terms of storytelling. The plot here is completely made up. Robert Hawkins learns of the Nazis' plans and tries to warn the world about the threat. After an incident in Spain, the Gestapo begins to pursue him. The American correspondent eventually joins the rebels and begins to fight the Germans. Events take place in Poland, France, Norway and Germany.

The campaign takes about 10 hours to complete. In it, the protagonist engages in various acts of sabotage, destroys tanks and kills fascists in large numbers. He has access to an arsenal that includes pistols (including those with a silencer), sniper and assault rifles, and machine guns. With particularly successful and spectacular shots, the time slowdown mode is activated for a short time. Sometimes they let you shoot from stationary guns. The online game is designed for a maximum of 12 participants. There are 4 maps for multiplayer battles. You can fight on the side of the USSR or the USA. Any weapon can be used. There is no leveling up and gaining access to new content, but there are different ranks. There is a partially destructible environment. A mini-map helps you navigate the area.
