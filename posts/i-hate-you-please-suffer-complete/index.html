Game Description#

I Hate You, Please Suffer Complete is a turn-based RPG story that revolves around a recently laid off woman who is forced to become an adventurer to pay the bills. The action takes place in a world where fighting monsters is an everyday profession, closely intertwined with everyday problems. The main character will have to assemble a team and find a way to survive in harsh realities, where danger awaits at every turn.

The passage process offers to earn money by any available means, be it completing large-scale tasks, hunting strange individuals for a reward, or even catching fish. In I Hate You, Please Suffer - Complete, the protagonist Ramona and her companions must face a world full of hostility and unpredictable inconveniences. The narrative reflects this hostility not only through difficult combat, but also through unexpected events, such as random ambushes or pursuit by some opponents.

The combat system is built around a squad of four characters with clearly defined roles, the flexible customization of which is achieved through equipment that allows you to adapt the abilities of each team member to specific tasks. For example, the character Jasper received a boost that adds a burning effect to his attacks, which opens up new tactical possibilities. The difficulty of the enemies scales with the level of the squad, maintaining tension throughout the journey and requiring gamers to constantly rethink their approach to battles.

This edition of the project significantly expands the original concept, offering more than 20 hours of content and 9 main quests. Players have access to a hidden storyline leading to several alternative endings. An important addition was the integration of Fishing Minigame 2, available through the arcade machine and supplemented with comments from the main characters, which immerses you deeper into the atmosphere of this unusual universe.
