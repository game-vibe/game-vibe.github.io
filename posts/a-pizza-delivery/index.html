Game Description#

A Pizza Delivery is about a surreal journey into the depths of other people’s destinies, disguised as an ordinary food delivery. Players will find themselves in a strange liminal space, inhabited by individuals stuck in time and thoughts. Among them are Noby, who is gradually losing his color from the inability to move on, and the Guy with a Backpack, who is trying to realize the emptiness in his relationship. Each meeting becomes an occasion for dialogue, full of symbolism and emotional discoveries. This meditative world is filled with secrets and makes you think about your own experiences, offering to share not only pizza with its inhabitants, but also a piece of sympathy.

The main character is a girl named Bee, a courier fulfilling her last order of the shift. Her path runs through constantly changing, almost dream-like landscapes, from gloomy city streets to a forest cemetery filled with orange light. Her faithful companion in this adventure is her scooter, on which she moves between locations. A Pizza Delivery’s narrative unfolds through moments of quiet introspection and interactions with eccentric characters. A simple task of delivering pizza turns into an exploration of a borderline state where reality intertwines with the subconscious.

The gameplay combines elements of exploration and solving simple puzzles, organically woven into the narrative. For example, in some episodes it is necessary to protect the order from heavy rain, which adds new challenges to the journey. Collectibles are scattered throughout the world, adding to the story and revealing the backstory of this mysterious place. The atmosphere of what is happening is emphasized by the musical accompaniment, consisting of synthesizer and piano melodies. Interacting with the world, the protagonist not only listens to other people’s stories, but also gradually discovers something new about herself.
