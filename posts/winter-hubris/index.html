Game Description#

Winter Hubris is about confronting not only external threats, but also internal decay. The inexorable spread of a terrible disease, the need to control hunger indicators and maintain the remnants of sanity become central elements of the narrative. The main character named Richard finds himself in complete isolation within the walls of an abandoned scientific complex in the mountains. His main task is to wait for the return of his team, which left him during the mission to retrieve the bodies. The protagonist’s survival directly depends on his ability to find scarce supplies and manage them wisely.

Protagonist state management:

Control of hunger indicator through the search for limited provisions. Monitoring sanity levels to prevent insanity. The fight against a mysterious disease that is gradually consuming the hero. Rational distribution of found supplies and medicines. Exploration of an abandoned complex in point-and-click format. The impact of the player’s key decisions on one of several endings.

The process of exploring locations is implemented in the format of a point-and-click quest, and the gloomy, picturesque style enhances the oppressive atmosphere of an abandoned research station. The gamer’s actions in Winter Hubris lead to one of several possible endings to the story. It is possible to turn off explicit scenes of body horror, which allows you to customize the level of immersion in what is happening. The ending directly depends on whether Richard can avoid death from hunger, madness, or complete absorption by the mysterious illness that has become his curse.
