Game Description#

Zenless Zone Zero is a role -playing game whose action takes place in a world standing on the edge of the abyss, where players take on the role of proxy, a conductor according to alternative dimensions, known as hollows. Players choose one of the two main characters, the wise or Belle, each of which leads the expedition to these dangerous areas. Target? Fight with creatures called Eterials, and go through various tests in these dimensions. Along the way, proofs hire agents and receive support from Bangbou - companions that increase the effectiveness of the battle. Such a command dynamics encourages experiments, as players combine skills and tactics to cope with more and more difficult tests.

The structure of the game includes two different modes: TV mode and combat mode. In TV mode, players explore the Hollowe in a style resembling the classic Dungeon Crawler, roam the surroundings, collect treasures and solve puzzles. When meeting with enemies, the game switches to Combat Mode-3D Hack-And-Slash, checking reflexes and time. Each battle includes a chain of attacks and the formation of a combo, which rewards players who effectively choose time for their actions.

The game takes place in the new Erid, a city living among the ruins, and Zenless Zone Zero introduces a number of unique fractions. From the Gentle House to the biker gang of Sons of Calydon - each faction brings its own highlight to the efforts to survive the city. These groups offer allies, missions and rivalry, which determine the player’s path, providing multi -layer experience in an unconventional future.
