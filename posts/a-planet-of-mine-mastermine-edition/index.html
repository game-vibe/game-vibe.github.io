Game Description#

A Planet of Mine MasterMine Edition is a comprehensive 4X strategy game in which the process of conquering space begins with detailed customization of the universe itself. Players are given the opportunity to set parameters for a procedurally generated star system, determining the type of sun, the number of planets and the distance to them. The path to dominance can lie through diplomacy, trade or open military clashes, and the conflict can be ended either by the complete defeat of the opponent or by signing a peace treaty. The artificial intelligence of the factions acts strategically, making decisions based on their own goals and excess resources. In the depths of space, you can also discover mysterious artifacts that shed light on a lost civilization.

There are 20 unique factions to choose from, including chicken space pirates and nuclear elephants. Prosperity in A Planet of Mine MasterMine Edition depends on many factors, forcing you to constantly keep all aspects of development under control. For successful expansion, you have to deal with a whole range of tasks:

competent management of resource collection and production. the gradual discovery of new technologies and buildings. establishing contacts with other inhabitants of the system. completing various missions to gain advantages.
