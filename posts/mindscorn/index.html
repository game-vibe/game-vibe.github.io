Game Description#

Mindscorn immerses you in the oppressive atmosphere of psychological horror, where every decision has weight. Limited ammunition turns any skirmish into a serious test, because each cartridge must be loaded into the weapon manually, counting down precious seconds. Strange creatures living in the dark corridors of the hotel tirelessly pursue their prey, ready to tear it into pieces at the slightest mistake. Survival here directly depends not on accuracy, but on the ability to hide, run away and open fire only in the most extreme cases, when there is no other choice left, and resource management becomes the key to life.

Rules of the deadly game in the cursed hotel:

Stealth and flight are the main tools for saving lives. Manually reloading a weapon makes you appreciate every shot fired. Unforgiving opponents, from whom it is easier to run away than to engage in battle.

The scene for these events is the notorious Royal Veil Hotel, shrouded in the mystery of the unexplained disappearances of many people. The investigation into this mysterious case came to a standstill after even the police officers sent there disappeared, which only strengthened the notoriety of this place. Mindscorn stars Oswald Wiebe, a disgraced journalist who has lost his job and family in his endless pursuit of fame and approval. He sees revealing the hotel’s secret as his last chance to get his life back on track, forcing himself to face not only external threats, but also the demons of his own past.
