Game Description#

In Unity of Command 2 you have to control your teammates, design routes and develop your own battle tactics. Transform from a novice into a seasoned commander and manage anti-Hitler coalitions on the Western Front, Africa, or take command of Allied divisions.

With a dynamic campaign, you can write your own history of World War II. Non-linear paths will lead you to completely different endings. In combat you will need to clear the fog of war, one of the innovations in the sequel to the cult classic Unity of Command II.

At the headquarters, players will manage the army, reorganize warriors, supply them with resources and do much more. This is one of the most important buildings from where commands for your army will be issued. The strategic component of the gameplay has been significantly improved. Get around the enemy from the rear, use aviation for unexpected attacks, scout the territory or bombard opponents.

Fierce battles between artificial intelligence will take place on the maps even without you. Complete secondary objectives, helping other parties to prevent control points from being captured and unfolding the story as you wish. In the gameplay, in addition to the non-linear campaign for the Allies, users will be able to take part in battles on the side of the Axis forces. Go to the scenario editor to create your own missions and maps.
