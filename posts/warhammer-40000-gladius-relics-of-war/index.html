Game Description#

Turn-based strategy - Warhammer 40,000: Gladius - Relics of War - united players in the knowledge of the history of the entire universe, where the turn-based type of construction and war is in no way accompanied by boredom. In this part we learn about Gladius Prime - a planet where a rich history combines the danger of the past and a new war in the present. Imperial scientists conducted a large-scale expedition to the surface of the planet and learned that the real danger is always kept not above, but in the depths.

The gameplay invites you to fight for one of 4 nations: Orcs, Necrons, Space Marines and Astra Militarium. All factions have their own technology tree, maintaining balance among the others. Own units, structures, history and main characters. All that’s left to do is start. The game also supports co-op and multiplayer modes with two types of play: “against each other” or “co-op”.

In a randomly built world, where elements and weather conditions will undermine the morale of your army, you will have to learn what violence, terrorism and a real lack of resources to advance through the plot are. The player will have to take control of ancient artifacts that do nothing but destroy everything around them.

All factions are waiting for the dust to settle from behind the Warp. This means that only one player will be left alive on Gladius Prime, which only accepts one winner to tell the true story. At that time, do not forget that each faction has its own leader. The orcs still want wealth, the empire still wants power. The Space Marines yearn for justice and to return home, while the Necrons are motivated by the complete destruction of everyone around them. You have the opportunity to create separate research teams, where each character can be endowed with a secret, ancient artifact and sent into battle against enemies who do not even know about the existence of such miracles.
