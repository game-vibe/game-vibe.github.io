Game Description#

Onirism features a young girl named Carol as the main character. She is full of energy, but at the same time absolutely disciplined. One day, a strange portal materializes next to her bed, from which creatures of unknown origin appear. They take the plush toy, hiding on the other side of the portal. Without the slightest hesitation, the heroine follows them, intending to return what was taken from her. Thus, she finds herself in Crearia - an amazing world inhabited by a wide variety of life forms.

The entire story in Onirism consists of 10 consecutive sections, showing users different parts of the local world (there are more than 20 in total). Fields, waterfalls, forests, mountain ranges and other places have their own special architecture, which creates unique conditions for passing a particular segment. As for the passage itself, here the heroine will have her own set of weapons, with the help of which she will be able to neutralize the enemies advancing on her. This can be either a long-range weapon (a water pistol or a rifle) or a short-range weapon (an umbrella). To replenish your ammunition, as well as keep your health at the same level, you need to collect the corresponding fragments scattered throughout the locations or hidden in chests. In addition to those things that are in a visible place, there are also hidden places in the locations. There, the user will discover particularly valuable items, but to find them, he will have to try hard.
