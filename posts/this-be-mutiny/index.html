Game Description#

This Be Mutiny is a tactical adventure centered around betrayal and infighting among the team. At the center of the story is a squad of 11 colorful characters united by a common goal, but everyone’s personal ambitions lead to inevitable rebellion. The gameplay is built on complex decisions, forcing the user to constantly choose which of his teammates to keep on the team and which to mercilessly throw overboard. Survival in an atmosphere of constant intrigue and insidious conspiracies becomes the main condition for achieving the cherished goal.

Initially, a team of brave pirates sets out in search of a legendary treasure, but the expedition quickly turns into a struggle for power. The turn-based, top-down combat system requires careful planning of every action, as any crew member could be an enemy. The story in This Be Mutiny is driven by the decisions you make, allowing for a variety of different endings and romances.

The visual execution of the project is distinguished by bright and colorful 2D graphics in anime style, emphasizing the uniqueness of each of the characters. This strategy RPG takes you deep into a story where family ties and loyalty are constantly tested. Not only the fate of the protagonist, but also the outcome of the entire treasure hunt depends on how skillfully one manages to maneuver between the interests of the warring factions on the ship.
