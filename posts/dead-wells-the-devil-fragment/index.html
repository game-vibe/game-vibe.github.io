Game Description#

Dead Wells: The Devil Fragment is an action RPG whose combat system offers a comprehensive approach to destroying enemies. Gamers are given the opportunity to throw opponents into the air and then perform long aerial combinations, which allows them to accumulate a special scale. Filling this meter will activate temporary buffs that can change the outcome of the battle. In addition, the arsenal of techniques includes parries and attacks from behind, which open up access to devastating charged strikes. The process of character development is tied to collecting devilish fragments that fall from defeated enemies. Confrontations with bosses with unique sets of attacks require maximum concentration from the gamer to avoid powerful attacks.

The events take place in a rural area called Grimthorn. It is here, in the world of Dead Wells: The Devil Fragment, that the protagonist will have to search for a mysterious artifact. These lands are in the grip of corruption, which awakens the dead and turns living beings into possessed devils. The cause of the disaster was the fall of an unholy comet, which split into many parts upon entering the atmosphere. One of these fragments, known as the Devil Fragment, became the epicenter of the infection. The places where these fragments fell received the ominous name Dead Wells. Every death in this harsh world is not the end, but an opportunity to study the habits of the enemy and develop new tactics for the next encounter.
