Game Description#

Silly Polly Beast is a dive into a nightmarish and often abstract underground world shrouded in a deadly fog. In this ominous dimension, the main character will have to adapt to cruel rules in order to survive. A special bracelet serves as the only indicator of danger, lighting up red when approaching a destructive haze. You can move around creepy locations not only on foot, but also with the help of a skateboard, which is also used as a weapon for close combat.

The protagonist of this story is a girl named Polly, who finds herself trapped in the afterlife dimension after escaping from an orphanage. Forced to fight for her freedom, she must unravel the nature of the mysterious beast tied to her destiny. During her journey in Silly Polly Beast, she will meet many strange creatures that are not always hostile, but are not allies either. The gameplay constantly changes perspective, switching between a top-down view and a side-scrolling view, which forces you to quickly adapt to new conditions.

Polly’s survival directly depends on mastering forbidden spells and developing her combat skills. The heroine’s arsenal includes firearms for aggressive shooting and magical abilities that become more deadly as you progress. In addition to combat, exploration of the world is filled with puzzle solving and moments where you need to act stealthily, hiding in the shadows. The girl’s main goal is not just to escape, but to search for the only thing that remains important to her - the mysterious Alice.
