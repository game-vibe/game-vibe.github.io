Game Description#

Buhei is a competitive action game that immerses you in crazy shootouts in multi-level arenas. Up to 8 fighters can fight simultaneously in local or online confrontations, sorting things out in free skirmish or team battle modes. To eliminate rivals, there is an extensive arsenal, including rapid-fire projectiles, clever traps and even a ping-pong paddle that repels enemy attacks. Characters are also able to cast supernatural spells or use artifacts that defy the laws of physics, such as a hot dog that shoots mustard. Each fight turns into a test of reaction and tactical thinking, forcing you to constantly adapt to changing conditions on the battlefield.

The variety of locations forces you to look for new approaches to combat. For example, the Mansion stage allows you to move between floors using a teleportation mechanism, and the Pirate stage allows you to use a ballista to control the center of the map. The Buhei project provides ample opportunities for self-expression through appearance customization that goes beyond choosing a style. Gamers have access to a rich palette of bright colors and many unusual facial features, allowing them to create a truly memorable avatar. This approach ensures that each new session will be different from the previous one not only tactically, but also visually, maintaining a high level of engagement in this chaotic process.
