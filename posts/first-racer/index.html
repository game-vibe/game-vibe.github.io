Game Description#

First Racer is an arcade race where victory is achieved not only through quick reactions, but also through proper preparation of the car. The project offers a wide range of competitive disciplines, covering both city streets and specialized tracks. For fans of extreme driving, there are cross-country races that require a special approach to control. Drifting competitions, in turn, test your car control in a controlled drift, where points are awarded based on angle, speed and style of execution. Each track has unique features, forcing you to adapt your tactics to achieve the best result.

A deep tuning system allows you to turn an ordinary city car into a real supercar. In First Racer you can not only improve technical characteristics that affect acceleration and handling, but also completely change the appearance of the vehicle. Available in a variety of colours, decals and rims to create your own unique style. You can demonstrate your creations and skills in several formats: in single races, in online battles against other racers, or competing with a friend on the same screen in split-screen mode.

Three paths to a victorious finish:

Classic circuit racing with the goal of being the first to cross the finish line. Drifting competitions where the skill of a controlled drift is assessed. Off-road races on gravel and dirt roads in specially prepared cars.
