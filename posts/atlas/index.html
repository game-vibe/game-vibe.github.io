Game Description#

Embark on a grand adventure as a pirate and explore the islands. ATLAS is a sandbox role-playing game in which players can do whatever they want. Travel the islands with hundreds of other players, fight with them or join alliances.

Create the ship of your dreams. More than a hundred different materials, many types of rafts and additions to the ship. Set up a flag, cannons, a mast, a rudder and hire a crew. The gameplay provides epic battles at sea, in which you can board enemy ships, shoot cannons, flamethrowers or other weapons that can smash your opponent’s ship to pieces.

Recruit AI-controlled supporting characters to perform various tasks on ship or land. Travel and fight in a squad with friendly players against other factions.

A massive world spanning 45,000 square kilometers. Many additional islands, caves, hidden locations and completely loaded areas with various objects. Get materials, food or look for treasures. Build up territories with your own buildings or create castles, store resources and defend yourself from other gamers.

Play in single player, PvP or PvE mode, create mods or creative servers with mini-modes. Become a full-fledged pirate owner in a multiplayer game, build the biggest castle and occupy the territory. Buy dozens of ships and modifications for them, get cool items and seize more and more initiative.
