Game Description#

InSuperfuse you can play online with a friend or for free on a pirate game you can join a team of up to 4 people in order to explore mystical dungeons online together over the Internet and fight the gods themselves. The gameplay of Superfuse is an action role-playing game in the comics style, drawing inspiration from Diablo 2. The mechanics are built on a similar principle: dungeons are randomly generated, and the main role is played by the loot that drops from defeated enemies. The opportunity to play for free invites friends to go together to destroy hordes of monsters, while simultaneously trying to learn the secret of Corruption. Multiplayer allows each participant to take on the hero of one of 3 classes.

Each hero has his own unique set of skills. Abilities are upgraded through an extensive talent tree. All players customize their characters individually, developing their own special battle tactics. You can travel between planets, passing many unique tests. Having killed the boss and successfully completed the challenge, heroes are awarded bonuses and enhanced loot. Equipment is divided into normal and epic. More than 50 types of legendary equipment are available. As the level increases, the standard parameters of the protagonist are enhanced: the supply of vitality, a higher energy limit and other characteristics. There are support stones that are necessary to strengthen your character. They have different properties and affect both passive and active skills.
