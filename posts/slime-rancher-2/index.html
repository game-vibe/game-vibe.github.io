Game Description#

Slime Rancher 2 continues the adventure of Beatrix LeBeau, who travels to Rainbow Island, making her way through a host of obstacles to create a slug farm. The platformer with construction elements features an open world where the user is invited to encounter a mass of slimes that must be collected using a special blaster-vacuum cleaner. Here you will also discover new species of slugs, explore a mysterious world, and build a greenhouse, placing it on a paradise island.

The sequel to the first part suggests that you transform into a girl named Beatrix, who is heading to mysterious lands, where there are a lot of different resources and technologies that are needed for breeding slugs. In the process, the girl will have to learn the secrets that are hidden here, and also build a farm in the winter garden. Here it is proposed to build glass walls, thanks to which the player observes beautiful and colorful landscapes. The protagonist will have to breed slimes on this farm, gradually revealing bizarre species. While traveling and exploring the island territories, you can find, for example, inflatable cotton slime, if you are not afraid to wander onto the Far-Far Ridge, and fisherman’s slime is also found in reservoirs. As she progresses, the heroine earns money from newbucks and collects resources that are needed for the construction, as well as for upgrading her winter garden and creating additional gadgets.
