Game Description#

Parisian Brasserie Simulator begins gameplay on a Parisian avenue: setting up tables, ordering ingredients, and creating a menu for the grand opening. The owner needs to set up the space in preparation for the first day, including developing seasonal menus based on French cuisine. Once preparations are complete, the doors open to welcome the first customers. The main task is to turn a small establishment into a recognized city institution.

With the start of service in Parisian Brasserie Simulator, the focus shifts to staff organization and operational efficiency. Assigning responsibilities, seating guests, and monitoring orders are key daily activities that directly impact customer satisfaction and bill payments. Prompt service for every guest is a key feature. The demo version of this project allows up to 14 gamers to collaborate on these management tasks in a setting with Haussmann-style architecture.

Beyond day-to-day service, managing inventory and profit margins is fundamental to profitability. The project requires tracking raw material inventories, negotiating with suppliers to set prices, and using cost analysis tools. Achieving goals related to customer satisfaction and service quality opens up opportunities for development. Meeting these criteria allows you to add new recipes and diversify your offerings, eventually attracting a larger audience.
