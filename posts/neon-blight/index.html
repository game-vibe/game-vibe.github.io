Game Description#

Neon Blight is an isometric metroidvania in a cyberpunk setting in which you will manage your own underground weapons store. When collecting unique weapons, you need to sell them at a high price. The game features a huge number of locations. The battles will take place in the scenery of the metropolis of the future, in dense forests, warehouses of large corporations, etc.

Most of Neon Blight’s elements are procedurally generated, changing the placement of items and some location details. While exploring the city, you will meet a variety of characters with their own character and history. They can give out quests of varying degrees of difficulty, in which you need to kill someone, rob someone, etc. By collecting unique trunks and accumulating monetary resources, you need to improve your store called “Eden” and expand your sphere of influence. Game Features:

classic cyberpunk style; unique and intense boss battles; hundreds of types of weapons and wide customization options; a huge number of improvements and modifications for barrels; store management with management mechanics; many characters and branching dialogues; a large number of secrets and hidden places.
