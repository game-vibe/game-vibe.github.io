Game Description#

Chaos Chain is a role-playing project, which takes place in a dystopian world, where every alley hides danger, and every corner is fraught with the possibility of betrayal. The plot takes place in Slade City, a gloomy metropolis where crime, corruption and constant struggle for power reign. As a gamer, you step into the well-worn shoes of a citizen caught in the suffocating grip of this city. This place is not for the faint of heart.

In Slade City, you don’t just need to survive, but maintain a sense of humanity while navigating an endless sea of ​​uncertainty. Users quickly learn that one wrong step can be the difference between life and death. Chaos Chain is all about choice, and those choices drive the story forward. Every decision you make ripples across the world, shaping the narrative and your relationships with the people of Slade City. The world is full of characters, each with their own motives, secrets and desires. Trust is a luxury that few can afford, and even fewer can maintain.

The game doesn’t hold your hand when you need to figure out who is a friend and who is an enemy. Sometimes you have to weigh the risks, and sometimes you have to be guided by pure survival instinct. But no matter which path you choose, there is no escape from the shadow world that surrounds you. The core gameplay revolves around navigating this treacherous landscape, where danger lurks around every corner. Combat is turn-based, requiring strategic thinking and careful planning.
