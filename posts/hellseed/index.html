Game Description#

The gloomy atmosphere inHELLSEED is filled with riddles and secrets that the protagonist must cope with. The main character finds himself in Italy in 1980, in the small province of Turin. Users will take on the role of a private investigator who is investigating a strange case. Doctors from a psychiatric hospital are worried about a missing employee who was last at home. Players will explore the estate of the missing doctor, starting from the rooms of his desolate apartment, studying objects and analyzing the situation. The detective needs to find evidence to get on the doctor’s trail. Gamers in HELLSEED will have to immerse themselves in first-person horror, where they must: Explore locations that will be shown in the first chapter of an intricate story campaign. You will have to carefully look around the rooms to find clues. Move through dark and frightening places, where there is a feeling of being an observer from the outside everywhere. The hero will encounter dark otherworldly forces that will interfere with the investigation. Explore the area, which is made using the photorealistic S2ENGINE HD engine, which creates high-quality graphics. Get acquainted with references to Italian traditions and superstitions that create an atmosphere of horror. Deal with puzzles where you need to interact with objects and surrounding interior objects. Users have access to a convenient inventory, which optimizes the arrangement of things. Hide from creatures that will begin to attack the detective after a while.
