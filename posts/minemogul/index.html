Game Description#

MineMogul is an opportunity to design and build a giant industrial complex in the bowels of the earth, where the main task is to tame chaos. The process is constantly complicated by unpredictable failures caused by a realistic physical model, which forces us to constantly search for creative solutions to keep the mechanisms working. The action takes place in a mysterious, deserted cave system, among the ruins of abandoned mining operations. Exploring these abandoned locations unlocks hidden resources and new secrets, and every cavern discovered holds potential for an ambitious tycoon. As production expands, the complexity only increases, making management a real challenge.

The basis of the gameplay is the creation of large-scale and interconnected production networks. You must navigate conveyor belts through winding shafts, connect different pieces of equipment, and use the laws of physics to create fully autonomous operations. At MineMogul, optimizing machine placement and finding innovative engineering approaches are the key to success. Overcrowded conveyors and parts flying around require constant monitoring and timely adjustment of the built system. The ultimate goal is to establish the smooth operation of the entire enterprise and achieve the status of an unrivaled industrial tycoon by subjugating the spontaneous disorder.
