Game Description#

A renovation crew simulator in which you take on the role of the boss and the only employee -House Flipper - allows you to destroy beautiful houses, but build beautiful apartments with amazing renovations in their place. In this game you can make rooms the way you see them in your life. The game also encourages you to use the latest repair tools, but one thing remains unchanged: you’ll have to take down walls with a sledgehammer!

People at House Flipper will come to you not only because of a new cosmetic renovation, but also with serious problems during the renovation. Sometimes you will inspect facilities where renovation work is being prepared. Sometimes it is necessary to change the location of pipes, change power supply units, deal with the power supply, which does not allow the use of a lamp or luminaire. The career mode of the game involves solving various problems that people had before repairs. We will do the repair work itself: plastering, wallpapering and other things ourselves.

There is also a “creator” mode in which we can use the full power of the game generator to create a house that is as close to our expectations as possible. More than a hundred interior items, about 10,000 finishing options, and all this in houses and apartments where everything around is being destroyed! In career mode, you will have access to an auction. Over time, when you can buy houses and renovate them the way you want, people will want to buy your masterpieces in the real estate market.
