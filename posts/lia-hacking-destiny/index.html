Game Description#

Lia: Hacking Destiny is a side-scrolling roguelite set in a world enslaved by hostile machines. In this setting, the remnants of humanity are waging a guerrilla war, becoming the last hope for freedom. Facing mechanized opponents requires sharp reflexes to dodge attacks and slide along walls. Each raid takes place in unique conditions thanks to the procedural generation of industrial complexes filled with traps and enemies. Successful survival directly depends on constantly improving your equipment and unlocking new abilities. Among the opponents there are some very extraordinary creatures: from an overprotective robot mother to a mechanical dinosaur in a post-apocalyptic zoo.

To counter the absurd system of total control, gamers are provided with an extensive arsenal. In Lia: Hacking Destiny, you can combine a variety of weapons, from sniper rifles and katanas to hand grenade launchers, creating unique fighting styles. In addition to destroying robots, one of the tasks is to rescue cats from captivity of algorithms, which provides support for an entire cat army. The depth of the passage is provided by several key elements that create a unique experience.

Basic mechanics and systems:

Hacking the environment to manage alarm levels and gain access to secret areas. Development of a base with non-player characters to obtain permanent improvements. Deep customization of equipment and skills to adapt to any threat.
