Game Description#

Cleared Hot is an arcade helicopter shooter in which the laws of physics become the main weapon in the hands of the pilot. The story follows a combat veteran who, after leaving the army, lives a secluded life in a Texas trailer. Having received an old helicopter at his disposal, he returns to the sky again, where he will face numerous opponents. The combat vehicle is equipped not only with machine guns and missiles, but also with a unique cable and magnet system that allows you to interact with the environment.

The gameplay offers dynamic aerial battles with enemy soldiers and gangs in armed pickup trucks. Missions in Cleared Hot are not limited to just destroying targets, requiring the player to master piloting. Tasks range from carefully transporting fragile cargo, such as boilers or even live chickens, to aggressive attacks on enemy fortifications. Using a cable, you can throw stones, cars, and even intercept incoming missiles at your opponents, turning any object on the battlefield into a deadly projectile.

In addition to arcade shooting, the project contains an element of tactics, implemented through the control of ground troops. It is possible to land soldiers to capture enemy territories or organize the defense of their own bases. To perform increasingly complex tasks, it is possible to purchase and upgrade helicopters, which allows you to create individual weapon configurations for specific combat conditions and style of play.

The action takes place in three different biomes, including desert canyons, dense jungles and arctic wastelands. Missions are carried out both during the day and at night, when night vision devices must be used to detect targets. The visual style features sophisticated design and warm colors, while professional voice acting with a distinctive Texas accent and a high-quality soundtrack enhance the atmosphere of the intense action movie.
