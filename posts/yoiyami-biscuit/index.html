Game Description#

Yoiyami Biscuit is a fast-paced, top-down roguelike shooter that pits you against hordes of enemies in a world filled with snacks. To help Rumia survive, she has access to over 100 different items that can be purchased between battles. These artifacts are capable of both passively enhancing characteristics and calling upon combat partners to help block enemy projectiles. Spiritual Strike Talismans play a vital role in tactics, allowing them to deal devastating damage or temporarily provide a character with powerful enhancements. Proper combination of all available means and the search for synergy between them becomes the key to successful completion. Each encounter with an enemy requires the gamer not only to be accurate, but also to think strategically.

The basis of the combat system is the unique mechanics of using snacks as weapons. Each type of treat has its own advantages and disadvantages, and its effectiveness increases as you gain experience in battles. In addition, Yoiyami Biscuit implements the ability to eat these same snacks to receive permanent bonuses and passive attacks. However, there is also a danger here: eating too much food makes Rumia sleepy. The plot tells the story of the sudden appearance of special Fountains of snacks in Gensokyo, which provoked chaos and a struggle for territory. Players will have to find out the reason for this strange and delicious incident.

The path to victory through mountains of treats:

Using a variety of snacks as danmaku projectiles. Eating treats to permanently enhance and unlock passive abilities. Purchase and combine over 100 items to create unique builds. Tactical use of Spiritual Strike Talismans to turn the tide of battle.
