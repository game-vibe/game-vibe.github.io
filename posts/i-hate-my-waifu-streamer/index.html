Game Description#

In I Hate My Waifu Streamer, the gamer will have to capture the attention of the streamer before other viewers. The main goal is to interact with a girl named Mio during her live broadcasts. Communication is based on writing chat messages and sending donations to stand out among other subscribers. This competition for recognition forms the basis of the gameplay, and financial support becomes a key way to develop relationships and attract Mio’s interest.

The interactive system of the title I Hate My Waifu Streamer is built on resource management and direct communication. Monetary investments affect the streamer’s level of sympathy, and sending gifts to unbox on air serves as clear evidence of support. Provocative questions also help set you apart from other fans, eliciting specific reactions from her and strengthening your bond. An additional possibility involves the dark web, a risky decision with unpredictable consequences for the plot and characters.

The decisions made form a non-linear plot with multiple endings, reflecting the protagonist’s growing obsession. Each action influences the development of a relationship that can develop into a romantic attachment to Mio. On the other hand, a feeling of rejection can lead to harassment of the streamer. This path will lead to different plot results, because any choice made during the game determines the ending of the story and the relationship of the main character with the girl.
