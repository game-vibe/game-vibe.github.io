Game Description#

The plot of The Falconeer tells about the secrets of already forgotten gods and ancient civilizations. The game combines fantasy, action and role-playing elements in an ocean world located on the Great Ursi. Take control of an armed man and a huge bird to soar upward and explore a gradually crumbling and beautiful world. Battles constantly take place across vast territories between militant groups and factions fighting for the secrets and mysteries hidden in these places.

In each level you will need to control an airborne fighter, fighting against numerous opponents. Enemies will be huge military airships, dragons, air and sea vessels, other birds and ground soldiers. Perform powerful attacks and destroy your opponents. Shells and lasers are constantly flying in the air, and maneuvering is the only salvation from shots.

Fight against aerial stingrays, deadly dragon-like creatures and human technology. Sometimes you can fight together with partners flying on the same birds that the main character controls. While you control the animal, the rider can shoot or parachute onto military targets. Without a person on your back, you will simply need to fly and dodge attacks, engage in close combat if possible, or take on a new ally.

Battles take place during huge tsunamis, storms and thunderstorms. The graphic component is at the highest level. Each minor character, be it a dragon or an airship, has unique abilities and weaknesses.
