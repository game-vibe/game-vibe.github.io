Game Description#

Gold Gold Adventure Gold will put the player in charge of a developing settlement, where currency is the main instrument of power. It is necessary to build a city and establish an economy to attract individuals hungry for profit. These adventurers take on assignments for reward, completing tasks that lead to the accumulation of funds to achieve your goals. In parallel with the management of the settlement, the giant creature needs care, feeding and training, gradually developing into a powerful force.

In Gold Gold Adventure Gold, adventurers are a key resource, and their loyalty is directly tied to their pay. Recruits of various origins, including humans, beastmen, mothmen, and undead, arrive in search of profit. The gamer assigns them missions, for example, reconnaissance or defense, rewarding their efforts with a coin. These characters also show independence by joining forces or investing their earnings in the construction of new buildings.

When the heroes clear the area of ​​monsters, the player will have to observe their existence from a detached position. One of the ways of development involves providing assistance: providing improved equipment and new skills for survival. There is an alternative option, in which warriors are considered as assets, sometimes used to feed your god-like beast. With each level gained in battle, these individuals move one step closer to the final, forbidden transformation that changes them forever.
