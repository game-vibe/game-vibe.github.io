Game Description#

Cowboy Life Simulator will take players to the Wild West of the 19th century and allow them to master the life of a cowboy, taking care of his family and daily fighting the dangers that the environment presents. The most powerful enemy is hunger. But the hero is not going to put up with everyday life and craves adventure. But the protagonist is not some experienced warrior or the best shooter in the Wild West, so other important farming tasks await him.

It is necessary to catch wild horses. In order to do the job more efficiently, you need to catch and find healthy young individuals. In Cowboy Life Simulator you can do construction and renovation. All the living creatures that the main character finds need a roof. Cattle need to graze in the meadows, as the pasture will prevent them from turning into a bag of bones. Local criminals and gangs will periodically carry out raids in order to rob civilians. You need to be ready to fight them back.

Everyday life in the Wild West cannot do without saloons. After a hard day at work, local bars will offer you a chance to relax with a glass of whiskey. Here you can play cards or fight with another drunkard. When the time comes and the cattle gain weight, the animals can be sold at the local market. The larger it is, the higher the revenue. The territory is represented by an open world, where many secrets and cowboy equipment are hidden. Just remember that the farther from home, the more severe the dangers that can await behind any hill.
