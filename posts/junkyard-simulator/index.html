Game Description#

Explore open environments in Junkyard Simulator in search of trash that can be used to upgrade and create cars. The main character’s business is searching for junk, purchasing parts and creating cars. The whole world is a dump, full of scrap metal and useful items. In the nooks and crannies of landfills, the character will find the best items.

Found items are sold for good money, but they are also used in the workshop. In the garage, the character updates furniture, cars, household and electrical appliances and improves the interior. Thanks to a crowbar and a crane, heavy objects are lifted, and with the help of a truck they are delivered to the warehouse. The protagonist constantly takes orders from his clients. They order individual or ready-made objects.

There are also mysterious containers scattered around the landfills, some of them are open, while the rest have to be broken into. These chests contain randomly generated items, including empty boxes or filled with rare items. Containers can also be purchased at the store.

The user will start with an empty garage, without a car or items. The first parts are in a simple landfill. Gradually, the character will become rich, open new outlets and receive more orders from customers. The gamer is not limited by tasks and does whatever he wants. Here you can restore cars or crash them while driving along empty tracks; destroy the environment using a crusher or hammer; earn money by opening containers with unknown contents and much more.
