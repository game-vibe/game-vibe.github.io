Game Description#

Lagoda Odyssey is a journey through a world imbued with the spirit of Slavic and Nordic legends, where dense forests, caves and thriving cities are open for exploration. Traveling through these lands is fraught not only with dangers, but also with peaceful activities. Valuable resources are obtained through fishing, farming, or mining, allowing you to prepare for the challenges ahead. Adventures unfold in the vast expanses of a vibrant world full of wonders and discoveries, where every corner is fraught with both opportunities and hidden threats that require ingenuity and courage from the heroes.

Battles with opponents take place in a step-by-step mode, requiring participants to think strategically. Success in battle is largely determined by the competent use of magic and various effects, as well as the exploitation of the elemental vulnerabilities of enemies. The composition of the squad is constantly changing, and the unique magical abilities of each companion in Lagoda Odyssey are customized using special elemental spheres. In addition to the main storyline, each companion has personal tasks that reveal their backstory and allow you to dive deeper into the narrative.

The main goal of the journey is to confront the monster that appeared in Lake Lagoda and disturbed the peace of the surrounding lands. To defeat this creature, you will not only have to hone your combat skills, but also enlist the support of loyal friends who can be found in a variety of settlements, from small villages to large cities. It is the joining of efforts and exploration of vast territories that becomes the key to restoring peace and order.
