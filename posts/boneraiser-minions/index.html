Game Description#

Boneraiser Minions plunges you into a very dark fantasy universe, where powerful creatures once lived, whom people called boneraisers. They were none other than necromancers - wizards who delved into the study of the dark arts and, with their help, learned to raise the dead from their graves, then making their slaves out of them. The main character is one of these magicians, who was caught red-handed and decided to resort to the only method practiced in those days - burning at the stake and then burial in a sacred temple.

When the guards and villagers attacked the protagonist of Boneraiser Minions, they easily tortured, burned and carried his stops to the tomb. But they can’t kill a dead person. Soon the hero rebelled and began to reassemble his army of the damned. One of the most powerful monarchs, King Gigalda, learned that the necromancer had returned and posed a huge threat. He gathers a large army and goes to storm the temple.

The gamer must move in real time through gloomy locations and systematically exterminate opponents. Then there is a chance to use magic and resurrect them, making them your servants. Before resurrection, the user can customize their characteristics and make a skeleton warrior or archer. For successfully completing a level, the user is awarded coins - the main currency in the game, which should be spent on improving the abilities of the dark sorcerer.
