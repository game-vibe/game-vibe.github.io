Game Description#

The Voidness - Lidar Horror Survival Game is a chilling sci-fi survival horror game that will send chills down your spine. Step into the shoes of space specialist Francesca Lee, who is tasked with exploring a newly discovered planet plunged into darkness and gloom. As you delve deeper into the void, you will soon realize that you are not alone. Use your long-range scanning device to navigate the treacherous environment, but be careful - every step you take could be your last. Upgrade your device, mark landmarks, and use scanning turrets to guide you through the darkness, all while being hunted by an unseen and malevolent force.

Breathe quietly and tread carefully, because even the slightest noise can give away your location. Enemies are always listening and watching, waiting for the right moment to strike. Can you survive the horrors of the Void and uncover the secrets of this mysterious planet? A compelling narrative awaits you that will keep you on the edge of your seat as you fight for your life. The fate of Francesca and her crewmates is in your hands. Will you be able to survive?
