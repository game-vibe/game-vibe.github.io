Game Description#

Cogmind is a sci-fi roguelike in which you play as a robot assembled from components of other machines. Cogmind combat is presented in a turn-based style. You can explore the big world, hack devices, quietly sneak into strategic places, or act directly and attack your opponents head-on. The gameplay does not limit you to actions. Play the way you want.

Your task is to assemble yourself from various modules. Depending on the installed components, new mechanics will be available to you. Become an impenetrable tank, capable of destroying everyone in your path, be a hidden assassin or a hacker, slaying enemies with a sword, etc. Gameplay can change dramatically if you lose your components. However, by picking up a module from an enemy, you again open access to previously inaccessible development branches. There are a huge number of machines that are not hostile towards you. Several difficulty levels are presented. The extra-difficult mode will appeal to those who are not looking for easy ways. Key Features:

construction and modification of a unique robot; dynamic pumping of the protagonist; a huge selection of classes, each with its own characteristics; procedurally generated world; seven different endings; Fully interactive and destructible environment.
