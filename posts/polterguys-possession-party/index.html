Game Description#

Polterguys: Possession Party throws eight ghostly players in a competition with an indirect confrontation. The main task is to lure opponents on the path of a stray monster. Gamers control spirits capable of instilling in ordinary objects and turning them into tools of deception. Each of 10 unique levels is filled with physical traps and the dangers of the environment. Success depends on the ability to use these elements for strategic elimination of rivals, while avoiding the creature pursuing all.

The PolterGuys: Possession Party gaming process is built on the forces obtained by introduction to objects. These abilities are used for battles, escape or sowing chaos on locations. Scattered in terms of amplification give temporary advantages. The main tool is the monster itself, redirected to nothing suspecting opponents. Such a system combines tactical maneuvering with dynamic action, where you need to apply accurate blows and dodge a constant threat.

The progress system of a terrible path opens up access to awards: avatars, emotions and customizable podiums. 20 collection looks for search and improvement, changing appearance and skills. Daily and weekly tests set constant goals, and the results are monitored in global leaders. The project supports a local mode with a screen separation for four, which also allows you to go out into an online general group. Gamers equip the personal space called the refuge for the currency collected in matches.
