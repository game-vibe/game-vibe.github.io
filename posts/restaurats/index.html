Game Description#

Restaurats is a medieval restaurant management simulator where the main characters are a team of resourceful rats. Gamers will have to take full control over all aspects of running a business in a fantasy world. The process includes not only preparing dishes according to a variety of recipes, but also submitting orders on time, as well as maintaining order on the tables. The development of the establishment occurs gradually, opening up access to new drawings to expand the tavern and improve its functionality. Characters can also be upgraded by increasing their skills and changing their appearance to make your rat chef the most stylish one yet.

Serving visitors requires special attention, because the guests of the tavern are orcs, vampires, skeletons and other mystical creatures. Each of them has unique preferences and requirements, which makes the task more difficult. In the Restaurats project, to achieve success, you need to work harmoniously in a team of four people, or cope with all responsibilities alone. The complexity of the recipes directly affects the rewards received, motivating you to explore more sophisticated culinary creations. Special bonus cards are issued daily that can affect dishes, staff abilities or financial income, introducing an element of chance into the gameplay.
