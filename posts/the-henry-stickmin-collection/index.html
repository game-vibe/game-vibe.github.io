Game Description#

The Henry Stickmin Collection - a collection of games about the adventures of Henry Stickmin. This is a series of humorous adventure games that give you freedom of choice. The Henry Stickmin Collection is an updated classic, represented by 6 different games in the series. Both canon endings are included, as well as completely new end credits.

Choice is at the core of The Henry Stickmin Collection’s gameplay. At each stage of the adventure you make a choice. For example, call Charles' friend or use the teleport? Depending on the action taken, the story develops in a certain direction. However, there are options that can lead to gameover. If you are lucky with the right options and manage to get through the game until the end without a single death, this also has its negative sides, since there is a high probability of missing out on a huge layer of fun.

Classic games have been brought up to today’s standards. The collection includes the episodes Prison Break, Diamond Theft, Airship Infiltration, Escape from the Complex and Mission Completion. The latter is a completely new game created specifically for the re-release and ends the entire saga. It features three times more content than the main games in the series. Take a trip down memory lane and relive incredibly fun adventures with Henry!
