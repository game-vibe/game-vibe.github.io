Game Description#

Cuisineer is a delightful mix of dungeon crawling, cooking, and restaurant management that takes you into the captivating story of Pom, an energetic adventurer who must save her family’s diner in Paella. Armed with her culinary skills, she travels beyond her hometown, engaging in culinary battles with monsters and collecting ingredients to prepare delicious dishes for her visitors. Offering a variety of culinary adventures with an extensive repertoire of over 100 recipes spanning the globe, from savory Popiah rolls to sweet Kaya jam and crispy chicken delicacies.

Players can personalize their kitchen and dining experience by collaborating with local artisans to create an inviting atmosphere and strategically serve each customer’s meal. Cuisineer features roguelike elements, as well as randomly generated dungeons that challenge players to navigate the delicate balance of health and stamina. The game’s commitment to a wholesome, food-centric experience is evident in its eye-catching and vibrant art style, which will capture the hearts of players looking for a fun escapade.
