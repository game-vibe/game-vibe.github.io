Game Description#

In the Barn Finders simulator, you need to explore abandoned warehouses, barns, bases and villages to find valuable items. Having discovered interesting things, you need to bring them to your pawnshop and put them up for sale, trying to get the highest possible price for your finds. Things can be very different: old watches, musical instruments, antiques, gold, weapons, vintage cars, motorcycles and much more.

Auctions and sales are an important gameplay element. With the help of bets, you can open access to previously closed opportunities and properties of the game. Barn Finders features 12 vast locations that contain their own caches and valuable resources. The land of Amerika (the prototype of the real America) contains many abandoned areas and buildings that need to be carefully searched. Not all useful things are in plain sight, so you should try harder and be especially careful. If there are few earthly resources, then there will be an opportunity to go explore the Moon.

As your revenue grows, you need to improve your store. In order to list items at a higher price, they must be repaired, cleaned, and brought into salable condition. It happens that there are no clients for some time. In this case, the game will offer you to pass the time on the basketball court. But you won’t have to worry about it for long. Uncle Bill will always find something to do, as competitors are not asleep and are trying to bypass your family business at every turn. By visiting various places, with the help of unique finds you can learn the history of the place and feel the unique atmosphere. If you manage to find exclusive parts for a car or motorcycle, you will be able to sell the vehicle at auction at a crazy price.
