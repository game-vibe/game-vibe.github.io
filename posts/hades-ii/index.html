Game Description#

Hades II, the sequel to the award-winning roguelike dungeon crawler, takes players beyond the underworld. You play as Melina, a princess of the underworld who, using dark sorcery, battles the Titan of Time. Supergiant Games builds on the success of the original game with intense action and endless replayability. Explore a deeper mythical world rooted in Greek myth and witchcraft. The story unfolds through victories and failures, keeping you on your toes.

Master powerful bunas from Olympian gods such as Apollo and Zeus and imbue your weapons with ancient magic. Hades 2 boasts a huge number of voiced characters, both new and familiar. Each playthrough promises new locations, challenges, updates and surprises. Unlock the secrets of the Altar of Arcana, tame your acquaintances, and collect reagents using Tools of the Unseen. Constant updates and god mode are designed for both casual and hardcore players. Supergiant’s signature style is vibrant environments, fluid 3D characters and immersive music.

Hades II is a larger, more detailed world with an ever-changing landscape. Players defeat enemies using dark sorcery and legendary weapons imbued with ancient magic. Bonuses from more than a dozen Olympian gods provide almost limitless opportunities for developing abilities, so no two playthroughs of the game are the same.

Interacting with a colorful cast of characters, both familiar and new, adds another layer of depth. Players make connections, experience unique story events, and uncover the secrets of the Arcana Altar. New locations, challenges, and upgrade systems keep the gameplay fresh, encouraging players to return to the game again and again.

Constant improvements and the return of God Mode will satisfy players of all skill levels. Hades II offers an experience fit for both gods and mortals, with escalating challenges for those seeking the ultimate test of their divine potential. Supergiant’s signature style - rich environments, fluid character animations and an immersive score - brings the mythical world to life.
