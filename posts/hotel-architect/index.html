Game Description#

Hotel Architect is an exciting management simulation game where you take control of various hotels and serve a diverse clientele around the world. Building and designing the hotel of your dreams from scratch is the essence of the project. It is possible to choose which events will take place in different areas of your hotel. Decorate everything with amenities, decor and decorate the interior with wallpaper and flooring. Make it unique and attractive to your guests. When you travel to different parts of the world, each area has its own challenges and guests have their own preferences.

Knowing your Hotel Architect clients like the back of your hand is the key to thriving in this virtual hotel industry. By tailoring your services to their needs, you can fit seamlessly into any environment. Success depends on three important advisors - an accountant, a manager and a lawyer. These trusty guides can help you navigate the complexities of the hotel business, but be careful. Rash decisions and illegal actions can jeopardize their effectiveness, which can lead to their failure. An accountant has economic wisdom to help you obtain loans and improve your credit rating by manipulating financial statements.

The manager helps in adopting new policies and finding business partners to generate additional sources of income. A lawyer who is always ready to defend you will fight any charges. In addition, he is a pro at negotiating subsidies and will ensure that your back is well covered. The title isn’t just about making money, it’s also about making smart choices, managing risks, and building a reputation that can withstand any challenge.
