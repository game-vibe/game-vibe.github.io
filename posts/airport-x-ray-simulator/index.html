Game Description#

Airport X-Ray Simulator is an adventure game in which gamers find themselves in the shoes of an airport security officer. Main task? Do not allow prohibited or dangerous items to be brought on board the aircraft. From the very beginning of the game, users find themselves in a busy airport environment, where the focus is on carefully scanning luggage. X-ray machines are their main tool, and users must be on alert for anything that doesn’t belong to them.

It’s not just about finding weapons: there are all sorts of oddities hidden inside the bags. The challenge is to make quick but accurate decisions, because time is always running out. Airport X-Ray Simulator features a variety of items that can be found in bags, which makes the game interesting. The luggage is filled not only with clothes and toiletries, but also with items that can be both completely legal and very suspicious. This creates a dynamic where gamers have to learn to differentiate between innocent objects and things that could pose a threat. It’s not always easy.

Sometimes a harmless object can look like something more dangerous on a scanner, and sometimes an adventure game throws up oddities that make you think. Thanks to the variety of items, no two rounds are the same, so there’s always something new to discover. A distinctive feature is the Rainbow Laboratory mode, which takes the game to a new level. Here, users can perform tasks that go beyond simply scanning bags.
