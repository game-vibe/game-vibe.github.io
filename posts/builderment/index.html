Game Description#

Builderment is a fun factory-building game that puts you in a world on the verge of running out of resources. In this virtual world, your mission is clear: go to a distant planet, collect vital materials and build a modern factory capable of teleporting these resources to Earth, thereby saving our precious planet. Automation and craftsmanship are at the heart of the project.

You start by extracting valuable resources and gradually move on to creating complex items. The magic happens when you lay out a network of conveyor belts to seamlessly transport materials between different factory buildings. Technological advancement is your best friend to improve production efficiency and empower artisans. What’s more, Builderment encourages collaboration by allowing optimized factory sites to be shared with other players via original blueprints.

As you dive into the game, you will find yourself building a huge industrial empire. Machines autonomously produce products, conveyor belts move materials efficiently, and scientific research drives innovation. Collected resources - from wood to iron and copper - become the lifeblood of your enterprise. You’ll even hire miners to ensure a constant supply of these essential materials. Builderment is a complex conveyor control game that allows you to control the direction and flow of materials using splitters and underground belts. Additionally, the game encourages development through exploration, unlocking advanced technologies, new production facilities, and complex crafting recipes.
