Game Description#

Momiji From Purgatory is a first-person adventure in which a young student finds himself embroiled in a series of frightening events after his infidelity is discovered. The campus, which was once an ordinary place of study, is transformed into a location filled with traps and a strange atmosphere. The main task is to save your true love, making your way through a dangerous environment. The protagonist’s path is complicated by persecution from obsessed fans, creating constant tension.

The plot development is built around the consequences of the main character’s romantic relationships. Initially, he was in a relationship with classmate A, but a few months later he became interested in another girl, classmate B. It was after the deception was revealed that the main action of the Momiji From Purgatory project begins, where one has to fight for love, overcoming horror. The final test for the character and his lover will be a confrontation with the main antagonist, who intends to make them pay a high price for what they have done.

This title is a single-player campaign where the world is explored from the perspective of the character. The narrative is linear and focuses on revealing the history of the relationship and subsequent revenge. The gameplay combines elements of horror and action-adventure, forcing you to constantly be on alert and look for a way out of confusing situations created on the territory of the educational institution.
