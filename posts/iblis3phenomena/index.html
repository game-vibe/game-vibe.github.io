Game Description#

İblis3:Phenomena is a first-person psychological horror that follows the investigation of a journalist who arrives in an abandoned Turkish village. His goal is to explore a mysterious house that has become the epicenter of paranormal activity. The events of the project are inspired by a real incident that occurred in 1994, and focus on slowly building horror. There are no complex puzzles or fast-paced battles here, as the focus is on creating an oppressive and realistic environment that keeps you in constant suspense. Every detail of the setting and sound enhances the feeling of anxiety.

At the center of the story is the tragic story of a young woman named Suna. Forced into marriage against her will at the insistence of her father, she comes to the grave of her late mother in despair. In a fit of grief, Suna unknowingly wishes for her mother to attend the wedding. It is this fatal desire that sets off a chain of terrifying consequences that are to be explored in İblis3:Phenomena. The project methodically reveals the details of this drama, making you feel the hopelessness and fear that the heroine faced many years ago.

The gameplay is based on exploring locations, where the frightening realism of the environment is combined with sudden sounds and light anomalies. Traveling around the house and its surroundings becomes a real test for the psyche. All elements are aimed at making you doubt the reality of what is happening and gradually reveal the truth about what happened after Suna’s careless wish. The challenge is to piece together the pieces of the tragedy and discover whether supernatural forces really exist or are just a figment of the imagination provoked by an old story of grief and loss.
