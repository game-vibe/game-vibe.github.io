Game Description#

Kapital: Sparks of Revolution is a city planning simulator that allows you to deal with all economic issues in managing the capital. Events start at a time when European countries find themselves in a deep systemic crisis. There is a class struggle and it becomes difficult to avoid a social explosion. The player finds himself in such conditions. What price must be paid to save Europe from anarchy?

Game mechanics combine elements of a city builder and a social manager. As the mayor of a metropolis destroyed by a devastating war, his top priority is rebuilding the city after the bombing. With a limited number of resources on hand, it is necessary to resolve various social conflicts and provide solutions to crisis situations. Society is strictly divided into classes. There are 3 of them: nobles, bourgeois and workers.

Nobles are the most prosperous and educated people. The bourgeoisie is engaged in business and earns huge amounts of money, while the workers are in the lowest strata of society, having only pennies and not receiving any education. The only thing that unites them is the desire to live better. In Kapital: Sparks of Revolution you need to find a way out of such a difficult situation, satisfying the needs of different segments of the population.

Crisis management is one of the most difficult tests for any government. Too many responsibilities and tasks. Resources are always limited. A decision must be made as to who gets the food first. Protests, threats and riots in such a situation are simply inevitable. The future of the community is in the hands of the mayor. Depending on the decisions made, the political system and structure of the state are developed.
