Game Description#

Die For The Lich is a dark journey whose ultimate goal is the throne of the powerful Lich. The path to it is blocked by mythical monsters that came straight from classical folklore, among which there are Strigoi and Vetals. Each attempt to break through the darkness becomes a unique test, since death here is not the end, but only the beginning of a new, unique journey. The hero will have to rise from defeat over and over again, adapting his strategy and becoming stronger with each new cycle.

At the heart of this roguelike adventure is not a deck of cards, but a clever combination of dice with a variety of equipment. The rolls on the dice must be correctly combined with weapons and amulets in order to defeat your opponents. The arsenal ranges from rusty shovels and coffin-lid shields to ghostly sabers and durable tower shields. The Die for the Lich project offers a deep improvement system that allows you to develop your character and unlock new abilities that change battle tactics.

To manage chaos and luck, special mechanics are provided that allow you to tip the scales in your favor. For example, you can find a Lucky Die to get the desired result, or use the Fusion Table to create custom dice. The Eyepatch ability allows you to re-roll units, and the activation of the Blood Moon makes battles more difficult, but increases the reward. This combination of randomness and strategic planning turns each playthrough into a tense tactical puzzle where every roll matters.
