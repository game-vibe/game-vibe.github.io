Game Description#

Afallon is a high fantasy role-playing game in which the world constantly evolves in response to the actions of the protagonist. Completed tasks forever change the appearance of settlements and camps, opening new paths or changing existing locations. As the hero’s power grows, the level of threat also increases: the usual opponents are replaced by stronger enemies, maintaining the difficulty at the current level. Changing the time of day directly affects the environment - at night, merchants go to bed, and bandits and ghosts appear on the roads. The world is also filled with random events, from meteorite impacts to surprise attacks on caravans, making each playthrough unpredictable.

You are invited to become a part of this world by creating a unique character. There are several races to choose from, including dwarves, and 8 classes, each of which has three branches of specializations. Traveling across the 40 square kilometer world of Afallon, heroes collect resources such as ore and herbs to create equipment and consumables. The crafting system is an important part of progression, allowing you to prepare for the challenges ahead. In addition, players have the opportunity to purchase and develop properties, such as taverns or sawmills, which generate passive income and provide access to rare recipes.

Those looking for more serious challenges will find large-scale dungeons with several bosses and unique designs. An example is the updated Shadowfall Depths dungeon, which features a new enemy - the Kraat Hivebreaker. For those who prefer shorter challenges, there are Challenge Stones scattered throughout the world. They offer a variety of tasks: from surviving against waves of enemies to timed boss battles. Successful completion of such activities is rewarded with valuable loot and useful resources necessary to further strengthen the character.
