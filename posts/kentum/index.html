Game Description#

Kentum is a sci-fi adventure where the death of the protagonist is only a temporary setback thanks to cloning technology. The main task is to revive civilization, relying solely on automation and our own strength. The basis for survival is the creation of a self-regulating environment, where automated mechanisms and conveyor belts turn the shelter into a full-fledged production complex. Here, coal is produced from bones and wood, and ingots are smelted from scrap metal, gradually building a complex system to provide themselves with everything they need in a world devoid of people.

It all begins with the awakening of a space scientist named Kent after 7,000 years of suspended animation. A shipwreck on a distant future Earth makes him the last of humanity. His only companion is the sarcastic AI assistant ORB, whose sharp tongue accompanies the hero on his odyssey. Together they will explore the world of Kentum, uncovering the secrets of the abandoned planet and using advanced technology to survive. Every new discovery and created object becomes a step towards restoring the lost heritage of the human race.

The post-apocalyptic Earth of the year 10,000 greets the traveler with an ever-changing climate and dangerous biomes, from mountains of garbage to jungle-overgrown ruins. Using a hoverboard and grappling hook, Kent scans unknown flora and fauna to discover new crafting recipes. Fighting evolved predators and gathering resources in harsh environments is necessary to expand your base. Improved equipment opens up access to previously inaccessible locations where even more valuable materials lurk, closing the cycle of exploration, survival and creation.
