Game Description#

In PLAN B: TerraForm, it is necessary to take up the colonization of the planet. To do this, it is necessary to collect resources on the surface of the continent and debug a system for supplying production to cities for further production. The most important resource is settlers. It is required to do everything you need to make the planet suitable for people to live. For example, you need to heat the atmosphere to the correct temperature, create water bodies, plant trees and much more.

First of all, it is important to build a machine for terraforming. It will help to correct the landscape and make it suitable for further buildings. Terraforming occurs in real time. In PLAN B: TerRaform there are greenhouse gases factories. With their help, you can heat ice or cause precipitation necessary to create lakes and reservoirs. Rare trees gradually grow into full -fledged forests. Buildings are required to be placed on special hexagonal hexes.

For the research, construction and production of resources, the entire planet can be used. The gameplay is based on the simulation of temperature, vegetation and weather conditions. At the beginning of the game, only a few settlers are available for basic work. Gradually, a small town turns into a multimillion metropolis with a whole complex ecosystem and its relationship.
