Game Description#

SONOKUNI is a high-intensity combat experience in a world shaped by cybernetic augmentation and ancient legends. The title follows Takeru, an assassin who navigates a war-torn landscape where technology and mythology intertwine. One mistake means death, but mastery of time manipulation gives a chance to turn the tide of events. Users engage in precise battles with enemies that force them to strain their reflexes and tactical thinking.

Every fight in SONOKUNI requires a balance of aggression and restraint, rewarding those who learn from past failures. A dystopian atmosphere permeates every encounter, heightening the sense of urgency. Opponents range from cybernetically enhanced warriors to otherworldly creatures, each with a unique attack pattern. Combat focuses on fluid movement, requiring the user to be precise in dodging, parrying, and striking.

Takeru’s ability to manipulate time creates strategic opportunities, allowing him to make adjustments to the course of battle. As the story progresses, hostile forces tighten their grip, and each victory brings only temporary relief. The setting combines cyberpunk aesthetics and supernatural influences, creating an environment where history and future collide. Biotechnology has changed society, creating a conflict between tradition and progress. The protagonist’s struggle reflects this encounter, forcing her to consider the cost of survival. The narrative unfolds through encounters rather than through exposition, emphasizing action as the vehicle of exposition.
