Game Description#

Slackers - Carts of Glory offers a fun online game where you can play online with a friend. You will find yourself in a mad race against time, trying to make it before the store closes. The drinks have run out and the only option is to grab a cart and hit the streets! Players will be able to play pirate for free, taking part in exciting races. Online online, you’ll fight gravity and test your reflexes as you control a cart as it hurtles down a hill.

The game’s unique charm lies in its chaotic physics. Forget everything you know about traditional racing games - these carts have a mind of their own. One minute you’re driving on a flat road, and the next you’re crashing sideways into a hedge. However, that’s part of the fun, and mastering the unpredictable moves becomes a hilarious challenge. You’ll find yourself leaning through corners, desperately trying to control the car with just your body weight.

Slackers doesn’t just test your car control skills. The trails are peppered with shortcuts and secret paths, encouraging exploration and risk-taking. Drinking along the way is for a reason - it replenishes stamina, allowing you to gain speed when needed. The game has 16 different tracks for you to conquer, and there’s plenty of variety to keep you coming back for “just one more run.”

The multiplayer aspect turns the chaos up to eleven. Team up with (or compete against) three friends online, making every race a laugh-out-loud spectacle. There’s nothing like watching your buddy’s cart flip over after he tried to take a corner too hard. Of course, in Slackers, karma can even things out, so don’t get too overconfident!
