Game Description#

InOUTBRK, playing online puts you at the helm of a stormrider, but without any real danger. This computer game takes you to a fictional version of America’s Tornado Alley, recreating the thrill of severe weather hunting. Play for free with a friend online and compete with other virtual storm chasers to be the closest (and safest, in this case) to these powerful storms. Enjoy online gaming, even if you play on a pirate.

Each storm is unique and based on real weather data. Task? Combine the awe-inspiring power of nature with the need to collect valuable data about the storm. You’ll have to wade through torrential rain, dodge debris, and maybe even get too close to a tornado to gather important information. Imagine yourself as a scientist behind the wheel. Upgrade your car, customize it to suit your chasing style and become the best hurricane chaser. Explore a massive 625 square kilometer map with varied landscapes, from rushing rivers to tranquil forests. OUTBRK is the multiplayer game you’ve been waiting for, so fasten your seat belts and get ready for the best ride of your life.
