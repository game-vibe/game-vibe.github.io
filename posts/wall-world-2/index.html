Game Description#

Wall World 2 continues the exploration of the vertical world. Players will control a complex spider robot as they delve into procedurally generated mines to mine resources and search for lost artifacts. The machine and its pilot will have to fight hostile organisms inhabiting the dark depths of the structure. Each expedition aims to discover forgotten technologies and uncover the fundamental secrets hidden within this massive structure. The entire experience is focused on survival, managing supplies, and battling the horrors that lurk in the darkness.

What awaits daredevils in Wall World 2:

Extraction of resources to improve equipment. Battles with hostile creatures. Research of generated mines and biomes. Upgrading the robotic spider and the pilot’s exosuit. Uncovering the secrets buried in the depths of the Wall.

The gameplay in this project involves a constant calculation of risk and reward. Monsters pose a constant threat, attacking the robotic spider on the surface of the Wall and ambushing inside the dug tunnels. Collected materials are used to upgrade the player’s mobile battle station and personal exosuit between missions. These upgrades provide access to more remote and dangerous areas of the environment, and characters in the city-sphere offer more options for customizing equipment.

The Wall itself is a dynamic environment, changing and presenting new challenges with each approach. Moving through different biomes reveals environmental anomalies that can either help or hinder the gamer’s journey. The central point of interest is the mysterious Hole - a location that promises answers, but also poses significant threats. The goal of this game is to navigate your way deeper into this world, surviving its dangers to discover what lies in the uncharted depths.
