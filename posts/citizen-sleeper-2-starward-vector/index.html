Game Description#

Citizen Sleeper 2: Starward Vector is a dice RPG set in a vast, chaotic sci-fi universe. You play as the Sleeper, an android with a malfunctioning body and a past you can’t remember. With a price on your head, you find yourself on the run in the Starbelt, a collection of habitats built in the asteroid belt and relentlessly pursued by their creators. The world is full of difficult decisions, dubious contracts and the constant need to balance survival and moral choices.

Here you take over an old, battered ship and assemble a motley crew of unique characters. Each team member has their own skills and backstory, and it’s your job to keep them alive by completing various risky contracts. The work ahead is dangerous: from asteroid exploration to risky robberies, and each of them is fraught with its own difficulties. How you manage your crew, ship, and resources can mean the difference between success and failure.

The core mechanics of Citizen Sleeper 2: Starward Vector revolve around rolling dice that determine the outcome of your actions. Every decision you make - whether it’s upgrading a ship, recruiting a new crew, or completing a mission - depends on your luck and available resources. There is a lot of freedom in the game; the gamer himself determines the direction of his journey through the Star Belt. Although things never go according to plan, it is part of the exciting process of exploring this ever-changing world.
