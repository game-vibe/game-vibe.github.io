Game Description#

Noble Legacy begins in the aftermath of the war, with the player taking over the inherited and neglected kingdom of Greenwood. The task is to manage these lands from a third party, personally controlling the process of revival in its streets and fields. All buildings, from ordinary halls to fortress walls, are erected under the personal control of the gamer. The process involves recruiting skilled settlers, furnishing their homes and meeting their needs to maintain high morale. The gameplay combines kingdom management with direct action on the ground.

In Noble Legacy, the craftsmen and inhabitants of the kingdom have unique traits and ambitions, guided by advanced AI. To maintain productivity, it is necessary to harmonize their ever-changing needs and personalities by managing social dynamics. This goal is achieved by improving housing, organizing leisure time and providing more advanced tools. Attracting high-ranking settlers opens up new crafting opportunities and professions, allowing for efficient production and maintenance of a growing settlement to maintain the morale of each subject.

This game includes a complex economic simulation where you have to direct the work of citizens, build resource chains and agricultural processes. The main function of this system is to ensure that the population is fed, warm and happy. Gold is obtained by selling goods to visitors and turning the village into a regional trading hub. It is also necessary to prepare for various disasters: severe weather, plague and bandit raids. For more global guidance, you can switch to a strategic top-down view, complementing the third-person perspective.
