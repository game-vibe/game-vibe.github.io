Game Description#

Super Bomberman R - an adventure about the cult hero Bomberman. On the pirate Super Bomberman R, you can play online for free with a friend, choosing one of 8 heroes. Competing online over the Internet, players travel into space to confront Emperor Bugler. Classic mode, online play and grand prix are introduced. The latter mode appears for the first time in the series. It is aimed at team play.

There are many new characters available that have never appeared in the franchise. All heroes have unique abilities. Story mode allows you to play alone or with up to 8 partners. Classic matches feature action-packed bouts with free online play options. In the Grand Prix, you can compete with two teams, fighting for the title of the best bombers in the universe. The gameplay allows you to run through interactive labyrinths, blowing up everything in your path.

Tasks are divided into several types: kill a certain number of enemies, eliminate all opponents, collect 3 keys, etc. Events take place on different planets, which are distinguished by unique biomes: ice continent, lava, forest and others. The difference from previous games in the franchise is the isometric perspective and the transition of the project to a full-fledged three-dimensional space. A special feature of the cards is their verticality, represented by several floors. You can destroy both walls and ceilings. It will take about 3-4 hours of real time to complete the story campaign.
