Game Description#

Sobas Gunpaw is a dynamic action game in the style of Bullet Heaven, where survival directly depends on the constant and systematic strengthening of the protagonist. At the heart of it all is an extensive skill system that provides access to numerous character upgrades. The progression process is built on the cyclical destruction of everything on the screen, which allows you to accumulate valuable assets. It is these materials collected from defeated enemies that open new branches in the branched tree of talents. This approach allows you to radically change combat tactics, adapting to the increasing complexity and types of opponents. Every decision made about choosing a perk has a direct impact on the effectiveness of clearing locations from hostile forces.

The key mechanics force you to constantly be on the move, destroying endless waves of enemies to collect resources. The entire gameplay of Sobas Gunpaw comes down to a continuous sequence of actions: eliminating threats, collecting the materials they drop, and immediately investing in new abilities from the development tree. This fast-paced and intense cycle creates a tense atmosphere, preventing you from relaxing even for a second. The project is conceived as a concentrated adventure, the complete passage of which is designed for a short session, taking approximately 4 hours. This condensed format allows for a complete and cohesive experience without having to spend dozens of hours mastering all the available mechanics.
