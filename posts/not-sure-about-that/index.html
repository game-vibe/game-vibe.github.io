Game Description#

Not Sure About That is an intellectual quiz where participants will have to demonstrate their knowledge in order to win a prestigious award - the Golden Brain trophy. The protagonist must overcome 555 multiple choice questions covering a wide variety of topics. Each correct answer allows you to move forward to the next test, while any mistake inevitably leads to a fall into the cold ocean waters. Fortunately for the test taker, there is a checkpoint after each question to save progress. The ultimate goal is not only to receive the coveted cup, but also to achieve a high result in the minimum time and with the least number of falls.

The whole action takes place in very unusual conditions: the main character named Tom finds himself in complete isolation in the middle of the endless ocean. To track your progress, Not Sure About That provides a concise interface that helps you evaluate your progress. A special green counter records the points scored, a red counter records each dive, and a timer mercilessly counts down precious time. The challenge itself has been carefully balanced so that the questions are neither overly easy nor insurmountably difficult, ensuring fair and fun gameplay for everyone. For convenience, all progress is saved automatically at regular intervals.
