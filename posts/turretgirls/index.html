Game Description#

Turretgirls offers to manage an operative with a turret reflecting the attacks of extraterrestrial forces from unstable wormholes. The main mission is to protect the generator, the only source of energy for planetary superweapies. Acting with a fixed rail platform, the heroine is opposed to the swarm of alien invaders that threaten the entire installation. The survival of the planet depends on this single defense against the invasion horde, since the retreat is impossible.

Success in Turretgirls depends on the combat effectiveness of the gamer, because each executed mission brings resources for upgrades. The resulting funds go to strengthen protective structures, the construction of front -line generators and an increase in general firepower. The rail turret platform itself receives systemic improvements based on the results in battle. High glasses also open elite accessories and different underwear, serving a tangible reward for successful actions against an alien threat.

The protagonist consists in a tactical shock unit to neutralize planetary aliens and wears a specialized uniform. This equipment is designed to be strategically destroyed in extreme conditions of the battle. During tense collisions, the costume is self -destroyed, which is a function for increasing the survival and maneuverability of the heroine. This feature sets the success of the task and effectiveness in battle above the structural integrity of vestments.

What awaits the defender at the front line:

Endless waves of alien invaders tearing from wormholes. Defense of a strategically important generator at all costs. Constant strengthening of turrets and opening new weapons systems. Castomization system with access to elite accessories and equipment. Specialized uniform, destroying in the battle to increase mobility.
