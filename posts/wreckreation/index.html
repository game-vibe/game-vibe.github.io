Game Description#

Users in Wreckreation will enjoy dynamic rides in racing cars, moving across vast locations. You will go out for a ride on unique tracks where there are jumps, twisted road elements and other dangerous tests. The game has a plentiful number of cars to choose from, as well as many locked cars that should be gradually unlocked as you progress through the single-player campaign. Players will have to tune their cars, changing the appearance with paint and body kits, as well as improving driving characteristics with the engine, wheels and other components.

In the process of passing Wreckreation, gamers have to move through levels during different weather conditions (rain, sun, clouds), day and night. In the background, you will listen to 16 built-in radio stations with energetic musical compositions that set the pace as you move along the road. On the tracks there will be a huge number of complex landscape obstacles, through which you need to get through maneuvering and correct calculation of actions. You need to gain acceleration and brake in a timely manner in order to skid into difficult turns at speed. Users will also have to try themselves as a route builder in a special constructor mode, which will allow them to assemble the desired routes using blank roads.
