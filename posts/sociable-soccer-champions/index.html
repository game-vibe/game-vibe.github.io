Game Description#

Sociable Soccer Champions is a fast-paced take on competitive soccer with fast-paced arcade gameplay at its core. Controls are intuitive, but mastery will require some practice. The arsenal of techniques includes powerful shots with spin on the ball, as well as acceleration and energy consumption systems. The key element is to build your own roster from an extensive database of over 14,000 licensed FIFPRO professionals. Each player card has unique attributes, allowing you to create tactically flexible teams to achieve victories.

You can test your strength in several formats. Career mode allows you to guide your favorite club or national team through a series of prestigious leagues and cup tournaments in order to win more than 80 trophies. For quick matches, Sociable Soccer Champions features friendlies, allowing you to pit any of the 1,400 available teams against each other. You can confront both artificial intelligence and a live opponent in the local multiplayer mode. Referees with different refereeing styles ensure that the rules are followed on the field.

Competitive elements:

Formation of a unique squad of more than 14,000 licensed FIFPRO football players. The use of powerful shots with spin of the ball and energy consumption systems in matches. Participate in career mode to win over 80 trophies with your club or national team. Host friendly matches between any of the 1,400 available teams. Competitions against artificial intelligence or a live opponent in a local game.
