Game Description#

Drug Dealer Simulator will allow you to create your own criminal empire without moral and legal consequences. The player starts in a shabby shelter and begins to develop his business. You can either become a dark genius or a cruel gangster. The player needs to purchase goods from the cartels, avoid the police and organize safe houses. You can sell goods both in small quantities and individually, and in large quantities to entire gangs. But extreme caution is required - each foray may be interrupted by the Drug Enforcement Agency, and one of the competitors may become angry. But the brave man is knee-deep in the sea.

Most of the gameplay consists of the following actions. You receive a message that someone wants to buy something. Find the product you need or take from your supplies, and then go out into the creepy and dangerous world. Avoid prying eyes or potentially dangerous people, find a secluded place and make a bookmark. Or bring the goods personally to the client. In this world, you are not the only one seeking wealth and power. You shouldn’t cheat your clients, at least not too often. Collaborate with the right people, make useful contacts, don’t get caught, and you can live a long enough life not only to earn wealth, but also to spend it brilliantly. And you can spend it usefully - on growing the gang, hiring new people, purchasing goods, houses, warehouses and decorations, and on parties. It’s up to you to decide. Game Features:

A drug dealer simulator in all its glory. Buy goods, deliver to customers, avoid the cops and don’t get involved in disputes without proper support. Create your own recipes. Maybe you have the talent of a chemist and you will set up the production of dope yourself. Deliver goods to bookmarks, personally into the hands of customers, or in large quantities to entire gangs. Expand your business. Buy and equip new shelters. Move from a shabby little room to a luxurious penthouse. If you live.
