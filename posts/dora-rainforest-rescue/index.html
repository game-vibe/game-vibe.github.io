Game Description#

Dora: Rainforest Rescue is an educational adventure that takes you into the heart of the rainforest on an important mission. The main goal is to save a unique ecosystem, which requires participants to be smart and attentive to the details of the world around them. A journey through the colorful jungle is filled with various challenges directly related to ecology and respect for nature. The gameplay involves exploring areas where, to advance through the story, you need to collect useful items and solve exciting puzzles that open access to new locations.

Dasha and her faithful friends never leave you in trouble, actively helping you overcome difficulties with tips and support. Project Dora: Rainforest Rescue emphasizes the value of teamwork, teaching the fundamentals of effective collaboration to achieve a common noble goal. Caring for animals is one of the central elements of the story, clearly demonstrating the importance of wildlife conservation. Each successfully completed task not only brings satisfaction, but also makes a real contribution to restoring the fragile balance of flora and fauna in this wonderful world.

Tasks of a young conservationist:

Solving logical riddles to overcome natural barriers. Collect special items needed to help forest dwellers. The study of an ecosystem through interaction with its flora and fauna. Joint missions with faithful comrades to save the jungle.
