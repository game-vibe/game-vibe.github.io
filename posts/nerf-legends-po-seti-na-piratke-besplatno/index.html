Game Description#

Nerf Legends is a competitive shooter that allows you to play online with a friend, participating in various modes of confrontation over the Internet online. On Pirates you can join teams for free or play in classic deathmatch modes. There is online play and single player mode. Nerf Legends takes place in a future world filled with robots.

The arsenal of weapons consists of powerful NERF blasters. Each barrel has its own unique characteristics and features. Battles take place on various interactive maps. The possibility of free online play offers you to choose your fighter and fight with other unique characters. To become a legend, you must learn to skillfully wield futuristic weapons. Only one champion will emerge victorious!

A total of 15 blasters from the Mega, Ultra and Elite lines are available. By defeating enemies, you can pump up, improve the barrels and customize their appearance individually. Battles take place in levels such as Fortress Siege, Ragnarok, Spaceport Shootout, and Trouble in the Jungle. During battles, it is necessary to collect such improvements as a magnetic attractor, freezing, search dart, etc.

In addition, you will need to fight 5 powerful bosses. The locations have springboards, springs and accelerators. They must be used for tactical purposes. Multiplayer matches are available for up to 8 people. There are challenges and puzzles that force you to make the most of your blasters.
