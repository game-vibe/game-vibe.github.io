Game Description#

Nuclear Gladiators 3000 is an opportunity to become the star of a brutal TV show, quench the thirst for spectacle of the bloodthirsty crowd and write your name in history forever. The winners will receive not only recognition, but also a place of honor in the Hall of Fame, as well as a glossy cover of Gladiators Today Magazine. These are the goals that fighters pursue in a retrofuturistic world, where survival has become the highest-rated entertainment, broadcast live for the amusement of the audience. Death here is just an annoying obstacle on the path to universal adoration and glory.

Events take place in 2040 in neon-lit arenas broadcast by the Blaster Vision Channel. Gamers will have to choose one of several gladiators, each of which has a unique progression system and set of abilities. As part of the Nuclear Gladiators 3000 project, they will have to face endless waves of disgusting mutants, rebels and other champions of the show. Each run allows you to improve your arsenal and skills, and between battles permanent power-ups are unlocked, increasing your chances of success in subsequent attempts to survive this bloody show.

The path to fame lies through completing special tasks that increase fame and provide access to exclusive bonuses. Fights take place in stylized pixel arenas, where you need to destroy hordes of enemies and dodge hundreds of projectiles. Each stage culminates in intense battles with powerful bosses guarding valuable rewards. Short and intense sessions require the fighter to constantly adapt, selecting new equipment and weapons to effectively destroy opponents and win the love of the audience.
