Game Description#

Delicious - The First Course is a culinary time management adventure where the main goal is to help Emily establish her career. The passage process is based on timely service to visitors, whose patience is strictly limited. Orders include both prepared foods and dishes that require timed preparation, which introduces an element of multitasking to the mechanics. The storyline is dedicated to the origins of the protagonist’s professional path, revealing her first steps in the restaurant business. The journey covers 8 unique establishments with a variety of world cuisines, from American classics to Indian and Mexican recipes.

Culinary variety of restaurants:

The American diner is a classic burger and fries establishment that requires speed and precision in order picking. Indian Restaurant - invites players to master the preparation of curries and other spicy dishes with multi-ingredient recipes. Mexican Cafe - Specializes in tacos and burritos, adding fresh ingredient combination mechanics to the gameplay.

Funds earned during levels allow you to purchase upgrades for each establishment. Delicious - The First Course provides a development system that covers both work tools and the interior. In addition to the main 80 challenges, an endless mode is available to hone your culinary skills.
