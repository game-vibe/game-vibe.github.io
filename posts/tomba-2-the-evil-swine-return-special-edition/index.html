Game Description#

Tomba! 2: The Evil Swine Return Special Edition is a special edition that offers a fresh look at the often overlooked sequel to the legendary platformer. The process of passing has been significantly simplified due to modern innovations. The save anywhere function is now available, eliminating the need to look for special places to record progress. There is also an option to rewind time, allowing you to undo an unsuccessful action or a missed jump. Despite these conveniences, the gameplay has retained its signature complexity and demanding reactions. The mechanics are based on a side-scrolling perspective with direct control of the protagonist. The entire adventure takes place in a fantasy world with a vibrant anime style.

The story begins with the treacherous invasion of the Evil Pigs, who seized neighboring territories. At the same time, Tabby, the protagonist’s loyal friend, disappears without any trace. Without wasting a second, the character sets off on a risky journey across the sea to find his comrade and put an end to the invasion. In Tomba! 2, the main task is to save the world from insidious invaders. For fans of the franchise, a special addition has been prepared in the form of a virtual museum. This gallery features a rich selection of archival materials, scanned manuals, promotional posters and high-quality illustrations. You can also find original design documents there.
