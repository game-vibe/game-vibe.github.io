Game Description#

Gamers in Dummynation will have to try themselves as a world invader, expanding their influence on the planet. The game is a geopolitical simulator where you need to develop your own strategy to improve your political position on the planet. Users will select one of the states to begin gradually expanding their borders. It is necessary to carry out military occupations, declare annexation of selected territories, and much more.

Dummying players will analyze the current situation, as well as establish relationships with other countries. It is necessary to enter into alliances or incite military conflicts in order to enter into direct conflict with opponents. It will be possible to use the resources needed for research and technology development. You need to balance and look for tactics that allow you to successfully dominate your opponents in the geopolitical arena of the world.

During the course of the game, players will find many opportunities that are revealed deeper in single or multiplayer mode. You will have to compete with other users for power, taking various actions during the given turn. You need to collect bonus points for leveling up and completing tasks. Gamers will be able to increase and extract valuable resources in captured territories in order to develop their own empire.
