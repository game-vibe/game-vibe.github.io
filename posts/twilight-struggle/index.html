Game Description#

Twilight Struggle takes place during the Cold War. The gameplay is presented in the form of a strategy, and you have to observe the world map and perform various actions. Each session provides you with original tasks and goals to accomplish. Move groupings of units and influence other factions to gain allies or increase your own power. The action covers historical moments that occurred from 1948 to 1967. Learn about and get involved in the Arab-Israeli conflicts, US peace rallies, the Cuban Missile Crisis and other similar incidents. Many moments brought the world to the brink of loss, such as nuclear annihilation. Control America or become the head of the USSR to change the course of history during the Cold War. Create coups d’etat and watch the DEFCON indicator, which goes down if power changes quickly. The winner is the one who took control over all of Europe, and the participants can start a nuclear war at any moment. Features of Twilight Struggle:

Matches of up to 2 participants at the same time, opposing each other. The ability to improve the economy of the state, increase combat power, search for allies from other countries or seize territories by military means. Asynchronous support for multiplayer matches. All events in the gameplay are actual moments that happened at that time.
