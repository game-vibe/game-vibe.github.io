Game Description#

Lumo 2 is the journey of a mature wizard through the labyrinths of changing dimensions, where every step is a test of logic and reflexes. Overcoming dangerous platforms, the protagonist finds himself either in the scenery of a haunted house or on board a spaceship, which forces him to constantly adapt to new conditions. The main goal is not only to find a way out, but also to collect carefully hidden collectibles: 25 vintage cassette tapes and 20 camouflaged ducks are scattered around the world, the detection of which requires remarkable care.

The world of this project is a huge puzzle consisting of more than 100 interconnected rooms, each of which functions as a unique box with a secret. Moving forward involves solving a variety of problems, including maintaining balance on a ball or participating in battles on star fighters. Mechanics are constantly evolving, offering fresh challenges and unexpected twists that change the usual rules. At its core, Lumo 2 is a modern reimagining of classic arcade adventures, inspired by British titles from the 80s and 90s.

The gameplay is designed to recreate the charm of the retro era, but with the use of modern technology to ensure smooth control. The developers have created an atmosphere of nostalgic adventure, where exploring the environment and discovering all its secrets is rewarded with vivid emotions. This isometric puzzle platformer challenges genre veterans and newcomers alike, offering a fun and varied challenge in a bizarre, ever-changing world.
