Game Description#

Puropu Defense Squad is a strategy action game where animate inanimate objects are forced to defend their existence from ruthless invaders from Earth. The central task for the fighters of the elite squad is the defense of the Extractor, which is the source of life for the entire Puropu civilization. Confronting aggressors requires not only direct participation in battles, but also the construction of complex defensive structures. Defenders have at their disposal an extensive arsenal of hundreds of defense mechanisms, including 15 types of traps and turrets. Additional opportunities for strengthening and synergy of buildings are opened through the Research Center. Each wave of opponents, including farmers or police officers, as well as cloning agents, tests your tactical ingenuity to the limit.

You can take part in the defense of the world of Puropa as one of several colorful characters. Each of the protagonists in Puropu Defense Squad has a radically different fighting style and their own talent tree. Choose from the explosive Mr. Box, the tactical Gray, the fast-paced Tomusashi, the piercing Ryush, and the blinding Denkyu. The entire campaign is available both solo and in co-op mode for a team of up to 4 people, and the difficulty is automatically adjusted to the number of participants. There are several test levels to complete all maps:

Normal, offering a balanced experience. Elite, increasing the durability and damage of enemies. Heroic, where maximum coordination is required. Endless, testing your endurance against never-ending waves.
