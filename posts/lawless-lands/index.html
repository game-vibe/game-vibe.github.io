Game Description#

Lawless Lands is a role-playing game in a dark fantasy setting. You will challenge the Darkness itself and explore the territory of Crossia - a formerly great kingdom, which is now plunged into poverty and crime. Hunger and death reign all around. The army has lost its power and the country is in turmoil.

You take on the role of a hero rising from the ashes. He challenges the entire empire and is the only one capable of restoring Crossia’s greatness. You and the protagonist need to overcome a thorny path that runs through the entire state. You will meet many different interesting characters, a huge number of traps and obstacles, as well as countless enemies ready to grind you into powder without giving you a single chance to carry out your plan.

The plot of Lawless Lands contains multiple endings. In addition to the main quests, the game features about 125 side quests. There is an element of randomness with 15 types of mutators and 650 unique situations. The election system allows for various moral actions to be taken throughout the campaign. The world is represented by 25 settlements and 320 points of interest. In total there are 42 unique sprites of characters of various races, each of which has its own character and history. The adventure features about 200 types of enemies in 20 different factions, not counting a huge selection of weapons and a wide variety of additional equipment.
