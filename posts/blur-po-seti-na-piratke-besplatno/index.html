Game Description#

Blur is an arcade race that allows you to take part in online competitions on the Internet, or compete on a pirate game with a friend. You can play online for free with random people from any corner of the planet. Whether going through the story campaign or using the free online game option, the main goal of each racer is only to reach the finish line first, leaving his opponents behind. Career mode offers you to fight with 9 bosses: Shannon, Hana, Verseweaver, Drake, Natalia, Claw, Ayumi and Harumi, Butcher and Mysterious Enemy.

The duel with each leader uses the same car as the opponent, putting the participants on equal terms. Blur’s story is told through emails and social media conversations. Many pages and sites refer to well-known brands, for example, Inner Tube, etc. Single or online games allow you to drive a huge number of licensed cars: Dodge Viper, BMW, Ford Transit and many others. All vehicles have a well-developed damage system and are represented by unique destructibility for each type of vehicle.

Multiplayer battles take place with the participation of 20 participants simultaneously. Splitscreen is only available for four players. There is a health scale showing the integrity of the car. You will have to drive along slightly modified streets of Los Angeles and London. You can not only participate in races, but also show a driving master class: perform stunts, jump from springboards, smash a car to smithereens, etc. - the more spectacular, the cooler. You can also dial up a special charge scale and ram your opponent’s vehicle, trying to disable it. The same energy can be spent on acceleration.
