Game Description#

Orbi Universo 2 puts the player at the helm of a civilization’s journey through history. It is necessary to lead society from its Neolithic origins to the development of atomic energy. The project operates on a system of nodes that links each component of the nation together. The actions taken produce cascading effects, changing the structure and future of the entire society. This network includes food production, trade, belief systems and conflict.

Become the architect of your civilization:

Manage a complex system of interconnected nodes. Lead your people from the Neolithic to the atomic age. Make decisions that create cascading effects in society. Find a balance between development, trade and war.

In Orbi Universo 2 you will have to shape the cultural and religious traits of your state through the decisions you make. The world contains many regions that have unique local resources and their own set of nodes for improvement. This requires adaptation to the environment and can lead to the creation of distinctive peoples. In addition to internal management, gameplay involves interacting with other powers on a dynamic map through diplomacy, establishing commerce, or waging war.

Maintaining stability and expansion require a constant influx of valuable resources. You can obtain the necessary materials either through trade agreements or through direct conquest. During the war, the outcome of battles depends on the tactical choices made by the player. Subsequently, the interaction of political, economic and military systems gives rise to unpredictable historical narratives based on the actions in the game.
