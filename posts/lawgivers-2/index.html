Game Description#

Lawgivers 2 gives you the opportunity to feel like a real politician, on whom the adopted bills, and sometimes the fate of the entire state, depend. In the unstable modern world, where there is a constant struggle for resources, it will be quite difficult to maintain peace agreements. Each contract must be thought out as precisely as possible with all the small details that cannot be avoided.

The main character’s goal is to become president. This is not an illusory dream, but something to which he is confidently moving, doing a great job every day. First of all, he needs to recruit the right and smart team that will allow him to advance in the political arena, both among parliament and among the people who carefully elect a candidate. And to do this, you need to take into account a lot of complex factors such as GDP, population growth, the relationship between taxes and the minimum wage, etc. At the same time, support from large world states remains important. If it is beneficial for them to bring the protagonist to the main post, then they can help him with this.

LoveGiveverse 2 depicts the realistic behavior of people in power. The assembled party must not only be professional and savvy in all legal issues, but also agree on a common opinion and goal. After all, if there are too many disagreements about the bills and their vision, then people will simply start running over to competitors, where they will be more comfortable, and will undermine the player’s reputation.
