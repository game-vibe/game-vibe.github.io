Game Description#

Warnament is a turn-based global strategy game that puts the player in charge of the fate of an entire nation. Progress is achieved through the conquest of new territories and steady development in the military, technological, cultural or political spheres. You have the opportunity to take part in historical scenarios or create completely original worlds using the built-in editor.

This approach opens the way to campaigns based on real events, or to alternative universes of your own design, where each province is governed separately. The global arena in Warnament is shaped by complex diplomacy and the intricacies of statecraft. Rulers declare wars, sign peace treaties, and enter into pacts and alliances with other world powers. An individual nation can guarantee the sovereignty of a union state or forcefully turn another into its vassal state.

Economic pressure is applied through the mechanisms of global trade or the strangulation of rivals with sanctions, while allies can be drawn into large-scale international conflicts. Among other things, opponents can be showered with direct insults. The armed forces can include a wide range of units: from infantry to nuclear bombs for the total destruction of the enemy. Fleets of cruisers, battleships and aircraft carriers establish dominance of the seas, while defensive fortresses protect their homelands. The player has the opportunity to deploy chemical weapons while operating outside the generally accepted laws of war.
