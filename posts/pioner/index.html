Game Description#

Set on the isolated, high-radiation island of Tartarus, PIONER offers a brutal test of survival. This area, previously used to maintain a system of bunkers and laboratories, is now infested with mutants, anomalies and wild animals. The space is divided into zones with PvE and PvP content, as well as social hubs for interaction. Some tasks take place in closed locations, available for both solo and group play. In addition to standard enemies, mini-bosses and powerful raid enemies pose a serious threat, and battles against real opponents take place in special arenas.

The basis of progress is completing plot and faction assignments. Interaction with factions increases their loyalty, opening access to improved deals. The usual health indicators and enemy levels are missing, and the emphasis is shifted to equipment characteristics such as rating, rarity and damage. The gameplay of PIONER requires a comprehensive approach to survival, forcing you to constantly monitor your character’s condition. To maintain strength you need:

satisfy hunger in a timely manner and provide the protagonist with rest; create and modify weapons on a workbench from improvised means and artifacts; Carefully manage bulky inventory to carry resources.
