Game Description#

Racers in Formula Legends have been competing in car races for several decades. The gameplay spans the period from the 1960s to the present day, offering 16 vehicle models based on historical prototypes. Races take place at 14 circuits based on famous racing locations. These tracks are not static, they change over the years and offer fresh challenges in each new era. This journey through time clearly shows the evolution of racing cars and track configurations.

Speed ​​alone is not enough to win on the track in Formula Legends. Players will have to manage strategic elements such as battery consumption, using the slipstream to overtake, and making timely pit stops. Weather conditions, such as asphalt wet from rain, affect the car’s handling and fighting tactics. The competitions feature fictional pilots based on real-life sports legends and possessing special skills.

Their abilities include careful handling of tires, mastery of driving in the rain or increased efficiency of pit stops, providing a tactical advantage. The story campaign allows players to relive key moments in racing history through a series of championships across different eras. In addition to the main narrative, in this game you can create your own races and entire seasons of competitions. The time trial mode offers you to compete for the best results in the global leaderboard. The project also supports modifications, allowing for detailed customization of the appearance of cars, helmets and even sponsor banners on the track for a personal touch.
