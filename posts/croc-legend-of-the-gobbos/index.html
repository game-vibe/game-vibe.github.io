Game Description#

Croc Legend of the Gobbos is a revival of the classic platformer in which a brave crocodile named Croc must save his adopted family. The insidious wizard Baron Dante, together with his minions Dantini, captured the Gobbo Archipelago, imprisoning its peace-loving furry inhabitants. Among the prisoners was their leader, King Rufus, who at the last moment managed to summon the magical bird Bini to protect the protagonist from danger. Now the main character must single-handedly confront the villain and free all his relatives from captivity.

The journey takes place across five unique worlds, including volcanic and icy islands, as well as underwater caves. The gameplay of Croc: Legend of the Gobbos involves overcoming a variety of obstacles using running, jumping and swimming. To neutralize enemies, use a tail attack and a ground strike. This updated 45-level adventure has been greatly improved. The developers not only redesigned the visual component in high resolution, but also introduced a modernized control scheme with an updated camera for a more comfortable experience.

The main goal in each stage is to find and release all the hidden Gobbos. Successful completion of this mission opens up access to new locations and brings the decisive battle with the powerful Baron Dante closer. As Croc moves through the levels, he collects colorful crystals that serve as keys to secret areas and additional challenges. Each zone of the archipelago is guarded by bosses, minions of the main antagonist, to defeat whom you need to use ingenuity and mastered skills, turning each battle into a tactical puzzle.
