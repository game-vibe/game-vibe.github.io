Game Description#

Out of Reach is an open-world survival game that offers the opportunity to play with a friend online, exploring the open world on a pirate ship for free. Two multiplayer modes are presented: online co-op and local confrontation. The adventure begins with the main characters being washed ashore after a shipwreck. Now you need to survive while searching for resources, equipment, weapons, etc. From the collected materials, a shelter is built, which helps protect against attacks from enemies and other players.

Using the opportunity of free online play, you can survive together and fight back your opponents. It is also possible to compete with other participants, trying to gain dominance on the island. Heroes are able to mine ore and stones, cut down trees, collect flax, hunt and much more. Copper, tin and bronze are very important resources. Network play allows you to collect common resources to build a huge base, equipping it with protective equipment.

For crafting and construction, workshops, furnaces, anvils and much more are used. Anyone can create armor or weapons to suit their individual style. Skills improve as you use them. You can trade resources with other players, or declare a bloody feud and take them by force. By creating a legendary sword or strengthening your fortress, you can become the most important robber on the entire island. Weapon for defense and siege: Ballista - charges very slowly, but shoots hundreds of meters; Trebuchet - requires a lot of resources, but breaks through walls; Traps - tripwires that catch enemies by surprise; Glider - a weapon for air attacks, with which you can bomb the bases.
