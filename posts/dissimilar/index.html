Game Description#

Set in an ancient castle with futuristic technology, Dissimilar challenges players to unravel long-forgotten mysteries. The basis of the entire process is navigation through the labyrinths of the citadel called the Vault. Filled with high-tech gadgets and robots, this place stands in stark contrast to its ancient walls. Success in passing depends not only on victories in turn-based encounters, but also on careful study of the environment. Fully hand-rendered visuals combined with a soundtrack that mixes orchestral and synth music create a unique atmosphere.

Basics of tactical passing:

Exploring locations to discover secret passages and narrative fragments. Analyze unique enemy behavior patterns to develop an effective strategy. Selection and management of a team of fighters with various specializations. Restoring a complete picture of the past through found pieces of information.

At the center of the story is a girl named Amelia. Her peaceful evening is interrupted when her best friend forces her to return to the Vault, a castle associated with childhood memories. This return turns out to be not at all sentimental, because the heroine becomes a participant in a mysterious game with unclear rules. The passage of Dissimilar is tied to unraveling a tangle of family secrets and confronting traumas hidden in the depths of the past.
