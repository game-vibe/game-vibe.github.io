Game Description#

DriveCrazy is an arcade madness that asks you to get behind the wheel of a miniature truck amid the total destruction of the apocalyptic landscapes of Japan. The main task is to survive in the face of constant pursuit by mysterious creatures. To avoid missiles and falling buildings, it is possible to move along vertical surfaces. The process of destroying environmental objects, such as pillars, trees and fences, is directly related to replenishing the supply of nitro acceleration. The passage is divided into separate levels, some of which end with a confrontation with giant creatures, including huge brown bears.

DriveCrazy’s narrative takes place in the vastness of abandoned cities and countryside, where there are no signs of human life. The entire structure of the project is built on the sequential overcoming of stages with various conditions. Some races are dedicated to battles with ordinary opponents, while others offer exclusively high-speed maneuvering among obstacles without enemy presence. The competitive element is implemented through an online rating system, which allows you to compete for first places in the record tables. The best results in terms of time and points scored are recorded for each individual race.
