Game Description#

Dungeon Rampage is a cooperative action role-playing game in which groups of up to 4 adventurers go into dangerous dungeons to destroy monsters and search for treasure. The project offers a choice of 8 unique heroes with distinctive fighting styles, from the classic Berserker to the exotic Combat Cook. A deep customization system allows you to fine-tune your characters to suit your preferences, using hundreds of equipment options and skills. Progression is built on honest receipt of rewards, and modern mechanisms for protecting against dishonest methods of passage provide equal conditions for all adventurers.

Each mission becomes a unique challenge thanks to a combination of pre-created and procedurally generated areas of locations. The journey through the world of Dungeon Rampage runs through crypts, canyons and forests filled not only with enemies, but also with hidden traps. To survive and advance towards their goal, adventurers need:

destroy hordes of opponents in fast-paced arcade battles; collect valuable loot to improve existing equipment; master the destructive abilities of new weapons and magical pets.

The plot sends the heroes to a brutal tournament known as the Underground Games. The reason for everything was the act of the tyrannical ruler, Lord Dinglepus, who dealt with the princess’s dog. Now brave warriors must go through a series of deadly trials in ever-changing labyrinths in order to ultimately fight the mad king and bring justice. Endless replayability ensures that every attempt will present a new challenge even to experienced fighters.
