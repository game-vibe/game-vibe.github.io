Game Description#

Deathground is a first-person survival horror game aimed at cooperative play. The story centers on people fighting for survival among bloodthirsty dinosaurs. There are different modes available in which you need to team up with three more players, trying to break through the jungle and military bases, fighting against powerful ancient lizards. The heroes will have to survive on the killing field, where every enemy poses a serious threat. It is necessary to act harmoniously and competently, constantly interacting with partners.

The single player mode in Deathground is a survival horror game where the hero alone survives against powerful monsters, hiding from them in the shadows and trying not to get noticed. You can’t get close, as they have a heightened sense of smell. There is a motion tracking device, similar to the one Ripley had in Alien Isolation. With its help, you can always find out where the enemy is approximately located.

There is a class system where each character has unique skills and character traits. When playing together, each hero has his own position in the team. There is randomization: the location and movement of dinosaurs, weather conditions, sequence of tasks and much more. The game stands out for its cruelty and incredible violence. There are elements of suspense and real horror. All dinosaurs have individual movement and attack patterns, and live in unique environments.
