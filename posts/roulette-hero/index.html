Game Description#

Roulette Hero is a project in which a squad of adorable animals confronts ruthless mechanical monsters, and the outcome of the battles is decided by the rotation of the roulette. Gamers will have to form a team of various animals, each of which has unique abilities. Some companions are distinguished by their attack speed, while others create impenetrable defense. The thoughtful placement of fighters on the cells of the roulette wheel and its subsequent launch activate their skills, creating powerful combinations and synergies that can turn the tide of even the most difficult fight.

Each round of confrontation with robots consists of eight moves, during which the difficulty steadily increases. To strengthen the squad, special cartridges are used that provide powerful improvements, and Augmentation Points. Roulette Hero features more than a hundred unique units belonging to 13 species of animals and over 100 different abilities, which opens up scope for tactical experimentation. The gameplay offers three modes, including fast runs, difficult challenges and endless waves of opponents, and the total number of levels at the start reaches 20.

Strategy in a whirlwind of chance:

Assembling a squad of animals, such as fast hares or hardy bears. Using more than 100 unique units and 90 enhancing items. The use of special mechanics, for example, bonus spins for collecting four or more animals of the same species. Confrontation with mechanical enemies, requiring constant adaptation of tactics. Choose from two starting characters with different playstyles.

Despite the element of chance introduced by roulette, success depends on proper planning and the ability to create effective combinations between fighters. The project offers not only a single-player campaign, but also an asynchronous multiplayer mode with global leaderboards, where you can compete with the results of other participants. Steam Workshop support and a creative mode allow the community to create their own content, expanding and enriching the original concept.
