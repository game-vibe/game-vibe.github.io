Game Description#

New Cycle is a dark city building sim and colony management simulator. This project is made in a post-apocalyptic setting. It takes place in the near future, where people are divided into tribes. In the story, after a powerful solar flare, the planet plunged into chaos. The player is offered to create a new civilization on the ruins of the old one and restore the unity of humanity. The user leads a group of survivors. He needs to transform their humble refuge into a thriving metropolis. Changing nature will hinder the achievement of goals. Due to problems with the atmosphere on Earth, destructive disasters began to occur, and seasonal cycles became very unstable.

The change of seasons visually transforms the map. For example: in winter, the location is covered with snow. The weather is changeable. In the New Cycle game, you need to grow food, extract minerals, create production chains, trade with other settlements, and research technologies. The main emphasis in the gameplay is on the development of industry. It is necessary to build many different factories here. The construction system allows you to freely place objects. If desired, you can divide the settlement into grids. The main resource in this video game is the villagers. The gamer will have to satisfy the needs of the population, monitoring morale, the number of workers and their efficiency. If this is not done, the townspeople will revolt. The user can define social rules for them.
