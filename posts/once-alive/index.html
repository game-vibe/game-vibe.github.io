Game Description#

Once Alive takes gamers into a world where survival is the ultimate goal and every step forward is fraught with mystery. Set in a post-apocalyptic setting, it puts you in the shoes of James and his brother as they navigate the effects of a global viral outbreak. The duo believe they are the last remnants of humanity, isolated in a desert landscape. Their journey takes them to Houseville, a settlement that promises to find other survivors, but what they find when they arrive is far from what they expected.

Instead of a welcoming community in Once Alive, they are greeted by an unsettling silence and the remnants of a life that once existed. As James and his brother explore the village, the story of HaustVille unfolds not through lengthy dialogue, but through an ecological narrative. Abandoned houses, hastily left behind items and subtle clues scattered throughout the area paint a grim picture of what could have happened. The absence of people is perhaps the most disturbing detail, leaving users to unravel the mystery of their disappearance.

The adventure cleverly uses these scattered remains to invite players into a world that feels alive even in its emptiness, with each object hinting at a larger plot. One of the main challenges is to navigate the dangerous wilderness surrounding the village. The virus that wiped out humanity has had a profound effect on the animal population, turning them into more aggressive and unpredictable creatures.
