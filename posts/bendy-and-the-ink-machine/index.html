Game Description#

Despite the fact that Bendy and the Ink Machine is a horror game with quite interesting graphics that keep you in the atmosphere even after beating the game, the developers manage to create a plot and a general feeling of fear through non-standard decisions regarding scares. We play as Henry Stein, who used to work as a cartoonist. One day he receives an invitation to the studio where he has been working for the last few years. The owner of the studio, his best friend, Joey Drew, is always happy to see his friend and talented artist.

When we arrive at the studio, we see the corpse of one of the cartoon characters on the floor. He is covered in ink, like everything around us. It even drips from the ceiling. Then Henry decides to start the ink machine to find out what is happening, and here we begin to look for the first objects. In the first-person horror game - Bendy and the Ink Machine - there are many interesting puzzles and logical tasks, which the developers focused on, despite the well-thought-out 5 chapters of the plot. Immediately after we launch the Ink Machine, we see Ink Bendy, also a character from the studio’s cartoon, running towards us.

The player’s main task is to get out of the studio and report it to the local police, but Bendy does not want to release his prisoner, so we begin to play ahead, trying to deceive the antagonist. After a little time, an ax, plunger or syringe falls into our hands, and later we get the most powerful weapon in the game - a metal pipe. With the help of melee weapons, we will be able to kill cartoon characters, while simultaneously moving towards the completion of the plot.
