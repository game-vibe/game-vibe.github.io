Game Description#

Cloudheim is a cooperative action role-playing game where the main task for a group of up to 4 adventurers is the revival of lands destroyed during Ragnarok. The physical combat system allows you to throw opponents into the air for long combos. A completely destructible environment becomes not just decoration, but a tactical tool for inflicting additional damage on enemies. The process of creating equipment and weapons is integrated directly into the gameplay, allowing you to craft items without having to stop at the menu. You can go it alone or team up with teammates to unleash devastating team attacks.

The central base for the heroes is Odin Shell, a flying fortress on the back of a giant turtle endowed with the power of a dead god. This sanctuary can be expanded and personalized by setting up a workshop and even running your own store. It is from aboard this citadel that accesses the world of Cloudheim, consisting of many floating islands and volatile dungeons. Exploring these locations is rewarded with valuable loot, crafting resources, and encounters with powerful enemies that require coordination and skill to defeat.

Events unfold in a reality that has experienced a global cataclysm, and now the gods have chosen heroes to restore harmony in a fractured world. Colorful creatures will help the protagonists on this difficult journey.

Allied creatures:

Pancakes are resourceful creatures that provide assistance in various endeavors. The Gnasher is a creature with an insatiable appetite that plays its part in the adventure. Swordcupine is a prickly but loyal companion, ready to follow the heroes.
