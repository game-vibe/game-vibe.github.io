Game Description#

The Rainman takes place in the small town of Crystalpine Grove, where tranquility once reigned. However, the city has recently been gripped by a series of chilling murders, all of which point to a mysterious killer known only as Rain Man. He attacks when it rains, hiding in the shadows and leaving a trail of violence and fear. Local authorities are baffled, and residents are left wondering who the next victim will be. You step into the shoes of Susie, a teenage girl working at a diner on the outskirts of town.

What starts out as a normal day at the diner soon takes a dark turn when you begin to uncover disturbing details about the people around you. It seems like Rain Man might be hiding in plain sight. As you interact with the quirky and shady characters who frequent the diner, you’ll have to be on your guard and watch your back - every choice you make could bring you closer to solving the mystery or sending you to an untimely end.

The Rainman has seven different endings, with branching paths that keep you coming back for more. Will you find the killer? Or will you become the next victim? Every decision, no matter how small, leads to a new result. The dialogue is filled with history, offering clues, red herrings and moments of horror that will keep you guessing. The 1980s setting adds an extra layer of nostalgia and creepiness, while hidden secrets and Easter eggs reward players who dig a little deeper.
