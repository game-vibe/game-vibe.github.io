Game Description#

Beloved Rapture is a custom JRPG that combines modern themes with classic gameplay. Travel through beautiful landscapes, fight turn-based battles and uncover many secrets. This mid-length adventure balances the protagonist’s personal story with the larger threats facing Alexandria. The main focus is on the plot and the interpersonal relationships between the characters. Each hero must go through their own personal trials before facing the bosses in the following dungeons.

Battles take place turn-based with a focus on difficulty and tactics. The player can enjoy a completely original 16-bit soundtrack and pixel art, lovingly created in the spirit of the SNES. No random encounters or forced character leveling - the player can focus on the story and not be distracted by completing side quests. The storyline is mature and mature, exploring themes of trauma, friendship and the LGBT community. The game features six playable characters, each with their own unique characteristics and skills. The player can choose classic or story mode to customize their gaming experience. The main character, Johan, a young man from the provinces, finds himself embroiled in a conflict that goes beyond his understanding. He must find his place in this situation, facing his family traumas, sexuality and his own demons. His allies are the mysterious Aiden, the son of an exiled warrior trained in the mystical arts, and Christa, a fugitive from a noble family seeking to stop the war before it reaches her family.
