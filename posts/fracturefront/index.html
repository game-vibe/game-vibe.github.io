Game Description#

Fracturefront is a third-person fantasy action roguelike in which players must stop the Dimensional Harvester Kaos, who is manipulating rifts to consume entire worlds. This powerful antagonist uses portals to instantly move and drain energy, and battles with him take place in several intense stages. Throughout the battle, he is able to call upon the echoes of fallen worlds, making the confrontation more difficult. The warrior’s ultimate mission is to prevent the collapse of the energy core, for which it is necessary to penetrate desecrated dimensions and destroy ominous altars that drain life forces.

To complete this task, there is a choice of several characters, who are presented not as traditional classes, but as variables in a system on the verge of collapse. Each of them is a unique force capable of bending the laws of reality. Progression in Fracturefront is driven by two mechanics. By collecting fragments of energy cores from different worlds, you can create powerful new equipment. In addition, by harnessing the shrapnel power of destroyed altars, the protagonist creates loyal allies to support him in battle, which adds strategic depth.

Traveling through rifts takes the hero to diverse and dangerous dimensions, where each foray is a risky battle for survival. Among the locations available for exploration are scorched deserts, gloomy forests, abandoned ruins and a cruel mechanical metropolis. You will have to confront constantly evolving opponents and colossal giant bosses. Enemies range from dancing zombies and a music-obsessed fat man to a fierce biker chick and a variety of other bizarre but deadly monsters.
