Game Description#

Fantasy Monster Hunt is a fast-paced action game where the main goal is to hold off countless enemies over short session matches. The character development system is based on gaining experience points for each defeated enemy. Reaching a new level opens access to power-up cards, which can significantly strengthen the combat capabilities of the selected warrior. What gives depth to the tactical process is the interaction between different abilities. For example, activating the Energy Sense skill can provoke a chain reaction of magical waves. A successful confrontation requires an analysis of opponents, because each type of monster has its own vulnerabilities, the use of which is the key to effectively eliminating the threat.

Fantasy Monster Hunt takes place on the battlefield, where the heroes must repel the attacks of incredibly huge hordes of monsters. Each session lasts 15 minutes, making the project ideal for short but intense scrums. During battles, enemies drop powerful relics that directly affect the protagonist’s firepower. To prevent a breakthrough of the defensive line, the gamer must:

wisely select a hero to suit your playing style. strategically use acquired skills in battle. Use dropped artifacts in a timely manner to gain power.
