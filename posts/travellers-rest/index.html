Game Description#

Travellers Rest gives the opportunity to live the life of an innkeeper. You own a small tavern that you dream to turn into a noisy and crowded place in the district. But to achieve this goal, you need to work a lot. Build a bro -up and cook the most delicious beer. Manage the farm and serve only the best products on the table. Satisfy customer requests and gain popularity so that a whole kingdom speaks of your institution!

Having opened your own tavern and providing overnight travelers, you will cook beer, engage in winemaking and provide only the highest quality alcohol. By increasing the level, new opportunities and development branches of your business will open. The Travellers Rest skills tree consists of 6 development branches: nature, construction, cooking, brewing, communication and magic.

The tavern should have a presentable look, so buy yourself with furniture, cosmetics, jewelry and provide a wide variety of entertainment for guests. By increasing the reputation, you will earn reputation points that allow you to reach a new level and unlock many opportunities.

To create unique dishes, explore the world around him, make transactions with merchants and control the farm. The more famous the tavern becomes, the more visitors will want to come to you and taste delicious dishes. Be prepared to accept a huge number of guests, among which may be [justify] [/justify] and the royal person!
