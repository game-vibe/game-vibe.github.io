Game Description#

The adventures in The Wreck follow the life of a failed screenwriter named Junon as she tries to cope with the challenges of adulthood. Players will follow a 36-year-old woman who experiences a turning point that derails her aspirations, career, and personal life. The game is a 3D visual novel that will showcase a melancholy story about sisterhood, motherhood, grief and survival in a cruel world.

The storyline in The Wreck will develop gradually through episodes that immerse the protagonist in the past. The girl faces a difficult situation when her mother is admitted to the hospital with a critical illness. Users will have to experience and feel the grief of the heroine, who begins to remember various episodes from her past, plunging into deep self-reflection. Users will be able to direct the development of the story by making choices in certain situations.

In the three-dimensional novel, you will often watch interactive cut scenes, where players need to select the desired actions on behalf of the protagonist. Junon will deal with his own psychological trauma, trying to achieve peace and harmony alone with himself. During the passage, gamers will go through story chapters and influence the girl’s perception, as well as the ending and the events that take place, in order to lift a suffering person out of depression.
