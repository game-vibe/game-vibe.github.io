Game Description#

Heaven’s Vault is an adventure game that follows the adventures of a female archaeologist. She explores an ancient cosmic nebula. The game features a bright hand-drawn design and a huge number of challenging puzzles. At the center of the story is Alia Elasra. She devoted her entire life to the study of the Nebula. She is helped by her faithful assistant - the robot Six. Together they try to uncover the secrets of the ancient past. One day, a roboticist from the University of Iox goes missing. While searching for him, the protagonist comes to a series of incredible discoveries, the most important of which is the secret of the Heavenly Refuge.

The main gameplay of Heaven’s Vault is the election system. The game remembers all your actions and builds an individual narrative. The further development of the story, the relationships between the characters and the ending depend on moral choices. Several different endings have been prepared, and to see them all, you will need to perform a variety of actions in certain chapters. The puzzles are based on finding objects, combining them, solving ancient chronicles and deciphering hieroglyphs. Puzzles also have a narrative element. If you decipher the letters incorrectly, the plot can take you in a completely different direction and lead you astray.
