Game Description#

Midnight Special is a survival horror with point-and-click elements that invites users into a world where every corner hides a secret. Set in the late 1980s on the isolated Boyd estate, gamers take on the role of Sarah, a nanny whose night shift quickly turns into a nightmarish ordeal. What starts as a simple evening of babysitting two children turns into a creepy game of survival when strange events unfold in the house. The atmosphere is tense as Sarah faces disturbing phone calls, unsettling sounds and the constant feeling that something is lurking over the horizon.

The game draws heavily on classic horror, combining elements of giallo films, slasher films, and sci-fi narratives from the 70s and 80s. These influences are reflected not only in the story, but also in the gameplay itself, which relies on nostalgic 90s point-and-click adventure mechanics. Every step takes you deeper into a world where nothing can be trusted. The title doesn’t rely on typical jump scares, but instead builds tension through environmental and psychological tension. Every detail, from the strange smells permeating through the floorboards to the strange behavior of the plastic dinosaur, makes users doubt the reality. What sets Midnight Special apart is its ability to maintain a balance between the creepy and the mysterious.
