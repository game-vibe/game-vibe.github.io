Game Description#

Fuzz Dungeon is an arcade adventure that combines elements of role-playing games, platformers, puzzles and many other genres. The plot begins when a sexy sasquatch amulet appears in the world, absorbing all living things. The main character will have to overcome 15 absurd levels to return everything to normal.

As the adventure progresses, the protagonist realizes that he can create a new society in which he no longer needs to work. The character is a cross between a witch, a rat and a dog. Each of the 15 stages has its own unique style. It can be either a 2D arcade game or a 3D platformer. You have to move between two worlds: real and fictional. Key features of Fuzz Dungeon:

15 unique levels with individual mechanics; musical score composed by Chris Parrello and Lobby Hotel; more than 90 minutes to complete the campaign story; replayability, allowing you to clear the level to improve the result.

Depending on the stage, different goals are set: you will need to shoot down floating heads, collect various stones, overcome obstacles, dodge asteroids in space, and much more. As a result, you need to get to the Fuzz Dungeon, where the whole story will come to an end and the fate of the world will be decided. Will the universe become devoid of bad ideas?
