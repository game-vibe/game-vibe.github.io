Game Description#

Immersion in Brutal Orchestra allows users to venture into a brutal world filled with insidious demons and grotesque monsters. The game is designed as a turn-based strategy with roguelike elements and deck building. Players will be exploring levels, searching for ancient artifacts and fighting treacherous monsters. You have to make deals with the devil and try to find a way out to save your own soul.

The protagonist in Hieronymus Bosch’s Brutal Orchestra dies and finds himself in purgatory, where he must wander through mysterious locations and cope with difficult challenges. The main task becomes revenge on the attacker who took Boschi’s life. You have to move through a wretched and dangerous world, where you can take revenge on crazy creatures and die with dignity. Each map is randomly generated, creating variety during playthrough or replay.

Gamers will explore a landfill in endless deserts, where in the distance there are peaks covered with eternal storms. You have to go forward through levels where there are monsters and deformed creatures that are aggressively opposed to the hero. It is necessary to build a deck of character abilities in order to alternately play attacking or defensive properties in battles in order to win the battle. Battles are filled with randomness and allow you to dynamically adapt to your opponents during the skirmish.
