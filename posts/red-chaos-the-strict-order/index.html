Game Description#

In Red Chaos - The Strict Order, a conflict over the future of a post-apocalyptic world erupts. Power in the oasis known as the Unsullied Garden was seized by the militaristic Iron Hand group, which established totalitarian control over the survivors. They are opposed by the Resistance, which unites former military personnel and dissatisfied citizens. Their mission is to overthrow a dictatorship by relying on guerrilla tactics to counterbalance the technological might of the enemy. The confrontation is built on the contrast between a well-equipped army and resourceful rebels.

Having taken command of one of the sides, gamers are immersed in the classic strategic gameplay of Red Chaos - The Strict Order. The basis is the construction of operational bases, the collection of valuable resources and the command of various troops in large-scale battles. Battles are fought using ground and air equipment, which can be improved, as well as the use of unique abilities and powerful superweapons to gain a decisive advantage. Strategic depth allows you to choose between blind defense of your fortress and rapid offensive operations to seize the initiative.

To master the basics, the project provides a series of missions that allow you to gradually learn the mechanics and unlock access to new combat units and buildings. In Skirmish mode, you can hone your skills in battles against artificial intelligence. The AI ​​has flexible settings: 4 difficulty levels, choice of behavior model and the ability to introduce restrictions, for example, allowing only aviation. The main test for strategists will be online battles, where you can compete with other players for high places in the rating table.
