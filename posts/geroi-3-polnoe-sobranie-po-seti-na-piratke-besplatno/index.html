Game Description#

Heroes 3 (Complete Collection) is the ultimate release of the cult turn-based RPG, incorporating the original part Heroes of Might and Magic III: The Restoration of Erathia, as well as two extensive addons - Armageddon’s Blade and The Shadow of Death. The pirate Heroes of Might and Magic III: Rebirth of Erathia can be played online for free. Allows you to go through the campaign with a friend or confrontation over the Internet online. You can fight locally. Includes free online play, single player campaign and map editor.

Heroes III takes place in a fantasy world. All heroes have unique abilities and characteristics. It is necessary to gather troops to travel across the continent. The combat is presented in a turn-based mode, where the player’s team must fight the enemy army. Heroes 3 (The Complete Collection) introduces an underground level and the Grail artifact for the first time for the series. Multiplayer or solo adventure allows you to capture cities and visit the various buildings available on the map.

By leveling up your characters, you can become stronger in melee or ranged combat, as well as discover new magical abilities. One move is equal to a day. By completing his turn, the player closes one day. Every day you can build a building and take a certain number of steps with your hero. You need to collect resources and artifacts to build buildings or upgrade characters. The resources are mercury, sulfur, crystals, gems and gold.

The map contains the Tree of Knowledge, the Creature Storage Monolith, and Pandora’s Box. Artifacts are divided into several types: treasures, small, large and relics. Each character has basic parameters such as attack, defense, magic and knowledge, as well as various secondary abilities that are upgraded to the third level: basic, advanced and expert. Magic is divided into 4 schools: earth, air, water and fire. Cities are represented by such buildings as the Castle, Stronghold, Tower, Inferno, Necropolis, Dungeon, Citadel and Fortress.
