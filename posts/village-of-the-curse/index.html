Game Description#

Village of the Curse is a depressing first-person horror game that puts you in the shoes of a journalist investigating the dark secrets of an isolated settlement. The hero will have to survive in an atmosphere of constant fear, because unpredictable and ruthless creatures are hiding in the shadows. Every meeting with these distorted entities becomes a test of strength. Exploring eerie locations, including an abandoned church, a witch’s house and a haunted cemetery, is fraught with mortal danger. Every corner of this cursed place is filled not only with secrets, but also with horror, ready to engulf any unwary adventurer.

Elements of grim investigative journalism:

Carefully study ominous locations in search of clues. Avoiding collisions with terrible creatures of darkness. Collecting scattered records to reconstruct the chronicle of events. Searching for the necessary items to carry out the decisive ceremony.

The tragic history of the village is gradually revealed through the collection of scattered notes, diaries and cursed relics. It is these objects that shed light on the events that took place here. The search for artifacts in Village of the Curse directly leads the protagonist to the final ritual, on which his future fate depends. Ultimately, the daredevil is faced with a choice: to get to the bottom of the truth hidden under the cover of darkness, or to forever become another victim and an integral part of the local curse.
