Game Description#

Duil is a side-scrolling action platformer. The player takes control of Duil, a mysterious knight. The mysterious and hostile world hides multiple secrets and surprises. The central character will face monsters, bosses and minions of the dark lord. The protagonist will fight, get new equipment and upgrade his own skills.

The plot begins with an army of evil spirits invading the Kingdom of Targat. Commander-in-Chief Vezruvil decides to attack peaceful lands and enslave them. The main character’s family was killed and thrown to the dogs. The character himself was sent to the dungeon as a prisoner, where he had to go through multiple sufferings. By chance, he is freed from his shackles, which helps him escape from prison and set out on the path of revenge.

The user travels through a two-dimensional world, chooses roads and performs various actions, which will affect the further fate of the plot. The gameplay features several endings. Along the way you will have to fight in fierce and dynamic battles with a wide variety of enemies. The skill tree helps you upgrade your techniques and distinctive abilities. Levels are generated randomly and also have several exits, hidden places and treasuries with useful items. The hero has a variety of weapons, magical abilities and options to choose from to help in battles.
