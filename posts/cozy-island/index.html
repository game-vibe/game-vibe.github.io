Game Description#

Cozy Island is set in a world full of surprises where gamers will have to explore and build. The main activity is traveling around the open-world island to collect resources and build a personal base. You can wander without a specific direction, discovering different locations at your own pace. Toys and furniture are scattered around the area, serving as the main materials for decorating the designated secret area. In addition, the island is inhabited by various animals, you can find them and bring them to your home.

Cozy Island’s core gameplay mechanics focus on resource extraction through farming. Gamers can plant and grow a variety of vegetables. Harvesting yields coins, the currency needed to run the gacha machine, which gives access to additional items. This machine gives out surprises that expand the possibilities for personalizing the database. The world also contains several mini-games that offer alternative activities to the main exploration and construction cycle. These elements bring structured goals to the free-flowing sandbox process.

The project supports both single and co-op modes, allowing friends to team up online. The experience is built around a physical sandbox that encourages experimentation with object placement and interaction. The design encourages a leisurely pace without imposing urgent tasks or time constraints. All actions and movement through locations are presented with a top view. The focus is on discovery, collecting and personal space within the island setting.
