Game Description#

Wanderburg is an open-world roguelike that places gamers at the center of a brutal ecosystem where entire castles on wheels move across procedurally generated lands. Each such stronghold is a predator, preying on weaker competitors and absorbing everything in its path for its own strengthening. The setting is filled with anachronistic technology, such as knights on motorcycles and steam-powered siege engines, which creates a unique atmosphere of the absurd Middle Ages. The main task becomes survival and constantly increasing the power of your mobile fortress, in order to eventually become the dominant force in this unusual world.

The development process is directly related to combat successes and exploration of locations. In Wanderburg, progression is made in the spirit of survivor projects, where victories over opponents allow you to improve the current configuration of the fortress. Driving vehicles with realistic physics requires mastering maneuvers to evade enemy attacks and take advantageous positions. To achieve dominance, you will need to perform a number of key actions:

equip the citadel with a variety of modules, including flamethrowers, cannons and wizard towers. destroy powerful bosses to subsequently expand your fortress. Travel through different biomes in search of resources and new goals. skillfully maneuver in battle using the features of the physical control model.
