Game Description#

FlyKnight is an old-school first-person Dungeon Crawler that places you in a world twisted by the curse of a powerful witch named Lunamotha. Your task as the squire of Flyknights is to break the witch’s spell, because of which the inhabitants of this world cannot grow wings. Early on, you find yourself separated from your comrades after being ambushed, but the mission remains clear: stop Lunamoth. Whether you choose to travel alone or team up with others, the journey will be filled with danger and potential.

Armed with a wide arsenal of weapons, you must adapt to any challenge. The main thing is to choose the right tool. For example, a swift blow can disarm an enemy, and a well-placed blow to the head can leave him unconscious. Choosing the right approach to every combat encounter is important and can mean the difference between life and death. Whether you’re hacking away with melee weapons or attacking from afar with a bow and arrow, every fight requires tactical thinking.

The title features hand-crafted environments filled with side paths that hide both loot and deadly threats. As you move from checkpoint to checkpoint, you’ll have to sharpen your skills to survive, especially when you encounter bosses that will haunt you. You can also team up with friends to play co-op to defeat the forces of Lunamoth together. Sharing items, strategizing, and using gestures to communicate add an extra layer of depth.
