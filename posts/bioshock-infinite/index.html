Game Description#

BioShock Infinite is a first-person shooter with multiple mechanics of a role-playing adventure in the style of science fiction and steampunk. The game will take place in 1912 in an alternative universe, in the flying city of the United States of America - Columbia. You need to control the private detective Booker Devitt, who has a task to find a cute girl named Elizabeth. Immediately after it is found, the main characters find themselves in the very epicenter of the war. The opposing sides will be the city authorities and the rebels, who consist of workers in Colombia. In addition to Booker and Elizabeth, the plot of Bioshock 3 includes: Zachary Hale Comstock is the antagonist of the game, who established his own order in the city, which many residents did not like. Daisy Fitzroy is the leader of the rebels from the Voice of the People organization. Gives the airship to the main character in exchange for some services. Cornelius Slate is Booker’s former colleague in the American army. Comstock fell into disgrace for organizing uprisings. Jeremiah Fink is the founder of a large manufactory, a very rich and influential man. The nightingale is a bird reaching 9 meters in height, Elizabeth’s guardian.

The gameplay includes shootouts on the streets of Columbia, using two weapons that Booker can carry with him (there are more types of firearms, but you need to choose wisely, according to your play style), the use of time gaps that the protagonist’s companion can create and studying the locations and history of the city using voice recordings and notes. The hero also has magical abilities that require a supply of mana to use in battle. Skills will help you control cars, other people, or shoot lightning at opponents.
