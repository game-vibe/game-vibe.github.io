Game Description#

All in Abyss: Judge the Fake introduces us to Asuha Senahara, a self-proclaimed gambling genius who seeks dominance in a special area where gambling rules. After challenging the ruling Witch of the gambling district, Asuha suffers her first defeat due to a mysterious power and falls into despair. Determined to take revenge, she begins an adventure of high-stakes poker, uncovering fraud, and amassing wealth. Her journey involves challenging her opponents to duels to the death, where either she or the Witches will die.

The main gameplay in All in Abyss: Judge the Fake is centered around one-on-one poker duels, Texas Hold’em style, where the goal is to wipe out your opponent’s chips. Asuha uses special skills related to her gambling talent to influence the course of fights by limiting her opponent’s actions or revealing hidden information. Success often depends on the strategic use of the All-In command, placing all available chips on the line at a crucial moment after taking an advantageous position. These mechanics expand the player’s decision-making capabilities beyond standard poker.

Players navigate the City, gathering information needed to uncover the secrets of the Witches' scam. In each chapter, gamers will have to fight a boss Witch, such as Ulu Amamino with her mysterious powers, the motherly but insane Yuragi Tenraku, or Lancille Kareido, the correctional officer. Uncovering their specific machinations is critical before the clash in the Colosseum. Exploring every corner of the city helps you find the right clues for these decisive confrontations.

In the City, there are opportunities to beat local gamblers for money or take on Poker Royale challenges to earn points for new skills and items. Winnings can be spent in the ALL OUT club, which can provide a luck bonus for future battles. This preparation and gathering of resources serves Asuha’s main goal in this game: to challenge the Witches, uncover their deception, and achieve victory through skill and investigation.
