Game Description#

BOOK OF HOURS is a role-playing game with point-n-click elements, the events of which take place in an occult library. The ancient building is located right on the seashore. Sitting within its walls you can hear the sound of the surf and the rain hitting the roof. For five centuries, the Hush House Library has been a center of knowledge.

But one day there was a terrible fire. Many books burned, and the librarian himself died under a huge rubble of shelves with books. The main character has unusual abilities - he is the only one who can restore the lost collection. He will have to acquire occult books, study them and unravel the mysteries of the world around him. By gradually restoring the lost books, the castle will be cleansed of destruction and the ancient curse placed on the walls of the fortress.

The game has a flexible indexing and cataloging system. It is necessary to collect a series of books, arrange the volumes in the right order and create a library of books that will be proud of more than one generation of readers. However, in BOOK OF HOURS you need to protect the library from fires, storms, theft and attacks from supernatural creatures. The library will be visited by various guests. They need help with knowledge: how to carry out tomb robberies; how to sabotage other people’s intrigues, how you can convince a person to stay at home with the help of poetry and much more.
