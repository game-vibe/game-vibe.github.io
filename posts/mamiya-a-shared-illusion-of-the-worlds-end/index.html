Game Description#

MAMIYA A Shared Illusion of the World’s End takes you to a post-apocalyptic world in which a small group of surviving guys find themselves. The universe is dying due to a strange and unexplored anomaly that is affecting humanity on the surface of the planet. The guys have to survive and look for ways to cope with problems that are caused by existential fears about the future and their demise. While wandering, they meet a mysterious boy named Mamiya, who is trying to help people out of a crisis. The protagonist will guide and reassure the survivors, referring to the fact that death is not so terrible and you need to be able to accept it.

Going into World Downfall Our Imaginary “Mamiya” players can expect:

A touching and dark story telling about a dying world, where people have no hope of salvation and are forced to wander through cities and say goodbye to life. Atmospheric personal experiences that touch on the philosophy and existential torment of each of the protagonists. Personal stories from the heroes that will tell about their past life and what they experience after the global upheaval. Routine observation of confused heroes who have no chance of survival. They have to come to terms with the finitude of existence and prepare for the final journey. The adventures of the mysterious Mamiya, who will provide moral support for others, trying to return them to a different path of understanding death.
