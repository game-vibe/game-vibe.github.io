Game Description#

Snowfall’s Mystery plunges into the mind of a student computer programmer named Elliot, who encounters a mysterious program during the cold winter of 1999. An ordinary floppy disk received from a friend becomes the source of inexplicable events when the Snowfall messenger contained on it begins a dialogue. The program demonstrates a frightening awareness of the hero’s inner world, and the mysterious Administrator gets in touch, pursuing unknown goals. The protagonist will have to figure out what is happening, coping with long-standing mental disorders in the atmosphere of technological melancholy of the late 20th century.

Dreaming of creating a robot assistant to combat loneliness, Elliot finds himself drawn into a philosophical journey. The narrative of Snowfall’s Mystery presents gamers with difficult moral dilemmas, asking them to make choices at key points. Each decision made directly affects the development of the plot, leading to one of two possible endings. This psychological adventure is designed in a minimalist two-color pixel style, enhancing the sense of anxiety and mystery that envelops the events happening to the character. There is additional depth hidden beyond the main plot.

Archive of forgotten memories and evidence:

A cassette demo of a song called Constellation. Transcript of a news broadcast about mysterious events. Autumn video made by Elliot himself. Photo of a character named Charlotte. A medical document that sheds light on the hero’s condition. Personnel file of an employee named Irwin. Fragments of correspondence between characters in another messenger.
