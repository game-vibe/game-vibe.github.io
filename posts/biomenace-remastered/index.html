Game Description#

BioMenace Remastered is a reimagining of the classic 2D platformer, the gameplay of which is based on dynamic shootouts and exploration of multi-level locations. The storyline takes place in the metropolis of Metro City, which was attacked by mutated creatures. The central figure of the story is secret operative Snake Logan, whose intelligence mission fails. After a plane crash, he must single-handedly confront hordes of monsters and find the source of their appearance. The protagonist needs to stop the evil scientist Dr. Mangle, saving survivors along the way. The adventure is divided into 3 original episodes, complemented by a completely new fourth chapter with unique content.

The updated version offers significantly improved gameplay and many technical improvements. The visual component has been redesigned to include support for widescreen screens, and the interface has become more modern. An original soundtrack from Bobby Prince accompanies the passage of BioMenace Remastered, creating an authentic atmosphere. In addition, the re-release introduces a number of qualitative improvements, allowing:

use a gamepad for more comfortable control. save progress at any point in the level thanks to quick saves. Instantly switch between original and updated graphics. appreciate smooth scrolling and background parallax effects.
