Game Description#

Earth vs Mars is a turn-based tactical struggle for the survival of the planet, where bizarre genetic engineering becomes humanity’s main trump card. To repel the alien threat, earthly commanders use not only standard army units, but also unique hybrid super-soldiers. The creation of these creatures occurs using a special Splice-O-Tron device, which allows volunteers to be crossed with representatives of the fauna. As a result of such experiments, swift cheetah flies appear on the battlefield, capable of spraying acid on the enemy from the air, or crushing human-rhino hybrids that break through enemy defenses.

This unusual army will be confronted by high-tech forces of invaders. The Martians use flying saucers, gravity tanks, and elite warriors who have spent years perfecting their martial arts in battle. Battles take place on a variety of maps, where tactical advantage is achieved through the intelligent use of each unit’s unique abilities and terrain features. As the conflict in Earth vs Mars progresses, the enemy also evolves, bringing new threats into the fray, including a living organic weapon known as The Creep.

The story campaign offers over 30 hand-crafted missions to complete, revealing the story of the invasion. The Martian aggression is explained by their long-standing interest in terrestrial life forms, whose atomic extract they used for their own purposes long before the start of a full-scale war. In addition to the single-player mode, the project provides the opportunity to fight with other commanders in online battles of the 1 on 1 format, where you can lead both the forces of Earth and the army of Martian invaders. A mode against artificial intelligence and an editor for creating custom maps are also available.
