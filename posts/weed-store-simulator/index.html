Game Description#

Weed Store Simulator is an economic strategy that asks you to build a criminal empire under the guise of a legal store. The entire gameplay is built on a risky balance between the world of law and underground activities. To survive and thrive, you must build complex relationships with various factions, including the police, mafia and street gangs. Any wrong step can lead to disaster: problems with law enforcement agencies threaten raids and business closures, and refusal to cooperate with the mafia will inevitably lead to supply disruptions. Ignoring local gangs will make the city streets deadly for an entrepreneur.

The basis of a double life in Weed Store Simulator is managing a cannabis store. Here, the entire production cycle is under full control: from the cultivation of plants and their subsequent sorting by quality to drying and final packaging of the finished product. However, for those who strive for really big money, this will not be enough. Behind the closed doors of a legal shop, a much larger and more dangerous enterprise is unfolding - the sterile and systematic production of white powder and blue crystals. It is these underground activities that provide access to windfall profits, but require courage and strategic thinking to manage risks.
