Game Description#

Once upon a Dungeon 2 is a continuation of the indie roguelike with new elements and mechanics in the gameplay. The developer took into account many errors in the creation of the first part, changing most of the process in this project. The user will play the role of a powerful fighter who will have to travel through dark dungeons and fight cold-blooded creatures.

As the game progresses, the main character obtains equipment and consumables. The combat system is hardcore and requires the gamer to have certain skills and strategy. In battles you will need to use abilities, block attacks or dodge them. The environment is made with a gloomy atmosphere and dark places to explore. Gradually, the character gains physical strength and learns new spells or melee techniques. Enemies are divided into 3 classes: regular, champions and bosses, and their locations are generated randomly.

Found materials and fragments are necessary to create rare and unique items. The user has a task - to get to the Fallen, the antagonist of the game, and destroy him. Before meeting the main boss, you will have to go through hundreds of deadly traps and opponents. Turn-based battles involve only the protagonist and several opponents. All levels are procedurally generated, and the items found can be traded. The user is able to complete minor quests and receive rewards.
