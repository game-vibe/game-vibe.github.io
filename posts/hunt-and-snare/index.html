Game Description#

Hunt and Snare is an open-world action-RPG with extensive sexual content for fans of furries - anthropomorphic creatures with animal-like body shapes and fur. Thanks to the large number of options for interacting with the environment on the Skig archipelago, this game has its own fan base and is constantly updated by the developers. In addition to erotic fantasies, the project also raises darker themes, for example, the war of the enslaved population against invaders led by a dragon, poverty and frequent outbreaks of unknown diseases that lead to epidemics.

The gameplay includes many different mechanics that make your stay in the virtual universe varied and unforgettable. Taking a rifle with him, the main character (he is created by the gamer in the character editor, where you can customize any part of the body) goes on a hunt for both targets for sexual pleasure and enemies who patrol the territory. Travel by sea takes place with the help of a frigate called “Nameless”, owned by the protagonist. It is necessary to recruit crew members to jointly participate in adventures both in the water and on land. There will be enemy ships on the water that you can board and seize the valuables stored on these ships. Each weapon used by the hero can be modified to improve accuracy or damage.

In addition to the main story campaign, gamers are offered the “Showroom” mode. In it you can choose the object of sexual fantasies for intimate pleasures, which occur without the process of capturing the target in the vastness of the archipelago. You can use a ready-made furry or create your own image. Also, there is a list of fetishes in which you can disable items that are not of interest.
