Game Description#

Spirit Island is a strategy game made in the style of a board game, in which you need to become the spirits of the earth and protect the island from colonizers who are sowing plague and destruction. The spirit interacts with the native islanders, helping to increase your strength to fight the foreigners. There is a place of magic in the world of Spirit Island. The countries of Europe are trying to expand their influence to distant lands, but the ancient gods are ready to thwart the plans of strangers.

You can play either alone or in co-op. The action takes place in an alternate world in 1700. Each protagonist has his own individual trait and certain elemental properties. You can choose any of them. Your characters will cooperate with the local Dhan, with the help of which they increase their power and confront their opponents. The joint campaign will allow up to 4 people to complete the story. Game Features:

8 different protagonists with unique characteristics; 36 different skills that can enhance skills; 22 basic abilities, with the ability to increase effects; 4 detailed large continents; 3 opposing factions: Brandenburg-Prussia, England and Sweden; 2 Fel cards and 10+ units of Fear available; 15 maps to control the Invader expansion.
