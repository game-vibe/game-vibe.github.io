Game Description#

Bloody Knuckles Street Boxing challenges those looking for shallow arcade-style fights, focusing solely on skill. The project deliberately creates a deep gap in the level of training of fighters, abandoning any auxiliary systems. There is no room for easy victories here, because every aspect of the battle is under complete control. Thanks to pressure-sensitive controls, the fighter becomes an accurate reflection of the gamer’s actions, requiring tactical understanding and lightning-fast reactions. This approach turns every fight into an intense test of boxing intelligence, where success depends only on personal skill.

On the streets where the events of Bloody Knuckles Street Boxing unfold, the arsenal of techniques includes over 30 variations of strikes with the ability to manually aim at the target. The key to victory is competent defense, built on timely body dodges and parries, opening windows for crushing counterattacks. The gameplay allows you to change your stance in real time, adapting to your opponent’s style and effectively managing distance. To hone your reflexes, there are training modes including AI sparring, shadow boxing and mitt work.

A realistic, physics-based clinch system adds another layer of tactical depth to fights. Each fight can end with a spectacular knockout with ragdoll animation that clearly demonstrates the power of the blow. For those who prefer to fight with friends, local multiplayer mode is available. In addition, the project offers ample opportunities for visual customization of your fighter, allowing you to create a unique athlete before entering the impromptu ring of city blocks.
