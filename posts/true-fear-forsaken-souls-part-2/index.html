Game Description#

True Fear: Forsaken Souls Part 2 is a continuation of the atmospheric horror, piercing each user with secrets and horrors hidden during the passage. The developers have added a non-linear story, rich mysteries, many horrors and unique puzzles.

Holly Stonehouse followed a trail of clues that led her from an abandoned family mansion to Dark Falls Hospital. And now the heroine again experiences the feeling that someone is already waiting for her inside. Now Holly will not just be a spectator, but the shadow will be embodied in something material, and will haunt her until the end of the plot. Face real threats in a hospital that comes to life at night. You will need to explore rooms, find clues, read notes and look at photos. All this is needed to collect the entire sequence of events that occurred in this terrifying place.

Take control of Holly and help her stay alive and find answers to her questions. Guess whether there was a 3rd daughter in the protagonist’s family, and the mother was truly crazy, or did someone set her up? In addition to questions about Holly’s family, it is necessary to unravel the mystery of a mysterious creature that has been pursuing the main character for several months. Move around the open game world and interact with almost all objects. The gamer can use the map to teleport over a long distance, reducing the time of pointless walking. There are more than 10 minutes of cinematic scenes and hundreds of entries that can be entered into the heroine’s personal diary.
