Game Description#

Under the Sand REDUX - a road trip game is an adventure game in which the player is invited to travel around the world by car. The universe around the main character experienced a terrible apocalypse, turning into a desert. Graphically, the project is made using low-detail textures to create its own style. The locations where the gamer is present are created randomly thanks to the use of artificial intelligence in the process of generating territories, which is responsible for randomizing the environment.

The time period used by the authors of this indie game is the early 80s of the last century, which is expressed in the nameless protagonist’s vehicle, destroyed architecture and music cassettes added to the project for collecting and further listening in the car. Due to global warming, which almost completely killed humanity and all its achievements throughout evolution, most places have turned into lifeless deserts with huge sand dunes. The gameplay consists of constantly searching for interesting locations that contain some secrets, as well as improvements for an old pickup truck owned by the main character. The goal of the protagonist’s journey is to fulfill the last wishes of the virtual protégé’s father.
