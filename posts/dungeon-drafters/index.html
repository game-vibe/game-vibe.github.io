Game Description#

Dungeon Drafters is a top-down, turn-based roguelike with an emphasis on tactical combat. The project uses anime style and card game mechanics. An adventurer with red hair is given control. It moves through cells. The indie product has mini-games. For example: fishing. In the title you can unlock new characters.

When confronted with opponents, the combat phase begins, and immediately, without switching to another screen, as in a JRPG. There are also no random encounters. All enemies are immediately visible on the map. In Dungeon Drafters, the heroine receives 5 random cards (200 varieties in total) from her deck each time she enters a duel.

They make it possible to dash, shoot fireballs, teleport, and so on. Magic spells do not cause fixed damage, but random damage within the minimum and maximum range for each specific spell. During the fight, you can simply move around. In one turn, you are allowed to perform up to 3 actions, then it’s your opponent’s turn. Cards remain in the place of killed enemies. They can be picked up after the end of the brawl. Cards are also obtained from chests, but if there are adversaries next to them, you will first have to defeat them all, otherwise the lock will not open. There are 5 types of dungeons available for exploration. Bosses meet there.
