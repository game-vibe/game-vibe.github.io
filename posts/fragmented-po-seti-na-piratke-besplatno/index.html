Game Description#

Fragmented is an open world survivalist that allows you to explore a hostile world inhabited by various creatures and monsters with a friend or alone. You can play pirate for free online, playing in co-op or intense confrontations against other players. Having connected online via the Internet, you need to survive in the vastness of an unknown planet. You need to collect resources to build your own defensive base or create unique equipment to help in battle. Events take place on the planet Rildan.

Local herbs and berries are available for collection. The hero of Fragmented is represented by the parameters of hunger and fatigue. You need to study advanced technologies, tame animals, craft weapons and fight powerful monsters. The free game includes traveling together across the vast expanses of the planet on futuristic transport. The online game features several types of PvP content and a cooperative mode. As you level up and level up, you need to unlock different styles and skills that will help you survive among hostile fauna.

Using the collected resources, you can erect buildings, create supports, containers and much more. Electronic devices consist of turrets, medical scanners, etc. Melee and long-range combat are available. By creating substances, hunger can be reduced. Crafting includes creating traps, torches, weapons, etc. Textiles allow you to create clothes, bags, armor, etc. Powerful bosses pose a huge threat. Battles with them take place due to the fact that you need to find their weak points. There are three difficulty modes: Normal, Normal and Hardcore. The latter is created for desperate players, where even the slightest mistake can cost your life.
