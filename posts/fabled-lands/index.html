Game Description#

Fabled Lands is an old-school RPG adventure. A large open world is presented, available for exploration. The setting immerses you in a fantasy world, in which you have to complete a huge number of main and secondary quests, fight, trade and level up your character. You need to explore an entire continent, taking on the role of a merchant, priest, scientist, thief, magician or soldier.

The accumulated funds allow you to buy ships, townhouses, temples and much more. The plot contains many unexpected twists and intrigues. By completing missions, they can introduce you to future allies and allow you to make enemies for yourself. The Fabled Lands campaign features hundreds of unique missions that will take players across the continent. Contains many forks and presents a system of moral choices. The journey takes place on the continent of Harkuna. Playing as the main character, you have to dive into his past.

Ships allow you to sail between islands, visit mysterious archipelagos, etc. They can be upgraded to effectively fight pirates or survive storms. You can visit mystical temples, collecting treasures and loot. On the way you encounter a wide variety of enemies: robbers, trolls, golems, flying heads, assassins, vampires, kelpies, demons, cultists, undead and others. Permanent death mechanics introduced. If you die once, all progress is lost and the adventure must begin from the beginning.
