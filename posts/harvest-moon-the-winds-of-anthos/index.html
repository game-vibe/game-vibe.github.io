Game Description#

Harvest Moon: The Winds of Anthos invites you to embark on an exciting journey through the enchanting world of Anthos. From the very beginning, you’ll be introduced to the quirky inventor Doc Jr. and a host of other characters who will become your companions on this adventure. What is your task? Revive the dormant Harvest Goddess and the Harvest Sprites, and reunite the scattered villages of Antos.

Antos was once a tranquil kingdom under the watchful eye of the benevolent Goddess of the Harvest and her faithful Sprites. Together they protected the earth from natural disasters, sheltering its inhabitants from hurricanes, earthquakes and other disasters. But the calm was shattered when a catastrophic volcanic eruption shook Antos to its foundations. Desperate to save the land, the Harvest Goddess and her Sprites exhausted their powers, isolating the villages from each other and trapping the unfortunate souls behind the thresholds of their homes. Hope remained in the form of a message in a bottle, sent by the Harvest Goddess herself in search of a savior. Now, a decade later, you have stumbled upon this message. With the help of Doc Jr. and a variety of characters, your mission is clear: awaken the Harvest Goddess, restore ties between villages, and restore Anthos to its former glory.

Farming comes to the fore with the introduction of Doc’s Expando Farm, allowing farming in a variety of environments, from snowy peaks to serene beaches. The barn will be your haven for raising a variety of animals - from ordinary cows and chickens to exotic creatures. Beyond farming, there’s a wide variety of activities on offer, from competing in competitions and festivals to forming romantic connections with five eligible bachelors and bachelorettes. You can even have unique pets like wolves and Bengal tigers, adding a unique twist to farm life. The key to the game is exploration - both on foot and on an unusual mountain. Venture into the mines to find valuable resources and use the Harvest Goddess statues to quickly move around the world. Don’t forget to capture the beauty of Antos in photos and selfies, creating lasting memories in this captivating world.
