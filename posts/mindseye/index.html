Game Description#

MindsEye is a world where the boundaries between reality and illusion are blurred. You step into the role of Jacob Diaz, a man with a dark past that is slowly slipping away from his grasp. With a neural implant that should help him recall memories but instead leads to even more confusion, Jacob must navigate a landscape fraught with danger. The American landscape of the near future is riddled with stories of corporate deceit, political intrigue, and the ever-present shadow of artificial intelligence. As Jacob, you go on secret missions that will take you to the most intense and unpredictable places, trying to piece together pieces of the past that won’t stay put.

As you progress through MindsEye, you will face many challenges, each more difficult than the last. High-speed chases force you to be on the edge, and combat scenarios force you to think quickly and act decisively. And it’s not just about pulling the trigger - every step matters, and every decision you make can move you closer or further from the truth. The world Jacob finds himself in is one where technology advances in ways that make you question what is real and what is constructed. Artificial intelligence is not just a tool, it is a constant, looming presence.
