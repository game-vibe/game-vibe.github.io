Game Description#

Manifest Destiny is a meta-pixel adventure in which the protagonist, as the creator of the virtual world, gains almost unlimited power over it. Instead of the usual weapons for battles and solving puzzles, he uses fundamental development tools. The process of overcoming obstacles is tied to creativity and imagination, allowing you to change the very logic of the environment. The opponents here are not just enemies, but elements of code that can be reprogrammed. The landscape ceases to be a static decoration and turns into a canvas that can be completely rebuilt. This approach to interacting with reality opens up many non-linear paths to move through each stage.

All the character’s actions are monitored by an internal karma system, which records both good deeds and downright destructive actions. Each decision in Manifest Destiny has its own price and significantly affects the development of a branching storyline, where the boundaries between fiction and reality are blurred. The protagonist will have to determine the fate of his creation, choosing the role of a merciful god or an oppressive tyrant.

Protagonist’s creative abilities:

Programming - allows you to change the logic of opponents, activate traps, or completely erase obstacles from the level. Art - gives you the ability to redraw maps, build bridges and deform the terrain to tactically isolate enemies. Music - controls the flow of battle through the background, provoking chaos or increasing the concentration of allies.
