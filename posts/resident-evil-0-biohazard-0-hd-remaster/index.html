Game Description#

Resident Evil 0 / biohazard 0 HD REMASTER – third-person survival horror, re-released with new, higher-quality texture resolution and improved animation and lighting. A “Wesker Mode” with another playable character has also been added. The number zero in the title means that the story told in this part is a prequel to the first part of the legendary series, which spawned many projects in a single universe and created a real fashion for zombies and horror films with elements of survival.

It’s 1998, and the S.T.A.R.S (Special Tactics And Rescue Service) team arrives in the Arklay Mountains, a suburb of Raccoon City, to investigate a series of massacres taking place in the forest. While approaching the landing point, the helicopter catches fire and makes an emergency landing. From this moment strange events begin that do not end until the end of the passage. The squad decides to split up to cover a larger area of ​​the forest. The player takes control of Rebecca Chambers and Billy Cohen.

The gameplay consists of exploring locations for the necessary items to advance through the plot. You can switch between protagonists at any time. It is also possible to send one of them, controlled by artificial intelligence, to explore other locations. If anything happens, he will call the gamer over the radio for help. The main characters differ in abilities and health levels. Rebecca is a medic, she knows how to mix herbs to enhance the healing effect, and Billy is more resilient, has a larger HP scale and has a lighter in his inventory. Zombies moving around the locations available in the game pose a great danger.
