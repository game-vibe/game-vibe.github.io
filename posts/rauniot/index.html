Game Description#

Rauniot is an adventure quest presented in an old-school isometric perspective. Events take place in a post-apocalyptic alternative world of the past. Explore the mysterious lands of Northern Finland. In 1975, a large-scale disaster occurred that dealt a huge blow to civilization. Humanity has never recovered.

Massive conflicts and wars have turned the once powerful human race into mere slaves of nature. The continents have been flooded by the oceans, and the remaining parts of the land are under poisonous waste from nuclear power plants. In such a crazy world, people began to turn into mutants. Women die and men eat their loved ones.

The passage of Rauniot starts in such terrible conditions. Having taken control of the brave Aino, it is necessary to prove to this world that even in the face of the apocalypse, people can remain humane and see people not as enemies, but as friends. Travel through a destroyed world, meet other heroes and join groups to survive and defend together.

Look for food, water, medicine, etc. Try to get electricity and gain new knowledge to help you progress. In order not to be left alone, you need to be sensitive and give yourself completely to the group. Go through many dangerous challenges as you travel through mystical wastelands and cities of a dystopian future.
