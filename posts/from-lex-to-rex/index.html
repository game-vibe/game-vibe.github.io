Game Description#

From Lex to Rex is an atmospheric adventure in a medieval setting, where you take control of a treacherous character named Lex. The hero wants to take a place in an aristocratic family, taking power away from Rex by deception. The gameplay is a point-and-click quest where you interact with interactive objects on the screen to solve intricate puzzles. The game is made in old-school pixel graphics, where you can visit many thematic locations. Gamers in From Lex to Rex have the opportunity to find out the secret of the royal curse.

During Lex’s adventures, users will meet a hysterical cook, a princess and her lost lover, as well as many other colorful and comical minor NPCs. The game immerses you in an atmosphere inspired by “Monkey Island”, “Indiana Jones” and “Hollywood Monsters”. The tasks in the story campaign are filled with humor and ironic situations in which the protagonist finds himself. To solve riddles, users will use objects found on the map. You will be able to combine things in your inventory, as well as interact with interactive interior elements. Gamers will have to make their way through various challenges, where the levels are divided into chapters. The game does not have a save system, but it is possible to continue the passage from an unlocked episode, where the narrative story is built in a linear manner.
