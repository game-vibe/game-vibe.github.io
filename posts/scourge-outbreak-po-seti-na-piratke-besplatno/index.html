Game Description#

Scourge: Outbreak invites you to go to the near future to play online with a friend on a pirate in a completely new setting, unlike other projects. The year is 2026, and the world is at the mercy of the Nogari Corporation. They are the creators of a completely new energy source called “Ambrosia”. And there are immediately those in the world who want to get this energy for themselves. The Echo squad falls under the control of gamers who will interact online via the Internet. This is an elite group of mercenaries hired specifically to carry out an operation against a powerful corporation. They have a number of tasks, and they will begin to complete them immediately after landing at their destination.

Scourge: Outbreak involves the use of standard shooter mechanics. The gamer moves through built locations, following from point A to point B. Depending on the construction of the level, there will be objects along the way that can be used as shelter (boxes, destroyed walls, etc.). Thanks to the radar located in the upper left corner of the screen, it becomes easier to navigate in space, and the gamer will immediately be able to detect opponents near him. By the way, they do not get involved in open battle, and try to stick to one tactic. So, we should expect that the fighters will try to go to the rear or push the allied detachment out of the point with suppressive fire. As for the arsenal, in most cases the trunks have all the usual properties. But some guns also have additional options, such as a power attack in an area, which allows you to deal damage to enemy units within the affected radius. Playing online involves cooperative play, where each user takes the place of a specific fighter. The possibility of free online play supports the option of simultaneous play for 4 people, as well as multiplayer for 8 people. In this mode, you can fight with each other, using the experience you earn to get new equipment. This will significantly increase efficiency in battle, which in turn will increase the chances of success.
