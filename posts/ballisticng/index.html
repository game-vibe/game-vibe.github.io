Game Description#

BallisticNG is a dynamic racing simulator in the setting of a sci-fi distant future, in which all available technologies have made an unprecedented leap thanks to the painstaking work of the best minds of the human race. In 2159, battles on the tracks take place in vehicles equipped with an anti-gravity unit that allows them to hover above the earthen surface. The game supports earning achievements from a pre-prepared list of 26 awards, a built-in level editor, a leaderboard, as well as modern technology - a virtual reality helmet. It helps you better understand the atmosphere of the game, created by an indie studio with a small staff. A feature of the gameplay is that vehicles are equipped with weapons of different calibers to destroy competitors.

Racing at crazy speed takes place on 35 tracks prepared in advance by the developers. In multiplayer and single-player races against artificial intelligence, the use of custom maps created by the community is allowed. The fleet consists of 16 ships hovering in the air, divided into 5 classes based on maximum speed. The single-player campaign is divided into 10 blocks, with a total of 98 events with different starting scenarios for an incredible level of gameplay variety. Multiplayer competitions are held in several ways: on one personal computer with a sufficient number of gamepads, over a local network, and with a constant, stable Internet connection. Players from all over the world will be able to play with each other, even using different PC operating systems.
