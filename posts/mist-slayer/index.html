Game Description#

Mist Slayer, an action roguelike game, puts you in the shoes of a time-traveling hero in a mystical war. Armed with unique weapons and skills, gamers find themselves in parallel universes, fight amazing opponents, collect bonuses and go on quests. You can play alone or in co-op with friends.

Mist Slayer features:

4 different characters, and even more hidden. 13 levels, including at least 36 randomly generated maps. A collection of over 10 bosses and a variety of enemies. Inventory of over 300 abilities, runes, items and advanced features. Various custom skins and constant updates.

Participants can choose from four characters, each with their own fighting style. The cowboy keeps his balance, the archer keeps his distance, the claymore delivers heavy blows, and the assassin puts agility first. Each character has a number of unique attacks and abilities, including dodging, defending, and parrying. The ability system is closely related to the philosophy of the five elements - metal, wood, water, fire and earth. These attributes are intertwined, allowing you to create original battle strategies by combining abilities. Hundreds of inscriptions, some of which have their own flaws, add extra spice to the battles. Ingenious combinations can lead to unexpected harmony, opening up space for innovative tactics.

Up to 4 gamers can participate in an online cooperative game: both friends and strangers. Special co-op missions and items increase the level of fun, enhancing the experience of what’s happening on screen. The attractiveness of the project is enhanced by the ability to unlock new characters and increase their level. As you move through the foggy world, you can discover new collections. A selection of over 300 abilities, runes, inscriptions and weapon sigils combined with an extensive character progression engine promises variety. Improved abilities gained through replaying make battles easier and more exciting. As an added treat, the game comes with a variety of cosmetic skins and accessories to customize your character’s aesthetics.
