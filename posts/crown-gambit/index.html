Game Description#

Crown Gambit puts a player in the kingdom called Meodred, possession, destabilized by the crisis of the throne of the throne. The balance of power in the state has been violated, and numerous applicants mobilize their troops to capture the crown, while the rebellion of commoners is gaining momentum. In the epicenter of these events, three inexperienced paladin are, forced to make decisions that will determine the future of the kingdom. The detachment consists of alisa, a fencer commanding blood; Hale, cleric with a mace; And Rollo, a tank using anchor as a weapon.

In Crown Gambit, the main task is to take an oath of fidelity to one of the applicants, directly contributing to its ascent to power through a series of tactical clashes. It is necessary to maneuver between rivals and tear off the conspiracies, while each plot choice has its own price and changes the narrative. Interaction with potential allies and opponents is crucial, since two -faces reign in the political situation, and no one can be trusted. These branched decisions form the principles of protagonists and direct events to one of the many possible finals.

All confrontations are resolved in near step -by -step card battle. The gameplay is focused on creating a unique deck of skills for each of the three characters in order to overcome various obstacles and withstand many enemies. During the battle, you can direct through the sacred relics a force known as the grace of ancestors in order to get a significant advantage over opponents. However, this ancient magic has a corrupting effect, and its use may have irreversible consequences for the souls of heroes.
