Game Description#

No, I’m not a Voenkom is a psychological thriller whose plot revolves around a sudden invasion of personal space. A sharp knock on the door becomes a catalyst for a whole series of tense events that test the protagonist’s endurance. Every interaction with the outside world becomes a difficult choice between trying to remain safe and risking succumbing to destructive curiosity. The oppressive atmosphere of an old apartment, where the air is saturated with the smell of dust and paint, increases the feeling of isolation. The main gameplay is tied to a constant feeling of anxiety, and chases through dark, abandoned places become a real test of composure, making the heart pound at a frantic rhythm.

The main character is an ordinary factory worker leading a joyless existence in an unremarkable small town. His life was a predictable routine, consisting of a stable job and mortgage payments at an extortionate 89% per annum for his own home. However, this gray stability collapses one gloomy evening. In No, I’m not a Voenkom, a familiar but worn-out and cluttered home becomes both the last bastion of safety and a death trap. Despite all the surrounding chaos, it is this cramped, uncomfortable space that feels like the only place that can be considered home.
