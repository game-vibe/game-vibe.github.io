Game Description#

Maktala: Slime Lootfest is an incremental project in which each battle opens up new ways to destroy countless hordes of opponents. To fine-tune and improve your character, a system for mining ore and processing precious stones is provided, allowing you to adapt the style of play to your liking. Significant support in battles is provided by a faithful archer, who independently enters the battle and exterminates enemies, being next to the main character. Among the hordes of monsters you can find rare, brilliant variations of creatures. Victory over such creatures is rewarded with special effects that strengthen the protagonist. The whole process requires active participation, since damage is controlled directly with the mouse, which distinguishes the title from classic idle projects.

The basis of development is obtaining equipment from defeated enemies. Rare items obtained in Maktala: Slime Lootfest can significantly increase a character’s combat power. In addition, to effectively destroy opponents, it is proposed to discover and improve various skills. Progression is built around a large-scale talent tree, where every node matters. Instead of trivial increases in damage, it opens up access to truly significant stat improvements. The combination of all these elements allows you to constantly find new approaches to exterminating slimes and more powerful creatures.
