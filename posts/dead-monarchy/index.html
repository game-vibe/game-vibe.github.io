Game Description#

Dead Monarchy is an addictive gameplay that puts you in the role of captain of a mercenary company, taking command and navigating a dangerous open world sandbox. This game offers a huge number of opportunities and dangers lurking around every corner. From fields to camps, caves to ruins, cemeteries to fortresses, the varied locations provide exciting environments for you to explore.

The combat system in Dead Monarchy is where the game really shines. With the ability to command up to 18 mercenaries, you must strategize and take into account various factors such as elevation, counterattacks, ranged combat, flanking and attack of opportunity. However, it’s not just about the tactical aspects; you also need to manage the morale of your soldiers. Witnessing the death of comrades or critical blows can break their resolve, so it is necessary to maintain their spirit.

Equipping your mercenaries with a wide selection of weapons and armor adds depth to the gameplay. Each item has its own unique models and characteristics, allowing you to customize your team to suit your preferred style. Plus, you can customize their benefits and characteristics, further increasing their versatility and strategic options. Advancing further means surviving increasingly challenging arenas that test your skills and resilience. Permanent death for your mercenaries and the risk of ending if your captain falls adds an intense level of consequence. The level of bravery is up to you: you can choose between manual saving and iron man mode.
