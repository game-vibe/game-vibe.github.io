Game Description#

Justin Wack and the Big Time Hack is a representative of the point-and-click adventure genre. It emulates classic 2D escape games. The video game is about time travel. The plot outline is as follows. Scientist Justin cannot fully recover from his breakup with Julia. One day, a portal appears due to a broken microwave. As a result of this incident, the scientist finds himself in the Stone Age, and the caveman Klut is transported to his time and takes the place of the hero. The protagonist discovers that in the past dinosaurs could talk, and people were still no different from animals. He decides to change the time flows so that the girl never leaves him. His activities attract the attention of harsh robots from the future, severely punishing anyone who tries to play with time.

In the video game Justin Whack and the Big Time Hack, all the heroes and heroines are voiced. The user receives three playable characters. You can switch between them. The plot is divided into 3 chapters. The user is forced to visit the same places three times. However, the settlement of old locations occurs in different time periods. Adventures are accompanied by humor. The gameplay here is traditional. You need to explore the area, collect useful items, use items from your inventory, chat with NPCs and solve relatively complex puzzles. Interactive objects are not highlighted, but the cursor changes when you hover over them. There is also a hint system. It is presented in the form of a woman, Daela, from the studio staff who created this indie project. This lady breaks the fourth wall and willingly shares advice.
