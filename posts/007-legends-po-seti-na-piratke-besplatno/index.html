Game Description#

On the pirate007 Legends you can play on the pirate for free, going through the storyline with a friend online. It is also possible to participate in other multi-user modes online via the Internet. The project was released in honor of the 50th anniversary of the James Bond franchise. Introduced online play and single player mode. The plot part reproduces six films about the hero, including Goldfinger, License to Kill, Die Another Day and others.

The gameplay places a huge emphasis on stealth mechanics. Enemies behave more suspiciously, reacting to any suspicious actions from the player. The possibility of free online play allows you to complete missions in co-op. Missions are available to quietly penetrate the enemy’s camp or missions with shelling on the run. There are three types of gadgets you can use: a smartphone, sleep darts, and a laser wristwatch. Almost every mission has transport control sections.

The combat system has been improved and the main character’s skills have been expanded. There are three difficulty levels to choose from. Local multiplayer allows up to four people to play in split-screen mode. The online mode of 007 Legends is designed for 12 players. There is a special modification called “double zeros”, taken from the Call Of Duty prestige system.

All maps from the single-player game are available in multiplayer. There is leveling that improves the hero’s abilities. The storyline includes Bomb Defuse, Escalation and Data Miner. The story starts with scenes from the movie Skyfall. After being wounded, Bond recalls his past adventures. They take you to different parts of famous paintings, inviting players to go through the hero’s entire legendary journey together with the hero.
