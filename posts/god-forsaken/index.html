Game Description#

GOD FORSAKEN is a dark adventure in which a handful of survivors must defy fate in a land abandoned by the gods. Having chosen his path as a mighty warrior, a mysterious warlock, an undead necromancer lord or an agile hunter, the protagonist gains a unique talent tree. Each battle against hordes of twisted monsters and powerful bosses is a test of not only reflexes, but also strategic thinking. In this world, where darkness gradually devours the light, the only salvation from madness is the flame of a torch, illuminating the road in the forbidden darkness of the night.

The environment in GOD FORSAKEN is fraught with not only hordes of enemies, but also many secrets. Exploring the long-forgotten ruins of ancient civilizations and dangerous underground labyrinths reveals mysterious documents scattered throughout, shedding light on the root cause of the all-consuming corruption. The equipment system offers huge variety with randomly generated item stats and hundreds of legendary artifacts.

The Fateful Monument guides the brave, and the Mirror of Fate opens the way for them, but each step deeper into darkness requires unimaginable sacrifices. The mechanics are based on free character development, which allows you to combine any weapon with a variety of starting skills. The combat experience is constantly evolving with a variety of unique skills and skill enhancement effects, reminiscent of a rune system that can be freely inlaid to enhance abilities. The hero will have to find the source of ancient evil, using all his power and cunning to snatch from the abyss at least a glimmer of hope for his people.
