Game Description#

Xenopurge puts you in the role of a Commander, controlling an elite squad from a remote command center. The main task is to eliminate aliens through indirect orders through the interfaces of several monitors. You will plan routes for fighters, set their priorities and determine tactics before they enter the combat zone. The team’s survival depends on this strategy and preparation, as you will have few direct ways to influence events once the operation begins.

Within each race in Xenopurge, the gamer will have the opportunity to recruit new fighters, each with their own initial tactical settings. You also equip your team with improved equipment, tailoring the unit to suit your leadership style. Missions take place on procedurally generated maps with different layouts of rooms and enemies, which ensures that each mission is unique. Each game session is designed to be completed in approximately 60 minutes and focuses on solving complex problems that require tactics.

The consequence of failure will be the final death of the members of your unit as part of the current mission. Whether the foray ends in success or complete failure, your command profile gains experience, unlocking new abilities and recruiting options for future operations. Your units operate in a dark, retro-futuristic setting, tracking down targets in closed corridors and facility rooms. The key challenge of this project is the need to be proactive rather than react to threats in real time.
