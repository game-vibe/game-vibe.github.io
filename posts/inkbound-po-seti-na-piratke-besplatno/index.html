Game Description#

Playing online with a friend inInkbound is a free way to immerse yourself in an online game where every word sparkles with dangerous magic. Over the internet online, you’ll be rescuing stories in the Athenaeum, a huge library containing countless stories. On the Pirate, unlock new classes, face unique threats and take on challenges that will require true skill from you.

The combat system differs from the traditional turn-based one. Here, players move freely and use abilities simultaneously in multiplayer mode. The pace of the game adapts to your needs - you can act with lightning speed when decisions are obvious, or slow down to strategize when the going gets tough.

Variety is the name of the game with Inkbound’s character classes. You’ll experiment with different options, constantly adjusting your approach mid-game to create the most powerful build. With abilities that can be summoned, skills that can be upgraded, and mountains of items that can be unlocked, you won’t be able to complete the game the same way.

Team up with friends or create new alliances in the Athenaeum - a social center where players are in full swing. Parties of four can take on challenges together, strategizing to create a team composition that will maximize their overall strength.

The stakes are high. The magical Athenaeum, the repository of every story ever written, is in danger. Its stories are driven by a mysterious force, and its inhabitants desperately need your help to save them. Will you be the hero who defends the written word?
