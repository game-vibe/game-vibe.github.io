Game Description#

Pro Cycling Manager 25 puts players in the role of team managers guiding the riders. The range of tasks includes selecting partners, recruiting cyclists and leading races on the tracks. The performance of these functions during the competitive season affects the final performance of the team. This project provides a framework for leading a professional cycling club with sporting goals.

In Pro Cycling Manager 25, attracting funding involves taking into account the criteria put forward by the funding parties. Conditions may relate to the geography of the investor’s activities, the ratio of young athletes in the composition, sporting achievements or the status of the only title sponsor. Leaders compare these provisions with the requests of these parties and the objectives of their association. This approach determines the financial and structural position of the sports association for the long term.

Finding sponsors requires continuous interaction and decision-making that determines the future development of the team. As the seasons progress, the circle of business connections expands, which encourages potential partners to send their proposals. The choice is between accepting current options or waiting for alternative proposals, since the interest of external structures may change. Managers have the right to enter into agreements with 1, 2 or 3 title sponsors separately, thereby determining the public naming of their roster. Game settings are unique to each session, allowing for a distinct experience.
