Game Description#

SWAPMEAT is a co-op roguelite shooter set on the strangest and most hostile planets in the galaxy. In this project, players will have to carry out raids on alien worlds inhabited by aggressive alien creatures in order to collect their flesh. A central mechanic is the ability to instantly replace your character’s body parts with organs borrowed from defeated enemies.

This approach allows you to constantly adapt to the combat situation, adopting the unique abilities of your opponents right during the battle. The process of survival on alien planets turns into a constant hunt for biomaterial. Each new body part, whether it’s legs for a triple jump or a torso that can deploy turrets, radically changes the tactical capabilities.

Opponents can rip off the hero’s limbs, but he is able to immediately respond in kind by appropriating enemy arms, legs, or even a head to use their power to his advantage. In SWAPMEAT, success depends on how quickly you can adapt to constantly changing conditions and enemies who seek to defeat intruders.

Key aspects of survival on alien worlds:

Foray into the depths of uncharted planets alone or in a squad of up to four members to complete missions together. Using a unique character modification system that allows you to replace body parts with captured organs and gain new abilities. Fight in third person with mutated aliens, each with their own unique body parts to capture. Collect valuable resources in the form of flesh to further strengthen and adapt your fighter to the growing threat from the local fauna.
