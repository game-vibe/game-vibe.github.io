Game Description#

Myths and Legends takes gamers into a world created 2,500 years ago where they can take part in various strategic activities. The title combines exploration, combat, trade and diplomacy, offering a new take on real-time strategy. Its design focuses on creating and managing city-states on procedurally generated islands, where no two playthroughs are the same. With a focus on resource management and tactical decision-making, users have a lot to think about as they try to build and maintain their power.

The world dates back to ancient history, specifically to 500 BC. This is where the magic happens - players interact with individual Greek factions, each with their own set of units and abilities. The factions are designed to feel authentic as they replicate the practices and strategies of ancient societies. On these islands, gamers will have to create a city-state capable of withstanding the challenges of the era, be it trade, alliances or war.

Forming pacts with neighboring factions or raiding enemy territories, the possibilities for expansion and conflict are endless. In Myths and Legends, every session is unique. The islands themselves are procedurally generated, allowing for a different experience each time you start a new game. Landscapes can vary greatly from one another, featuring a variety of biomes that can influence the strategies users must use to survive and thrive.
