Game Description#

Call of Sentinels plunges you headlong into an epic struggle against the forces of darkness. Choose your champion from a growing list, each with unique skills and fighting styles. Hone your combat skills against the relentless hordes in the Arena, or take on dangerous journeys across varied landscapes in Campaign. Unleash devastating combos and master tactical maneuvers to defeat treacherous enemies and colossal bosses. Each victory unlocks valuable upgrades, expanding the hero’s capabilities and shaping his legend. The fate of the world hangs in the balance. Will you answer the call?Five Heroes of Call of Sentinels: Stoic Guardian: Impenetrable defense and devastating strikes. Agile Bladedancer: Whirls through enemies with lightning-fast strikes. Sorcerer: Unleash devastating spells and manipulate the very fabric of reality. Stealthy Marksman: Precision and cunning from afar .Mysterious Alchemist: Poisons, potions and explosive surprises.

Test your strength against endless waves of enemies as you climb the career ladder and accumulate points. Embark on a dangerous journey across three different landscapes, each with their own challenges and secrets. Earn powerful upgrades by completing quests and defeating enemies. Customize your hero’s arsenal with a variety of weapons and equipment. Uncover the secrets of the Order of the Guardians and discover new heroes who will champion their cause.
