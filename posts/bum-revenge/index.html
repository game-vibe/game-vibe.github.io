Game Description#

Bum: Revenge is a first-person crime simulator centered on the life of a homeless person. The plot begins after the protagonist is beaten by his wife’s lover, Dennis. Waking up in the hospital with divorce papers, the hero spends 6 months on the street, becoming addicted to alcohol and drugs. Players enter this world with nothing, exploring the squalid environment of the city’s back streets. The main goal is to survive in new conditions and take revenge.

In Bum: Revenge, survival mechanics are directly tied to the use of substances. It is necessary to control the character’s cravings for them to maintain his performance and prevent withdrawal symptoms. To pay for the required alcohol and drugs, you will have to collect bottles, beg, and also engage in theft and deception. This game features 4-player online co-op mode, allowing teams to work together to complete missions and cause chaos in the open world.

The world map is divided into territories under the control of various factions. To expand influence, you will have to confront the bosses of each gang and defeat them, which will grant respect and control over their people. The fight for areas involves the use of created or found dirty weapons against rivals. In addition, the use of prohibited substances causes dynamic hallucinations that distort the perception of reality during gameplay. This adds a new dimension to the outdoor survival simulation.
