Game Description#

The Crosshair X software application for gamers will allow you to freely customize your sight in any way for games focused on shooter gameplay mechanics, and gives the final answer to the question: “how to make a sight for Rasta or CS GO?” Players can choose one of the presented presets to apply it or create their own based on it. The project has the ability to make the desired skin and size for the cursor from scratch, using a flexible editor with a settings window. The user can decide on the color, size, width and thickness of the lines that form the crosshair when firing. You can also configure how lines react to various conditions that will trigger the programmed pattern.

Thanks to Crosshair X, you will be able to achieve high and stable shooting accuracy when playing shooters like CS GO and Fortnite, as well as survival games in the spirit of Rust, where not all types of weapons have the coveted mark in the center of the monitor. Integration with Xbox and Game Bar technologies is available to select your preferred crosshair type. Blocks with the “Lines” section allow you to change the width and placement of the lines that will be displayed on the screen. It will be possible to make the stripes transparent or bright and contrasting in order to better stand out from the general gaming background. In the “Center dot” area you will be able to calibrate the parameters of the point that is located in the center. Next, users will be able to choose the color and size of the sight to achieve the desired result. It is necessary to collect convenient and unique contours that will not distract the eye, while giving an advantage over other competitors by individually adjusting the cursor elements.
