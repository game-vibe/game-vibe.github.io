Game Description#

A city planning simulator, civilization development and real-time strategy - Dawn of Man is one of the few games in the genre where human survival is presented in this form. For example, we start playing on behalf of the leader of a primitive tribe, and we need to get people to collect resources. Due to constant work, the brain begins to improve, and the first people appear in the world who think more or less intelligently.

The first period is the Stone Age. Going through all 10,000 years of evolutionary development of mankind, we will see how the emergence of a brain like ours took place. Yes, we will only reach the Iron Age, but this will not stop us from capturing such a history of development. The main activity in the game will be hunting. By killing mammoths, bison, deer, lions and rhinoceroses, we will take the bones to make new tools and weapons; collect meat to ensure our tribe survives as long as possible; cut skins to provide clothing for people.

The second direction of work is gathering. Berries, fruits, vegetables, wood, stone, iron ore - the earth gives all this free of charge. Over time, we will study planting technology so that people can begin to plant and grow entire crops. When constructing your first buildings, do not forget to build a warehouse to stock up on provisions for a rainy day. It is advisable to place some building resources here in case you want to move.

Expand your settlement by building houses, help people research new technologies, including the wheel, weapons and fortifications of that time. Don’t forget that our ancestors were greedy for huge structures, so put up huge statues so that they can at least pray to someone. Also, nature will be on your side if you start taking care of it from the early stages of the emergence of a rational person.
