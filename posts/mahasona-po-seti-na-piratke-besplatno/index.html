Game Description#

InMahasona you can either team up with random users or play online with a friend on pirate. Here, a group of teenagers are under control as they try to discover an ancient treasury. But there is only one problem - this treasury belongs to a demon named Mahasona, and he will not let his property be taken away so easily. Therefore, in order to achieve what you want and get hidden value, you need to coordinate your actions online online, as well as monitor what is happening in the game.

Mahasona is designed for team gameplay. Therefore, you can create a team in advance and go to the server, or start the search yourself, and then find yourself in the company of random users. As soon as the session has started, you need to select a character, after which the system will take the participants directly to the scene. Visibility in the location is very limited, so the value of the torches that the characters have increases. In addition, each teenager has his own unique ability, through which you can diversify the gameplay and simplify your search. It is worth noting that simply finding the place where the treasury is kept is not enough. To do this, you should first find several artifacts that will indicate the location of the most important thing. The possibility of free online play allows you to control not only children, but also the demon himself. He has a special skill that allows him to highlight all seekers in a small radius. His task is to prevent other gamers from taking a valuable item by catching them and setting up ambushes. Online play also allows for sequential role changes. So, someone who was previously a seeker will now take the form of a demon and will prevent others from fulfilling their plans.
