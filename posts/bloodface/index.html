Game Description#

Bloodface is an action role-playing game, the events of which unfold in a cruel medieval world engulfed in the flames of civil war. The protagonist will have to restore order using a merciless combat system with an emphasis on close combat. There is complete freedom to choose tactics: from secretly eliminating enemies to violent head-on collisions. An extensive arsenal of all kinds of weapons and a variety of combat techniques allow you to choose your individual style of play. In the process of delivering justice, the hero faces a difficult moral choice, balancing between the role of the people’s savior and the ghosts of his own dark past. To overcome all the challenges, you need to constantly improve your character, increasing his attributes and discovering new skills.

The cause of total chaos was a popular uprising against the tyranny of the monarchy. After the flight of the royal family and the dissolution of the army, bandit groups seized power on the streets, plunging the lands into anarchy. The process of exploring the world of Bloodface demonstrates the dark atmosphere of the ruthless Middle Ages, from the intricate city streets to the deep forest thickets. The environment feels alive with citizens going about their daily business, and for a deeper immersion you can:

engage in dialogues and trade with characters. watch the occasional street brawl. interact with animals in settlements.
