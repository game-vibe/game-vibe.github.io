Game Description#

A real cooking simulator - Cooking Simulator is an ambiguous opportunity to try yourself in the role of a real chef. You will be able to cook several hundred dishes, learn real recipes for most of the appetizers served, and much more. In order to give the game more realism, people who devoted themselves to cooking for several decades worked on the creation.

The game itself has about 80 complex recipes, but there are also simple ones, like “cooking tomato paste” or “onion rings for baking.” In addition, real ingredients have been added to the game, the grams of vegetables served and other things have been observed. There is too much realism in Kitchen Simulator to leave it behind. There is a professional kitchen around you. A real electric grill, several gas stoves, an oven, food processors, etc. Kitchen utensils also don’t leave you looking for something you need and writing to the developers about it. Several dozen frying pans, pots and other accessories, without which you will not be able to surprise your guest.

Features of the game: - Use fruits, vegetables, meat in cooking. Check for freshness by simply holding the product in your hands; - Use seasonings and watch how a delicious steak turns from a raw piece of meat into medium-rare beef; - The game follows real laws of physics for those who want to devote part of their life to cooking.
