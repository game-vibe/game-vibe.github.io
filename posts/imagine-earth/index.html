Game Description#

Imagine Earth is a sandbox strategy where the goal is to create your own race and further develop it. Humanity needs a lot of different resources for a normal life, but it will always want more. The map is three-dimensional, made in the form of a globe, rather than a flat surface, this allows you to observe your planet from any angle.

A special feature will be the realistic modulation of all kinds of disasters, both those that occurred due to human influence and due to nature. A forest may catch fire, a volcano will wake up and flood the surrounding areas with lava, due to global warming, glaciers will begin to melt, and much more. It is necessary to develop the planet carefully and not destroy organic matter at the very beginning of history.

The presented campaign mode allows you to visit 9 planets. According to the scenario, the race under your control is not the only one in the world; on one planet there can be up to 7 contenders for supremacy under the control of artificial intelligence. Not all of them will be peaceful; it is necessary to develop the infrastructure, represented by more than 50 buildings with 80 levels of pumping, for defense or attack on their possessions for overall victory.

The economic component of Imagine Earth is about collecting natural resources for your own development and building trade relations with other colonies to exchange needed supplies, such as money, electricity, oil, and so on. For the evolution of all spheres of life, a technology tree is provided; by gradually studying it, you can discover many new buildings and units.
