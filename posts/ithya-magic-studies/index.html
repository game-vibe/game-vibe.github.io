Game Description#

Ithya: Magic Studies is a game that works as a background utility combined with task aids. This project features hand-drawn animated environments designed to support focused work or creative pursuits. The title uses a fantasy setting for its visuals and overall theme. Basic features and settings are managed through a point-and-click interface.

Inside Ithya: Magic Studies, users have access to environment settings and a selection of available species. Gamers can open various backgrounds, such as forest landscapes or images of ancient cities, to use them as a visual background. In addition, it is possible to change the conditions in these locations, choosing daytime, nighttime or rainy weather. Ambient sounds complement 10 distinct music selections ready to listen to.

Customization allows you to decorate a virtual room by choosing decor. There is a collection of creatures: you can find 10 creatures called Elementals to populate your space. To organize things, there are built-in assistants: a case list manager and a timer using the pomodoro method. These features are presented as tools to assist while studying, writing, drawing, or planning board gaming sessions.
