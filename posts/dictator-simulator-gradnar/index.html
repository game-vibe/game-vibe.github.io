Game Description#

Dictator Simulator: Gradnar is a political simulator where the government of the fictional Eastern European state of Gradnar is associated with the constant threat of a coup. In the role of dictator Vyslan Gatskolki will have to determine the fate of an entire nation, maneuvering between the democratic vector of development and harsh autocracy. Each decision directly affects key indicators, including the economy, army loyalty and party support. Neglect of these systems inevitably leads to the overthrow of the regime. In addition to internal affairs, it is necessary to build a foreign policy, choosing between peaceful coexistence and aggressive campaigns. Non-linear dialogues open up new scenarios, leading to one of many endings.

Military conflicts in Dictator Simulator: Gradnar are resolved through intense card duels, where tactics and timely supply of the army play a decisive role. Successful campaigns allow you to annex captured lands, expanding the borders of the empire. However, power is not only about politics. Personal enrichment, collecting certificates for valuable items and storing treasures in a personal account are an integral part of the experience. To maintain control, the ruler must:

enter into alliances or provoke enemies to start a war. provide supplies to the army and carry out strategic strikes. accept or reject bribes, indulging personal greed.
