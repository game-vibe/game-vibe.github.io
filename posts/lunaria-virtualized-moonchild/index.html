Game Description#

LUNARiA -Virtualized Moonchild- is a narrative-driven adventure game that explores the intersection of virtual reality and human connection. The story centers on T-BIT, a high school student and exceptional gamer who finds himself in a mysterious virtual space known as the Moon World. Here he encounters an AI avatar named LUNAR-Q, who is the mascot of the lunar exploration project. The adventure focuses on the developing relationship between T-BIT and LUNAR-Q as they overcome the difficulties of the Lunar World.

Their interactions reveal deep themes of isolation, camaraderie and the search for understanding. The narrative unfolds through a series of dialogues and events that challenge the characters' ideas about reality and virtual existence. LUNARiA -Virtualized Moonchild- invites users to immerse themselves in an atmosphere that combines elements of adventure and introspection. Gameplay involves exploring the virtual environment, solving puzzles, and having meaningful conversations with LUNAR-Q.

The title aims to provide a thought-provoking journey that resonates with players on an emotional level, highlighting the potential of virtual space to create genuine human connections. As T-BIT and LUNAR-Q delve deeper into the lunar world, they encounter various challenges and obstacles. The game challenges gamers to test their skills and problem-solving abilities, from overcoming treacherous obstacles to solving complex puzzles. Along the way, they meet other characters, both human and artificial intelligence, who play an important role in their journey.
