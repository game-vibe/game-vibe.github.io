Game Description#

Virtual Surfing may be suitable for fans of sports games. The title offers to harness the sea and ocean waves. The project lives up to its name. This is an arcade surfing simulator. It boasts well-developed water physics. It has different weather conditions and several locations.

At the very beginning, the gamer is asked to enter a name. And then the user is offered to customize the appearance of his surfer (there is a choice of gender) and customize the board. You can create a personal design for it or choose a ready-made skin. It also allows you to determine what material it will consist of. Virtual Surfing has training, free mode and career. In it you need to participate in various competitions, complete challenges, unlock new levels, and receive achievements. Achievements earned during the passage are displayed in the main menu in the form of cups and other trophies. Most often, swims are timed. Before the timer expires, you need to catch a medium or high wave and ride it, while doing tricks and collecting bonuses.

You can control the athlete’s movements using a keyboard and mouse or a gamepad. The buttons are responsible for weight distribution, jumping and stuff like that. Here you can perform various tricks, perform combos and turn on time slowdown. It is possible to change camera angles.
