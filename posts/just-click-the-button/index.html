Game Description#

Just Click The Button is an unusual project where the central element is a single button, and pressing it multiple times launches a whole chain of gameplay transformations. The whole process comes down to a choice between endless development and the desire to achieve one of the four available endings. The path to each of the finals directly depends on the chosen strategy. The combination of acquired abilities determines the ease of passing and achieving a specific outcome, which encourages the deliberate formation of your style. You can purposefully select skills for a specific purpose, or you can open almost all skills, turning what is happening on the screen into real chaos.

The project is a synthesis of a clicker with the mechanics of an arena shooter and reverse bullet hell. Just Click The Button is based on constant evolution, driven by user actions. The development system offers several areas for strengthening.

Gameplay evolution:

Raise your character’s level to access new development branches. Unlock active abilities to directly impact gameplay. Receive passive skills that provide permanent bonuses. Automate the clicking process using special improvements. Modify a button by changing its functionality. Destroy waves of enemies in dynamic arena shooter mode. Choose a specific set of skills to reach the end. Complete the passage with one of four unique endings.
