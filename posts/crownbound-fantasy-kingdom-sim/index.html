Game Description#

Crownbound - Fantasy Kingdom Sim, whose central mechanic is the indirect control of subjects, immerses you in a world where adventurers have their own will. Warriors, mages, robbers and clerics roam the lands freely, guided by personal goals, fears and ambitions. These heroes are not faceless units; They gain experience in battles, discovering new skills and combat techniques. In their travels, they can find legendary swords and enchanted staves, but none of them are immune from permanent death. When one legend falls, a new recruit always rises to take its place, ready for greatness.

The ruler faces a complex task of systematic expansion and strategic strengthening of his kingdom. Warrior guilds, mage towers, and forges must be built to help adventurers unlock their potential through the research of powerful upgrades. In Crownbound - Fantasy Kingdom Sim, the prosperity of a power depends on the monarch’s ability to cope with invasions of monsters, repel sieges and reveal ancient secrets. All this is achieved by the forces of wayward mercenaries, who can be distracted by chasing squirrels instead of protecting the borders.

The monarch’s power is based not on orders, but on gold. Instead of direct control over units, as in traditional strategies, here you have to influence them through a system of rewards and generous rewards. Proper distribution of the treasury allows you to motivate heroes to destroy monsters and explore dungeons. The wisdom of a ruler determines whether his kingdom will prosper or fall into chaos due to unruly adventurers and greedy traders. The fate of the throne lies in the hands of the one who can inspire and manipulate his subjects, using the treasury as his main weapon.
