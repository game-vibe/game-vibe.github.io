Game Description#

Swords and Souls Legacy Collection brings together 5 individual games from the Flash era into one compilation. This collection offers a wide range of experiences, allowing you to fight for recognition in a special arena, become a wanted person on the run, or solve puzzles through special actions. You have to create heroes, systematically improve their parameters and interact with mechanics based on the principles of role-playing projects and racing action games.

Each title in the collection poses unique challenges, ranging from turn-based tactical battles to navigation using in-game physics. In Swords and Souls Legacy Collection, one of the projects is an RPG hybrid, tasking the player with creating a Soul and then improving its abilities through a variety of mini-game training. After careful preparation, the hero tests his strength in duels with a series of opponents in the arena, gradually striving to rise in the overall ranking of fighters.

The other two titles are fast-paced runners, offering the role of an agile robber traversing urban landscapes, jumping between buildings, avoiding traps and facing off against guards while collecting loot. Their continuation introduces multi-level locations, new enemies and encounters with unique bosses. A different experience is an action racing game where the challenge is to outwit pursuing police forces during a 42-mile drive to the border. A customizable car is under control, breaking through road barriers and fighting off airborne attacks along the entire route.
