Game Description#

Lumote: The Mastermote Chronicles is a game about two opposing forces, blue energy versus red energy. The ruler of the first is Lumoth, while only being able to jump and control the creatures of the bright and colorful three-dimensional world in which he travels, the second is controlled by Hypnomoth. With the help of the strange and unusual inhabitants of his world, using his abilities, Lumoth, step by step, captures platforms and makes his way through fantastic landscapes, solving many puzzles. Depending on who controls the local residents - the spendthrifts - they either help or hinder in the development of territories and the search for the main enemy.

The world in the game is divided into three biomes, access to each of which appears after you have gained control over all the towers of the previous one. Accessing and taking control of each tower means leveling up, mastering new mechanics, and beginning the next stage of testing. By the way, the towers are also a repository of information about the history of the world of mots, encrypted in pictograms. The game can be classified as an arcade, it is a 3D platformer, rich in puzzles, which can be played alone or together with someone who also wants to immerse themselves in the whimsical and colorful world of mots. To succeed in this game, it is important not to waste time and carefully think through every step. Well, having developed a strategy to take over the world, you just need to start acting!
