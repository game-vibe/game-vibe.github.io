Game Description#

VORON: Raven’s Story tells an emotional story about life and death from an unusual perspective. The main character is a raven who is destined to become a guide to lost souls, helping them find their way to a new world. Starting his journey as a chick, he gradually turns into a mythical creature from ancient legends. However, his very first mission turns into disaster when an ancient evil awakens, disrupting the established order. Now the feathered protagonist will have to not only fulfill his destiny, but also confront the darkness that threatens all existence, and also take care of his own family.

The basis of the adventure is the exploration of a small open world, created under the influence of Scandinavian mythology. Cozy and picturesque landscapes can hide both hidden secrets and serious challenges. The VORON: Raven’s Story project implements an interesting system for learning the Language of Souls: at first, the characters' speech is incomprehensible, but as you interact with them and find stone tablets with the legends of the world, the protagonist begins to understand their stories and requests, fulfilling the last wishes of the deceased.

Laws of flight and service to souls:

Freedom in the sky is achieved through a unique control system that allows you to independently decide when to flap your wings or fold them for a dive. Collisions do not lead to death, because the raven does not have a health bar - it is always ready to rise and continue on its way. Careful exploration of the environment is rewarded by the discovery of secret places and artifacts that shed light on the history of the world. For complete immersion in the role, there is a separate button responsible for the characteristic crow cry.
