Game Description#

The Dark Queen of Mortholme starts from the victory of the Land overlord over the invading hero. The conflict, it would seem, is exhausted, but the applicant is inexplicably returning, meeting the right death over and over again. This violence cycle takes place in a two -dimensional world with a fixed screen, where the player uses the queen’s arsenal to multiply pacify the insolent. The plot reveals the secret of the stubbornness of the antagonist, offering a short adventure combining action with a rich history.

With the continuation of the clashes of The Dark Queen of Mortholme, it introduces dialogue elections that shift the emphasis on the battles. Such interactions allow the gamer to study the point of view of the hero and the motives of his tireless attacks. Between the two opponents, a connection may arise, turning the same type of battles into something more complex and psychological. The storyline plunges into the topics of change and affection, forcing him to doubt the original relationship between the powerful queen and her, at first glance, an insignificant enemy.

The player’s decisions during dialogs directly affect the development of the plot and the final result. This game offers several endings depending on the choice: empathize with the hero or preserve the absolute power of the queen. Such a structure with a branching narrative challenges the established role of a protagonist as a final boss. As a result, the relationship of the two characters can evolve, leading to a denouement that differs from the original concept of an infinite confrontation.
