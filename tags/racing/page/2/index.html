SCHROTT

Release Date: 2025/11/14

Game Description SCHROTT is a roguelike racing game where performing crazy stunts and collecting valuable loot provide the basis for deep vehicle customization. Instead of ordinary machines, there are absurd designs that lend themselves to all sorts of improvements to wreak even more chaos. Each race turns into a hunt for resources that allow you to turn your car into a unique weapon of destruction on the road. Arcade controls make the gameplay accessible and intuitive from the first minutes, and high-speed action does not let you relax....

Assetto Corsa Rally

Release Date: 2025/11/13

Game Description Assetto Corsa Rally is an uncompromising simulator that challenges drivers to push powerful cars to their limits. Each track, be it the gravel roads of Wales or the asphalt serpentines of Alsace, is recreated using laser scanning technology. This approach made it possible to transfer the real width of roads, the profile of roadsides and all surface irregularities into virtual space with the highest accuracy. Each race turns into a tense test, where the slightest mistake due to the treacherous terrain can become fatal, and success depends on instant decisions and filigree driving techniques....

NASCAR 25

Release Date: 2025/11/12

Game Description NASCAR 25 takes you through the challenging journey from novice to motorsport legend. His career starts in a modest home garage, where he will prepare his first car for races in the ARCA Menards series. Gradually moving up the racing hierarchy, it will be possible to conquer the NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series, Xfinity Series championships and, finally, fight for the main cup. Success depends not only on skill on the track, but also on strategic decisions: hiring personnel, finding sponsors and developing new parts for the car, building its reputation through results and communication with the press....

Nitroglycerine!

Release Date: 2025/11/10

Game Description Nitroglycerine! is a test where the main task is to transport a deadly substance along difficult routes. The process of controlling a heavy tractor is subject to the laws of realistic physics, and the vehicle itself is completely unsuitable for overcoming obstacles along the way. Any mistake in piloting, sharp turn or collision can lead to the detonation of nitroglycerin and instant failure of the mission. Even climbing moderately steep slopes requires precise timing, and putting too much pressure on the engine via turbocharging risks stalling the engine....
