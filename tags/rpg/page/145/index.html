Starbound

Release Date: 2016/07/22

Game Description Starbound is a 2D side-scrolling indie RPG adventure with graphics that reference the classic 8-bit titles of the last century. The project has become widespread thanks to the ability to play with a friend in co-op mode on different platforms, as well as a well-developed system of achievements and rewards that motivate you to improve your own skills and collect all kinds of items for crafting and collecting. The surrounding world is presented in the form of open territories, where there are no prohibitions on colonizing unknown planets or performing secondary tasks; you can even not touch the story campaign at all....

Death Road to Canada

Release Date: 2016/07/21

Game Description Death Road to Canada is a zombie apocalypse game. It differs from other projects on this topic in its gameplay and humor. It’s a mixture of beat ‘em up and roguelike. The premise here is simple: in the USA, the dead began to rise from their graves and eat living people. After which their victims were also resurrected. The remainder of the population decided to move to Canada because this country was not affected by the epidemic....

Dead Age

Release Date: 2016/07/14

Game Description Dead Age has an excellent and non-linear story, consisting of many choices that lead to 6 different endings. You will have to carefully study the text of the tasks, otherwise the members of your squad of survivors will inexorably dwindle. The point of the game is to survive as many days as possible, replenishing food supplies, tools and new survivors. But don’t think that everything is so simple, in addition to collecting, the game has daily events that will fill your every new day in the game with various difficulties or opportunities....
