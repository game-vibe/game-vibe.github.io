Void Sols

Release Date: 2024/11/12

Game Description Void Sols places gamers in a minimalistic, top-down world that redefines the essence of soulslike gaming. The world is a huge, abstract labyrinth in which users will have to explore its depths and survive in harsh conditions. From the very beginning, you find yourself in a dark and confusing maze, where there is no map, no directions - only an inexorable desire to move forward. All you control is a triangle-shaped character, and there are no words to explain why you are here and what your goal is....

Everholm

Release Date: 2024/11/11

Game Description Everholm, a delightful indie game, is an RPG that seamlessly blends farming, friendship, and mystery. The plot centers on a girl, Lilly, who finds herself on a magical island, where everyone recognizes her, but her own memories elude her. As she delves deeper into the story, she discovers a startling truth - her sister, Melanie, has gone missing, and the island holds the key to her whereabouts. One of the hallmarks of Everholm is its open-ended nature, allowing players to determine the pace of their adventure....
