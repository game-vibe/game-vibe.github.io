Victory is achieved not by brute force, but by a competent selection of evolutionary improvements, turning each battle into a tactical puzzle. Release Date: 2025/11/03

Game Description Slaves of Magic is a tactical project where every decision on the battlefield has enormous weight. The appearance of enemy reinforcements is heralded by purple magical seals, and over time the enemy onslaught only intensifies, becoming more frequent and powerful. The combat system, based on sequential activation of units depending on their initiative, forces you to carefully plan every step.
