Singled Out

Release Date: 2019/09/04

Game Description Singled Out is a test of reaction speed and observation, where the task is to accurately identify a target in a crowd. The player is presented with a kaleidoscope of eccentric creatures, but only one of them matches the three descriptive characteristics provided. An inexorable ten-second timer forces you to act as quickly as possible, because any mistake or delay leads to failure. The search process turns into an intense visual puzzle, where every second counts and extreme concentration on the smallest details of appearance is required....
