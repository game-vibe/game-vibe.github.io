NeverAwake

Release Date: 2022/09/28

Game Description NeverAwake is a 2D side-scrolling action platformer with beautiful hand-drawn graphics about a girl named Rem. She is still a child of 10 years old and suffers from regular nightmares in which she strives to conquer everything that brought her resentment and pain in real life. Her opponents are not the classic monsters from banal dreams, but those who were somehow able to damage her psyche. These are school friends and just classmates, dentists who hurt when they pulled out teeth and even yard dogs that try to bite her every morning when she goes to school....
